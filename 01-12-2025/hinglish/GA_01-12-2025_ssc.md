## TOPIC 1: World AIDS Day 2025

### Kya hua?

- **1 December 2025** ko World AIDS Day manaya gaya.  
- 2025 ka global theme: **“Overcoming disruption, transforming the AIDS response”** (WHO/UNAIDS).  
- India level par campaigns me **prevention, testing, ART treatment, stigma reduction** par जोर diya gaya.  
- UNAIDS ke approx estimate: Bharat me **~24 lakh log HIV ke saath** jee rahe hain; adult (15–49 yrs) HIV prevalence ~**0.2%**.  

### SSC exam points:

- World AIDS Day: **1 December** (हर साल fix).  
- 2025 theme: **Overcoming disruption, transforming the AIDS response**.  
- PLHIV India (approx): **2.4 million**.  

**Memory Trick – “1 Dec = AIDS + Overcome & Transform”**

> Yaad rakho: **“1 December ko AIDS ka message – disruption ko Overcome karo, response ko Transform karo.”**

- India ke liye याद रखने layak numbers:

## TOPIC 5: Ponduru Khadi – GI tag

### Kya hua?

- **Ponduru Khadi** – आंध्र प्रदेश ke **Srikakulam** जिले ki ultra‑fine **hand‑spun, hand‑woven cotton khadi** hai.  
- Isko 2025 me **Geographical Indication (GI) tag** mila (certificate 2025 me, news December 2025 me चर्चित).  
- Ye khadi अपने **बहुत महीन धागे, मजबूती aur परंपरागत करघा तकनीक** ke liye जानी जाती hai.  

### SSC exam points:

- Ponduru Khadi kis state se? → **Andhra Pradesh**.  
- Kaun sa fabric? → **Hand‑spun, hand‑woven cotton khadi**.  
- GI tag – Ministry of Commerce ke under **GI Registry, Chennai** se.  

**Memory Trick – “Ponduru = AP Khadi”**

> Line: **“Ponduru ka kapda – Andhra ki special khadi.”**

## TOPIC 6: Siliserh Lake, Rajasthan – Ramsar Site

### Kya hua?

- Rajasthan ke **Alwar district** me स्थित **Siliserh Lake** ko 2025 me **Ramsar Site** declare kiya gaya.  
- Ye ek **human‑made freshwater lake** hai – 1845 me **Maharaja Vinay Singh** ne Alwar city ke drinking water ke liye banvaya tha.  
- Ye **Sariska Tiger Reserve** ke buffer zone me aata hai; bird‑watching, biodiversity aur groundwater recharge ke liye important wetland hai.  
- Is designation ke baad India ke Ramsar sites ki sankhya **~96** ho gayi, aur Rajasthan ke Ramsar sites ~5.  

### SSC exam points:

- Siliserh Lake – **Alwar, Rajasthan**, human‑made freshwater lake.  
- Built in **1845** by **Maharaja Vinay Singh**.  
- Ramsar site → **wetland of international importance**.  

**Memory Trick – “Siliserh = Sariska ka Saathi”**

> Line: **“Sariska ke bagal me Siliserh – Rajasthan ka Ramsar her.”**
