## TOPIC 1: World AIDS Day 2025 – “Overcoming disruption, transforming the AIDS response”

### Kya hua?

- **1 December 2025** ko poori duniya me **World AIDS Day** observe hua.  
- 2025 ka **global theme (WHO/UNAIDS): “Overcoming disruption, transforming the AIDS response”**.  
- India me Union Health Ministry ne **Vigyan Bhawan, New Delhi** me national event kiya – HIV prevention, testing, treatment par focus.  
- **UNAIDS estimate** ke mutabik India me approx **2.4 million (24 lakh) log HIV ke saath jee rahe hain**, adult (15–49 yrs) prevalence ~**0.2%**.  
- ART coverage ke kaaran AIDS‑related deaths last 10–15 saal me kaafi gire, lekin key populations me risk ab bhi high hai.  
- (Official source: WHO – https://www.who.int/campaigns/world-aids-day/2025, UNAIDS/UNAIDS India country data)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Health sector = **human capital & productivity** → long‑term growth, labour force quality.  
- Govt ka health spend, NHM/NACO projects → **budget, fiscal policy, social sector schemes** ke questions me link hota hai.  
- MCQ angle:  
  - World AIDS Day kab? → **1 December**.  
  - Theme 2025? → **“Overcoming disruption, transforming the AIDS response.”**  
  - India HIV numbers (approx) – 24 lakh PLHIV.

### Memory Trick – “AIDS 2025: Overcome & Transform”

> Line yaad rakho: **“2025 me AIDS ka message – disruption ko overcome karo, response ko transform karo.”**

Code:  
- **O–T** = **Overcome – Transform** → AIDS response ka **O.T.** operation.

## TOPIC 2: World Inequality Report 2026 – Global & India Picture

### Kya hua?

- **World Inequality Lab** ne **World Inequality Report 2026** release ki – 2025 ke aas‑paas ka global wealth & income distribution data.  
- Global level par:  
  - **Top 10% logon ke paas ~75% global wealth**,  
  - **Bottom 50% ke paas sirf ~2% wealth**.  
- India ke liye report ke key numbers:  
  - **Top 1% ke paas ~40% national wealth**,  
  - **Top 10% ke paas ~65% wealth**,  
  - **Top 10% income ka ~58% lete hain**, bottom 50% income share ~15% ke aas‑paas.  
- Report ka message: inequality **policy choices** ka result hai, koi natural law nahi.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Banking & economy me **inequality, inclusive growth, financial inclusion** core themes hain.  
- Mains answer me data quote kar sakte ho:  
  - “India me top 1% ke paas ~40% wealth hai – banking system ko inclusive finance, PSL, small borrower credit par zyada focus karna hoga.”  
- MCQ me:  
  - “Global top 10% wealth share approx?” → **75%**.  
  - “India me top 1% wealth share approx?” → **40%**.

### Memory Trick – “10–75, 1–40, 10–65”

> Ek code line banao: **“Duniya ke 10 logon me se 1 log 75% paisa pakde hai; India me top 1 log 40% aur top 10 log 65% wealth pakde hain.”**

Numbers yaad:  
- **World:** 10 → 75%  
- **India wealth:** 1 → 40%, 10 → 65%.

## TOPIC 3: Women, Peace & Security (WPS) Index 2025/26 – India Rank 131

### Kya hua?

- **Georgetown Institute for Women, Peace and Security (GIWPS)** + **PRIO** ne **WPS Index 2025/26** publish kiya.  
- Index 3 pillars measure karta hai: **Inclusion, Justice, Security for women**.  
- **Denmark** top par (Rank 1, score ~0.939).  
- **India ka rank: 131 out of 181 countries**, score ~0.607 – matlab inclusion, justice, security me significant gaps.  

(Official report: https://giwps.georgetown.edu/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/WPS-Index-2025-Report.pdf)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Bank me **gender diversity, women SHG, microfinance, mudra, financial inclusion** sab exam favourite topics.  
- Mains me: “Discuss India’s performance on gender indices” – wahan **WPS Index 2025/26 me India 131st** likh sakte ho.  
- MCQ:

  - Top country? → **Denmark**.

Code: **D‑1, I‑131**.

## TOPIC 5: Ponduru Khadi – GI Tag for Fine Cotton from Andhra Pradesh

### Kya hua?

- **Ponduru Khadi** – Andhra Pradesh ke **Srikakulam district** ki ultra‑fine hand‑spun, hand‑woven cotton khadi.  
- **Geographical Indication (GI) tag** 2025 me register hua (certificate Nov 2025 me, public reporting mid‑Dec 2025).  
- Khadi ki speciality:  
  - Local desi cotton varieties se bana **bahut hi ultra‑fine, durable fabric**.  
  - Traditional **charkha spinning & handloom weaving** techniques.  
- GI Registration: **Geographical Indications Registry, Chennai** (under Ministry of Commerce & Industry).

(Reference: Drishti IAS, TOI coverage Dec 2025)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- GI tags = **rural livelihood, exports, branding**; khadi = **MSME, cottage industry, financial inclusion**.  
- Regional rural banks / PSBs ko aise clusters me **credit, working capital, marketing support** dene ka scope.  
- MCQ:  
  - “Ponduru Khadi GI tag kis state ka product hai?” → **Andhra Pradesh**.  
  - “Khadi kis type ka fabric?” → **hand‑spun, hand‑woven cotton**.

### Memory Trick – “Ponduru = Premium Khadi of AP”

> Line: **“Ponduru ka kapda – Andhra ka ultra‑fine khadi padar.”**

Code: **Ponduru–AP–Khadi** (3 words tightly linked).
