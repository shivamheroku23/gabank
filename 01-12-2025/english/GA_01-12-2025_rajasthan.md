## TOPIC 1: Siliserh Lake – New Ramsar Site

### Kya hua?

- In 2025, **Siliserh Lake** in **Alwar district, Rajasthan** was designated as a **Ramsar Site** (wetland of international importance).  
- It is a **human‑made freshwater lake**, constructed in **1845** by **Maharaja Vinay Singh** to supply drinking water to Alwar city.  
- The lake lies in the **buffer zone of Sariska Tiger Reserve** and is important for birds, aquatic biodiversity and groundwater recharge.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Direct question: latest Ramsar Site of Rajasthan? → **Siliserh Lake (Alwar)**.  
- Construction year – **1845**, by **Maharaja Vinay Singh**.  
- Important static link with **Sariska Tiger Reserve, wetland ecology, bird habitat and tourism**.

**Memory Trick – “Sariska ka Saathi – Siliserh”**

> Imagine: **“Siliserh sits beside Sariska – wetland friend of the tiger landscape.”**

## TOPIC 2: Rajasthan Green Budget 2025‑26 – Solar Didi & Free Power

### Kya hua?

- Rajasthan presented a **Green Budget of around ₹27,800 crore** for 2025‑26 focusing on climate‑friendly measures.  
- Key highlights (short):  
  - Shift **government offices and PHED pumping stations** gradually to **solar power** (Hybrid Annuity Model).  
  - Provide **free induction cooktops** to about **1 lakh families** to promote clean cooking.  
  - Launch **“Solar Didi” programme** – train around **25,000 women from self‑help groups (SHGs)** in solar installation and maintenance.  
  - Expand **Chief Minister Free Electricity Scheme** – free power limit for poor families raised from **100 units to 150 units per month**.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Budget‑based questions:  
  - Focus areas – **renewable energy, clean cooking, eco‑cities, circular economy**.  
  - “Solar Didi” = women SHG + solar skill, 150 free units of electricity.  
- Useful example in RAS mains answers on **green growth, women empowerment, energy security**.

**Memory Trick – “3S–1F Green Budget”**

> Code: **3S–1F**  
> - **S – Solar offices (govt offices on solar)**  
> - **S – Solar Didi (women SHGs in solar)**  
> - **S – Safe induction stove (clean cooking)**  
> - **F – Free 150 units of power**

## TOPIC 3: Rajasthan Krishi Micro‑Irrigation Subsidy 2025

### Kya hua?

- Rajasthan government continued its **micro‑irrigation (drip & sprinkler)** drive under the **Krishi Subsidy 2025**.  
- Aim: replace traditional **flood irrigation** with **water‑saving technology**, especially in drought‑prone regions.  
- As per typical scheme structure (from compilations):  
  - **Small and marginal farmers** – subsidy up to **about 70% of system cost**.  
  - Other farmers – subsidy around **50–55%**.  
- Application is largely through an **online portal**, with field verification and direct benefit transfer (DBT) to the farmer’s bank account.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Rajasthan is water‑scarce; **micro‑irrigation schemes** are frequently asked.  
- Questions may focus on **subsidy percentage, beneficiaries (small/marginal vs others), and DBT mechanism**.

**Memory Trick – “70–50, paani ki bachat pakki”**

> Line: **“70% for small, 50–55% for others – drip saves water, boosts yield.”**

## TOPIC 4: Mukhyamantri Ayushman Arogya (MMA) Yojana – Portability

### Kya hua?

- Rajasthan’s state version of Ayushman Bharat PM‑JAY is **“Mukhyamantri Ayushman Arogya (MMA) Yojana”**, offering high financial protection for health.  
- By 2025, the scheme’s key highlight was **portability**:  
  - Beneficiaries can avail **cashless treatment outside Rajasthan** as well, in empanelled hospitals of other states.  
- Between April and November 2025, about **7,900+ patients** reportedly took treatment outside Rajasthan under portability, with treatment value running into several crores.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Major state health insurance scheme:  
  - Questions: name, nature (health insurance), meaning of portability, beneficiary coverage.  
- For Police/REET/Patwari level, direct question:  
  - “MMA Yojana relates to which sector?” → **Health insurance / cashless treatment**.

**Memory Trick – “MMA = Medical Move Anywhere”**

> Think: **“With an MMA card, a Rajasthan beneficiary can medically ‘move anywhere’ in India for cashless treatment.”**

## TOPIC 5: Payments for 12 Welfare Schemes – From 15 December 2025

### Kya hua?

- As per media reports, the Rajasthan government started **crediting payments for 12 welfare schemes** from **15 December 2025**.  
- The cluster includes schemes like **old‑age pensions, widow assistance, disability pensions and farmer/labour support**, etc.  
- Idea: **clustered payments on one date** for better timeliness and easier monitoring.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Date‑based fact: **15 December 2025** – start of clustered payments for 12 schemes.  
- Useful one‑liner in social security/DBT questions.

**Memory Trick – “15 December = 12 schemes ki salary”**

## TOPIC 6: Rising Rajasthan MoUs – ₹9 Lakh Crore Projects Grounding

### Kya hua?

- After the **Rising Rajasthan Global Investors Summit (Dec 2024)**, the state set a target to **ground (start implementation of) projects worth about ₹9 lakh crore by December 2025**.  
- Review reports suggest that by late 2025, projects worth around **₹7 lakh crore** had already entered implementation phase.  
- To speed up implementation, the state actively pushed **RIICO’s Direct Land Allotment Scheme–2025**.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- For economy/industry questions:  
  - Target value – **₹9 lakh crore**, timeline – **by December 2025**.  
  - Role of **RIICO** and land allotment in industrialisation.
