## TOPIC 1: World AIDS Day 2025

### What happened?

- **1 December 2025** was observed as **World AIDS Day** across the globe.  
- The **global theme** for 2025 was: **“Overcoming disruption, transforming the AIDS response”** (WHO/UNAIDS).  
- In India, campaigns focused on **prevention, testing, ART treatment and stigma reduction**.  
- According to UNAIDS, India has around **2.4 million people living with HIV (PLHIV)**; adult (15–49) prevalence is about **0.2%**.

### SSC exam points:

- World AIDS Day: **1 December** (fixed date).  
- Theme 2025: **“Overcoming disruption, transforming the AIDS response”**.  
- India PLHIV (approx): **2.4 million**.

**Memory Trick – “1 Dec = AIDS + Overcome–Transform”**

> “On 1 December, the AIDS message is – overcome disruption, transform the response.”

## TOPIC 2: World Inequality Report 2026

### What happened?

- The **World Inequality Report 2026** was released by the **World Inequality Lab**.  
- Globally:  
  - The **top 10%** own about **75% of global wealth**,  
  - The **bottom 50%** own only about **2%**.  
- For India:  
  - **Top 1%** hold about **40% of national wealth**,  
  - **Top 10%** hold about **65% of wealth**,  
  - **Top 10%** receive about **58% of national income**.

### SSC exam points:

- Theme: **economic inequality (income & wealth)**; publisher: **World Inequality Lab**.  
- Key India numbers:  
  - Top 1% wealth share ≈ **40%**; top 10% ≈ **65%**.

**Memory Trick – “10–75, 1–40, 10–65”**

> “Worldwide 10% people hold 75% wealth; in India 1% hold 40% and 10% hold 65% wealth.”

## TOPIC 5: Ponduru Khadi – GI tag

### What happened?

- **Ponduru Khadi** is traditional, ultra‑fine **hand‑spun, hand‑woven cotton khadi** from **Srikakulam district, Andhra Pradesh**.  
- It received a **Geographical Indication (GI) tag** in 2025.  
- It is famous for very fine yarn made from local desi cotton varieties and traditional charkha and handloom techniques.

### SSC exam points:

- State: **Andhra Pradesh**.  
- Product type: **Hand‑spun, hand‑woven cotton khadi**.  
- GI tag – registered with the **GI Registry, Chennai**.

**Memory Trick – “Ponduru = AP khadi”**

> “Ponduru cloth is Andhra’s premium khadi.”

## TOPIC 6: Siliserh Lake, Alwar – Ramsar Site

### What happened?

- **Siliserh Lake** in **Alwar district, Rajasthan** was designated as a **Ramsar Site** in 2025.  
- It is a **human‑made freshwater lake**, built in **1845** by **Maharaja Vinay Singh** to supply drinking water to Alwar city.  
- The lake lies in the **buffer zone of Sariska Tiger Reserve** and is important for birds, biodiversity and groundwater recharge.

### SSC exam points:

- Siliserh Lake – **Alwar, Rajasthan**.  
- Built in **1845** by **Maharaja Vinay Singh**.  
- Now recognised as a **Ramsar wetland of international importance**.

**Memory Trick – “Siliserh, Sariska’s partner”**

> “Siliserh is Sariska’s partner – Rajasthan’s Ramsar treasure.”
