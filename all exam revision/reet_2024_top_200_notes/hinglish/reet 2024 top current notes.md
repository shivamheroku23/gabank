## A. Rajasthan Budget, Economy &amp; Schemes

- Rajasthan ka **first Green Budget 2025–26** Dy CM &amp; Finance Minister **Diya Kumari** ne present kiya, total size approx **₹5.37 lakh crore**.
- Green Budget ka main focus: **Bijli (energy), Sadak (infrastructure), Paani (water), environment‑friendly projects**.
- Budget me **6,400 MW extra power capacity** add karne ka target rakha gaya (green energy push).
- Rajasthan me **9 Greenfield expressways** banane ke liye approx **₹60,000 crore** ka provision Green Budget 2025 me diya gaya.
- Urban sanitation projects (sewerage, solid waste management, pink toilets) ke liye approx **₹12,050 crore** allocate kiye gaye.
- Rajasthan Green Budget me **Ram Jal Setu Link** project ke liye lagbhag **₹9,300 crore** ka provision hai (irrigation + drinking water).
- Drip irrigation ko promote karne ke liye Green Budget 2025 me approx **₹1,250 crore** rakhe gaye.
- Rajasthan ka Green Budget 2025 India ke **state‑level green budgeting** ke strong examples me count kiya ja raha hai.
- Green Budget ka ek important objective – **renewable energy projects** (solar, wind) ke through Rajasthan ko “green energy hub” banana.
- Budget 2025–26 me fiscal discipline ke saath‑saath **capital expenditure** (infrastructure par kharch) ko bhi priority di gayi.
- Rajasthan ne apne Green Budget me **climate‑resilient infrastructure** (flood control, drought management) par bhi focus kiya.
- Rajasthan budget me **women‑centric schemes** (health, safety, enterprise support) ke liye alag allocations rakhe gaye.
- Budget me **youth &amp; skill development** ke liye IT training centres, coaching support, start‑up incubation ke proposals aaye.
- Green Budget ka ek aim – **electric mobility** ko promote karna (e‑buses, charging infrastructure) specially urban areas me.
- Rajasthan ne Budget 2025 me **tourism circuit development** (desert, heritage, eco‑tourism) ke liye bhi funds allocate kiye.
- Budget me **agriculture diversification** (horticulture, medicinal plants, organic farming) ka bhi focus mention hua.
- Rajasthan Budget 2025 me **MSME support** – interest subsidy, capital subsidy, aur land allotment ke simplified norms include kiye gaye.
- Budget me **health infrastructure** – new medical colleges, nursing colleges, diagnostic centres ke expansion par bhi जोर diya gaya.
- Green Budget concept se examiner question bana sakta hai: “Rajasthan ka first Green Budget kab, kisne, approx kitne amount ka?”

## B. Investment, Industry &amp; Infrastructure – Rajasthan Specific

- September 2025 me PM Narendra Modi ne Rajasthan ke liye approx **₹1.22 lakh crore** ka bada development package announce kiya.
- PM package ka focus: **clean energy, tribal upliftment, connectivity, welfare schemes** – specially tribal regions ke liye.
- **Mahi Banswara Atomic Power Project** is package ka ek major component hai (nuclear power development).
- Package me Rajasthan ke Jaisalmer, Jalore, Sikar, Phalodi jaisi jagahon me **large‑scale solar projects** ka plan include hai.
- Transmission infrastructure ke liye is package me approx **₹13,180 crore** rakhe gaye (power evacuation ke liye important).
- December 2024 me PM ne Rajasthan me **9 projects** (~₹11,000 crore) inaugurate kiye aur **15 projects** (~₹35,300 crore) ka shilanyas kiya.
- In December projects me **Ramgarh Barrage &amp; Mahalpur Barrage** jaise bade water projects bhi shamil the.
- Package me govt office buildings par **rooftop solar plants** lagane ka bhi decision liya gaya (green governance).
- Rajasthan Govt ne Mines &amp; Petroleum sector me **Rising Rajasthan Pre‑Summit** ke dauran approx **₹63,463 crore** ke MoUs sign kiye.
- In MoUs ka main aim: **industrial investment, job creation, aur mining‑based value addition** ko push karna.
- CM **Bhajan Lal Sharma** ne investors ko upcoming **Rising Rajasthan Global Investment Summit** me actively participate karne ka invite diya.
- Rajasthan Govt ne **Rajasthan Investment Promotion Scheme (RIPS) 2024** launch ki – sunrise sectors ke liye enhanced incentives ke saath.
- RIPS 2024 me **tourism, IT/ITeS, manufacturing, services, women‑led start‑ups** ko special benefits diye gaye.
- RIPS 2024 ka main focus: **new investment attract karna + existing industries ke expansion ko support** karna.
- Rajasthan Govt ne Dubai me investment promotion meet organize kiya – **Rising Rajasthan 2024 summit** ke liye global investors ko pitch kiya.
- Dubai roadshow me **ease of doing business, low cost of operations, land availability, solar potential** highlight kiya gaya.
- Investment promotion meet ko **Industries Minister Col. Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore** ne lead kiya.
- Rajasthan Govt ne **Vedanta group** ke saath milkar state me over **₹1 lakh crore** investment plan announce kiya.
- Vedanta ke under **Hindustan Zinc Limited** approx **₹30,000 crore** invest karega – zinc capacity double + fertiliser plant ke liye.
- Vedanta ke under **Cairn Oil &amp; Gas** approx **₹35,000 crore** invest karega – oil &amp; gas production capacity badhane ke liye.
- Vedanta ka yeh plan approx **2,00,000 jobs** aur **500+ downstream industries** create karne ki potential rakhta hai.
- Renewable energy transmission ke liye **Apraava Energy** ko Rajasthan me ek interstate transmission project award hua.
- Apraava project me approx **200 km** ki transmission line aur ek pooling station banega, 5.5 GW RE power evacuate karne ke liye.
- Apraava Energy ka headquarter **Mumbai** me hai – exam me “HQ‑based” question aa sakta hai.
- Rajasthan ke **Aandhi village (Jaipur district)** me green technology use karke zero‑waste model develop kiya ja raha hai.
- Aandhi village me **100 kg capacity** ka organic waste bio‑methanation plant government school me install hai.
- Aandhi model me **vermi‑filtration, constructed wetlands aur resource recovery centre** se wastewater &amp; solid waste treat kiya jata hai.
- Zero‑waste village concept ka aim: **waste ko resource me convert karna + environment friendly rural development**.
- Jaipur ko 2025 me **25th IIFA Awards (Silver Jubilee edition)** ka host city choose kiya gaya.
- Rajasthan Tourism Department ne IIFA ke saath MoU sign kiya – is se state ka **film tourism aur branding** strong hoga.
- Jaipur IIFA 2025 me **IIFA Digital Awards** naam se ek naya category introduce hoga, OTT &amp; digital content ke liye.
- Rajasthan me chal raha yeh investment wave exam ke liye important hai – “Rising Rajasthan”, “RIPS 2024”, “Vedanta investment” etc. pe questions aa sakte hain.

## C. Governance, Digital Reforms &amp; Rights (Rajasthan)

- Rajasthan desh ka **pehla state** बना जिसने voter rolls (electoral rolls) ki **100% digitisation** complete ki.
- Digitisation **Special Intensive Revision** exercise ke under ki gayi – exam me yeh term aasakta hai.
- Rajasthan ne voter mapping approx **97%** tak achieve ki – booth level par voters ka accurate digital mapping hua.
- Digitised rolls ki wajah se claims‑objections phase me per booth approx **30 voters** ke documents tak hi limit reh gayi.
- Digital voter rolls se **duplicate entries, fake voters aur repeated verification** problems me kaafi reduction aayi.
- Rajasthan ne election management me **Booth Level Officers (BLOs)** ko central role diya – unke ground work se mapping successful hui.
- Rajasthan Govt ne RTI movement ke itihaas ko celebrate karne ke liye **Beawar** me **RTI Museum** ki foundation stone rakhi.
- Beawar ko **RTI movement ki janmabhoomi** maana jata hai – exam me place‑based question possible.
- RTI Museum ka aim: **RTI history document karna, case studies preserve karna, rights‑based movements ko support karna**.
- RTI Museum event me speakers ne Rajasthan me approx **13.5 lakh** logon ki pension digital issues ki wajah se band hone ka issue highlight kiya.
- Ye discussion **digital exclusion** aur welfare schemes ke beech link ko highlight karta hai – important governance angle.
- Rajasthan Govt ne Ajmer ke **Foy Sagar Lake** ka naam badalkar **“Varun Sagar”** rakha.
- Ajmer ke **King Edward Memorial Bhawan** ka naam badalkar **“Maharishi Dayanand Vishrant Grih”** rkha gaya.
- Ajmer Municipal Corporation ne in renaming ke official orders issue kiye – decolonisation of public spaces ke example ke roop me.
- Varun Sagar ke paas **ghat aur Lord Varun ki murti** banane ka plan hai – religious &amp; cultural connect ke saath.
- Rajasthan ne 2025 me **Pravasi Rajasthani Diwas** Jaipur me organise kiya – NR Rajasthanis ke saath connect + investment attraction ka event.
- Pravasi Rajasthani Diwas me approx **₹1 lakh crore** ke projects/proposals showcase hue.
- Pravasi Rajasthani Diwas me CM Bhajan Lal Sharma aur Union Minister **Piyush Goyal** ne Rajasthan ki investment potential highlight ki.
- Pravasi Rajasthani Diwas ka main theme: **“Cultural roots strong karo, development me contribution badhao”** – exam me conceptual question possible.
- Ajmer ne **All India Kinnar Mahasammelan** host kiya – 10‑day conference, approx **2000 participants** transgender community ke.
- Kinnar Mahasammelan **Anita Bai** ki memory me organise hua – social inclusion aur third gender rights ka major event.
- Is event me **Bahuchara Mata** ki pooja hui – cultural + religious aspect, exam me “which goddess” type ka question aasakta hai.
- Conference me medical, banking, counselling jaisi services ke stalls lage – transgender community ke empowerment ke liye.
- Rajasthan Govt ne **e‑governance** ko push karne ke liye health sector me **e‑Swasthya Samvad** digital platform launch kiya.
- e‑Swasthya Samvad under, week me **do baar** (Tuesday &amp; Thursday) video conferencing meetings conduct hoti hain.
- e‑Swasthya Samvad sessions me liye gaye decisions par **72 ghanton ke andar ATR (Action Taken Report)** file karna compulsory hai.
- Platform ka aim: **medical colleges, hospitals, staff &amp; students** ke beech coordination aur accountability improve karna.
- Rajasthan ka governance model ab **digital + participatory + rights‑based** direction me move kar raha hai – REET ke liye summary ya conceptual question ban sakta hai.

## D. Environment, Climate &amp; Infra – Rajasthan Focus

- Rajasthan ka **first Namo Biodiversity Park (Namo Van)** **Pratap Bandh, Alwar** me inaugurated hua.
- Namo Biodiversity Park ko **“green lung”** ke roop me design kiya gaya – local air quality improve karne ke liye.
- Ye park **urban green cover** badhane, ecological balance restore karne aur citizens ko nature se connect karne ka medium hai.
- Namo Biodiversity Park ka inauguration Union Environment, Forest &amp; Climate Change Minister **Bhupender Yadav** ne kiya.
- Park me local flora fauna ko conserve karne ke saath‑saath awareness trails, eco‑education zone banane ka plan hai.
- Aandhi gaon ka zero‑waste model environment ke liye **best practice case study** ban raha hai.
- Bio‑methanation plant me food &amp; agro waste se **biogas + electricity** generate hoti hai – school ke liye sustainable energy source.
- Vermifiltration &amp; constructed wetlands se wastewater ko treat karke **reuse** kiya jata hai (irrigation ya greenery ke liye).
- Rajasthan ke desert areas me solar &amp; wind energy projects se **clean energy + local employment** dono create ho rahe hain.
- 2,000 MW wala solar park project Rajasthan ke “**renewable powerhouse**” status ko aur strong karega.
- Rajasthan me Apraava Energy ka 5.5 GW RE evacuation project national grid ke **green transition** me critical role play karega.
- Rajasthan ka Green Budget climate adaptation (flood, drought) ke saath mitigation (renewables, green infra) par balancing focus deta hai.
- Forest &amp; biodiversity conservation ke liye Rajasthan me **new eco‑parks, safari zones, reserve upgradation** jaisi announcements bhi hui hain.
- Climate change se impacted communities (farmers, pastoralists) ke liye drought‑proofing aur water harvesting schemes ka विस्तार ho raha hai.

## E. Education, Health &amp; Social Sectors – Rajasthan

- Rajasthan ne **medical education Hindi medium** me start karke national level par unique initiative liya.
- Hindi medium MBBS initially **Dr Sampurnanand Medical College, Jodhpur** aur **Barmer Medical College** me shuru hua.
- Ye MBBS courses **Marwar Medical University** se affiliated hain.
- Academic session **2024–25** se students English ya Hindi medium choose kar sakte hain.
- Is initiative ka aim: **rural, Hindi‑medium background wale students** ko medical education me equal opportunity dena.
- Rajasthan ne **e‑Swasthya Samvad** ke through medical education &amp; health administration me continuous feedback system create kiya.
- Medical colleges me faculty, residents, interns apne issues real‑time raise kar sakte hain – ATR system se timely solution ensure hota hai.
- Rajasthan ke teacher **Mohammad Imran Khan Mewati** ko **GEMS Education Global Teacher Prize 2025** (USD 1 million) ke liye shortlist kiya gaya.
- Imran Khan ne rural schools me **technology‑enabled teaching**, teachers training aur environment education par kaafi kaam kiya.
- Unki recognition Rajasthan ki **school education image** ko global level par positive highlight karti hai.
- Rajasthan ke budget me school education ke liye digital classrooms, lab upgradation, girl education schemes ke liye bhi allocation diya gaya.
- Rajasthan me transgender community ke liye Ajmer ka **All India Kinnar Mahasammelan** ek symbolic step hai – social inclusion ke liye.
- Conference me transgender persons ke legal rights, health issues, livelihood options par sessions hue.
- Social justice ke context me RTI Museum aur digital pensions issue bhi exam ke liye important interconnected topics hain.
- Rajasthan health sector reforms me **tele‑medicine, digital health records, hospital management system** jaisi cheezen gradually expand ho rahi hain.
- Rural health &amp; nutrition front par Rajasthan **Poshan Abhiyaan**, anaemia reduction programs, maternal &amp; child health initiatives ko integrate kar raha hai.

## F. National &amp; International High‑Value Current Affairs (REET ke liye)

- 23 August 2023 ko ISRO ke **Chandrayaan‑3** mission ne chandrama ke **south polar region** ke paas successful soft‑landing ki.
- Chandrayaan‑3 lander ka naam **“Vikram”**, aur rover ka naam **“Pragyan”** hai.
- Chandrayaan‑3 ki success se India chandrama ke south pole ke paas soft‑landing karne wala **pehla desh** bana.
- ISRO ka **Aditya‑L1** mission India ka pehla dedicated **solar (Sun) observation mission** hai.
- Aditya‑L1 ko **Lagrange Point L1** ke aas‑paas halo orbit me place karne ka plan hai – jahan se Sun ka continuous observation possible hoga.
- India ne **G20 Presidency 2023** hold ki, official theme tha – **“Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam – One Earth, One Family, One Future”**.
- New Parliament Building ka inauguration 2023 me hua – yeh **Central Vista Redevelopment Project** ka important component hai.
- **PM Vishwakarma Yojana** traditional artisans &amp; craftspeople ko ID card, skill training, toolkit aur concessional loans provide karti hai.
- **PM SVANidhi Yojana** urban street vendors ko working capital loans deti hai – unko formal financial system me lाने ka प्रयास.
- **PM‑KISAN Samman Nidhi** under eligible farmers ko saal me **₹6,000** (₹2,000 × 3 instalments) DBT ke through milte hain.
- **Ayushman Bharat – PM‑JAY** under poor &amp; vulnerable families ko har saal up to **₹5 lakh** ka health insurance cover milta hai.
- **Jal Jeevan Mission** ka main aim – **हर rural household ko functional tap water connection** provide karna (Har Ghar Jal).
- **Atal Pension Yojana** unorganised sector workers ko 60 saal ki age ke baad fixed minimum monthly pension ensure karti hai.
- **Stand‑Up India Scheme** SC/ST &amp; women entrepreneurs ko greenfield enterprises ke liye bank loans facilitate karti hai.
- **Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao** ka focus – declining child sex ratio improve karna aur girl education promote karna.
- **Khelo India** programme ka aim – grassroots sports ko develop karna aur young sports talent ko identify &amp; support karna.
- **Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin)** – rural areas me open defecation free (ODF) status achieve karna, toilets &amp; waste mgmt ko improve karna.
- **Swachh Bharat Mission (Urban)** – solid waste management, door‑to‑door collection, scientific processing of municipal waste par focus.
- **Digital India** programme 2015 me launch hua – aim: digital infrastructure, e‑governance, digital services ko universal banana.
- **National Education Policy (NEP) 2020** ne school education structure **5 + 3 + 3 + 4** introduce kiya.
- NEP 2020 Indian languages &amp; mother‑tongue ko early education me promote karta hai – isi line me Rajasthan ne Hindi‑medium MBBS start kiya.
- **International Solar Alliance (ISA)** India &amp; France ne milkar launch kiya, iska HQ **Gurugram (Haryana)** me hai.
- **UNCCD COP14** 2019 me India me **Greater Noida** me host hui – desertification aur land degradation par focus tha.
- India me **Consumer Price Index (CPI)** compile &amp; release karne ki responsibility **National Statistical Office (NSO)** ki hai.
- **World Health Organization (WHO)** ka headquarter **Geneva, Switzerland** me hai.
- **United Nations Organisation (UNO)** ka headquarter **New York, USA** me hai.
- **UNESCO** ka headquarter **Paris, France** me hai, education, science &amp; culture ke liye kaam karta hai.
- **UNICEF** children ke rights, welfare &amp; development ke liye dedicated UN agency hai.
- **World Bank Group** low &amp; middle‑income countries ko development projects ke liye financial &amp; technical assistance deta hai.
- **International Monetary Fund (IMF)** ka main role – global monetary stability aur member countries ko balance of payments support.
- **SAARC** (South Asian Association for Regional Cooperation) ke founding members initially **7 countries** the (India, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, Nepal, Bhutan, Bangladesh, Maldives).
- **China** SAARC ka founding member nahi hai – yeh trick‑type question exams me baar‑baar aata hai.
