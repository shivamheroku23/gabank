## TOPIC 1: Rajasthan Green Budget 2025–26 – “Green Growth ka Roadmap”

### Kya hua?

- 2025–26 ke liye Rajasthan ne apna **first Green Budget** present kiya; Dy CM &amp; Finance Minister **Diya Kumari** ne approx **₹5.37 lakh crore** ka budget pesh kiya.  
- Focus teen pillars par tha – **Bijli (energy), Sadak (infrastructure), Paani (water)** – saath hi climate‑friendly projects par जोर.  
- Budget me approx **6,400 MW extra power capacity**, **9 greenfield expressways (≈ ₹60,000 crore)**, **Ram Jal Setu Link (≈ ₹9,300 crore)**, urban sanitation &amp; green cities ke liye **≈ ₹12,050 crore**, aur **drip irrigation ke liye ≈ ₹1,250 crore** ka provision rakha gaya.  
- Saath hi women‑centric schemes, youth &amp; skill development, MSME support, tourism circuits, agriculture diversification aur health infrastructure ko bhi boost diya gaya.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- RAS/REET me direct fact aa sakta hai – **first Green Budget**, **FM: Diya Kumari**, total size, 9 expressways, Ram Jal Setu Link, 6,400 MW etc.  
- Mains answers me ye example **climate‑sensitive planning, SDGs, green growth, renewable hub Rajasthan** ke evidence ke roop me likh sakte ho.  
- State finance, irrigation, power, women &amp; youth schemes se related MCQs me ye figures baar‑baar use hongi.

**Memory Trick – “5.37 ka Green BSP”**

> Yaad rakho: **“5.37 lakh crore ka budget – Bijli 6,400 MW, Sadak 9 expressways, Paani Ram Jal Setu”** – BSP (Bijli‑Sadak‑Paani) se poora Green Budget image ban jaata hai.

## TOPIC 2: PM ka ₹1.22 Lakh Crore Package &amp; December 2024 Projects – Infra &amp; Energy Push

### Kya hua?

- **September 2025** me PM **Narendra Modi** ne **Banswara** se Rajasthan ke liye approx **₹1.22 lakh crore** ka development package announce kiya.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

### Kya hua?

- **Rising Rajasthan Pre‑Summit** ke dauran Mines &amp; Petroleum sector me approx **₹63,463 crore** ke MoUs sign hue; CM **Bhajan Lal Sharma** ne investors ko main summit me भाग lene ke liye invite kiya.  
- Dubai me ek investment promotion meet organise hui jahan delegation ko **Industries Minister Col. Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore** ne lead kiya – focus tha **ease of doing business**, operational cost kam, land availability, solar potential etc.

## TOPIC 4: Digital Governance – Voter Roll Digitisation, RTI Museum Beawar &amp; Ajmer Renaming

### Kya hua?

- Rajasthan desh ka **pehla state** bana jahan **voter rolls ki 100% digitisation** complete hui; yeh kaam **Special Intensive Revision** exercise ke under hua, jisme **≈ 97% voter mapping** achieve ki gayi.  
- Digitised rolls se claims‑objections phase me per booth approx **30 voters** ke documents tak hi limit reh gayi, duplicate entries &amp; repeated verification ka issue kaafi kam hua; isme **Booth Level Officers (BLOs)** ka field work key role me raha.  
- **Beawar (Ajmer district)**, jise RTI movement ki janmabhoomi maana jata hai, wahan **RTI Museum** ka foundation stone rakha gaya – yeh museum RTI history, case studies aur rights‑based movements ka resource centre banega; event me approx **13.5 lakh pensions** ke digital exclusion issue ko highlight kiya gaya.  
- Ajmer me British‑era naamon ka decolonisation kiya gaya – **Foy Sagar Lake** ka naam **“Varun Sagar”**, aur **King Edward Memorial Bhawan** ka naam **“Maharishi Dayanand Vishrant Grih”** rakha gaya; Ajmer Municipal Corporation ne official orders issue kiye, aur Varun Sagar ke paas ghat + Lord Varun ki murti plan hui.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- State GK me pooch sakte hain: **first state with fully digitised voter rolls**, **Beawar – RTI movement birthplace + RTI Museum**, Ajmer renaming ke new names.  
- Governance answers me ye sab **transparency, citizen rights, decolonisation of spaces, digital exclusion** ke solid examples hain.  
- 13.5 lakh pensions ka issue welfare schemes me digital divide ke risk ko highlight karta hai, jo mains ke लिए बहु‑उपयोगी point hai.

**Memory Trick – “3 D: Digitisation, Democracy, Dayanand”**

> Yaad rakho: **“Digitised voter rolls, Beawar ka RTI Museum, Aur Ajmer ka Dayanand Vishrant Grih – teen D: Digital, Democracy, Dayanand.”**
