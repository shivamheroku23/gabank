## TOPIC 1: FEMA (Guarantees) Regulations 2026 – 15 Din ka Reporting Rule

### Kya hua?

- RBI ne **Foreign Exchange Management (Guarantees) Regulations, 2026** notify kiye, jo **residents aur non‑residents ke beech guarantee transactions** ko govern karte hain.  
- Isme kaha gaya hai ki jo party guarantee issue/modify/invoke kar rahi hai (surety, principal debtor ya creditor) usse linked **reporting details authorised dealer (AD) bank ko har quarter ke khatam hone ke 15 calendar din ke andar** bhejni hongi.  
- AD banks ko consolidated data RBI ko **quarter ke khatam hone ke 30 din ke andar** submit karna hoga.  
- Delayed reporting par **Late Submission Fee (LSF)** lagega – ek fixed amount + guarantee ke amount ka chhota percentage, delay ke duration ke hisaab se.  
- Trade credit guarantees ke alag quarterly returns ko is unified framework me merge kiya ja raha hai, taaki reporting simple aur traceable ho.

(Official context: RBI Notification – Foreign Exchange Management (Guarantees) Regulations, 2026; Photo: https://www.taxmann.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/RBI_FEMA_Guarantees.png)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- MCQ me pooch sakte hain: **FEMA (Guarantees) Regulations 2026 kis cheez se related hai? → foreign exchange guarantees**.  
- AD banks ke liye compliance, risk aur FX exposure reporting me ye bahut zaroori hai – especially **bank guarantees, standby LC, performance guarantees** ke case me.  
- Mains/interview me isko example bana sakte ho ki RBI **data‑driven supervision** ke liye granular reporting framework bana raha hai.

### Memory Trick – “15 + 30 = guarantee reporting ka combo”

> Yaad rakho: **“15 din customer se AD bank, 30 din AD se RBI”** – yehi FEMA Guarantees reporting ka basic code hai.

## TOPIC 2: Trend and Progress of Banking in India 2024-25 – Frauds Kam, System Strong

### Kya hua?

- RBI ne apni annual report **“Trend and Progress of Banking in India 2024-25”** release ki, jisme **scheduled commercial banks, NBFCs, payment banks, SFBs** ki health ka review hai.  
- Report ke mutabik **banking sector capital, profitability aur asset quality** ke hisaab se resilient bana hua hai – NPAs aur provisioning ratios me overall improvement dikhaya gaya.  
- Fraud data me **cases ki sankhya pichhle saal ke mukable ghati** hai, lekin kuch bade cases ki wajah se **total amount** ab bhi significant hai – isliye vigilance important.  
- NBFCs ke credit aur role me growth, aur digital payment ecosystem (UPI, cards, wallets) ka scale bhi highlight kiya gaya.  

(Official context: RBI – Report on Trend and Progress of Banking in India 2024‑25; Photo: https://www.rbi.org.in/scripts/BS_Coverpage_TPBI2025.jpg)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Ye report **banking awareness** ka ek comprehensive source hai – MCQ me pooch sakte hain ki ye **kaun jari karta hai, kya cover karta hai**.  
- Mains me aap likh sakte ho ki **fraud cases ki sankhya kam hui hai, par fraud amount ab bhi high hai**, isliye risk management aur technology‑based monitoring critical hai.  
- NBFCs, SFBs, payments banks ke baare me jo points hain, unhe GA me fact‑snippet ke roop me yaad rakhna useful hai.

### Memory Trick – “TPBI = ‘Trend, Progress, Bank India’ – health report”

> Code: **“TPBI = health report of banks + NBFCs”** – jab bhi Trend &amp; Progress dikhe, samajh lo banking system ki report hai.

## TOPIC 4: MGNREGA – 125 Din ka Rozgar Guarantee, Rural Demand Boost

### Kya hua?

- January 2026 ke discussions me ye point front par raha ki **MGNREGA (Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act)** ke तहत **guaranteed workdays 100 se badha kar 125** tak le jaane ka निर्णय liya gaya hai.  
- Is se rural households ko **25 extra din ka potential wage income** milega, khas kar un jilon me jahan seasonal unemployment zyada hai.  
- Yojana me **water conservation, rural infrastructure, plantation, land development** jaise projects ko priority mil rahi hai.  
- Budgetary provision aur timely payment, MIS tracking ke through isko ground par implement kiya ja raha hai.

(Official context: Rural development policy updates on MGNREGA expansion; Photo: https://www.insightsonindia.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/mgnrega_workers.jpg)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- MGNREGA directly **rural income, consumption aur informal credit** par impact dalta hai – banks ke liye iska effect **savings accounts, small loans aur NPAs** par bhi hota hai.  
- MCQ me pooch sakte hain: **MGNREGA me ab kitne din ka rozgar guarantee propose kiya gaya? → 125 din**.  
- Mains me rural demand ke context me isko quote kar sakte ho ki **extra 25 din = zyada paisa, jo FMCG, agri‑inputs aur rural banking products me demand create karega**.

### Memory Trick – “MGNREGA 100 se 125 – extra 25 din ka safety net”

> Line: **“100 se ek kadam aage – MGNREGA 125 din, gaon ka extra safety net”**.
