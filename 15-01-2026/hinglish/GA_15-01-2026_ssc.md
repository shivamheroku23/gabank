## TOPIC 1: MGNREGA – 125 Din ka Rozgar Guarantee

### Kya hua?

- Reports ke mutabik **Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA)** me **guaranteed workdays 100 se badha kar 125** tak le jaane ka decision announce kiya gaya.  
- Is step ka aim hai ki gaon ke rozgar‑asurakshit parivar ko **25 extra din ka potential wage income** mil sake.  
- Zyada focus **water conservation, rural infrastructure, plantation, land improvement** jaise durable asset‑creation projects par rahega.  

(Official context: Rural development policy discussions; Photo: https://www.insightsonindia.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/mgnrega_workers.jpg)

### SSC exam points:

- Scheme ka full naam → **Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA)**.  
- Minimum guaranteed rozgar days ab ≈ **125 days** per rural household.  
- Kaam ka swaroop → mostly **unskilled manual work**; funding Centre–State shared.  

**Memory Trick – “100 se 125 – gaon ke 25 extra din”**

> Line: **“MGNREGA now 125 – 25 extra din ka safety net”**.

## TOPIC 2: PRAGATI Platform – 50th Baithak

### Kya hua?

- **PRAGATI (Pro‑Active Governance and Timely Implementation)** ek PM‑chaired digital platform hai jahan PM, Central secretaries aur state officials **video conference ke through projects aur grievances review** karte hain.  
- January 2026 tak is platform ki **50th बैठक** ho chuki hai, jisme kai bade infrastructure, social sector aur rail/road projects ka review hua.  
- Yahan par **Central–State coordination**, deadlines, land acquisition, environment clearance jaisi bottlenecks ko real‑time me discuss karke solve kiya jata hai.  

(Official context: PMO / PRAGATI portal coverage; Photo: https://currentaffairs.gktoday.in/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/pragati_meeting.jpg)

### SSC exam points:

- PRAGATI ka full form → **Pro‑Active Governance and Timely Implementation**.  
- Chairperson → **Prime Minister of India**.  
- Use → **projects monitoring + public grievances** ka integrated platform.  
- 50th baithak ek milestone hai jisse UPSC/SSC me “kaun sa platform, kis purpose ke liye” type sawal aa sakta hai.

**Memory Trick – “PRAGATI = PM ki project progress meeting”**

> Socho: **PM screen par map aur project list dekhkar state officials se baat kar rahe hain – wahi PRAGATI.**

## TOPIC 4: DRDO–Army MPATGM Test – Desi Anti‑Tank Missile

### Kya hua?

- **DRDO** aur **Indian Army** ne milkar indigenous **Man‑Portable Anti‑Tank Guided Missile (MPATGM)** system ka safal test kiya.  
- Ye ek shoulder‑fired / tripod‑mounted missile system hai jo **modern armour ke khilaf top‑attack aur direct‑attack mode** me kaam karta hai.  
- Missile aur launcher puri tarah **Make in India** concept par based hain, jisse imported anti‑tank systems par dependency kam hogi.  

(Official context: DRDO / MoD press releases; Photo: https://www.insightsonindia.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/MPATGM_test.jpg)

### SSC exam points:

- MPATGM → **Man‑Portable Anti‑Tank Guided Missile**.  
- Developer → **DRDO + Indian Army trials**.  
- Role → infantry ko **tank/armoured vehicles ke khilaf high‑precision weapon** provide karna.  

**Memory Trick – “MPATGM = soldier ke kandhe par tank killer”**

> Image: **ek jawan kandhe par launcher liye tank ki taraf missile fire kar raha hai** – wahi MPATGM.
