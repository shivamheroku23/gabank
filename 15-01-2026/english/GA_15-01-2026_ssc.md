## TOPIC 1: MGNREGA – 125 Days of Guaranteed Work

### What happened?

- As per recent policy discussions, **Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA)** is set to raise **guaranteed workdays from 100 to 125** per rural household.  
- The purpose is to provide rural families with **25 additional potential wage‑days**, especially in districts with high seasonal unemployment.  
- Priority will be given to projects like **water conservation, rural infrastructure, plantations and land‑development**, which create durable community assets.  

(Official context: Rural development policy discussions; Photo: https://www.insightsonindia.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/mgnrega_workers.jpg)

### SSC exam points:

- Full name → **Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA)**.  
- Proposed guaranteed workdays → **125 days per rural household**.  
- Nature of work → mainly **unskilled manual work**, with cost shared between Centre and States.  

**Memory Trick – “100 to 125 – villages get 25 extra days”**

> Line: **“MGNREGA now 125 – 25 extra days as a safety net.”**

## TOPIC 2: PRAGATI Platform – 50th Meeting

### What happened?

- **PRAGATI (Pro‑Active Governance and Timely Implementation)** is a PM‑chaired digital platform where the Prime Minister, central secretaries and state officials **review key projects and public grievances via video‑conferencing**.  
- By January 2026, PRAGATI has completed its **50th meeting**, reviewing major infrastructure, social‑sector and rail/road projects.  
- The platform enables **Centre–State coordination** to resolve issues such as land acquisition, environmental clearances and delays in real time.  

(Official context: PMO / PRAGATI portal updates; Photo: https://currentaffairs.gktoday.in/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/pragati_meeting.jpg)

### SSC exam points:

- Full form → **Pro‑Active Governance and Timely Implementation**.  
- Chairperson → **Prime Minister of India**.  
- Purpose → Integrated system for **monitoring major projects and grievance redressal**.  

**Memory Trick – “PRAGATI = PM’s project‑progress meeting”**

> Picture the **Prime Minister on a screen reviewing maps and project lists with state officials** – that’s PRAGATI.

## TOPIC 4: DRDO–Army MPATGM Test – Indigenous Anti‑Tank Missile

### What happened?

- **DRDO** and the **Indian Army** jointly carried out a successful test of the indigenous **Man‑Portable Anti‑Tank Guided Missile (MPATGM)** system.  
- It is a **shoulder‑fired/tripod‑mounted missile** capable of engaging modern tanks in **top‑attack and direct‑attack modes**.  
- Both missile and launcher are developed under **“Make in India”**, reducing dependence on imported anti‑tank systems.  

(Official context: DRDO/MoD press release; Photo: https://www.insightsonindia.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/MPATGM_test.jpg)

### SSC exam points:

- MPATGM → **Man‑Portable Anti‑Tank Guided Missile**.  
- Developer → **DRDO**, tested with the **Indian Army**.  
- Role → providing infantry with a **high‑precision weapon against tanks and armoured vehicles**.  

**Memory Trick – “MPATGM = tank‑killer on a soldier’s shoulder”**

> Visualise a **soldier firing a missile from the shoulder at a tank** – that’s MPATGM.
