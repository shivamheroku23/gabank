## TOPIC 1: DSIR – Deep‑Tech Startups ke लिये IRDPP Rules Relax

### Kya hua?

- **Department of Scientific & Industrial Research (DSIR)** ne  
  apne **Industrial Research & Development Promotion Programme (IRDPP)** ke तहत  
  **deep‑tech startups** ko promote karne ke लिये rules को आसान किया.  
- IRDPP ka उद्देश्य:

  - उद्योग aur research institutions ke बीच **R&D projects** ko support karna,  
  - engineering, environment, natural sciences, agriculture, medicine, social sciences etc. me  
    आवेदन को **funding + recognition** देना.  
- नये फैसले se:

  - deep‑tech / IP‑driven startups ke लिये recognition norms simplify kiye gaye,  
  - application process आसान,  
  - ज़्यादा startups ko R&D grants mil सकेंगे.

### SSC exam points

- IRDPP का संचालन कौन करता hai? → **DSIR (Ministry of Science & Technology ke तहत)**.  
- Deep‑tech startups – ऐसे startups jo **advanced science/engineering, IP, R&D** par आधारित hote hain.

**Memory Trick – “IRDPP = Industrial R&D Promotion Programme”**

> Yaad rakho: **IRDPP** = **Industrial R&D Promotion Programme** –  
> DSIR ka scheme jo deep‑tech ko push karta hai.

## TOPIC 2: NITI Aayog – Trade Watch Quarterly (5th Edition)

### Kya hua?

- **NITI Aayog** ne apni **‘Trade Watch Quarterly’** report ka  
  **5वाँ edition** release kiya – yeh रिपोर्ट India ke **external trade trends** ka analysis deti hai.  
- Report me:

  - FTA partner देशों ke साथ बढ़ते trade deficit,  
  - key export/import sectors (electronics, machinery, chemicals, services) ka review,  
  - logistics & port bottlenecks,  
  - global demand slowdown ka असर बताया gaya.

### SSC exam points

- ‘Trade Watch Quarterly’ किस संस्था की report hai? → **NITI Aayog**.  
- Theme: **external trade, exports, imports, FTA impact**.

**Memory Trick – “Trade Watch = NITI ka trade ka watchman”**

> NITI Aayog **trade ka watchman** बनकर quarterly रिपोर्ट deta hai –  
> इसीलिये naam hai **Trade Watch**.

## TOPIC 3: PPP Infra Pipeline – लगभग ₹17 Lakh Crore (3‑Year Plan)

### Kya hua?

- सरकार ne अगले **3 साल** ke लिये  
  करीब **₹17 lakh crore** ke **Public‑Private Partnership (PPP)** projects ki **infra pipeline** तैयार की hai.  
- मुख्य sectors:

  - राष्ट्रीय राजमार्ग, expressways,  
  - ports & shipping,  
  - renewable ऊर्जा,  
  - urban infra (metro, water, waste management),  
  - digital infra (data centres, broadband).

### SSC exam points

- PPP ka full form – **Public‑Private Partnership**.  
- PPP ka idea – **सरकार + निजी क्षेत्र मिलकर infra banayen**, risk aur investment share karein.

**Memory Trick – “PPP = Public + Private + Project”**

> Socho PPP = **Public + Private + Project** –  
> तीनों milkar बड़ी infrastructure scheme बनाते हैं.

## TOPIC 4: HPCL Visakhapatnam – LC‑Max Residue Upgradation Facility

### Kya hua?

- **Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Ltd (HPCL)** ne  
  **Visakhapatnam refinery (Andhra Pradesh)** me  
  दुनिया की पहली **LC‑Max residue upgradation facility** commission ki.  
- LC‑Max technology:

  - refinery ke heavy residue oil ko  
    ज़्यादा मूल्य वाले fuels (जैसे petrol/diesel range products) me convert karti hai,  
  - इससे refinery ki efficiency aur profitability बढ़ती hai.

### SSC exam points

- HPCL – ek **Maharatna PSU** (oil & gas sector).  
- LC‑Max residue upgradation facility – **Visakhapatnam refinery, Andhra Pradesh** me.

**Memory Trick – “LC‑Max = Low‑value Crude ko Max value”**

> LC‑Max – low‑value residue ko **maximum value** fuel me बदले.

## TOPIC 5: BIS – SHINE Scheme for Women

### Kya hua?

- **Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS)** ne apne 79th Foundation Day par  
  ek नयी initiative launch ki – **SHINE**:  
  **“Standards Help Inform and Nurture Empowered women”**.  
- उद्देश्य:

  - NGOs, Self‑Help Groups (SHGs) ke माध्यम se महिलाओं ko  
    **product standards, safety, quality marks (ISI etc.)** ke बारे me training dena,  
  - taaki वे apne घर aur samaj me **safe aur standard products** चुन सकें,  
  - quality‑based micro‑enterprise / livelihood activities ko बढ़ावा mile.

### SSC exam points

- SHINE scheme kis संस्था ki hai? → **BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards)**.  
- Theme – **women empowerment + consumer awareness + standards**.

**Memory Trick – “SHINE = Standards Help INform & Empower”**

> SHINE – **Standards Help INform & Empower** women –  
> naam se hi idea clear hai.
