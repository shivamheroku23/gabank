## TOPIC 1: HDFC Bank – Digital Rupee (e₹) ko SmartGateway se जोड़ना

### Kya hua?

- **HDFC Bank** ne announce kiya ki usne RBI ke **Central Bank Digital Currency (CBDC) – e₹** ko  
  apne **SmartGateway** payment platform se integrate kar diya hai.  
- Ab merchants jo HDFC SmartGateway use karte hain,  
  वे **online payments ke लिये e₹ (Digital Rupee)** accept कर sakte hain –  
  UPI/cards ke साथ‑साथ एक नया option.  
- Customer side se:  
  - अगर customer ke पास **e₹ wallet** (RBI ke pilot me) hai,  
  to wo merchant website/app par सीधे **e₹ se payment** kar sakta hai.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- **Digital Rupee pilot** – RBI ke important projects me se ek,  
  jiske ecosystem me banks incremental use‑cases बना रहे हैं.  
- Exam me puch सकते hain:

  - e₹ किस प्रकार ka instrument hai? → **RBI‑issued legal tender in digital form**.  
  - Kaun‑sa private bank ne SmartGateway + e₹ integration announce kiya? → **HDFC Bank**.

### Memory Trick – “HDFC = e₹ ka E‑Gateway Friend”

> Socho: **H D F C** =  
> **H**olds **D**igital **F**iat **C**urrency gateway –  
> यानि HDFC ne e₹ ko apne gateway ka friend बना लिया.

## TOPIC 2: DSIR – Deep‑Tech Startups ke लिये IRDPP Rules Relax

### Kya hua?

- **Department of Scientific & Industrial Research (DSIR)**  
  ne apne **Industrial Research & Development Promotion Programme (IRDPP)** ke तहत  
  **deep‑tech startups ke लिये recognition rules को आसान** करने का फैसला किया.  
- IRDPP kya karta hai?  
  - R&D projects (engineering, environment, natural sciences, agri, medicine, social sciences) ko  
    **financial support + recognition** deta hai.  
- New relaxations:

  - eligibility norms soft kiye gaye ताकि **IP‑driven, deep‑tech startups** easily cover ho सकें,  
  - application process simplified,  
  - industry‑academia collaboration ko encourage.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Deep‑tech startups long‑term R&D aur patents par आधारित होते हैं –  
  inko financing करना traditional banks ke लिये risky hota hai.  
- DSIR jaise schemes se unka **equity/soft funding** strong होता hai →  
  banks ko later‑stage debt dena relatively सुरक्षित लगता hai.  

### Memory Trick – “IRDPP = Industrial R&D Power Programme”

> IRDPP ko yaad rakho: **Industrial R&D Power Programme** –  
> jo deep‑tech ko power deta hai, DSIR ke through.

## TOPIC 3: NITI Aayog – Trade Watch Quarterly (5th Edition)

### Kya hua?

- **NITI Aayog** ne apni external sector analysis series ka  
  **5th edition – ‘Trade Watch Quarterly’ report** release kiya (2025‑26 ke latest data ke साथ).  
- Report ke मुख्य बिंदु:

  - **FTA partners ke साथ trade deficit** me तेज़ बढ़ोतरी,  
  - electronics, machinery, chemicals jaise sectors me imports का तेज़ बढ़ना,  
  - कुछ sunrise sectors (services, software exports, pharma) me surplus,  
  - लक्षित policy reforms – logistics, trade facilitation, export incentives – par जोर.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Trade deficit → **Current Account Deficit (CAD)** → rupee, interest rates, bond yields par असर.  
- Report ka naam और तैयार करने वाला body – straight factual question बन सकता hai:

  - “Trade Watch Quarterly किसने निकाला?” → **NITI Aayog**.  

### Memory Trick – “Trade Watch = NITI ka external CCTV”

> Socho NITI Aayog ek **CCTV camera** se बाहरी व्यापार dekh raha hai –  
> us camera ka naam hai **Trade Watch**.

## TOPIC 4: 3‑Year PPP Infra Pipeline – लगभग ₹17 Lakh Crore

### Kya hua?

- Government ne अगली **3 साल** ke लिये  
  लगभग **₹17 lakh crore** ke **Public‑Private Partnership (PPP) infra projects** ki pipeline तैयार की hai  
  (AffairsCloud / PendulumEdu me cover, infra policy statements par आधारित).  
- Sectors:

  - highways,  
  - ports & shipping,  
  - renewable energy,  
  - urban infra (metros, water supply, waste management),  
  - digital infra (data centres, broadband).  

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- PPP projects ka major हिस्सा **debt financing** se आता hai:

  - Term loans,  
  - project finance,  
  - bonds,  
  - infrastructure investment trusts (InvITs) – sab me banks / DFIs involved रहते हैं.  
- Exam me kahi bhi puch सकते hain:

  - PPP ka full form,  
  - PPP ke फायदे (risk sharing, off‑budget infra, efficiency) etc.

### Memory Trick – “PPP = Public + Private + Paisa”

> PPP = **Public + Private + Paisa** –  
> सरकार aur private sector milkar पैसा lagate हैं, infra banta hai.

## TOPIC 5: HPCL – Visakhapatnam LC‑Max Residue Upgradation Unit

### Kya hua?

- **Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Ltd (HPCL)** ne  
  Andhra Pradesh ke **Visakhapatnam refinery** me  
  दुनिया की पहली **LC‑Max residue upgradation facility** commission ki (AffairsCloud report).  
- LC‑Max technology:

  - भारी residue oil ko उच्च‑margins वाले fuels me convert karti hai,  
  - refineries ki **product yield aur profitability** बढ़ाती hai,  
  - low‑value residual fuel oil पर निर्भरता कम karti hai.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Refinery expansion / upgradation projects high‑capital intensive hote हैं –  
  HPCL jaisi PSU ko finance karne me consortium banks, bond investors ka बड़ा रोल hota hai.  
- Energy infra se जुड़ा यह example mains me **“Make in India + refinery modernisation”** ke under use हो सकता hai.

### Memory Trick – “LC‑Max = Low‑value Crude ko Maximum value”

> बस yaad: **LC‑Max** –  
> low‑value residue ko **maximum value** fuels me बदलने wali tech.
