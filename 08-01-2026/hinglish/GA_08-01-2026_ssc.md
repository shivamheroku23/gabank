## TOPIC 1: RBI Report – Bank NPAs Low, Agriculture Sector me Stress

### Kya hua?

- RBI ki latest report / Financial Stability analysis (coverage **8 January 2026**) ne dikhaya:  
  - Scheduled commercial banks ka **Gross NPA ratio ~2.1–2.2%** tak gir gaya (multi‑year low),  
  - Net NPA ~**0.5%** ke aas‑paas.  
- Ye improvement **balance sheet clean‑up + better recovery + IBC** ki wajah se hai.  
- Lekin composition change ho gaya:  
  - **Agriculture sector** ka share ab NPAs me **sabse bada** ho gaya,  
  - Retail me **unsecured personal loans / credit cards** ki fast growth future risk point hai.  

### SSC exam points:

- NPA: Non‑Performing Asset – 90 din se zyada overdue loan.  
- Agriculture NPAs ka share bada hone ka matlab rural distress &amp; loan waiver pressure.  
- Report: asset quality improve, lekin **risk pattern shift** ho raha hai.  

**Memory Trick – “2–0.5, par Kisan pe nazar”**

> Line yaad rakho: **“Bank NPA 2–0.5, par kisan ke loan par nazar tight.”**

## TOPIC 3: PLI Scheme – Steel &amp; Mobile Manufacturing Boom

### Kya hua?

- **Production Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme** (April 2020 se) ka detailed impact data 8 Jan 2026 coverage me aaya:  
  - **Crude steel output** 2020–21 ke 103.54 MT se badh kar **144.30 MT (2023–24)**.  
  - Finished steel consumption **136 MT+** reach hui.  
  - Electronics sector me total output **₹5.5 lakh crore (2020–21)** se **~₹9.5 lakh crore (2023–24)**.  
  - Mobile phone manufacturing value **₹4.22 lakh crore**, jo total electronics output ka **44.3%** hai.  

### SSC exam points:

- PLI scheme launch: **2020**, aim – manufacturing &amp; exports boost.  
- Key sectors: electronics (mobiles), steel, medical devices, autos, pharma, etc.  
- Mobile manufacturing – India ek **major hub** ban raha hai.  

**Memory Trick – “PLI = Produce Local with Incentive”**

> PLI = **“Produce Local with Incentive”** – steel &amp; mobiles dono me production jump.

## TOPIC 4: Indian Gas Exchange (IGX) – ₹600–700 Crore IPO Plan by Dec 2026

### Kya hua?

- **Indian Gas Exchange (IGX)** – desh ka pehla online **natural gas trading platform** –  
  ne approx **₹600–700 crore** ka IPO plan announce kiya:  
  - Target listing: **December 2026** (news: 7–8 Jan 2026 range).  
  - IPO se kareeb **22% equity stake sale** hoga.  
- IGX ki parent company **Indian Energy Exchange (IEX)** ko apna stake 47% se 25% tak reduce karna hai (regulatory requirement).  
- IGX ka aim:  
  - current gas trading ~5.4 mmscmd se market share ko 2029 tak 5% aur 2030 tak 7% ke aas‑paas le jaana.  

### SSC exam points:

- IGX – **Indian Gas Exchange**, natural gas spot/term trading platform.  
- Parent: **IEX – Indian Energy Exchange Ltd**.  
- IPO amount: **₹600–700 crore (approx)**.  

**Memory Trick – “Gas ka Share Market – IGX IPO”**

> Socho gas ka bhi “stock exchange” hai – **IGX** –  
> jo ab **apne hi shares** stock market me bechne ki tayari me hai.
