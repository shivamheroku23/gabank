## TOPIC 1: Chintan Shivir on Indian Wool Sector in Rajasthan

### Kya hua?

- The **Central Wool Development Board (CWDB), Ministry of Textiles** organised a **Chintan Shivir** on the **Indian wool sector** at **Avikanagar, Rajasthan** in January 2026.  
- The event was held in collaboration with **ICAR–Central Sheep and Wool Research Institute (CSWRI), Avikanagar**.  
- Focus: **Challenges, opportunities and future prospects** of the wool value chain – from sheep rearing to processing and marketing.  
- Key objectives included: reducing dependence on imported fine wool, promoting **sustainable sheep rearing**, boosting **R&amp;D and technical textiles**, and expanding **Common Facility Centres (CFCs)** for small producers.  

(Official context: CWDB &amp; Ministry of Textiles’ wool sector consultation; Photo: https://www.drishtiias.com/images/uploads/1768657589_PCS-BANNERS-15.webp)

### SSC exam points:

- Event: **Chintan Shivir on Indian Wool Sector**.  
- Place: **Avikanagar, Rajasthan**.  
- Institutions: **CWDB (Ministry of Textiles)** + **ICAR–CSWRI**.  
- Aim: strengthen wool value chain, branding, and export competitiveness.  

**Memory Trick – “Avikanagar = wool chintan”**

> Link **AviKANAGAR** with **“AVIn (sheep) + wool”** – Rajasthan = chintan on wool sector.

## TOPIC 2: Gujarat launches ‘Poshan Udaan 2026’

### Kya hua?

- **Gujarat government** launched **‘Poshan Udaan 2026’** during the **Makar Sankranti / Uttarayan** festival.  
- Idea: use the popularity of the **kite festival** – with kites and messages promoting **nutrition and health awareness** across the state.  
- The initiative is implemented under **ICDS (Integrated Child Development Services)** through **Anganwadi centres**, led by the **Department of Women and Child Development**.  
- Focus areas: **dietary diversity, less junk food, safe drinking water, sanitation practices**, and better nutrition for **children, adolescent girls, and pregnant &amp; lactating women**.  

(Reference: State government nutrition campaign updates; Photo: https://www.drishtiias.com/images/articles/1768657490_PCS-BANNERS-15.webp)

### SSC exam points:

- State: **Gujarat**.  
- Scheme/initiative: **‘Poshan Udaan 2026’**.  
- Linked to: **Makar Sankranti kite festival** + **ICDS / Anganwadi network**.  

**Memory Trick – “Patang + Poshan in Gujarat”**

## TOPIC 3: Tamil Nadu to set up India’s first ‘Sovereign AI Park’

### Kya hua?

- On **13 January 2026**, the **Government of Tamil Nadu** signed an MoU with **Sarvam AI** to establish **India’s first Sovereign AI Park in Chennai**.  
- The park will host **full‑stack AI infrastructure** – GPU‑based data centres, indigenous **Large Language Models (LLMs)** and application‑layer tools.  
- A key focus is on developing **Indic‑language LLMs (especially Tamil)** to bridge the digital divide for non‑English speakers.  
- The project will be implemented with the **Tamil Nadu e‑Governance Agency (TNeGA)** to integrate AI into public service delivery.  
- Sovereign AI = a country/state building AI using its **own data, infrastructure and talent**, ensuring **data sovereignty and strategic autonomy**.  

(Reference: State government announcement &amp; IndiaAI Mission context; Photo: https://www.drishtiias.com/images/articles/1768657475_PCS-BANNERS-16.webp)

### SSC exam points:

- State: **Tamil Nadu**.  
- Project: **India’s first Sovereign AI Park**.  
- City: **Chennai**.  
- Concept: **Sovereign AI** linked with **IndiaAI Mission** and **strategic autonomy**.  

**Memory Trick – “Chennai = Sovereign AI city”**

> Think of **Chennai** labelled as **“India’s first Sovereign AI Park”** on a map.

## TOPIC 4: India Post delivers first‑ever ONDC order as logistics provider

### Kya hua?

- The **Department of Posts (India Post)** has successfully delivered its **first ONDC order** as a **logistics service provider** on the **Open Network for Digital Commerce (ONDC)**.  
- The inaugural shipment was **walnuts** from a seller in **Srinagar, Jammu &amp; Kashmir** to a buyer in **New Delhi**.  
- India Post managed the entire logistics chain – **first‑mile pickup, sorting, transportation and last‑mile delivery**, using its internal logistics software integrated with ONDC.  
- With India Post onboard, ONDC can leverage **1.6 lakh+ post offices**, making it easier for remote rural sellers and MSMEs to reach national markets.  

(Reference: India Post–ONDC integration updates; Photo: https://www.drishtiias.com/images/articles/1768657475_PCS-BANNERS-15.webp)

### SSC exam points:

- Logistics provider: **India Post**.  
- Network: **ONDC – Open Network for Digital Commerce**.  
- First order: **walnuts from Srinagar to New Delhi**.  
- Linkage with schemes: supports **MSMEs** and **One District One Product (ODOP)**.  

**Memory Trick – “Walnut from Kashmir via India Post on ONDC”**

> Picture a parcel of walnuts stamped with **“India Post + ONDC”** travelling from **Srinagar to Delhi**.

## TOPIC 5: IOA launches National Olympic Education and Development Programme (NOEDP)

### Kya hua?

- The **Indian Olympic Association (IOA)** launched the **National Olympic Education and Development Programme (NOEDP)** to strengthen India’s **Olympic ecosystem from grassroots to elite level**.  
- A key step is the formal **reactivation of the National Olympic Academy (NOA)** in **Ahmedabad**, as the hub for Olympic education, research and dialogue.  
- IOA President and sprint legend **P.T. Usha** has been ratified as **President of the NOA**, while Olympic medallist **Gagan Narang** has been appointed as **Director**.  
- The NOA will collaborate with the **International Olympic Academy (Olympia)** to align Indian programmes with global Olympic standards and the Olympic Charter.  

(Reference: IOA announcements on NOEDP; Photo: https://www.drishtiias.com/images/articles/1768657464_PCS-BANNERS-16.webp)

### SSC exam points:

- Organisation: **Indian Olympic Association (IOA)**.  
- Programme: **National Olympic Education and Development Programme (NOEDP)**.  
- NOA location: **Ahmedabad**.  
- Leadership: **P.T. Usha – President, Gagan Narang – Director**.  

**Memory Trick – “NOEDP = Usha &amp; Narang for Olympic education”**

> Imagine **P.T. Usha** and **Gagan Narang** standing in front of a board labelled **“National Olympic Academy – Ahmedabad”**.
