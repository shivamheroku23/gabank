## TOPIC 1: Chintan Shivir on Indian Wool Sector at Avikanagar

### Kya hua?

- In January 2026, the **Central Wool Development Board (CWDB)** organised a **Chintan Shivir on the Indian wool sector** at **Avikanagar in Rajasthan**.  
- The programme was held in collaboration with **ICAR–Central Sheep and Wool Research Institute (CSWRI), Avikanagar**.  
- The discussions focused on reducing India’s **heavy dependence on imported fine wool** and strengthening the entire **wool value chain – from sheep rearing to processing and branding**.  

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Avikanagar, CWDB and CSWRI are key Rajasthan‑based centres for **sheep &amp; wool research**, making them likely factual points in RAS/Police/Patwari exams.  
- The wool sector supports **rural livelihoods, tribal areas and desert economy**, which can feature in development, geography and economy‑based questions.  

**Memory Trick – “Avika‑nagar = sheep &amp; wool hub in Rajasthan”**

> Mark **Avikanagar** on your mental map as **“sheep &amp; wool chintan”** centre.

## TOPIC 2: Rajasthan Budget 2025‑26 – First Green‑Theme Budget &amp; Climate Focus

### Kya hua?

- On **19 February 2025**, Rajasthan’s Deputy CM &amp; Finance Minister **Diya Kumari** presented the **Rajasthan Budget 2025‑26** in the Assembly, described as the state’s **first “green theme” budget**.  
- The budget announced measures like a **Climate Adaptation Plan‑2030**, **Tree Outside Forest (ToFR) Policy**, **Agro‑forestry push**, **Rajasthan Green Challenge Fund** and **Green Aravali Development Project**.  
- The green budget focuses on 10 areas such as **climate adaptation, sustainable agriculture &amp; water, green energy, recycling &amp; circular economy, green financing and capacity building**.  

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Direct Q: **Which Rajasthan budget is known as the first green‑theme budget? → 2025‑26.**  
- The named schemes – **Climate Adaptation Plan‑2030, Green Challenge Fund, Green Aravali project** – are important for environment, economy and policy sections.  

**Memory Trick – “2025‑26 = Rajasthan’s Green Budget”**

> Whenever you see **“Rajasthan Budget 2025‑26”**, imagine the whole budget document coloured **green**.

## TOPIC 3: Youth &amp; Jobs – Rajasthan Employment Policy‑2025 &amp; Vivekananda Employment Assistance Fund

### Kya hua?

- Rajasthan Budget 2025‑26 has a dedicated focus on **youth and employment**.  
- The government announced a new **“Rajasthan Employment Policy‑2025”** to create a framework for **job creation, skilling and private sector hiring** in the state.  
- A **₹500‑crore “Vivekananda Employment Assistance Fund”** has been proposed to support youth employment and entrepreneurship initiatives.  
- The government has targeted recruitment for about **1.25 lakh government posts** and **1.50 lakh jobs in the private sector** in the coming year.

## TOPIC 4: Transport &amp; Infrastructure – Expressways, Atal Pragati Path, Jaipur Metro

### Kya hua?

- The 2025‑26 budget gives major emphasis to **roads and transport infrastructure**:  
  - More than **₹60,000 crore** proposed for **nine Greenfield Expressways** in the state.  
  - Under **“Atal Pragati Path”**, works will be taken up to connect around **250 villages with population above 5,000** with improved roads, at a cost of **₹500 crore**.  
  - **Jaipur Metro** is planned to be extended from **Sitapura Industrial Area to Ambawadi** and further to **Vidyadhar Nagar (Todi Mor)**, with an approximate provision of **₹12,000 crore**.  

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Map/matching questions can link **Atal Pragati Path, Greenfield expressways, Jaipur Metro extension** to Rajasthan.  
- The combination of urban metro projects and rural roads is important for **infrastructure and regional development** questions.  

**Memory Trick – “Three pillars: Green Expressways – Atal Pragati Path – Jaipur Metro”**

> One line: **“Rajasthan 2025‑26 = Green expressways + Atal Pragati Path + Jaipur Metro extension.”**

## TOPIC 5: Drinking Water &amp; Energy – Jal Jeevan Mission (Urban) &amp; Free Electricity

### Kya hua?

- The budget announces several measures for **drinking water and energy**:  
  - About **₹425 crore** for rural drinking water and a target of **20 lakh new household water connections** in the coming year.  
  - Under the **“Chief Minister Jal Jeevan Mission (Urban)”**, a provision of **₹5,830 crore** has been made to expand piped water supply in urban areas.  
  - In energy, the state aims to add more than **6,400 MW of power capacity**, along with **50,000 new agricultural connections** and **5 lakh domestic connections**.  
  - The **Chief Minister Free Electricity Scheme** proposes **up to 150 units per month of free electricity** for eligible domestic consumers, linked with solar promotion.  

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Key figures – **20 lakh water connections, ₹5,830 crore for Jal Jeevan (Urban), 6,400+ MW, 150 units free power** – can appear in factual questions.  
- These measures tie directly into topics on **village development, agriculture, and urbanisation** in Rajasthan.  

**Memory Trick – “20 lakh water, 150 units power”**

> Simple code: **“20L connections + 150 units free”** = Rajasthan’s water &amp; power focus in Budget 2025‑26.

## TOPIC 6: India Post’s First ONDC Order – Relevance for Rajasthan’s MSMEs

### Kya hua?

- At the national level, **India Post** has delivered its **first order as logistics service provider** on the **Open Network for Digital Commerce (ONDC)**.  
- The order was a shipment of **walnuts** from a local seller in **Srinagar (J&amp;K)** to a consumer in **New Delhi**, fully managed through India Post’s logistics system integrated with the ONDC protocol.  

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- For Rajasthan aspirants, this topic matters because **ONDC + India Post** can provide a strong digital marketplace for **Rajasthan’s handicrafts, agro‑products and ODOP items** in the future.  
- It is a good example for mains answers on **digital commerce, MSMEs and rural market access**.  

**Memory Trick – “ONDC + India Post = national digital bazaar”**

> Visualise small producers from Rajasthan connecting to buyers across India through **ONDC powered deliveries by India Post**.
