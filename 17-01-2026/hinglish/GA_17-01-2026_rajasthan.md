## TOPIC 1: Avikanagar me Indian Wool Sector par Chintan Shivir

### Kya hua?

- January 2026 me **Central Wool Development Board (CWDB)** ne **Avikanagar (Rajasthan)** me **Indian wool sector** par ek **Chintan Shivir** organise kiya.  
- Event **ICAR–Central Sheep and Wool Research Institute (CSWRI), Avikanagar** ke saath milkar hua.  
- Focus tha ki India ka **fine wool import par heavy dependence** kam ho, aur **sheep rearing se leke processing &amp; branding** tak poori value chain ko strong banaya ja sake.  

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Avikanagar, CWDB aur CSWRI – **sheep &amp; wool research** ke important Rajasthan‑based centres hain, jo RAS/Police/GK me factual question ban sakte hain.  
- Wool sector se **rural employment, tribal areas aur desert economy** directly linked hai – yeh development/eco type questions me use ho sakta hai.  

**Memory Trick – “Avika‑nagar = AVI (sheep) ka research nagar”**

> Dimaag me map pe Avikanagar ko **“sheep &amp; wool chintan”** likh kar mark kar lo.

## TOPIC 5: Drinking Water &amp; Energy – Jal Jeevan Mission (Urban) &amp; Free Electricity

### Kya hua?

- Budget 2025‑26 me **drinking water &amp; energy** ke liye kaafi announcements ki gayi:  
  - Rural areas me **peene ke paani ke liye ₹425 crore**, aur agle saal **20 lakh gharon** ko new water connection dene ka target.  
  - **“Chief Minister Jal Jeevan Mission (Urban)”** ke तहत **₹5,830 crore** ka provision, jisse shehri areas me piped water supply ko बढ़ाया jayega.  
  - Energy side par, agle saal **6,400 MW se zyada nayi बिजली generation**, **50,000 naye agricultural connections** aur **5 lakh domestic connections** dene ka plan.  
  - **Chief Minister Free Electricity Scheme** me नीजी consumers ko **150 units/month tak free** बिजली dene ka prastaav, solar promotion ke saath.  

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Direct fact: **Jal Jeevan Mission (Urban) – ₹5,830 crore, 20 lakh connections, 6,400 MW target, 150 units free electricity** – ye data type Qs me aa sakta hai.  
- Water &amp; power schemes Rajasthan ke **village development, agriculture &amp; urbanisation** topics me favourite ban sakte hain.  

**Memory Trick – “20 lakh water, 150 unit power”**

> Simple code: **“20L connections – 150 units free”** = Rajasthan water &amp; power focus 2025‑26.
