## TOPIC 1: RBI Central Complaints Processing Centre (CRPC) – Grievance Redressal ka National Hub

### Kya hua?

- **Reserve Bank of India (RBI)** ne apni **Integrated Ombudsman Scheme** ko aur strong banane ke liye ek naya **Central Complaints Processing Centre (CRPC)** announce kiya hai.  
- Ye CRPC ek **centralised receipt &amp; processing hub** hoga jo **email aur physical post se aane wali saari complaints** ko filter karega.  
- Jo complaints online **CMS portal (https://cms.rbi.org.in)** par file hongi woh direct system me jayengi, jabki **email/post** wali complaints CRPC ke through screen hongi.  
- Scheme **1 July 2026** se revised framework ke saath effective hogi jahan ek hi platform se complaints ko RBI Ombudsman / Deputy Ombudsman tak bheja jayega.  
- Revised scheme ke under **compensation limit ₹30 lakh** tak ho sakti hai (financial loss ke cases me) aur harassment/time-loss ke liye bhi additional compensation ka provision diya gaya hai.  

(Official context: RBI announcement on Integrated Ombudsman Scheme &amp; CRPC; Photo: https://currentaffairs.adda247.com/wp-content/uploads/multisite/sites/5/2026/01/17091034/Untitled-design-2026-01-16T205317.518-300x169.webp)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Integrated Ombudsman Scheme + CRPC = **customer protection, consumer rights, grievance redressal** – ye sab banking awareness ka core हिस्सा hai.  
- Prelims/MCQ angle:  
  - CRPC kis scheme ke under hai? → **RBI Integrated Ombudsman Scheme**  
  - Start date? → **1 July 2026 (revised scheme ke saath)**  
  - Maximum compensation? → **₹30 lakh**  
- Mains/interview me isse **“customer‑centric regulation”** aur **financial consumer protection** ka example ke roop me use kar sakte ho.  

### Memory Trick – “C = Complaints, C = Centre, C = CMS”

> Code yaad rakho: **CRPC = Central Complaints Processing Centre, CMS ke saath linked, Ombudsman Scheme ke under** – teen C: *Complaints–Centre–CMS*.

## TOPIC 2: SEBI ke naye Mutual Fund Rules – Performance-Based Expense Structure

### Kya hua?

- **SEBI** ne mutual funds ke liye naya **performance-based expense structure** notify kiya hai jo **1 April 2026** se लागू hoga.  
- Ab funds ek **Base Expense Ratio (BER)** charge karenge, jisme basic running cost covered hogi, aur uske upar **performance-linked expenses** add kiye ja sakte hain (defined limits ke andar).  
- Brokerage aur transaction cost par **caps reduce** kiye gaye hain, aur **baaki saare charges ko alag se clearly disclose** karna hoga.  
- Governance norms ko bhi tighten kiya gaya hai – **board oversight, risk management, mis‑selling control** par जोर diya gaya hai.  
- Objective: **cost transparency badhana, investor ke interest ko align karna aur over‑charging ko curb karna**, especially actively managed schemes me.  

(Official context: SEBI circulars on Mutual Fund expenses reform; Photo: https://currentaffairs.adda247.com/wp-content/uploads/multisite/sites/5/2026/01/17091032/Daily-gk-update-16th-january-2026-1-300x169.webp)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Mutual funds banking exams ka regular topic hain – **TER / expense ratio / SEBI regulation** ke questions frequently aate hain.  
- MCQ pointers:  
  - Effective date? → **1 April 2026**  
  - Kisko introduce kiya? → **SEBI**  
  - Key concept? → **Base Expense Ratio + performance-linked expense**  
- Mains me ye ek strong example hai ki kaise **SEBI investor protection + transparency + governance** ko strengthen kar raha hai.  

### Memory Trick – “BER = Base Expense, Performance ke upar bonus”

> Socho mutual fund ka bill do parts me hai: **“Base” (BER) + “Bonus” (performance-linked)** – April 2026 se यही logic chalega.

## TOPIC 3: India ke Forex Reserves – $392 Million ka Increment, Total ~ $687.19 Billion

### Kya hua?

- Mid‑January 2026 ke RBI data ke mutabik **India ke foreign exchange reserves** me **$392 million** ka halka sa rise record hua.  
- Total forex reserves approx **$687.19 billion** tak pahunch gaye – jo global standards ke हिसाब se comfortable buffer mana jaata hai.  
- Breakdown me dekha gaya ki **foreign currency assets (FCA)** me thoda decline tha, lekin **gold reserves me sharp increase** ne overall reserves ko upar push kiya.  
- **SDRs (Special Drawing Rights)** aur **IMF ke saath reserve position** me halka negative change dekha gaya, lekin net position positive bani rahi.  
- High reserves ka matlab hai **external stability, import cover, rupee defense capacity aur investor confidence** me improvement.  

(Official context: RBI weekly statistical supplement on forex reserves; Photo: https://currentaffairs.adda247.com/wp-content/uploads/multisite/sites/5/2026/01/17091036/Untitled-design-2026-01-16T205623.795-300x169.webp)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Foreign exchange reserves, composition (FCA, gold, SDR, IMF) aur level – **RBI &amp; economy GA ka standard topic** hai.  
- MCQ me pooch sakte hain:  
  - Latest reserves approx kitne? → **$687 billion+** (range based Q)  
  - Reserves me kaunsi item hoti hai? → FCA, gold, SDR, IMF position.  
- Mains me **external sector stability, rupee management, balance of payments** wale answers me ye data useful hai.  

### Memory Trick – “6–8–7 = strong buffer”

> Approx number yaad rakho: **“687 bn”** – code: **6–8–7**; socho **6–8–7 ek shield hai jo India ko external shocks se protect kar raha hai**.

## TOPIC 4: Shri Praveen Vashista – Central Vigilance Commission ke naye Vigilance Commissioner

### Kya hua?

- January 2026 me **Shri Praveen Vashista** ko **Central Vigilance Commission (CVC)** me **Vigilance Commissioner** ke roop me नियुक्त kiya gaya.  
- Vashista ji **1991‑batch ke IPS officer (Bihar cadre)** hain; unhone **Bihar Police, CBI, Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA)** jaise महत्त्वपूर्ण पदों par kaam kiya hai.  
- Unka appointment **CVC Act, 2003** ke under hua, aur unhone **16 January 2026** ko शपथ ग्रहण ki.  
- CVC ek **apex anti‑corruption body** hai jo Central Government offices, PSUs, banks aur financial institutions me vigilance ko oversee karta hai.  

(Official context: CVC press release / notification on appointment; Photo: https://currentaffairs.adda247.com/wp-content/uploads/multisite/sites/5/2026/01/17091009/Untitled-design-2026-01-16T211903.542-300x169.webp)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Public sector banks, insurance companies, aur CPSEs me **vigilance &amp; anti‑corruption framework** ko CVC closely monitor karta hai – isliye CVC &amp; Vigilance Commissioner ka naam exam me aa sakta hai.  
- MCQ points:  
  - New Vigilance Commissioner? → **Praveen Vashista**  
  - Service background? → **IPS, 1991‑batch**  
  - Parent body? → **Central Vigilance Commission (CVC)**  
- Interview/mains me governance aur ethics se जुड़े answers me **CVC + CBI + Lokpal** ka linkage explain karna important hota hai.  

### Memory Trick – “PV = Vigilance Power”

> Naam yaad rakhne ke liye code banao: **P (Praveen) + V (Vashista) = PV = ‘Vigilance Power’ in CVC**.
