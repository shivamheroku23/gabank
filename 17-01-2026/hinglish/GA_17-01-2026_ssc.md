## TOPIC 1: Indian Wool Sector par Chintan Shivir – Avikanagar, Rajasthan

### Kya hua?

- **Central Wool Development Board (CWDB), Ministry of Textiles** ne January 2026 me **Avikanagar, Rajasthan** me **Indian wool sector** par ek **Chintan Shivir** organise kiya.  
- Event **ICAR–Central Sheep and Wool Research Institute (CSWRI), Avikanagar** ke saath milkar hua – focus tha **challenges, opportunities &amp; future prospects** of wool sector.  
- Aim tha ki India ki **fine wool imports par dependency kam** ho, aur **sheep rearing + wool processing + branding + export** poori value chain strong ho.  
- Key ideas: **sustainable sheep rearing, technical textiles me R&amp;D, Common Facility Centres (CFCs) expand karna, MSME linkages** ko improve karna.  

(Official context: CWDB / Ministry of Textiles consultation on wool sector)

### SSC exam points:

- Place: **Avikanagar, Rajasthan**.  
- Institutions: **CWDB + ICAR–CSWRI**.  
- Theme: **Indian Wool Sector – challenges, opportunities, future roadmap**.  

**Memory Trick – “Avikanagar = AVI (sheep) + wool chintan”**

> Yaad rakho: **“AVIKA” = bhains nahi, ‘avi’ = sheep** – Avikanagar = wool par chintan shivir.

## TOPIC 2: Gujarat ka ‘Poshan Udaan 2026’ – Patang se Poshan tak

### Kya hua?

- **Gujarat Sarkar** ne **Makar Sankranti / Uttarayan** ke time **‘Poshan Udaan 2026’** launch kiya.  
- Idea simple hai: popular **kite festival** ka use karke **kites par nutrition &amp; health messages** likhkar logon me जागरूकता phailana.  
- Initiative **ICDS (Integrated Child Development Services)** ke under **Anganwadi centres** ke through chalaya jaa raha hai; nodal dept **Department of Women &amp; Child Development, Gujarat** hai.  
- Focus: **balanced diet, kam junk food, safe drinking water, sanitation** aur **children + adolescent girls + pregnant &amp; lactating women** ka better nutrition.  

### SSC exam points:

- State: **Gujarat**.  
- Campaign: **‘Poshan Udaan 2026’**.  
- Linked with: **Makar Sankranti / Uttarayan (kite festival)** + **ICDS / Anganwadi network**.  

**Memory Trick – “Patang Ude, Poshan badhe”**

> Dimaag me image banao: **Ahmedabad ke sky me kites jisme likha hai ‘Poshan Udaan 2026’**.

## TOPIC 3: Tamil Nadu ka pehla ‘Sovereign AI Park’ – Chennai

### Kya hua?

- **13 January 2026** ko **Tamil Nadu Government** ne **Sarvam AI** ke saath MoU sign kiya, jisme **India ka pehla Sovereign AI Park** **Chennai** me banega.  
- Park me **GPU-based data centres, indigenous Large Language Models (LLMs), AI tools** – poora **full‑stack AI infra** develop hoga.  
- Special focus **Indic‑centric AI**, especially **Tamil language LLMs** par hai, taaki non‑English users bhi advanced AI ka use kar saken.  
- Project **Tamil Nadu e‑Governance Agency (TNeGA)** ke saath milkar implement hoga – govt services me AI integrate karne ke liye.  
- “Sovereign AI” ka matlab – **apna data, apna infra, apne experts** – yani AI me **data sovereignty + strategic autonomy**.  

### SSC exam points:

- State: **Tamil Nadu**.  
- Project: **India’s first Sovereign AI Park**.  
- City: **Chennai**.  
- Linked mission: **IndiaAI Mission**, digital autonomy.  

**Memory Trick – “Chennai = India ka Sovereign AI hub”**

> Map pe Chennai ke upar likho – **“1st Sovereign AI Park of India”**.

## TOPIC 4: India Post ka ONDC par pehla order – Srinagar se Delhi tak walnuts

### Kya hua?

- **Department of Posts (India Post)** ab **Open Network for Digital Commerce (ONDC)** par **logistics service provider** ke रूप me onboard ho chuka hai.  
- India Post ne apna **first ONDC order** deliver kiya – **walnuts** jo ek local seller, **Srinagar (J&amp;K)** se **New Delhi** ke customer tak bheje gaye.  
- Pura chain – **first‑mile pickup, sorting, transport, last‑mile delivery** – India Post ne apne logistics software ko ONDC protocol ke saath integrate karke manage kiya.  
- Is se ONDC ko **1.6 lakh+ post offices** ke network ka फायदा milega; rural artisans, farmers, MSMEs ko national market tak access mil sakta hai.  

### SSC exam points:

- Logistics provider: **India Post**.  
- Network: **ONDC – Open Network for Digital Commerce**.  
- First order: **walnuts, Srinagar → New Delhi**.  
- Relevance: **MSME, ODOP, digital commerce &amp; logistics**.  

**Memory Trick – “Walnut parcel on ONDC”**

> Socho ek parcel jisme likha hai **“Walnuts from Srinagar – Delivered by India Post on ONDC”**.

## TOPIC 5: IOA ka National Olympic Education and Development Programme (NOEDP)

### Kya hua?

- **Indian Olympic Association (IOA)** ne **National Olympic Education and Development Programme (NOEDP)** launch kiya hai, jiska aim hai **India ka Olympic ecosystem** grassroots se lekar elite level tak मजबूत karna.  
- Iske under **National Olympic Academy (NOA)** ko **Ahmedabad** me formally reactivate kiya gaya hai – ye **Olympic education, research, dialogue** ka centre banega.  
- IOA President aur sprint legend **P.T. Usha** ko **NOA ki President** aur Olympic medallist **Gagan Narang** ko **Director** banaya gaya hai.  
- NOA, **International Olympic Academy (Olympia)** ke saath collaborate karega, taaki Indian programmes **Olympic Charter** ke standards ko meet kar saken.  

### SSC exam points:

- Organisation: **Indian Olympic Association (IOA)**.  
- Programme: **NOEDP – National Olympic Education and Development Programme**.  
- NOA location: **Ahmedabad**.  
- Leadership: **P.T. Usha – President, Gagan Narang – Director**.  

**Memory Trick – “NOEDP = Usha + Narang @ Ahmedabad”**

> Yaad rakhne ke liye line: **“Usha–Narang team, Ahmedabad NOA dream”**.
