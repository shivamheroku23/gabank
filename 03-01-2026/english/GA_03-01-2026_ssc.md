## TOPIC 2: Integrated e‑Bill System – Fertiliser Subsidies

- On **1 January 2026**, at Kartavya Bhawan, New Delhi:  
  Union Minister **J.P. Nadda** (Chemicals &amp; Fertilisers) launched the  
  **Integrated e‑Bill System for Fertiliser Subsidies**.  
- Purpose:  
  - To process nearly **₹2 lakh crore** of annual fertiliser subsidies  
    through an **online, paperless, end‑to‑end digital system**.  
- Features:  
  - companies submit their claims/bills online,  
  - integration of **iFMS** with **PFMS** → real‑time tracking and better control,  
  - FIFO processing and digital audit trail → reduced delays and fraud.

### SSC exam points

- Ministry: **Chemicals &amp; Fertilisers**.  
- Sector: **Fertiliser subsidies**.

### Memory Trick – “Fertiliser bills go e‑Bill”

> “Fertiliser ke bill – ab file nahi, mile e‑Bill.”

## TOPIC 3: Health &amp; National Security Cess – New Burden on Sin Goods

- Government decided that on **tobacco, pan masala, gutkha** and similar **sin goods**,  
  the earlier **GST Compensation Cess** will be gradually replaced by  
  an **Additional Excise Duty + Health &amp; National Security Cess**.  
- Aim:  
  - discourage harmful consumption,  
  - raise funds for **health systems** and **national security**‑related expenditure.

### SSC exam points

- Health &amp; National Security Cess applies to **sin goods** such as tobacco/pan masala.  
- It is an **additional indirect tax/cess** levied on top of existing taxes.

### Memory Trick – “HN = Health + Nation”

> HN Cess = extra tax **in the name of Health and Nation** on sin goods.

## TOPIC 4: Land Stack – Digital Land Records (DILRMP)

- Under the **Digital India Land Records Modernisation Programme (DILRMP)**,  
  the government adopted the **Land Stack** concept –  
  integrating land records, maps and encumbrances digitally in one place.  
- Pilot implementation is underway in **Chandigarh** and **Tamil Nadu**,  
  using GIS‑based land data on a unified platform.

### SSC exam points

- DILRMP relates to **land record computerisation and modernisation**.  
- Land Stack = land data in a **digital “stack”/layer** accessible to stakeholders.

### Memory Trick – “Land Stack = Land’s Digi‑Folder”

> Think of all land documents kept in a single **digital folder** – that is Land Stack.

## TOPIC 5: RBI Financial Stability Report &amp; Capital Conservation Buffer (CCB)

- In its **Financial Stability Report (FSR)**, RBI noted that:  
  - in a **baseline/moderate stress** scenario, a few banks may have to  
    draw down their **Capital Conservation Buffer (CCB)**,  
  - but system‑wide capital adequacy remains comfortable.  
- CCB is a part of **Basel‑III capital norms**, about **2.5% of RWAs**.

### SSC exam points

- CCB belongs to the **Basel‑III capital framework**.  
- Role: an **extra cushion** to absorb losses during downturns.

### Memory Trick – “CCB = Cushion Capital Buffer”

> CCB is a **Cushion Capital Buffer** –  
> a safety cushion for banks in bad times.
