## TOPIC 3: K-4 Missile Test from INS Arighaat (Sea-based Nuclear)

- India successfully test‑fired the **K-4 medium‑range ballistic missile**.  
- Launch platform: **INS Arighaat** – a nuclear‑powered ballistic missile submarine (SSBN).  
- Range: about **3,500 km**.  
- Developed by: **DRDO**.  
- It is a solid‑fuel missile with advanced navigation.  
- Purpose:
  - Strengthen **sea‑based nuclear deterrence**,  
  - Complete and reinforce India’s **nuclear triad** (Land, Air, Sea),  
  - Improve **second‑strike capability**.

**Exam points:**

- K-4 is what type of missile? → **Submarine‑launched ballistic missile (SLBM)**.  
- Approx range: **3,500 km**.  
- Launched from: **INS Arighaat**.  
- Developed by: **DRDO**.

**Memory Trick:**

- “K‑4 ≈ 3.5K km range” – name “4” and range “3.5”.  
- Line: **“K‑4 – launched from a submarine, can hit up to 3.5K km away.”**

## TOPIC 12: Jal Seva Aankalan & Jal Jeevan Mission

- **Jal Seva Aankalan:**
  - Launched by Dept of Drinking Water & Sanitation (DDWS), Ministry of Jal Shakti.  
  - A **Gram Panchayat‑led digital water service functionality assessment tool** on the JJM portal.  
  - Objective: enable Har Ghar Jal Gram Panchayats to self‑assess their **water service delivery**.  
  - Deadline: Har Ghar Jal Gram Panchayats are expected to finish the assessment by **26 Jan 2026**.

  - Ministry: **Jal Shakti**.

  - Service level: **55 LPCD**, BIS:10500 quality.  
  - First 100% state: **Goa**; first UT: **Dadra & Nagar Haveli and Daman & Diu**.

## TOPIC 14: Defence Contracts & DRDO Pinaka LRGR

### Defence contracts (MoD)

- The Ministry of Defence (MoD) signed capital contracts worth about **₹4,666 crore**:
  - Around **4.25 lakh CQB carbines** (~₹2,770 cr) – with Bharat Forge Ltd & PLR Systems Pvt Ltd for the Army & Navy.  
  - **48 Heavy Weight Torpedoes** (~₹1,896 cr) – with WASS Submarine Systems S.R.L., Italy – for **Kalvari‑class submarines (Project‑75)**.  
  - Torpedo deliveries are to begin from **April 2028** and finish by **early 2030**.
- **Project‑75:**
  - 6 Kalvari‑class diesel‑electric attack submarines (Scorpene design).

### Pinaka LRGR 120

- The **Pinaka Long Range Guided Rocket (LRGR 120)** had a successful maiden flight test at ITR Chandipur.  
- Range: **about 120 km**.  
- Designed by:
  - **Armament Research & Development Establishment (ARDE)**,  
  - **High Energy Materials Research Laboratory (HEMRL)**,  
  - with support from **DRDL & RCI** – all DRDO labs.  
- The Pinaka system:
  - Named after Lord Shiva’s bow **Pinaka**.  
  - Became famous in the Kargil War for neutralising high‑altitude positions.

**Exam points:**

- CQB carbine procurement, heavy torpedoes & Project‑75 are key defence updates.  
- Pinaka LRGR: **range 120 km, developed by DRDO**.

**Memory Trick:**

- ₹4,666 crore: “4 guns, 6 torpedoes, 6–6 numbers” – an easy rhythm.  
- Pinaka LRGR:
  > “Shiva’s bow – striking up to **120 km**.”
