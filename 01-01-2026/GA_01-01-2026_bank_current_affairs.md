## TOPIC 1: New RBI Digital Banking Framework (From 1 Jan 2026)

### Kya badla?

- 1 Jan 2026 se, **Internet banking, Mobile banking, USSD, SMS banking, UPI** – in sabko run karne ke liye banks ko:
  - **Special authorisation + strict rules** follow karne padenge.
- Banks ko:
  - Customer ka **explicit consent** lena hoga digital services enable karne se pehle.
  - Har operation (debit/credit, registration, limit change, etc.) par **alert** dena mandatory (SMS/Email/Push).
  - Customer ko **registration / de‑registration (on/off)** options clear dene honge for each channel.
  - Fraud / failed transaction / unauthorised transaction me **liability + grievance redressal** clear likhna hoga.
- Ye pura framework digital banking ko **secure, transparent, accountable** banane ke liye hai.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- RBI ka **naya framework** – direct MCQ material:
  - “From which date did new digital banking framework come into effect?”
  - “Which services need special authorisation under new RBI rules?”
- Descriptive / mains me short note type question:
  - “Write short note on RBI’s 2026 digital banking authorisation framework.”

### Memory Trick – CAAL

**Code word: `CAAL`**

- **C – Consent:**  
  Digital service auto-on nahi; written/digital consent zaroori.
- **A – Alerts:**  
  Har transaction / activity par SMS/Email/Push – “digital CCTV”.
- **A – Access control:**  
  Customer khud decide kare:
  - Net banking on/off  
  - Mobile banking on/off  
  - Channel choice (sirf mobile, sirf net, dono, etc.)
- **L – Liability & grievance:**  
  Fraud me kiska loss?  
  Complaint kitne din me solve?  
  Bank ko proper grievance system rakhna hoga.

**Ek line yaad:**  
> 2026 – Digital Banking **CAAL**: Consent, Alerts, Access, Liability.

## TOPIC 2: BSBD Accounts – Full Digital + Free Services

### Kya badlega?

- **BSBD** = Basic Savings Bank Deposit account (Jan Dhan jaisa flavour, no-frills).
- **1 April 2026** se (rule 1 Jan 2026 ke around announce/notify):
  - Sab banks ko BSBD holders ko **full digital banking** dena hoga:
    - Mobile banking  
    - Internet banking
  - BSBD accounts par:
    - **No minimum balance** requirement  
    - **Free cash deposits** – branch, ATM, business correspondent (BC)  
    - **Free ATM/Debit card + renewal**  
    - **Minimum cheque book facility free**  
    - **Free account statement**
- Existing customers apna normal account **BSBD me convert** kara sakte hain (on request).

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- BSBD = **financial inclusion** theme – frequent topic.
- Possible questions:
  - “BSBD accounts ke liye mandatory full digital banking from which date?”
  - “BSBD account ke key features – no minimum balance, free cash deposits etc.”

### Memory Trick – “BSBD = 4F”

**Code word: `4F`**

- **F – Full digital** (mobile + net banking)  
- **F – Free deposits** (branch, ATM, BC)  
- **F – Free ATM/debit + basic cheque book/statement**  
- **F – Free from minimum balance** (no minimum balance)

**Line:**  
> BSBD = **4F account** – Full Digital, Free deposits, Free card, Free from minimum balance.

## TOPIC 3: PAN–Aadhaar Linking (From 1 Jan 2026)

### Kya badla?

- 1 Jan 2026 se agar **PAN Aadhaar se link nahi hai**:
  - PAN **inoperative / blocked jaisa treat** ho sakta hai.
- Impact:
  - Income Tax Return file karne me problem  
  - Naya bank account open karna mushkil  
  - Loan, credit card, govt services me dikkat
- Banks & govt bodies **strictly PAN–Aadhaar status check** karein ge.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- **KYC + tax + banking** ka intersection – favourite area.
- Possible questions:
  - “Consequences of inoperative PAN?”
  - “From Jan 2026, why is PAN–Aadhaar linking critical?”

### Memory Trick – “PAN bina Aadhaar = LOCK”

- PAN card = **chabi**; Aadhaar = **handle**.  
- Aadhaar handle nahi laga → chabi se lock nahi khulega.

**Line:**  
> 2026 se PAN-Aadhaar na jode, toh **financial gate LOCK** ho jaye.

## TOPIC 4: Weekly Credit Score Updates (From 1 Jan 2026)

### Kya badla?

- Pehle: credit score approx **15 din me ek baar** update hota tha.
- Ab: 1 Jan 2026 se **weekly update** rule.
- Matlab:
  - EMI time pe bharoge → **score jaldi improve**.
  - EMI miss / default → **negative impact bhi jaldi show**.
- Banks ke liye:
  - Loan decision **recent behavior** par based.
  - **Risk assessment more accurate**.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Consumer lending, credit card, EMI – sabka base **credit score**.
- Expected MCQs:
  - “From Jan 2026, credit score data must be updated at least how often?”
  - “Faster credit score update ka benefit kya hai – better risk assessment etc.”

### Memory Trick – “Sunday Score Report”

- Socho har **Sunday** tumhara credit ka “**Report Card Day**” hai.
- Poore week ka behavior → ek **naya score** ban ke bank ke paas chala gaya.

**Line:**  
> Har week, credit ka result – **good boy ya defaulter decide.**

## TOPIC 5: Credit/Debit Card Rules Change – Lounge, Rewards, Charges

### Kya badla?

- Bade banks (SBI, HDFC, ICICI, IDFC First, etc.) ne **card features modify** kiye:
  - **Airport lounge access**:
    - Ab mostly **minimum spend condition** se linked.  
    - Spend target achieve nahi kiya to lounge access **disable / limit**.
  - Kuch categories par (online gaming, wallet load, etc.):
    - **Additional charges / lower reward points.**
  - Kai cards me:
    - Overall **reward structure trim / categories change.**
- Objective:
  - Bank ka **cost control**  
  - Jinko card use nahi karte, unko **premium benefit nahi** dena  
  - High-cost benefits ka **misuse control.**

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Banking awareness me **credit card features + charges + benefits** regular topic hai.
- Possible questions:
  - “Lounge access is now often linked to minimum spends – reason?”
  - “Which type of transactions may attract new fees or reduced rewards?”

### Memory Trick – “Lounge tabhi, jab Swipe zyada”

- Character: **Lalu – Lounge Lover**  
- Rule: “Lalu ko lounge tabhi milega, **jab swipe zyada karega.**”

**Code:**  
> **LS Rule = Lounge = Spending.**

## TOPIC 6: GoI G-Sec Re-issue – 6.48% GS 2035

### Kya hai?

- Government of India ne **“6.48% Government Security 2035”** ka **re-issue** announce kiya.  
- **Notified amount:** ₹32,000 crore (nominal), **price-based auction, multiple price method.**  
- RBI Mumbai Office auction conduct karega (02 Jan 2026).  
- GoI ke paas option hai ki extra **₹2,000 crore** tak **additional subscription retain** kare.  
- Up to **5% notified amount** non-competitive bidding ke through individuals/institutions ko milega.  
- Bidding RBI ke **Core Banking Solution – E‑Kuber system** par hoti hai.  
- Security **“When Issued” trading** ke liye eligible hai (RBI guidelines 2018).

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Concepts:
  - **Multiple price auction vs uniform price auction.**
  - **Non‑competitive bidding facility (5% quota).**
  - **E‑Kuber** – RBI ka core platform for govt securities.
  - **“When Issued”** market – pre‑issue trading.
- Ye sab SEBI/RBI/Bank mains ke liye favourite **theory points** hain.

### Memory Trick – “6.48 – 32K – WI”

**Code line:**  
> “6.48 waali 2035 bond – **32K crore, E‑Kuber par, When Issued bhi on.**”

Bas yaad: 6.48% GS 2035 + **multiple price + non‑competitive 5% + E‑Kuber + WI.**

## TOPIC 8: RBI Static – Foundation & Subsidiaries

### Key facts (yaad rakhna hi rakhna):

- **Establishment:** 1 April 1935 (Hilton Young Commission recommendation).  
- **RBI Act:** Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.  
- **Nationalisation:** 1 January 1949 (Transfer to Public Ownership Act, 1948).  
- **HQ:** Initially Kolkata, 1937 se Mumbai.  
- **First Governor:** Sir Osborne Smith.  
- **First Indian Governor:** C.D. Deshmukh.  
- **Current Governor (2025):** Sanjay Malhotra (took office 11 Dec 2024).  
- **Central Board of Directors:** up to 21 members (Governor, up to 4 Deputy Governors, govt nominees, local board directors).  

**Major subsidiaries:**

- **DICGC** – Deposit Insurance & Credit Guarantee (bank deposits insurance).  
- **BRBNMPL** – Bharatiya Reserve Bank Note Mudran Pvt Ltd (note printing – Mysuru & Salboni).  
- **IFTAS** – Indian Financial Technology & Allied Services (IT services for banking).  
- **ReBIT** – Reserve Bank Information Technology (cybersecurity).

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Ye sab **classic static banking** questions hain – har exam me baar‑baar repeat hote hain.  
- RBI ke subsidiaries (DICGC, BRBNMPL) toh almost **must‑know** hai.

### Memory Tricks

- **Establishment & Nationalisation:**  
  > “RBI 35 me paida, 49 me sarkari.”

- **First vs First Indian:**  
  > “Smith pehle, Deshmukh pehle Indian.”

- **Subsidiaries code:** `DBIR`
  - **D** – DICGC  
  - **B** – BRBNMPL  
  - **I** – IFTAS  
  - **R** – ReBIT

## TOPIC 10: Jal Jeevan Mission & Jal Seva Aankalan (Water Services)

### Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM)

- **Launch:** 15 August 2019.  
- **Ministry:** Ministry of Jal Shakti.  
- **Goal:** Functional Household Tap Connection (FHTC) to all rural homes.  
- **Service level:** 55 Litres per capita per day (LPCD), BIS:10500 quality.  
- **Revised deadline:** 2028 (as per Union Budget 2025‑26).  
- First milestones:
  - First state 100%: **Goa**  
  - First UT: **Dadra & Nagar Haveli and Daman & Diu**

### Jal Seva Aankalan

- Dept of Drinking Water & Sanitation (DDWS), Ministry of Jal Shakti ne **Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) portal** par **Gram Panchayat‑led digital drinking water service functionality assessment tool** launch kiya.  
- Objective:
  - Har Ghar Jal Gram Panchayats apne **water service delivery** ka self‑assessment kar sakein (community-owned assessment).  
- Target:
  - Sab **Har Ghar Jal Gram Panchayats** se expectation – **26 Jan 2026** tak Jal Seva Aankalan complete.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Rural infrastructure, water, sanitation – priority schemes; **mains GA & essay** topics.  
- Loan products, PSL (priority sector lending) context me bhi JJM ka zikr aata hai.

### Memory Trick

- **JJM:**  
  > “15 August ko PM ne gift diya – Har Ghar Jal ka target.”

- **Jal Seva Aankalan:**  
  > “Gaon khud apna water report card banayega – app par.”
