## TOPIC 1: India–Fiji Agriculture MoU – 5 Saal ka Extension

### Kya hua?

- New Delhi me India ke **Agriculture Minister Shivraj Singh Chouhan** aur Fiji ke **Minister for Agriculture &amp; Waterways Tomasi Tunabuna** ke beech bilateral meeting hui.  
- Dono deshon ne **agriculture cooperation MoU ko 5 saal (2026–2031)** ke liye extend karne par agree kiya.  
- Saath hi **Joint Working Group (JWG) on Agriculture &amp; Food Security** banane ka faisla hua jo MoU ka implementation monitor karega.  
- Key cooperation areas: **student/researcher exchange, training &amp; capacity building, small farm machinery, digital agriculture, climate-resilient crops, post-harvest loss reduction**.

### SSC exam points:

- MoU extension sector → **Agriculture &amp; food security**.  
- Duration → **2026–2031 (5 years)**.  
- Nayi body → **Joint Working Group (JWG)** on agriculture &amp; food security.  
- Focus → training, technology transfer, mechanisation, digital agriculture.

**Memory Trick – “India–Fiji 5-year farm bridge”**

> Line: **“India–Fiji farm MoU + 5 saal + JWG on Agriculture &amp; Food Security.”**

## TOPIC 3: National IED Data Management System (NIDMS) – NSG ka Bomb-Data Brain

### Kya hua?

- Union Home Minister **Amit Shah** ne **NSG garrison, Manesar (Gurugram)** se virtually **National IED Data Management System (NIDMS)** launch kiya.  
- NIDMS ek **secure national digital platform** hai jo **IED (Improvised Explosive Device) se जुड़ी saari data** ko ek jagah store, standardise aur analyse karta hai.  
- System me device type, explosive material, circuitry, location, modus operandi, forensic findings jaisi details **common format** me feed hoti hain; AI/ML tools se pattern analysis hota hai.  
- Ye system **ICJS-2 (Integrated Criminal Justice System)** se integrated hai aur **NIA, state ATS, state police, CAPFs** jaise agencies ko graded access deta hai.

### SSC exam points:

- NIDMS full form → **National IED Data Management System**.  
- Developer → **NSG (National Security Guard)**, MHA ke under.  
- Role → **IED-related data ka national repository + pattern analysis platform**.  
- Integration → **ICJS-2** ke saath “One data – One entry” principle.

**Memory Trick – “NIDMS = India ka bomb-data brain”**

> Socho: **ek central ‘brain’ jisme saari IED incidents ki memory store hai – wahi NIDMS.**

## TOPIC 4: Pravasi Bharatiya Divas (PBD) 2026 – 9 January ka महत्व

### Kya hua?

- **Pravasi Bharatiya Divas (PBD) / NRI Day** har saal **9 January** ko manaya jata hai – is din **1915** me **Mahatma Gandhi South Africa se Bharat wapas aaye the**.  
- Bada PBD convention **biennial (हर दो साल)** hota hai; **18th PBD Convention** **Bhubaneswar, Odisha** me **8–10 January 2025** ko hua tha.  
- 18th convention ka theme tha: **“Diaspora’s Contribution to a Viksit Bharat”**.  
- 2026 me koi main convention plan nahi hai (next ~2027), lekin 9 January ko phir bhi **seminars, cultural programmes, outreach events** ke through PBD observe kiya ja raha hai.

### SSC exam points:

- PBD date → **9 January**.  
- Significance → **Gandhi ji ka Bharat wapsi din (1915)**.  
- 18th PBD Convention → **Bhubaneswar, Odisha; 8–10 January 2025; theme – “Diaspora’s Contribution to a Viksit Bharat.”**  
- Convention frequency → **biennial**, lekin PBD day har saal.

**Memory Trick – “9 January – Gandhi returns, PBD begins”**

> Code: **“9 Jan 1915 Gandhi wapas → 9 Jan har saal Pravasi Bharatiya Divas.”**

## TOPIC 5: World Hindi Day vs Hindi Diwas

### Kya hua?

- **World Hindi Day (Vishwa Hindi Diwas)** har saal **10 January** ko manaya jata hai – ye **1975 me Nagpur me hui pehli World Hindi Conference** ki anniversary se जुड़ा hai.  
- **Hindi Diwas** India me **14 September** ko manaya jata hai – is din **1949** me Constituent Assembly ne Hindi ko Bharat ki **rajbhasha (official language)** ke roop me adopt kiya tha.  
- 2026 me World Hindi Day par embassies, cultural centres, universities ne **kavya-pathan, essay competition, seminars, cultural events** organise kiye.  
- 2026 ke liye globally ek single official theme clearly announce nahi dikh raha, isliye exams me zyadatar **date, place aur difference** par questions banenge.

### SSC exam points:

- World Hindi Day → **10 January**, base → **Nagpur World Hindi Conference (1975)**.  
- Hindi Diwas (India) → **14 September**, base → **1949 me official language adoption**.  
- World Hindi Day mainly **Indian missions abroad + global Hindi promotion** ke liye.

**Memory Trick – “10 Jan global, 14 Sep national”**

> Line: **“10 Jan = World Hindi Day (global); 14 Sep = Hindi Diwas (India ke andar).”**

## TOPIC 6: National Youth Day 2026 – “Ignite the Self, Impact the World”

### Kya hua?

- **National Youth Day (Yuva Diwas)** har saal **12 January** ko manaya jata hai – ye **Swami Vivekananda** ( जन्म: 1863 ) ki **jayanti** hai.  
- Government of India ne 1984 me National Youth Day declare kiya tha; **1985 se ye rozana 12 January** ko observe ho raha hai.  
- **National Youth Day 2026 ka theme** hai: **“Ignite the Self, Impact the World”** – yani pehle khud ko majboot banao, phir society par positive impact lao.  
- Desh bhar me schools, colleges, NSS units, youth organisations ne **runs, debates, cultural programmes, sewa activities** organise kiye.

### SSC exam points:

- National Youth Day date → **12 January**.  
- Personality → **Swami Vivekananda**.  
- Declaration → GoI ne **1984 me**, pehli baar celebration **1985** me.  
- Theme 2026 → **“Ignite the Self, Impact the World.”**

**Memory Trick – “12 January – Vivekananda, ignite self”**

> Code: **“12 Jan – Swami Vivekananda Jayanti – theme ‘Ignite the Self, Impact the World’.”**
