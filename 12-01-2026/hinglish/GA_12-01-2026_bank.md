## TOPIC 1: Bank of Baroda – Bank PD se Standalone Primary Dealer (SPD) ki taraf

### Kya hua?

- January 2026 me **Reserve Bank of India (RBI)** ne **Bank of Baroda (BoB)** ko in-principle approval diya ki wo apna **Bank Primary Dealer (Bank PD)** business ek **wholly-owned Standalone Primary Dealer (SPD) subsidiary** me shift kare.  
- Abhi BoB ke andar hi ek department PD activity karta hai; restructuring ke baad **alag SPD company** hogi jiske paas **alag licence, capital base aur risk framework** hoga, jo sirf G-Sec market par focus karegi.  
- Primary Dealers RBI-authorised entities hoti hain jo **Government securities (G-Secs) ke auctions me underwriting** karti hain aur **primary + secondary market** me active trading karti hain.  
- RBI data ke hisaab se Bharat me abhi **7 SPDs aur 14 Bank PDs** hain; BoB ka move Bank PD group se SPD group ki taraf transition hoga.  
- In-principle approval conditions ke saath aata hai – **capital adequacy, governance, ring-fencing, risk management systems** sab satisfy karne honge.  

(Official context: RBI press releases on primary dealer authorisations – https://www.rbi.org.in &gt; Press Releases)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- GA me PD se related questions common hain – **G-Sec market, government borrowing, SLR portfolio, yield curve** sab ke context me.  
- Difference yaad rakho: **Bank PD = bank ke andar PD desk**, jabki **SPD = alag company, dedicated G-Sec dealer**.  
- Mains/interview me PSBs ke **capital-market focused subsidiaries** (SPD, merchant bank, AMC, broker) par discussion aa sakta hai.

### Memory Trick – “BoB: Bank PD se SPD”

> Line: **“2026 me BoB – Bank PD se Standalone Primary Dealer subsidiary ki taraf.”**

## TOPIC 2: Skydo ko RBI ka PA-CB Licence – Export Payments ke liye

### Kya hua?

- RBI ne **Skydo** ko **Payment Aggregator – Cross Border (PA-CB)** ke roop me final authorisation de diya hai.  
- Skydo platform Indian **exporters, freelancers, MSMEs** ko allow karta hai ki wo overseas buyers se multiple currencies me payment receive karein, jo baad me **authorised dealer (AD) bank ke through INR me settle hoti hain**.  
- PA-CB licence ke under Skydo **merchants onboard** kar sakta hai, **overseas collection accounts** me funds le sakta hai aur FEMA rules follow karte hue **AD Category-I bank ke through settlement** kar sakta hai.  
- Pehle Skydo ke paas in-principle approval aur sandbox experience tha; ab wo **completely RBI-regulated cross-border payment aggregator** ban gaya hai.  

(Official context: RBI – list of authorised Payment Aggregators/Payment Gateways, https://www.rbi.org.in)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- RBI ne payment aggregators ko **licenced category** bana diya hai; **PA-CB** specifically **cross-border (export/import) online payments** ke liye hota hai.  
- Banks in entities ke saath **settlement bank, AD bank, FX partner** ke roop me kaam karte hain – definition &amp; abbreviation par MCQ aa sakta hai.  
- PA-CB entities **formal FX channels** ko strengthen karti hain, jisse **service export receipts, compliance aur AML monitoring** improve hota hai.

### Memory Trick – “Skydo = PA-CB for exporters”

> Yaad rakho: **“Skydo → sky-level global collections, PA-CB licence ke saath exporters ke liye.”**

## TOPIC 3: PayG – PA-P + PA-CB dono authorisation

### Kya hua?

- RBI ne **PayG** ko do authorisations diye hain:  \n  - **Payment Aggregator – Physical/Proximity (PA-P)** domestic transactions ke liye,  \n  - **Payment Aggregator – Cross Border (PA-CB)** export-linked online payments ke liye.  
- PA-P ke roop me PayG **card/UPI/QR based POS payments** ke liye merchants onboard karega; PA-CB ke roop me **cross-border e-commerce/export payments** process karega.  
- Is dual licence ki wajah se PayG ek **omni-channel acquiring stack** offer kar sakta hai – **in-store + online + cross-border** – ek hi platform par.  
- RBI ne is authorisation ko **minimum net-worth, escrow/nodal account safeguards, KYC/AML aur tech risk controls** jaise conditions ke saath diya hai.  

(Official context: RBI – authorisation of Payment Aggregators, https://www.rbi.org.in)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Ye example dikhata hai ki RBI **fintech aggregators ko formal regulatory perimeter** me laa raha hai – shadow risk kam karne ke liye.  
- Banks ke liye merchant acquiring space me **partnership vs competition** dono angle par sawal ban sakte hain – PayG jaise players **POS + QR + cross-border** solutions de rahe hain.  
- Digital payments, **financial inclusion aur export facilitation** jaise topics me PA-P/PA-CB ke full form aur role par direct MCQ aa sakta hai.

### Memory Trick – “PayG = do P: PA-P + PA-CB”

> Hook: **“PayG ke paas P + P – PA-P (domestic proximity) aur PA-CB (foreign payments).”**

## TOPIC 4: IPPB–Choice Wealth Digital Investment Platform

### Kya hua?

- **India Post Payments Bank (IPPB)** ne **Choice Wealth Private Limited** (Choice International Ltd ki subsidiary, SEBI-registered investment platform) ko select kiya hai ek **pan-India digital investment platform** banane ke liye.  
- Ye platform **IPPB mobile app + post offices + Gramin Dak Sevak network** ke through customers ko **mutual funds, sovereign gold bonds (SGBs), kuch govt schemes aur chhote investment products** tak pahunch dega.  
- **Choice Wealth tech + execution** handle karega, jabki IPPB **front-end distributor** ban kar fee income earn karega (payments bank khud lending nahi kar sakta).  
- Onboarding **completely digital (e-KYC, risk profiling, goal-based tools)** hoga, jis se remote villages me bhi log systematically invest kar sakenge.  

(Official context: IPPB announcements on digital investment platform partnership – https://ippbonline.com)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- IPPB ko GA me **financial inclusion, doorstep banking, postal network** ke context me baar-baar poocha jata hai.  
- Ye deal dikhati hai ki payments banks **interest income ke bajay fee-based distribution** (investment, insurance, pension) se revenue kama sakte hain.  
- Mains/interview me ye ek strong example hai **“post office se portfolio”** – rural households ko investment products se jodne ka.

### Memory Trick – “Post office se portfolio”

> Socho: **“IPPB + Choice Wealth = mutual fund &amp; SGB ab post office network se bhi.”**

## TOPIC 5: Morgan Stanley ka FY26 GDP Projection – 7.6%

### Kya hua?

- January 2026 ki ek research note me **Morgan Stanley** ne India ka **real GDP growth FY26 ke liye 7.6%** project kiya hai.  
- Ye number **National Statistical Office (NSO)** ke first advance estimate (**~7.4%**) se thoda higher hai.  
- Report ke mutabik growth drivers: **strong domestic demand, sarkari capex, services exports ka momentum, inflation ka ease hona**, etc.  
- Risks: **El Niño pattern (mid-2026 se), global trade slowdown, private capex revival me delay**; fir bhi India ko **fastest-growing major economy** ke roop me dekha gaya hai.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- GDP projections by **NSO, RBI, World Bank, IMF, rating agencies, brokerages** – sab prelims GA me favourite one-liners hote hain.  
- Mains me 7.6% forecast ko use karke India ko **“growth outlier”** ke roop me justify kar sakte ho, aur credit growth, NPA, profitability se link kar sakte ho.  
- Macro outlook directly **interest rates, lending appetite, asset quality** par impact karta hai – bank exams ke theory parts me ye context useful hai.

### Memory Trick – “7.6% – Morgan ka FY26 number”

> Short code: **“NSO ~7.4%, Morgan Stanley ~7.6% – India fastest-growing major economy.”**
