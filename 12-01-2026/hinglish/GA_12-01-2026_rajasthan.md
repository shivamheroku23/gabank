## TOPIC 1: Cold Wave – Rajasthan ke Kai Jilon me School Chhuttiyan &amp; Late Timings

### Kya hua?

- January mid 2026 me tez **cold wave** ki wajah se Rajasthan ke kai districts ne **school holidays aur timings change** kar diye.  
- **Jaipur** me government + private schools me **class 1–5 ke liye 12–13 January** tak chhutti declare ki gayi.  
- **Sikar** me class 1–5 ki chhutti **17 January 2026** tak extend ki gayi, aur class 6–12 ke liye timings roughly **11 baje se 4 baje tak** kar diye gaye.  
- **Jhunjhunu, Jaisalmer, Nagaur, Bharatpur, Dausa, Jalore** jaise jilon me bhi junior classes (kayi jagah class 8 tak) close rakhkar senior classes ko late-morning slot me shift kiya gaya, IMD ke cold-wave alerts ke base par.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Direct fact-based sawal – **kaun se districts, kaun se classes** ke liye cold wave chhuttiyan thi – pooche ja sakte hain.  
- Ye example **district-level disaster / seasonal risk management** aur bachchon ki suraksha ka recent case-study hai.  
- Mains me climate change, cold waves aur administration response wale answers me Rajasthan-specific example ke roop me use kar sakte ho.

**Memory Trick – “Thand zyada, school subah der se”**
