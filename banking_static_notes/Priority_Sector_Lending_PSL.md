# Priority Sector Lending (PSL) – Static Notes

Priority Sector Lending (PSL) India ke banking system ka **core policy tool** hai.  
Iska main aim hai ki bank ka credit sirf bade corporates tak limited na rahe, balki **agriculture, MSME, weaker sections, housing, etc.** tak bhi pahunch sake.

---

## 1. PSL – Definition (Exam Style)

- RBI ke guidelines ke hisaab se **kuch sectors ko “priority sector” declare kiya gaya hai**.  
- Scheduled Commercial Banks (aur SFBs etc.) ko **apne Adjusted Net Bank Credit (ANBC) / Credit Equivalent of Off‑Balance Sheet Exposure (CEOBE)** ka **minimum certain %** in priority sectors me dena compulsory hota hai.  
- Ye minimum % ko hi **PSL target** bolte hain.

> Simple line:  
> **“PSL = RBI ka rule jisme banks ko kuch important sectors (agriculture, MSME, etc.) ko minimum % credit dena zaroori hota hai.”**

(Exact % targets alag‑alag bank categories ke liye RBI circulars me hote hain – exam year ke hisaab se latest numbers zaroor verify karein.)

---

## 2. Objectives of PSL

1. **Inclusive Growth & Development:**
   - Agriculture, MSME, weaker sections, low‑cost housing jaisa core sectors ko support.  
2. **Balanced Regional Development:**
   - Backward / underbanked areas me credit flow badhana.  
3. **Financial Inclusion:**
   - Small borrowers, poor households ko formal credit system me lana.  
4. **Employment Generation:**
   - MSME, small businesses, services sector ko credit dekar jobs create karna.  
5. **Social Justice:**
   - Weaker sections ko subsidised / priority credit ensure karke inequality kam karna.

---

## 3. Major Categories Under PSL (Concept Level)

RBI PSL ko broadly kuch main categories me divide karta hai. High‑level list:

1. **Agriculture**  
2. **Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME)**  
3. **Export Credit**  
4. **Education**  
5. **Housing**  
6. **Social Infrastructure**  
7. **Renewable Energy**  
8. **Others** (jaise weaker sections ke liye kuch specific loans, etc.)

Har category ke exact sub‑limits, ticket size, eligible activities RBI circulars me given hote hain (time‑to‑time update hote rehte hain).

---

## 4. Agriculture – PSL ka Core Pillar

Agriculture PSL ko 3 broad heads me divide kiya jata hai:

1. **Farm Credit (Direct Agriculture):**
   - Crop loans (short‑term),  
   - Kisan Credit Card (KCC),  
   - Farm mechanisation (tractor, power tiller, pump sets),  
   - Allied activities (dairy, poultry, fisheries etc.).

2. **Agriculture Infrastructure:**
   - Storage, warehousing, cold storages, pack houses, grading, processing units, etc.  
   - Irrigation infrastructure, minor bridges, etc. (RBI list ke hisaab se).

3. **Ancillary Activities:**
   - Input dealers, fertiliser / seed dealers, custom hiring centres, etc.  
   - Agriculture produce marketing, agri‑product value chain me services.

Exam me mostly:

- “Farm credit PSL me count hota hai ya nahi?”  
- “Agriculture infrastructure PSL me aata hai ya nahi?” jaisi conceptual questions aate hain.

---

## 5. MSME – Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises

- **MSME** sector PSL ka important hissa hai:  
  - Manufacturing units, service units (small ticket size ke),  
  - Micro enterprises, small enterprises, medium enterprises (definition investment/turnover based, time ke hisaab se change hoti hai – exam year ke liye latest MSME definition check karein).

Concept points:

- **Micro enterprises** ke liye PSL me **sub‑target** hota hai (minimum % of ANBC).  
- Working capital (Cash Credit / Overdraft) + term loans dono PSL me count ho sakte hain (subject to rules).

---

## 6. Other Major PSL Categories – Short Overview

### 6.1 Export Credit

- Eligible exporters ko diya gaya credit (up to certain limit / borrower category) PSL me count ho sakta hai (RBI norms ke hisaab se).

### 6.2 Education

- Students ko **education loans** (domestic / overseas) up to certain ceiling amount PSL category me aate hain.

### 6.3 Housing

- Certain ceiling tak ke **housing loans to individuals** (self‑occupied / self‑construction) PSL me cover hote hain.  
- **Affordable housing** ke liye PSL ke through special support.

### 6.4 Social Infrastructure

- Schools, healthcare facilities, drinking water projects, sanitation etc. ke liye small / medium projects financing PSL me aata hai (limit RBI define karta hai).

### 6.5 Renewable Energy

- Solar, wind, small hydro, biomass, etc. jaisi renewable projects ke liye loans (up to certain project / household limits) PSL me included hain.

---

## 7. Weaker Sections – Special Focus

“**Weaker sections**” PSL ke andar ek important sub‑category hai.  
Isme generally (RBI definition ke hisaab se) aate hain:

- Small & marginal farmers,  
- Scheduled Castes (SC), Scheduled Tribes (ST),  
- Self Help Groups (SHGs),  
- Minority communities (as notified),  
- Persons with disabilities, etc.

Banks ko **PSL ke under weaker sections ke liye bhi separate sub‑target** meet karna hota hai (actual % target exam year ke liye RBI circular se check karein).

---

## 8. PSL Targets – Concept (Numbers Without Fixing)

Different bank categories ke liye PSL target structure alag hota hai:

- **Domestic Scheduled Commercial Banks** (public + private) –  
  - Overall PSL ka ek minimum % of ANBC target.  
  - Farming, small & marginal farmers, micro enterprises, weaker sections ke liye sub‑targets.

- **Foreign Banks** –  
  - Branch network size (≥20 branches, &lt;20 branches) ke basis par different PSL framework.

- **Small Finance Banks (SFBs)** –  
  - PSL target generally **higher** hota hai (kyunki inka focus unserved / underserved segments par hai).

Kyuki ye % numbers time‑to‑time revise ho sakte hain, exam ke liye:

- **Latest RBI Master Directions / circular** se PSL targets (overall, sub‑category wise) update karna zaroori hai.

---

## 9. PSL – Reporting, Shortfall & Priority Sector Lending Certificates (PSLCs)

### 9.1 Reporting & Monitoring

- Banks quarterly / annual basis par RBI ko PSL data report karte hain.  
- RBI check karta hai ki bank ne target meet kiya ya nahi.

### 9.2 Shortfall

- Agar bank PSL target meet nahi karta:
  - to usko kuch specified funds / schemes (jaise RIDF etc.) me deposit karna padta hai  
    (exact arrangement time ke hisaab se RBI guidelines ke through defined hota hai).

### 9.3 Priority Sector Lending Certificates (PSLCs)

- PSLC ek **marketable instrument** hai jahan ek bank apne PSL “achievement” ko dusre bank ko bech sakta hai:  
  - Seller bank: PSL target se zyada lending ki hai – PSLC sell kar sakta hai.  
  - Buyer bank: PSL target se kam hai – PSLC buy karke requirement meet kar sakta hai.  
- PSLC se:
  - PSL target meet karna flexible hota hai,  
  - aur actual credit flow market demand ke hisaab se better distribute hota hai.

---

## 10. Exam Special One‑Liners – PSL

1. PSL = **RBI ka mandated minimum % lending** certain priority sectors me (agriculture, MSME, etc.).  
2. PSL ka main motto – **inclusive growth, balanced development, weaker sections ka support**.  
3. Agriculture PSL – **Farm credit + Agri infrastructure + Ancillary activities**.  
4. MSME sector PSL me – micro, small, medium enterprises ko working capital + term loans.  
5. PSL me **weaker sections** ke liye alag sub‑target hota hai (SC/ST, small & marginal farmers, SHGs, etc.).  
6. Different categories of banks (domestic, foreign, SFBs) ke liye **alagay PSL targets** hote hain.  
7. PSL shortfall ke case me banks ko specified funds me **contribution karna pad sakta** hai.  
8. PSL Certificates (PSLCs) – banks ke beech PSL achievement ko trade karne ka instrument.

## 11. Memory Tricks – PSL

**Trick 1 – “A–M–E–H–S–R”**

Priority sectors yaad rakhne ke liye:

- **A** – Agriculture  
- **M** – MSME  
- **E** – Export  
- **H** – Housing  
- **S** – Social infrastructure  
- **R** – Renewable energy  

(Plus others / weaker sections as per RBI list.)

**Trick 2 – “PSL = Public Service Lending”**

PSL word yaad rakhne ke liye:

- **Priority Sector Lending ≈ Public Service Lending** –  
  yaad rahega ki ye **public / social objectives** ke liye lending hai, sirf profit ke liye nahi.

Aap is file ke saath exam year ke hisaab se:

- exact PSL % targets (overall + sub‑targets),  
- latest RBI Master Direction ka link,  
- aur important circular numbers  

ko apne personal notes me add kar sakte ho, taaki exam me bilkul updated raho.
