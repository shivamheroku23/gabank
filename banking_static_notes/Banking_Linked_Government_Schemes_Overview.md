# Banking-Linked Government Schemes – Overview

Ye file un **important Government schemes** ka short static overview deti hai  
jin ka **direct link banking / financial inclusion** se hai.  
(Amounts, latest changes etc. exam year me CA se update karna zaroori hai.)

---

## 1. PMJDY – Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana

- **Launch Objective:**  
  - हर household ke liye **basic bank account**,  
  - financial inclusion (banking, remittance, credit, insurance, pension).  
- **Main Features (concept level):**
  - Basic Savings Bank Deposit Account (BSBDA) – zero/minimum balance.  
  - RuPay debit card (accident insurance cover ke sath – exam year ke hisaab se amount).  
  - DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer) ke liye platform – govt subsidies, welfare payments.  
  - Overdraft facility (conditions ke sath).

> Exam line: PMJDY = India ka **flagship financial inclusion programme** – “Every household to have a bank account”.

---

## 2. PMJJBY – Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana

- **Type:** Life insurance scheme.  
- **Target Group:** 18–50 years ki age group ke savings bank account holders.  
- **Coverage:** Ek fixed sum assured on death (natural/accidental) – amount exam year ke hisaab se check karo.  
- **Premium:** Per year small premium, directly bank account se auto‑debit.  

> PMJJBY = “low‑cost life insurance through bank accounts”.

---

## 3. PMSBY – Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana

- **Type:** Accidental insurance scheme.  
- **Target Group:** 18–70 years ke savings bank account holders.  
- **Coverage:** Accidental death / disability par fixed insurance cover.  
- **Premium:** Very low annual premium, bank account se auto‑debit.

> PMSBY = “low‑cost accident insurance via bank account”.

---

## 4. APY – Atal Pension Yojana

- **Type:** Pension scheme for unorganised sector workers.  
- **Objective:**  
  - Old age me **guaranteed minimum pension** ensure karna (pre‑defined slabs).  
- **Target Group:** 18–40 years ke individuals with bank account.  
- **Features:**
  - Monthly contribution based on entry age & chosen pension slab.  
  - Government co‑contribution specific eligible category ke liye (policy/time ke hisaab se conditions).

> APY = “pension for unorganised sector through bank auto‑debit”.

---

## 5. Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY) – MUDRA Loans

- **MUDRA:** Micro Units Development and Refinance Agency Ltd.  
- **Objective:**  
  - Non‑corporate, non‑farm small/micro enterprises ko loans.  

- **Loan Categories (Concept Level):**
  - **Shishu** – smaller ticket loans (up to lower slab).  
  - **Kishore** – middle range loans.  
  - **Tarun** – higher range micro / small enterprise loans.  

(Exact upper limits exam year ke liye update karo.)

- **Bank Link:**
  - Banks, RRBs, MFIs, NBFCs etc. ke through MUDRA loans sanctioned;  
  - refinance & guarantee support MUDRA/NHB/NABARD etc. ke mechanism se.

---

## 6. Stand Up India Scheme

- **Objective:**  
  - SC / ST and women entrepreneurs ke liye greenfield enterprise finance.  
- **Loan Range:**  
  - Certain slab ke between term loan & working capital (exact range exam year ke hisaab se).  
- **Bank Link:**
  - Scheduled Commercial Banks ke branch level par at least 1 SC/ST & 1 woman entrepreneur ko support.

> Focus – **new enterprise creation among SC/ST & women** through bank loans.

---

## 7. PM SVANidhi – Street Vendor’s AtmaNirbhar Nidhi

- **Objective:**  
  - Urban street vendors ko working capital loan provide karna jisse wo business resume/expand kar sakein.  
- **Features (concept):**
  - Small working capital loan (initial slab + later enhancement),  
  - timely repayment par interest subsidy / incentives,  
  - digital transaction reward etc.  

Banks + NBFCs is scheme ke implementing institutions hain.

---

## 8. Other Schemes with Strong Banking Link (Concept Level)

- **Kisan Credit Card (KCC):**  
  - Farmers ke liye simplified credit limit for crop loans & allied activities;  
  - banks issue KCC, PSL agriculture ke target me count.  

- **Interest Subvention Schemes:**  
  - Short‑term crop loans par interest subvention;  
  - timely repayment par additional subvention – net effective interest kam.  

- **Housing Credit Linked Subsidy Schemes:**  
  - Affordable housing ke liye home loans par interest subsidy (PMAY etc. ke under).  

(Exact scheme names, rates, limits time‑to‑time change hote hain – exam ke liye current year ki official info se update karein.)

---

## 9. Exam Special One‑Liners – Banking Linked Schemes

1. PMJDY – financial inclusion ke liye **basic bank account + RuPay card + DBT platform**.  
2. PMJJBY – bank account holders ke liye **low‑cost life insurance** scheme.  
3. PMSBY – bank account holders ke liye **accidental insurance** scheme.  
4. APY – unorganised sector ke liye **pension scheme**, contributions bank account se auto‑debit.  
5. PMMY / MUDRA – micro / small business units ke liye loan, categories – **Shishu, Kishore, Tarun**.  
6. Stand‑Up India – SC/ST & women entrepreneurs ke liye **greenfield enterprise loans**.  
7. PM SVANidhi – urban street vendors ke liye **working capital loan + digital incentives**.  
8. KCC – farmers ke liye **flexible credit limit**; PSL agriculture ka core tool.

## 10. Memory Trick – “J–J–S–A–M–S–V–K”

- **J** – Jan Dhan (PMJDY) – bank account.  
- **J** – Jeevan Jyoti (PMJJBY) – life cover.  
- **S** – Suraksha (PMSBY) – accident cover.  
- **A** – Atal Pension (APY) – old age pension.  
- **M** – Mudra – micro business loans.  
- **S** – Stand‑Up India – SC/ST & women entrepreneurs.  
- **V** – SVANidhi – vendors (street vendors).  
- **K** – KCC – Kisan Credit.
