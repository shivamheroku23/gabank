# Punjab National Bank (PNB) – Static Notes

## 1. Basic Profile

- **Full Name:** Punjab National Bank (PNB)  
- **Type:** Large Public Sector Bank  
- **HQ:** New Delhi  
- **Establishment:** 1894 (Lahore – undivided India)  
- **Special Identity:**  
  - India ke **sabse purane Swadeshi banks** me se ek (Indian ownership ke saath establish).  
- **Official Website:** https://www.pnbindia.in  

(Tagline ko exam year ke hisaab se alag se note karo, kyunki marketing lines time ke sath change ho sakti hain.)

## 2. Historical Background – PNB

- **1894:**  
  - Lahore (ab Pakistan me) me **Punjabi / Indian promoters** ke dwara ek “swadeshi” bank ke roop me establish hua.  
  - Motive – Indian business & public ke liye Indian owned bank provide karna.  
- **Pre‑independence period:**  
  - North India me trade, business, individuals ke liye banking services provide karta tha.  
- **Post‑independence:**  
  - Lahore se operations shift aur India ke different regions me expansion.  
- **Bank Nationalisation (1969):**  
  - PNB un 14 major banks me se ek tha jise **1969 me nationalise** kiya gaya.  
  - Iske baad se PNB ek **Public Sector Bank** ban gaya.  
- **Recent Consolidation (Concept):**  
  - Recent years me PNB ke sath kuch PSBs ka merger hua (exam year ke hisaab se kaun kaun se banks, uska exact detail alag se yaad rakho).  

> Exam line: PNB – “First Swadeshi bank among large Indian banks, founded in Lahore in 1894.”

## 3. Role & Business Areas of PNB

- **Retail Banking:**  
  - Savings / current accounts, FDs, RDs, retail loans (home, personal, vehicle, education).  
- **Corporate & MSME Banking:**  
  - Working capital finance, term loans, trade finance.  
- **Agriculture & Rural Banking:**  
  - Kisan Credit Cards, crop loans, allied activities finance.  
- **Government Business:**  
  - Govt schemes implementation, pension, DBT, subsidy payments.  
- **Digital Banking:**  
  - Net banking, mobile banking, cards, digital products.

## 4. PNB – Identity & Special Points

- **Swadeshi Identity:**  
  - PNB ka establishment purely Indian management / ownership ke saath hua – isliye “Swadeshi bank” ki image.  
- **Branch Network:**  
  - Specially North India region me strong presence; all‑India network bhi large.  
- **Mergers / Consolidation:**  
  - Recent PSB consolidation phase me PNB ke size aur branch network me further expansion (anchor bank ke roop me).
