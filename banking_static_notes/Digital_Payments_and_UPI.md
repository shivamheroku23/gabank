# Digital Payments and UPI – Static Notes

Ye file India ke **digital payments ecosystem** (UPI, BHIM, QR, BBPS, AePS, RuPay, wallets, etc.) ka exam‑oriented overview deti hai.

---

## 1. Digital Payments – Overview

Digital payments ka matlab:

- Cash ke bina **electronic mode** se payment karna:  
  - internet banking, mobile banking, cards, UPI, wallets, etc.  
- India me digital payments revolution ka backbone:
  - **RBI** (regulator),  
  - **NPCI** (retail payment systems operator),  
  - Banks + Fintechs.

---

## 2. NPCI – National Payments Corporation of India

- **Type:** Not‑for‑profit company (Section 8)  
- **Role:** Umbrella organisation for retail payment systems in India.  
- **Promoted by:** Indian banks under guidance of RBI & IBA.  
- **Major systems / products:**
  - IMPS, UPI, BHIM, RuPay, NACH, AEPS, BBPS, etc.

NPCI digital payments ke liye backbone rails provide karta hai; regulation RBI ke under.

---

## 3. UPI – Unified Payments Interface

### 3.1 Basic Concept

- **Unified Payments Interface (UPI)** ek real‑time payment system hai jo:

  - multiple bank accounts ko ek hi mobile app se link karne deta hai,  
  - **VPA (Virtual Payment Address)**, mobile number, Aadhaar, account + IFSC etc. se payments possible banata hai.  

- Operator: **NPCI**, regulator: **RBI**.

### 3.2 Key Features

- **24×7, 365 days** instant payments.  
- Person‑to‑Person (P2P) and Person‑to‑Merchant (P2M) transactions.  
- Pay / collect feature (payment request).  
- Uses **IMPS infrastructure** as back‑end.  
- VPA handle se banks ka account number share kiye bina payment possible.  

### 3.3 UPI Apps

- BHIM app by NPCI,  
- Bank apps (SBI, HDFC, etc.),  
- Third‑party apps (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, etc. – as per regulatory approval).

> Exam line: UPI = **NPCI ka real‑time mobile based funds transfer system**.

---

## 4. BHIM – Bharat Interface for Money

- BHIM – **UPI based** mobile app launched to promote digital payments.  
- Developed by NPCI, promoted by Govt of India.  
- Simple interface for UPI transactions (send/receive money, scan & pay).

---

## 5. QR Codes, Bharat QR, UPI QR

- **QR (Quick Response) Code:**  
  - Machine readable code (square barcode type) jisme merchant / payment info store hota hai.  
- **UPI QR / Bharat QR:**  
  - Customer simply scan karta hai, amount enter karta hai, UPI PIN se pay karta hai.  
- Merchant ke liye:
  - kharcha kam (no POS hardware),  
  - QR sticker se digital payment accept.

---

## 6. RuPay – Domestic Card Scheme

- **RuPay** – India ka domestic card payment network, operated by NPCI.  
- Types:
  - RuPay debit cards, credit cards, prepaid cards.  
- Objective:
  - Low cost card payments,  
  - financial inclusion (PMJDY accounts ke cards mostly RuPay).  

> Exam question aata hai: “RuPay card network ko kaun operate karta hai?” – **NPCI**.

---

## 7. AEPS – Aadhaar Enabled Payment System

- **AEPS:** Aadhaar Enabled Payment System – NPCI product.  
- Aadhaar number + biometric (fingerprint/iris) ke basis par bank account se transactions (credit, debit, balance enquiry, etc.).  
- Use:
  - Rural areas me **Business Correspondent (BC) / CSP** ke through cash in–out.  
- Financial inclusion ke liye important tool.

---

## 8. BBPS – Bharat Bill Payment System

- **Bharat Bill Payment System (BBPS):** NPCI ka integrated bill payment system.  
- Features:
  - Different billers (electricity, gas, DTH, water, insurance premium, etc.) ke bills ek platform se pay kar sakte ho.  
  - Interoperable; online + physical outlets dono.  

> Exam line: BBPS – **interoperable bill payment system**, NPCI operator.

---

## 9. Prepaid Payment Instruments (PPIs) – Wallets etc.

- **PPIs:**  
  - Instruments jahan pehle se value load karke future payments karte ho.  
- Types (concept level):
  - Closed system wallets (only issuing entity ke liye – e.g., some merchant wallets).  
  - Semi‑closed wallets (multiple merchants within network).  
  - Prepaid cards etc. (regulatory classification time ke hisaab se check karo).  
- Regulator: RBI (PSS Act ke under).

---

## 10. Digital Banking Channels – Overview

- **Internet Banking** – browser based.  
- **Mobile Banking** – app based.  
- **USSD Banking (*99#)** – feature phones ke liye simple menu based banking (UPI over USSD).  
- **Cards** – debit, credit, prepaid (PoS, e‑commerce, contactless).  

Digital channels banking ko 24×7, location‑independent bana dete hain.

---

## 11. Security & Customer Protection – Concept Points

- 2‑factor authentication (something you know – PIN/password; something you have – device/SIM; something you are – biometric).  
- Transaction alerts (SMS / email / app notifications).  
- Limits on per transaction / per day usage.  
- RBI guidelines on customer liability in unauthorised electronic transactions.

---

## 12. Exam Special One‑Liners – Digital & UPI

1. UPI – **NPCI ka real‑time mobile based payment system**, regulator – RBI.  
2. UPI me **VPA (Virtual Payment Address)** se pay/receive possible, account number share nahi karna padta.  
3. BHIM – UPI based app, developed by **NPCI**, promoted by Govt of India.  
4. RuPay – **domestic card payment network**; operator – NPCI.  
5. AEPS – **Aadhaar Enabled Payment System**, BC outlets se Aadhaar + biometric ke through transactions.  
6. BBPS – **Bharat Bill Payment System**, interoperable bill payment platform.  
7. NPCI – IMPS, UPI, RuPay, NACH, AEPS, BBPS jaisi **retail payment systems** operate karta hai.  
8. RBI – payment systems ka **regulator** (PSS Act, 2007 ke under).
