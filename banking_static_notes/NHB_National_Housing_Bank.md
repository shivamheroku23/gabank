# National Housing Bank (NHB) – Static Notes

## 1. Basic Profile

- **Full Name:** National Housing Bank (NHB)  
- **Type:** Apex Institution for Housing Finance  
- **HQ:** New Delhi  
- **Establishment:** 9 July 1988 (NHB Act, 1987)  
- **Main Focus:**  
  - India me **housing finance system** ka development & regulation.  
- **Ownership:**  
  - Initially RBI promoted institution; later ownership pattern me change aaya –  
    - exam year ke hisaab se latest shareholding Government / RBI / others ka verify zaroor karein.  
- **Official Website:** https://nhb.org.in  

---

## 2. Background & Establishment Timeline

- 1980s me housing sector ke liye dedicated apex financial institution ki need feel hui.  
- Housing finance companies (HFCs) aur housing sector ko **organised, long‑term funding** ki zarurat thi.  
- Is context me **National Housing Bank Act, 1987** pass hua.  
- **9 July 1988** ko NHB ne operations start kiye as an apex institution for housing finance.  
- Role:
  - HFCs ko refinance,  
  - housing finance policy support,  
  - housing sector ke liye new products / instruments develop karna.

---

## 3. Objectives of NHB

- **Housing finance system ka development and promotion**:  
  - Housing finance institutions (HFCs, banks) ko encourage karna.  
- **Housing finance institutions ke operations ka regulation and supervision:**  
  - Sound and stable housing finance system ensure karna.  
- **Affordable housing ko promote karna**, especially lower & middle income groups ke liye.  
- Housing sector me **resource mobilisation** (refinance, bonds, etc.) ke through long‑term funds available karwana.

---

## 4. Major Functions of NHB (Concept Level)

### 4.1 Refinance to Housing Finance Institutions

- NHB banks & HFCs ko refinance provide karta hai:  
  - taaki wo individuals ko home loans de sakein,  
  - housing projects / bulk loans finance kar sakein.

### 4.2 Regulation & Supervision of HFCs

- Housing finance companies ke regulation & supervision ka role (policy framework time ke hisaab se RBI / NHB ke beech responsibilities me changes hue – current exam year ke liye updated framework check karein).  
- Objective:  
  - Housing finance institutions ki financial soundness,  
  - fair practices,  
  - customer protection.

### 4.3 Housing Sector Development & Policy Support

- Affordable housing, slum upgradation, rural & semi‑urban housing me policy and schemes ke through support.  
- Government ko housing policy, interest subsidy schemes, etc. ke design me inputs.  
- Research, data collection, market studies on housing and housing finance.

### 4.4 Promotion of New Instruments

- Housing finance ke liye capital market instruments, securitisation, mortgage backed securities, etc. ka development (conceptual role).  
- Banks & HFCs ke liye guidelines aur best practices.

## 5. NHB ke Important Role – Exam Point of View

(Note: Specific schemes jese interest subsidy, credit linked subsidy schemes, etc. time ke hisaab se change hote hain, isliye unko separately update karo.)
