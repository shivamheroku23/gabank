# EXIM Bank – Export-Import Bank of India

## 1. Basic Profile

- **Full Name:** Export-Import Bank of India (EXIM Bank)  
- **Type:** Apex Export Finance Institution / Export Credit Agency  
- **HQ:** Mumbai, Maharashtra  
- **Establishment:** 1 January 1982 (Export-Import Bank of India Act, 1981)  
- **Main Focus:**  
  - India ke exports aur international trade ko promote karne ke liye finance & support.  
- **Ownership:**  
  - Government of India ka specialised financial institution (shareholding details exam year ke hisaab se check karein).  
- **Official Website:** https://www.eximbankindia.in  

---

## 2. Background & Establishment Timeline

- Rapid export growth ke liye **specialised export finance institution** ki need feel hui.  
- Is purpose se **Export-Import Bank of India Act, 1981** pass hua.  
- **1 January 1982** se EXIM Bank ne operations start kiye.  
- EXIM Bank ko design kiya gaya as:  
  - export finance ka apex institution,  
  - jahan se exporters ko medium/long-term credit mil sake,  
  - traders & projects ke liye external finance arrange ho sake,  
  - aur overseas investment / lines of credit ke through trade relations strong ho sake.

---

## 3. Objectives of EXIM Bank

- Indian exports ko **promote, finance, facilitate and coordinate** karna.  
- Exporters ko **short, medium, long-term finance** provide karna.  
- Overseas buyers, projects, institutions ko finance / lines of credit ke through support.  
- Indian companies ke **overseas investment** (joint ventures, subsidiaries, etc.) ko support karna.  
- Export finance ecosystem (banks, institutions, Govt agencies) ke beech coordination.  

---

## 4. Major Functions of EXIM Bank (Concept Level)

### 4.1 Financial Assistance to Exporters

- Exporters ke liye:
  - pre‑shipment finance, post‑shipment finance (medium/long term nature),  
  - supplier’s credit, buyer’s credit arrangements, etc.  
- Capital goods, projects, consultancy exports ke liye specialised credit products.

### 4.2 Lines of Credit (LoC)

- Foreign governments / institutions / development banks ko **lines of credit** provide karna,  
  - jinke through wo Indian goods & services import kar sakein.  
- Isse India ka trade aur diplomatic relations dono strengthen hote hain.

### 4.3 Overseas Investment Finance

- Indian companies ko foreign countries me:
  - joint ventures,
  - wholly owned subsidiaries,  
ke liye finance/support (policy norms ke under).

### 4.4 Guarantees & Other Support

- Export credit guarantees, performance guarantees, bid bonds, etc. ke ecosystem me role (coordination with other institutions).  
- Export promotion ke liye advisory services, market studies, sectoral reports, etc.

### 4.5 Research, Advisory & Promotion

- Foreign markets, sectors, products par research studies.  
- Exporters ke liye advisory services – market entry strategy, risk analysis, etc.  
- Government ko export promotion policies me inputs.

## 5. EXIM Bank vs Normal Commercial Bank – Simple Difference

- **Commercial bank:**
  - Sabhi sectors ko general banking services, domestic + foreign both.  
- **EXIM Bank:**
  - Specially **international trade / exports / overseas investment** ke liye focused institution.  
  - Product design, risk assessment, funding sources – sab export oriented.
