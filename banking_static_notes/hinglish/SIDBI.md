# SIDBI – Small Industries Development Bank of India

## 1. Basic Profile

- **Full Name:** Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI)  
- **Type:** Apex Development Financial Institution for MSMEs / Small Industries  
- **HQ:** Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh  
- **Establishment:** 2 April 1990 (SIDBI Act, 1989)  
- **Main Focus:**  
  - MSMEs (Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises), small industries, service sector units, start‑ups ke liye finance & development support.  
- **Ownership:**  
  - Development financial institution under Government of India ecosystem  
  - (Exact shareholding – exam year ke hisaab se latest data check karein.)  
- **Official Website:** https://www.sidbi.in

## 2. Background & Establishment Timeline

- Earlier small industries finance ka kaam largely **IDBI** ke through hota tha.  
- MSME / small industries segment ko dedicated focus dene ke liye alag apex financial institution ki need feel hui.  
- Isliye **SIDBI Act, 1989** pass hua.  
- **2 April 1990** ko SIDBI ne operations start kiye.  
- Time ke sath SIDBI ne MSME ke liye refinancing, direct assistance, venture capital, credit guarantee support, etc. me role expand kiya.

> Exam line: SIDBI = MSME sector ka apex financial institution.

## 3. Objectives of SIDBI

- Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises ke liye **adequate and timely credit** ensure karna.  
- MSMEs ke liye friendly financial products develop karna (term loans, working capital support, etc.).  
- MSME sector ki competitiveness, technology upgradation, modernisation support karna.  
- Employment generation, entrepreneurship development, export promotion me support.  
- MSMEs ka ecosystem improve karna – infrastructure, cluster development, innovation, start‑ups, etc.

---

## 4. Major Functions of SIDBI

### 4.1 Refinance to Banks / FIs

- SIDBI **commercial banks, small finance banks, NBFCs, micro‑finance institutions** ko refinance provide karta hai,  
  - taaki ye institutions MSMEs ko loans de sakein.  

### 4.2 Direct Finance to MSMEs

- Term loans, project finance, working capital support directly MSME units ko (policy ke hisaab se).  
- Green / energy efficient projects, technology upgradation, infrastructure projects, etc. me direct lending.

### 4.3 Promotional & Development Activities

- Cluster development programmes (industrial clusters / MSME clusters ke liye).  
- Entrepreneurship development programmes, skill development.  
- Start‑up ecosystem support (equity / quasi‑equity support through funds etc., policy initiatives).  

### 4.4 Risk Capital / Venture Support (Concept)

- MSME / start‑up ventures me risk capital support ke liye different funds / schemes ke through participation.  
- Innovation, technology‑based enterprises ko encourage karna.

### 4.5 Policy Advocacy & Coordination

- MSME sector ki issues, constraints, policy suggestions – Government & regulators ko recommend karta hai.  
- Different institutions (banks, industry associations, govt departments) ke beech coordination.
