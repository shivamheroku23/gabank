# Important Banking Terms and Ratios – Quick Glossary

Ye file **important banking terms / ratios / acronyms** ko 1–3 line me explain karti hai.  
Revision ke time aap isko ek **mini‑dictionary** ki tarah use kar sakte ho.

---

## 1. Monetary Policy & Liquidity Terms

### 1.1 CRR – Cash Reserve Ratio

- Bankko apne **NDTL (Net Demand and Time Liabilities)** ka certain % **cash** form me RBI ke paas rakhna padta hai.  
- Is % ko **CRR** bolte hain.  
- RBI CRR ko badha/ghata kar banking system me liquidity control karta hai.

### 1.2 SLR – Statutory Liquidity Ratio

- Bankko apne NDTL ka certain % **approved securities + cash + gold** me rakhna hota hai.  
- Isko **SLR** kehte hain.  
- Liquidity & solvency ensure karne ke liye.

### 1.3 Repo Rate

- **Repo (Repurchase) Rate** – wo interest rate jis par banks **short‑term funds RBI se borrow** karte hain, government securities ko collateral rakh kar.  
- RBI repo rate badha/ghata kar economy me interest rate trend influence karta hai.

### 1.4 Reverse Repo Rate

- Wo rate jis par banks **excess funds RBI ke paas park** kar sakte hain (short term).  
- RBI ke liye liquidity absorb karne ka tool.

### 1.5 Bank Rate

- Long‑term lending rate jisse RBI certain type ke long‑term funds banks ko deta hai (historically important).  
- Monetary policy transmission ka ek indicator rate.

### 1.6 MSF – Marginal Standing Facility

- Emergency / overnight funding window – banks **RBI se higher rate par funds** le sakte hain (SLS/CRR deficiency situations etc. ke liye, conditions ke sath).

## 2. Asset Quality & NPAs

### 2.1 NPA – Non‑Performing Asset

- Loan / advance jisme borrower **interest or principal 90 days se zyada overdue** ho jaye (general norm).  
- NPA categories – substandard, doubtful, loss (RBI norms ke hisaab se).

### 2.2 Gross NPA

- Bank ke total NPAs ka amount (bilkul aggregate) – **provisions minus kiye bina**.

### 2.3 Net NPA

- **Gross NPA – provisions** ke baad ka amount.  
- Net NPA indicator bank ki residual asset quality dikhata hai.

### 2.4 Provisioning

- NPA aur doubtful assets ke against bank apne profit se **reserve (provision)** banata hai,  
  taaki future loss ke impact ko absorb kiya ja sake.

---

## 3. Profitability & Efficiency Ratios

### 3.1 NIM – Net Interest Margin

- **Net Interest Income (NII) / interest earning assets**.  
- NII = Interest earned on loans & investments – Interest paid on deposits & borrowings.  
- High NIM → better interest spread.

### 3.2 ROA – Return on Assets

- **Net profit / total assets**.  
- Bank ki assets par profitability dikhata hai.

### 3.3 ROE – Return on Equity

- **Net profit / shareholders’ equity**.  
- Shareholders ke liye return show karta hai.

### 3.4 Cost‑to‑Income Ratio

- **Operating expenses / operating income**.  
- Lower cost‑to‑income ratio → better efficiency.

---

## 4. Liquidity & Structural Ratios

### 4.1 CD Ratio – Credit–Deposit Ratio

- **Total advances / total deposits**.  
- Batata hai deposit ka kitna portion loans me deploy hua.  
- Too high → liquidity risk; too low → under‑utilisation of resources.

### 4.2 CASA Ratio

- **Current Account + Savings Account deposits** ka **total deposits** me % share.  
- Higher CASA ratio → cheap cost of funds (kyunki CA & SA interest cost kam / zero).

### 4.3 Capital Adequacy Ratio (CAR / CRAR)

- **Regulatory capital / risk‑weighted assets**.  
- Jitna high CAR, utna zyada loss absorption capacity.  
- Basel norms ke basis par regulator minimum CAR prescribe karta hai.

---

## 5. Account & KYC Related Terms

### 5.1 KYC – Know Your Customer

- Bank ko customer ki **identity, address, beneficial ownership** verify karna mandatory hai.  
- KYC norms = identity proof, address proof, photograph etc.

### 5.2 AML – Anti‑Money Laundering

- Laws & procedures jisse illegal money ko banking system me legit dikhane se roka jata hai.  
- Banks suspicious transactions report karte hain (STR, CTR) etc.

### 5.3 CFT – Combating Financing of Terrorism

- Terrorism financing ko detect & prevent karne ke liye compliance framework (often AML ke sath mention).  

### 5.4 FATF – Financial Action Task Force

- International body jo AML/CFT standards banata hai.  
- India FATF member hai.

---

## 6. Payment System Terms

### 6.1 RTGS – Real Time Gross Settlement

- Large value, real‑time, gross settlement system; RBI operate karta hai.

### 6.2 NEFT – National Electronic Funds Transfer

- Deferred net settlement based retail funds transfer system.

### 6.3 IMPS – Immediate Payment Service

- 24×7 instant funds transfer, operated by NPCI.

### 6.4 UPI – Unified Payments Interface

- NPCI ka real‑time mobile based payment system, VPA ke through payments.

### 6.5 NACH – National Automated Clearing House

- NPCI ka bulk, repetitive transactions ke liye electronic clearing system.

---

## 7. Balance Sheet Terms (Basic)

### 7.1 Assets (Bank ke context me)

- Loans & advances, investments, balances with RBI & other banks, fixed assets, etc.

### 7.2 Liabilities

- Deposits (savings, current, term), borrowings, other liabilities, equity, reserves.

### 7.3 Off‑Balance Sheet Items

- Guarantees, letters of credit (LC), derivatives contracts etc.  
- Inka **credit equivalent** risk exposure calculate karke capital adequacy me include kiya jata hai.

---

## 8. Other Important Acronyms

- **SLR:** Statutory Liquidity Ratio – NDTL ka % in liquid assets.  
- **CRR:** Cash Reserve Ratio – NDTL ka % as cash with RBI.  
- **NDTL:** Net Demand and Time Liabilities – deposits + certain borrowings (net of some items).  
- **ALM:** Asset–Liability Management – bank ki assets & liabilities ka maturity / interest rate risk manage karna.  
- **PSL:** Priority Sector Lending – RBI specified sectors me mandatory credit.  
- **PCA:** Prompt Corrective Action – weak banks ke liye RBI ka supervisory framework.
