## TOPIC 1: International Year of Rangelands and Pastoralists (IYRP) 2026

### Kya hua?

- **UN General Assembly** ne resolution **A/RES/76/253** adopt karke **2026 ko “International Year of Rangelands and Pastoralists (IYRP)”** declare kiya.  
- Resolution **15 March 2022** ko 76th UNGA session me **consensus se** pass hua, lead **Mongolia** ne liya, dozens of co-sponsor countries ke saath.  
- Implementation ke coordination ke liye **FAO (Food and Agriculture Organization)** ko nodal UN agency banaya gaya, jise Member States ke saath milkar awareness activities karni hongi.  
- Focus: **grazing lands, pastoral communities, livestock-based livelihoods, biodiversity, climate change, food security**.

### SSC exam points:

- International Year 2026 → **Rangelands and Pastoralists**.  
- Proclaiming body → **UN General Assembly**; resolution no. **A/RES/76/253**.  
- Lead UN agency → **FAO**.  
- Reason: rangelands &amp; pastoralism ka role **biodiversity, climate, food security, rural livelihoods** me highlight karna.

**Memory Trick – “IYRP = Rangelands &amp; Pastoralists”**

> IY**R**P me **R + P** = **Rangelands &amp; Pastoralists** – 2026 ka code.

## TOPIC 2: DRDO ka 12-Minute Scramjet Test – Hypersonic Technology

### Kya hua?

- **DRDO ke DRDL lab (Hyderabad)** ne **9 January 2026** ko **full-scale actively cooled scramjet combustor/engine** ka long-duration ground test kiya.  
- Test **Scramjet Connect Pipe Test (SCPT) Facility** me hua jahan **scramjet engine 12+ minute** tak continuous chala – hypersonic propulsion ke liye record-level runtime.  
- Ye system **future hypersonic cruise missiles** ke liye design ho raha hai jo **Mach 5 se zyada speed** par air-breathing propulsion use karega.  
- Defence Minister aur DRDO chairman ne isko India ko **few hypersonic-capable nations** ki list me le jane wala step bataya.

### SSC exam points:

- Lab/location → **DRDL, Hyderabad (DRDO)**.  
- Propulsion type → **scramjet (supersonic combustion ramjet)**, speed **Mach &gt; 5**.  
- Runtime → **12+ minutes** ground test.  
- Purpose → **hypersonic cruise missile programme**.

**Memory Trick – “DRDL 12 min = hypersonic win”**

> Code: **DRDL + 12 min + hypersonic** – teen keyword ek line me.

## TOPIC 3: India ka pehla Indigenous Commercial Quantum Computer – IIIT Dharwad

### Kya hua?

- Karnataka ke **IT/BT minister** ne announce kiya ki **India ka pehla indigenously built commercial quantum computer**  
  Bengaluru-based deep-tech startup **QpiAI** ke saath partnership me **IIIT Dharwad** campus par deploy kiya jayega.  
- Yahan **Centre of Excellence in Quantum AI and Computing** banaya ja raha hai jahan pehle phase me **25-qubit system**,  
  next 2–3 saal me **~1,000 qubits** tak scale up ka roadmap hai (media reports ke mutabik).  
- Ye machine **quantum computing + AI** applications ke liye use hogi – cryptography, optimisation, drug discovery, logistics planning, etc.  
- Ye project India ke **quantum mission** aur Karnataka ke deep-tech roadmap ke under aa raha hai.

### SSC exam points:

- “India’s first indigenous commercial quantum computer” → **IIIT Dharwad, Karnataka**, startup partner **QpiAI**.  
- Qubits → first phase around **25 qubits**, target **1,000 qubits** few years me.  
- Use-cases → cryptography, optimisation, AI/ML, scientific computing.

**Memory Trick – “Dharwad = Desh ka quantum start”**

> Yaad rakho: **“Quantum commercial start – IIIT Dharwad ke saath.”**

- January 2026 ke starting me **Chief Justice of India Surya Kant** ne is paperless court ka inauguration virtually kiya.  
- Yahan case filing, scrutiny, evidence, orders, judgment – sab kuch **digital mode** me hota hai, bina physical files ke.  
- Initiative ko **“green jurisprudence”** aur judiciary me digital transformation ka model project maana ja raha hai.

### SSC exam points:

- India ka pehla fully paperless district court → **Kalpetta (Wayanad), Kerala**.  
- Inaugurated by → **Chief Justice of India (Surya Kant)**.  
- Purpose → **paperless, faster, eco-friendly judicial process**.
