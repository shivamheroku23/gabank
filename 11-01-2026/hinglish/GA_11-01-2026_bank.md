## TOPIC 1: UN ka International Year of Rangelands and Pastoralists, 2026

### Kya hua?

- **UN General Assembly** ne resolution **A/RES/76/253** ke through **2026 ko “International Year of Rangelands and Pastoralists (IYRP)”** declare kiya.  
- Resolution **15 March 2022** ko 76th UNGA session me **consensus se adopt** hua tha, lead role **Mongolia** ka tha, 60+ co-sponsoring countries ke saath.  
- Implementation ke liye **FAO (Food and Agriculture Organization)** ko nodal UN body banaya gaya hai – Member States ko rangelands, pastoralism, climate, food security par awareness events karne ko encourage kiya gaya.  
- Focus: **grazing lands, pastoral communities, livestock-based livelihoods**, climate adaptation, biodiversity aur poverty reduction.  
  (Official source: UN Digital Library – A/RES/76/253, https://digitallibrary.un.org/record/3965711)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- India me **pastoral &amp; livestock economy** kaafi bada hai – yahan se **priority sector lending (agriculture, animal husbandry)** ke angles niklenge.  
- Rangelands aur pastoral families ke liye **KCC loans, dairy finance, SHG/JLG lending, FPOs, NBFC-MFIs** sab relevant ban jate hain.  
- Climate-resilient agriculture &amp; livestock ko support karne ke liye **green &amp; sustainable finance products** ka demand badhega.  
- Interview/mains: “How can banks support pastoralist and rangeland communities in the context of IYRP 2026?”

### Memory Trick – “RP = Rangelands &amp; Pastoralists”

> 2026 ko yaad rakho: **“International Year – Rangelands &amp; Pastoralists (IYRP)”**.  
> IY**R**P → **R**angelands &amp; **P**astoralists.

## TOPIC 2: DRDO ka 12-Minute Scramjet Combustor Test – Hypersonic Mission Boost

### Kya hua?

- **DRDO ke Defence Research and Development Laboratory (DRDL), Hyderabad** ne **9 January 2026** ko  
  **full-scale actively cooled scramjet combustor/engine** ka long-duration **ground test** kiya.  
- Test **Scramjet Connect Pipe Test (SCPT) Facility** me hua jahan scramjet engine **12+ minutes tak continuous run** hua – hypersonic propulsion ke liye bahut bada record.  
- Ye test **hypersonic cruise missile programme** ke liye path-breaking milestone maana ja raha hai – pehle sub-scale aur short-duration tests ho chuke the; ab full-scale active cooling validate hua.  
- Defence Minister aur DRDO Chairman ne is milestone ko India ko **few advanced hypersonic nations** ke club me le jane wala step bataya.  
  (Official source: PIB – https://pib.gov.in/PressReleasePage.aspx?PRID=2213017)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Defence manufacturing &amp; hypersonic tech me progress → **indigenous defence industry** ko funding, project finance, working capital ki zarurat.  
- Banks ke liye **defence corridor projects, PSU defence companies, strategic MSME suppliers** me lending opportunities &amp; risk understanding important ho jata hai.  
- External sector/FX angle: indigenous tech se **defence import bill kam** ho sakta hai, jo macro-stability ke liye positive hai.  
- Science &amp; Tech based GA questions me “scramjet = supersonic combustion ramjet, Mach &gt; 5” jaise points pooche ja sakte hain.

### Memory Trick – “12 minutes Scramjet, DRDL Hyderabad”

> Line: **“DRDL Hyderabad – scramjet 12 minutes, hypersonic credit.”**  
> DRDL + 12 min + hypersonic = teen keywords.

## TOPIC 3: Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V) Communication – India ka Road Safety Push

### Kya hua?

- Transport Ministry ki briefings ke hisaab se India **2026 ke end tak nationwide Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V) communication system** roll out karne ki planning kar raha hai.  
- V2V me gaadiyon me ek **dedicated wireless module** hota hai jo **internet/mobilenetwork ke bina** nearby vehicles ke saath real-time data share karta hai – speed, direction, sudden braking, blind-spot position, fog conditions, etc.  
- System ka aim: **rear-end collisions, fog pile-ups, sudden braking crashes** jaisi situations ko kam karna – especially highways par.  
- Estimated project cost media reports ke mutabik approx **₹5,000 crore** hai; pehle phase me **nayi vehicles** ke liye mandate aayega, baad me retro-fitment discuss ho sakta hai.  
  (Details multiple CA sources – Deccan Herald, NDTV, GKToday; exact implementation notified rules aane par clear honge.)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Auto sector + ITS (Intelligent Transport Systems) me **capex finance**, **leasing**, **vendor finance**, **insurtech tie-ups** ka scope badhega.  
- V2V data se **usage-based motor insurance, telematics loans** jaise products design ho sakte hain – retail banking &amp; NBFCs ke liye nayi product line.  
- Road-safety improvements indirectly **NPAs in commercial vehicle loans** ko kam karne me help kar sakte hain (less accidents, downtime).  

### Memory Trick – “V2V by 2026, 5k crore safety fix”

> Yaad rakho: **“2026 tak V2V nationwide, ~₹5,000 crore ka road safety design.”**

## TOPIC 4: RBI ne 35 NBFCs ka CoR Cancel kiya – Regulatory Clean-Up

### Kya hua?

- **Reserve Bank of India** ne December 2025 me orders pass karke **35 NBFCs ka Certificate of Registration (CoR)** cancel kar diya;  
  ye information **January 2026** me consolidated form me public domain me highlight hui.  
- Cancellation **Section 45-IA(6), RBI Act 1934** ke under ki gayi – reason broadly **regulatory non-compliance** (prudential norms, reporting, governance etc.).  
- Saath hi **16 NBFCs ne apna CoR voluntarily surrender** kiya, jise RBI ne accept karke unka NBFC status bhi khatam kiya – reasons: business exit, restructuring, CIC criteria meet karna, etc.  
- CoR cancel hone ke baad ye entities **NBFC business officially nahi chala sakti**; unko lending etc. ke liye dusre regulatory route follow karna padega.  
  (Official detail: RBI Press Releases – NBFC CoR cancellations, December 2025, https://rbi.org.in &gt; Press Releases)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Direct MCQ points:  
  - **35 NBFCs – CoR cancelled for non-compliance**,  
  - **16 NBFCs – CoR cancelled on voluntary surrender**,  
  - Relevant section: **45-IA(6), RBI Act**.  
- Ye step **shadow banking risk** ko control karne, regulatory arbitrage ko kam karne aur depositor/borrower protection ke liye hai.  
- Mains/interview: “Why is strong NBFC regulation critical for India’s credit system and financial stability?”

### Memory Trick – “35 cancel, 16 surrender – 45-IA ka danda”

> Code: **“35 cancel, 16 surrender; Section 45-IA ka regulator thunder.”**

## TOPIC 5: India – Pehla Desh jahan Bio-Bitumen Commercial Scale par

### Kya hua?

- CSIR labs **CRRI (Central Road Research Institute)** aur **IIP (Indian Institute of Petroleum)** ne milkar  
  **agri-waste (khas kar crop residue/stubble) se bio-bitumen banane ki pyrolysis technology** develop ki.  
- **7 January 2026** ko New Delhi me ek technology-transfer event me Transport Minister ne announce kiya ki  
  **India duniya ka pehla desh ban gaya jahan bio-bitumen technology ko industrial/commercial scale par le jaya gaya**.  
- Lab tests ke baad bio-bitumen ko **15–30% blending** ke saath conventional bitumen me mix karke use kiya ja sakta hai;  
  ek trial stretch **Jorabat–Shillong Expressway (NH-40)** par bichhaya bhi ja chuka hai.  
- Estimate: sirf **15% blending** se hi **₹4,500 crore** tak FX saving possible hai; long-run me imported bitumen ka **₹25,000–30,000 crore** ka bill replace ho sakta hai.  
  (Official source: PIB – Bio-bitumen via pyrolysis technology transfer, https://pib.gov.in/PressReleseDetail.aspx?PRID=2212118)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Road infrastructure me **green materials** ka use badhne se **project appraisal, ESG-linked lending, green bonds** ka scope strong hota hai.  
- Bio-bitumen supply chain me **refiners, pyrolysis plants, logistics, highway contractors** sabko funding chahi hogi – banks/NBFCs ke liye naya lending segment.  
- Crop-residue ka productive use stubble burning ko kam karke **climate &amp; air pollution risk** manage karne me help karega – sustainable finance narrative ka hissa.  

### Memory Trick – “Farm se Road – Bio-Bitumen Mode”

> Line: **“Farm residue se road tak – India ka bio-bitumen world-first tag.”**
