## TOPIC 2: Jaipur Monuments ki Entry Fees Badhi – Foreign Tourists ke liye Naye Rates

- Department ke mutabik ye hike lagbhag **10 saal baad** ki gayi hai taaki monuments ke maintenance, security aur visitor facilities ko fund mil sake.

- Economic angle: higher ticket se **conservation + tourism infra** dono ko paisa milta hai.

## TOPIC 3: Rajasthan Factory (Amendment) Bill 2025 – Mahilaon ke Night Shift &amp; 60-Hour Week

### Kya hua?

- Rajasthan Vidhan Sabha ne **Factory (Rajasthan Amendment) Bill 2025** pass kiya jisme  
  **mahilaon ko factories me raat me bhi kaam karne ki anumati** di gayi (safety conditions ke saath).  
- Weekly working hours ka upper cap **48 ghante se badha kar 60 ghante** tak allow kiya gaya, saath hi 6-ghante wali flexible shifts ka provision.  
- Sarkar ka kehna hai ki is se **industrial output, investments aur women employment** dono badhenge; opposition ne women safety par concerns raise kiye.  
- Employers par night shift me **safe commute, security &amp; internal complaints committee** jaise provisions implement karne ka pressure hoga.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- One-liner: **Factory (Rajasthan Amendment) Bill 2025** → women night shift allowed + weekly hours **upto 60**.  
- RAS mains me labour reforms, women employment aur industrial growth ke context me important case-study.  
- Police/administration answers me night shift safety &amp; workplace conditions ke baare me is example ka use ho sakta hai.

**Memory Trick – “Raat ki shift, factory me mahilaon ka uplift”**

> Line: **“Factory Amendment 2025 – women night shift legal, safety responsibility legal.”**
