## TOPIC 1: Public Suggestions for Rajasthan Budget 2026‑27 – 25,000+ Inputs

### Kya hua?

- The Rajasthan government invited **public suggestions** for the upcoming **Rajasthan Budget 2026‑27** through an **online portal, email and social media**.  
- According to reports, more than **25,000 suggestions** were received, with major themes being **youth employment, education, agriculture, women’s safety, health and roads**.  
- The Finance Department has indicated that these suggestions will be **sorted and analysed category‑wise**, and feasible ideas may be incorporated into the budget.  

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- This is a live example of **participatory budgeting and jan‑bhagidari**, important for RAS/Police mains answers on **good governance and transparency**.  
- A factual question can also ask: **Which Rajasthan budget received over 25,000 public suggestions via portal? → Pre‑budget exercise for 2026‑27.**

## TOPIC 3: Rajasthan Budget 2025‑26 – Environment &amp; Green Energy Measures

### Kya hua?

- The **Rajasthan Budget 2025‑26**, described as a **“Green Theme” budget**, includes several measures for **environment and green energy**.  
- Key elements include a **Climate Adaptation Plan‑2030**, **Green Aravali Development**, a **Tree Outside Forest (ToFR) Policy**, promotion of **agro‑forestry**, and stronger support for **green mobility/EVs and renewable energy**.  
- Solar and wind projects are to be supported through steps like **land banking, grid upgradation** and facilitation of **private investment**.  

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Rajasthan is already a **national leader in solar and wind energy**; green budget steps reinforce this status and are important across **geography, environment and economy** sections.  
- MCQs can ask: **Climate Adaptation Plan‑2030 or Green Aravali Development Project belongs to which state? → Rajasthan.**  

**Memory Trick – “AAA for Rajasthan Green: Adaptation, Aravali, Agro‑forestry”**

> Code: **AAA = Adaptation Plan + Aravali Green + Agro‑forestry** for green Rajasthan.

## TOPIC 6: Andhra Pradesh – Kakinada Green Ammonia Project (Relevance for Rajasthan)

### Kya hua?

- At the national level, **Andhra Pradesh** will host the **world’s largest green ammonia project** at **Kakinada**, developed by **AM Green**.  
- The project aims at **1.5 MTPA green ammonia capacity by 2030**, with an investment of about **USD 10 billion**, converting an existing ammonia–urea complex into a fully **renewable‑energy‑powered** green ammonia facility.  

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- This topic is important for Rajasthan aspirants as part of **green fuels, energy transition and climate policy** in India.  
- MCQ angle: **Where will the world’s largest green ammonia project come up? → Kakinada, Andhra Pradesh.**  

**Memory Trick – “A for Andhra, A for Ammonia”**

> Link: **“Andhra Pradesh – Ammonia hub at Kakinada”**.
