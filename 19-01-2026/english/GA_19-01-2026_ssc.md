## TOPIC 2: PLFS December 2025 – Female LFPR &amp; WPR at Yearly Highs

### Kya hua?

- The **Periodic Labour Force Survey (PLFS) Monthly Bulletin for December 2025** shows that **female Labour Force Participation Rate (LFPR)** and **Worker Population Ratio (WPR)** reached their highest levels of the year.  
- Overall LFPR (15+ years) increased to **56.1%** (from 55.8% in November), with **rural LFPR at 59.0%** and urban LFPR at **50.2%**.  
- **Female LFPR (15+ years)** rose to **35.3%**, with **rural female LFPR at 40.1%** and urban female LFPR around **25.3%**.  
- Overall WPR climbed to **53.4%**, while the **overall unemployment rate** remained broadly stable at **4.8%**.  
- PLFS is conducted by the **National Statistical Office (NSO)** under **MoSPI**, now providing monthly estimates using the **Current Weekly Status (CWS)** approach.  

### SSC exam points:

- Survey: **PLFS – Periodic Labour Force Survey**.  
- Conducted by: **NSO, Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation (MoSPI)**.  
- Overall UR (Dec 2025): **4.8%**, female LFPR (15+): **35.3%**.  

**Memory Trick – “PLFS: 35.3% female LFPR, 4.8% UR”**

> Lock these two numbers together: **“35.3 and 4.8”** as a pair.

## TOPIC 3: GeM’s Womaniya Initiative – Seven Years of Women-Led Procurement

### Kya hua?

- The **Government e‑Marketplace (GeM)** has completed **seven years of the Womaniya initiative**, launched on **14 January 2019**.  
- Womaniya aims to increase the participation of **women‑led Micro and Small Enterprises (MSEs)** and **Self‑Help Groups** in **government procurement**.  
- As of 14 January 2026, **over 2 lakh women‑led MSEs** are registered on GeM, having secured **orders worth over ₹80,000 crore**.  
- Womaniya orders now account for about **4.7% of GeM’s total order value**, exceeding the **3% mandatory target** for women‑owned enterprises.  

### SSC exam points:

- Platform: **Government e‑Marketplace (GeM)**.  
- Initiative: **Womaniya** – launched **2019** to support women entrepreneurs in procurement.  
- Orders for women‑led MSEs: **₹80,000 crore+**, share ~**4.7%** of total GeM orders.  

**Memory Trick – “Womaniya @ GeM = 2 lakh women MSEs, ₹80,000+ crore orders”**

> Visualise a GeM dashboard showing a **“Womaniya” badge with 2 lakh sellers and ₹80,000 crore**.
