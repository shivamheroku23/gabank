## TOPIC 1: Canara Bank–NBSL – BHIM Plug‑in se UPI “inside” Canara ai1Pe

### Kya hua?

- **Canara Bank** ne **NPCI BHIM Services Limited (NBSL)** ke saath partnership karke apne **Canara ai1Pe** app me UPI ke liye **bank plug‑in model** adopt kiya hai.  
- Is model me bank **BHIM UPI ke technology stack** ko directly plug‑in ke रूप me use karta hai – yani **BHIM ka engine, Canara ka app**.  
- Canara Bank is model ko adopt karne wala **India ka pehla bank** ban gaya hai; is se bank ko alag se apna UPI engine maintain karne ki ज़रूरत kam ho jaati hai.  
- **NBSL**, jo **NPCI ki 100% subsidiary** hai, BHIM app ko manage karti hai (BHIM 2016 me PM ke द्वारा launch hua tha) aur ab apni technology ko plug‑in ke रूप me banks ko दे रही hai.  
- Plug‑in approach se **security, resilience aur scalability** improve hoti hai, aur banks apne **core banking functions** par focus kar sakte hain jabki UPI ke tech upgrades NBSL handle karta hai.  

(Official context: NPCI/NBSL &amp; Canara Bank announcements; Photo: https://cdn.guidely.in/images/sliders/176035399766.webp)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- UPI, NPCI, BHIM aur plug‑in models – ye sab **digital payments** section ke favourite topics hain.  
- MCQ angle:  
  - Pehla bank jo NBSL plug‑in model adopt kare? → **Canara Bank**  
  - BHIM ko kaun manage karta hai? → **NPCI BHIM Services Limited (NBSL)**  
  - Canara ka UPI‑enabled super‑app? → **Canara ai1Pe**  
- Mains/interview me is example se **Digital Public Infrastructure + private innovation** ka combo explain kiya ja sakta hai.  

### Memory Trick – “BHIM inside Canara”

> Socho **Canara ai1Pe** ek smartphone app hai jiske andar chhota sa **BHIM engine** laga hai – caption: **“BHIM inside”**.

## TOPIC 2: SBI ke nayi ATM &amp; IMPS Charges – 21 se 23, aur free se paid slabs

### Kya hua?

- **State Bank of India (SBI)** ne **1 December 2025** se ATM charges aur **15 February 2026** se IMPS charges revise karne ka फैसला kiya.  
- **Non‑SBI ATMs par transactions**:  
  - Savings accounts ke liye ab bhi **mahine me 5 free transactions** milte hain (non‑SBI ATMs par).  
  - Free limit cross hone ke baad har **cash withdrawal** par ab **₹23 + GST** lagega (pehle **₹21 + GST** tha).  
  - Non‑financial transaction (balance enquiry etc.) par ab **₹11 + GST** (pehle ₹10 + GST) ka charge lagega.  
- **Online IMPS charges (selected slabs)**:  
  - **₹0–₹10,000** → free.  
  - **₹10,000–₹25,000** → free (online channel).  
  - **₹25,000–₹1 lakh** → **₹2 + GST** (pehle free).  
  - **₹1 lakh–₹2 lakh** → **₹6 + GST**.  
  - **₹2 lakh–₹5 lakh** → **₹10 + GST**.  
- Salary/special accounts jaise **defence, central govt, pension, railways aur Shaurya Family Pension accounts** par IMPS charge hike लागू nahi hoga.  

(Official context: SBI circular on revised charges; Photo: https://cdn.guidely.in/images/sliders/176035399766.webp)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- ATM &amp; IMPS charges directly **customer service &amp; charges** wale MCQs me aa sakte hain – ye typical factual area hai.  
- Pointers:  
  - Non‑SBI ATM cash withdrawal after free limit → **₹23 + GST**.  
  - Online IMPS ₹25k–₹1 lakh slab → **₹2 + GST**.  
  - Kaun exempt? → **salary &amp; special accounts (defence, CGSP, pension etc.)**.  

### Memory Trick – “21 se 23, mid‑value IMPS ab free nahi”

> Line yaad rakho: **“Non‑SBI ATM: 21 → 23; IMPS ₹25k+ par ₹2/₹6/₹10 + GST.”**

## TOPIC 3: OECD ka Global Minimum Corporate Tax Deal – 15% ka global floor

### Kya hua?

- **Organisation for Economic Co‑operation and Development (OECD)** ne **United States aur 100+ deshon** ke saath milkar **global minimum corporate tax** par agreement final kiya hai.  
- Deal ke तहत ek **15% minimum corporate tax floor** ka framework banaya gaya hai jo बड़े multinational companies ke **profit shifting aur tax avoidance** ko control karne ke liye design hua tha.  
- Latest compromiseme US‑based कई MNCs ko kuch हद tak carve‑out mila hai – badle me dusre deshon ko un par **extra top‑up taxes** nahi lagane honge.  
- Idea yeh hai ki global level par ek **समन्वित minimum tax**, tax havens ke ज़रिए aggressive planning ko limit karega.  

(Official context: OECD global tax reform; Photo: https://cdn.guidely.in/images/sliders/176035399766.webp)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Ye topic **international taxation, FDI, MNC behaviour, cross‑border banking** se जुड़ा hai – jo economy GA me पूछे ja sakte hain.  
- Typical MCQ:  
  - Global minimum corporate tax framework ka lead body? → **OECD**  
  - Indicative rate? → **15%**  
- Mains me ise **BEPS (Base Erosion and Profit Shifting)** aur tax cooperation ke context me quote kiya ja sakta hai.  

### Memory Trick – “OECD = 15% global floor”

> Simple code: **“OECD minimum tax = 15% floor for big multinationals.”**

## TOPIC 4: PLFS December 2025 – Female LFPR high, UR stable

### Kya hua?

- **Periodic Labour Force Survey (PLFS) Monthly Bulletin – December 2025** ke mutabik **female Labour Force Participation Rate (LFPR)** aur **Worker Population Ratio (WPR)** apne साल ke **highest level** par pahunch gaye.  
- Overall LFPR (15+ years) **56.1%** ho gaya (November: 55.8%); **rural LFPR 59.0%** aur urban LFPR approx **50.2%** raha.  
- **Female LFPR (15+ years)** December me **35.3%** tak badh gaya – rural female LFPR **40.1%**, urban female LFPR **~25.3%** ke आसपास.  
- Overall **WPR 53.4%** tak pahunch gaya; **unemployment rate (UR)** lagbhag **4.8%** raha (rural 3.9%, urban 6.7%).  
- PLFS ko **National Statistical Office (NSO), MoSPI** conduct karta hai; January 2025 se ye **monthly estimates** CWS (Current Weekly Status) approach se deta hai.  

(Official context: PLFS December 2025 bulletin; Photo: https://cdn.guidely.in/images/sliders/176035399766.webp)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- LFPR, WPR, unemployment – ye sab **labour market + macro‑economy** topics hain jo exams me direct factual Qs ke रूप me aate hain.  
- Points yaad rakho:  
  - Survey: **PLFS**, conducting body: **NSO (MoSPI)**.  
  - Overall UR December 2025: **4.8%**.  
  - Female LFPR (15+) December 2025: **35.3%**, rural female LFPR: **40.1%**.  

### Memory Trick – “35.3% female LFPR, 4.8% UR”

> Ek pair note karo: **“Female LFPR ~35% &amp; unemployment ~4.8%”** – dono numbers ek saath yaad rakhne ke liye.
