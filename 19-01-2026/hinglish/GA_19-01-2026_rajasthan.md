## TOPIC 6: Andhra Pradesh – Kakinada Green Ammonia Project (Rajasthan ke liye exam angle)

### Kya hua?

- All‑India level par **Andhra Pradesh** ke **Kakinada** me **AM Green** dwara **world’s largest green ammonia project** set up ki ja rahi hai, jiska final target **1.5 MTPA capacity by 2030** hai.  
- Project approx **USD 10 billion** investment ke saath existing ammonia‑urea complex ko green ammonia facility me convert karega, jo puri tarah **renewable energy (solar + wind)** par chalega.  

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Ye topic national current affairs me **green fuels &amp; energy transition** ke angle se important hai – Rajasthan me bhi green hydrogen/renewables discuss hote hain.  
- MCQ me pucha ja sakta hai: **world’s largest green ammonia project kaha? → Kakinada, Andhra Pradesh**.  

**Memory Trick – “AP = Ammonia Power at Kakinada”**

> Yaad: **“A for Andhra, A for Ammonia”** – Kakinada = global green ammonia hub.
