## TOPIC 2: PLFS December 2025 – Female LFPR &amp; WPR yearly high par

### Kya hua?

- **Periodic Labour Force Survey (PLFS) Monthly Bulletin – December 2025** ke mutabik **female Labour Force Participation Rate (LFPR)** aur **Worker Population Ratio (WPR)** is saal ke **highest level** par pahunch gaye.  
- Overall LFPR (15+ years) **56.1%** (Nov: 55.8%), jisme **rural LFPR 59.0%** aur urban LFPR **50.2%** ke आसपास raha.  
- **Female LFPR (15+ years)** badhkar **35.3%** ho gaya – rural female LFPR **40.1%**, urban female LFPR approx **25.3%**.  
- Overall **WPR 53.4%**, jabki **unemployment rate (UR)** broadly stable **4.8%** par raha (rural 3.9%, urban 6.7%).  
- PLFS ko **National Statistical Office (NSO), MoSPI** conduct karta hai; yeh ab **CWS (Current Weekly Status)** basis par monthly estimates deta hai.  

### SSC exam points:

- Survey: **PLFS – Periodic Labour Force Survey**.  
- Conducting body: **NSO, MoSPI**.  
- Key numbers: **female LFPR (15+) ~35.3%, overall UR ~4.8%**.  

**Memory Trick – “PLFS: 35.3 &amp; 4.8 – women up, jobless stable”**

> Pair yaad rakho: **“35.3% female LFPR, 4.8% unemployment”**.

## TOPIC 3: GeM ki Womaniya Initiative – 7 saal, ₹80,000+ crore women‑led procurement

### Kya hua?

- **Government e‑Marketplace (GeM)** ki **Womaniya initiative** ko **7 saal** complete ho gaye (launch: **14 January 2019**).  
- Goal: **women‑led Micro &amp; Small Enterprises (MSEs)** aur **Self‑Help Groups** ko **government procurement** me jyada participation dena.  
- 14 January 2026 tak **2 lakh se jyada women‑led MSEs** GeM par register ho chuki hain, jinhone milkar **₹80,000 crore se adhik orders** hasil kiye hain.  
- Womaniya ke through aane wale orders GeM ke total order value ka lagbhag **4.7%** hissa ban gaye hain – jo mandatory **3% target** se kaafi upar hai.  

### SSC exam points:

- Platform: **GeM – Government e‑Marketplace**.  
- Initiative: **Womaniya (2019)** – women‑led MSEs &amp; SHGs ke liye.  
- Orders: **₹80,000+ crore**, share around **4.7%** of total GeM orders.  

**Memory Trick – “2 lakh women MSEs, 80k crore – Womaniya score”**

> Socho GeM dashboard par ek badge hai: **“Womaniya: 2 lakh sellers, ₹80,000+ cr orders”**.
