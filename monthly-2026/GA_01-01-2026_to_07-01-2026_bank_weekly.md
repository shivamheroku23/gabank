## THEME 1: RBI Digital Banking Framework – CAAL Rule (1 Jan 2026)

### Kya hua?

- **1 Jan 2026** se RBI ka **naya digital banking framework** लागू hua, jo cover karta hai:  
  - Internet banking, mobile banking, UPI, USSD, SMS banking etc.  
- Banks ko ab:  
  - Har digital service ke लिये **special authorisation** + detailed conduct norms follow karne honge.  
  - Digital services enable karne se pehle **explicit customer consent** lena hoga.  
  - Har major activity (debit/credit, reset, registration, limit change) par **alerts (SMS/Email/Push)** bhejna होगा.  
  - Har channel ke लिये clear **on/off (registration / de‑registration)** options dene honge.  
  - Fraud / failed / unauthorised transactions par **liability &amp; grievance redressal** framework clearly define karna होगा.

### Bank exam angle

- Code word: **CAAL** –  
  - **C – Consent**,  
  - **A – Alerts**,  
  - **A – Access control**,  
  - **L – Liability &amp; grievance**.  
- MCQ fodder: effective date (1 Jan 2026), covered services (internet, mobile, UPI etc.), CAAL ka full form.

### Memory Trick – “2026 ka CAAL”

> Line: **“2026 se Digital Banking pe CAAL chalega – Consent, Alerts, Access, Liability.”**

## THEME 2: BSBD “4F” Accounts, PAN–Aadhaar &amp; Weekly Credit Score

### Kya hua?

- **BSBD (Basic Savings Bank Deposit) accounts** – Jan Dhan type no‑frills accounts – ke लिये rules:  
  - **1 April 2026** se (notification 1 Jan ke aas‑paas):  
    - Sab banks ko BSBD holders ko **full digital banking (net + mobile)** dena hoga.  
    - **No minimum balance**, **free cash deposits** (branch/ATM/BC), **free ATM/debit card**, basic cheque book &amp; statement free.  
- 1 Jan 2026 se:  
  - **PAN–Aadhaar link na hone** par PAN ko **inoperative treat** kiya ja sakta hai → ITR, accounts, loans par असर.  
  - **Credit bureaus** ko data **weekly update** karna होगा (pehle ~15 din me ek baar).

### Bank exam angle

- BSBD = **4F account**:  
  - **Full digital**, **Free deposits**, **Free card/basic services**, **Free from minimum balance**.  
- PAN–Aadhaar: compliance + KYC + tax – banking integration ka classic example.  
- Weekly credit score:  
  - Recent EMI behaviour **jaldi reflect**, risk assessment अधिक timely.

### Memory Tricks

- BSBD → **“4F”**.  
- PAN–Aadhaar → **“PAN bina Aadhaar = financial gate LOCK”**.  
- Weekly score → **“Sunday score report”** – har week ek नया report card.

## THEME 4: Tax &amp; Governance – Sin Goods Cess, e‑Bill, Land Stack, CCB

### Kya hua?

- **Sin goods (tobacco, pan masala, gutkha)** par pehle lagne wala **GST Compensation Cess**  
  धीरे‑धीरे **Additional Excise Duty + Health &amp; National Security Cess** me convert ho raha hai,  
  jisse health + national security ke लिये dedicated revenue mile.  
- **Integrated e‑Bill System** – fertiliser subsidy (~₹2 lakh crore) ke लिये  
  पूरी तरह digital billing &amp; payment workflow launch (Chemicals &amp; Fertilisers ministry).  
- **Land Stack (DILRMP)** – pilot digital platform jo **land records, cadastral maps, ownership &amp; encumbrances** ko integrate karke  
  banks ko secure lending ke लिये verified land data APIs se provide karega.  
- RBI ke **Financial Stability Report** me stress‑test scenarios ke context me **Capital Conservation Buffer (CCB)** ka ज़िक्र –  
  Basel‑III ka additional **2.5% RWA** capital cushion.

### Bank exam angle

- Sin tax → health &amp; security cess – indirect tax + welfare linkage.  
- e‑Bill → subsidy payments digital hone se **reconciliation, working capital &amp; fraud risk** par असर.  
- Land Stack → mortgage / agri lending ke लिये **title risk, double‑mortgage, fraud** kam karne ka tool.  
- CCB → capital norms / Basel‑III favourite topic.

### Memory Tricks

- Old **Compensation Cess (CC)** → new **Health &amp; National (HN) Cess** → **“CC gaya, HN aaya.”**  
- Land Stack → **“Land ka digi‑folder.”**  
- CCB → **“Cushion Capital Buffer”** – झटके absorb karne ka cushion.

### Kya hua?

- Agriculture Minister **Shivraj Singh Chouhan** ne states ko advisory di ki  
  **PM‑KISAN, PMFBY (Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana)** aur अन्य agri schemes ke funds  
  **March 2026 tak तेज़ी se utilise** karein – taaki farmers ko time par DBT/claims mil sake.  
- **PMFBY &amp; WBCIS** ko aage ke seasons ke लिये continue + reform karne ka निर्णय (tech‑based assessment, faster claims).  
- **Grameen Bharat Mahotsav 2025** – New Delhi me SHGs, FPOs, artisans ke rural products ka national showcase.  
- ICAR / agri universities ne **नयी high‑yielding / climate‑resilient crop varieties** release ki – long‑term yield &amp; repayment capacity improve hone ke लिये.  
- PM‑KISAN, fertiliser subsidy, LPG subsidy, PM‑Awas, JJM, health schemes – sab ko DBT architecture se link karne par जोर।

### Bank exam angle

- PM‑KISAN: **100% DBT** into bank accounts → Jan Dhan + KYC importance.  
- PMFBY/WBCIS: banks **premium debit + claims channel** – agri PSL, NPA control.  
- Rural events (Grameen Mahotsav) – SHG‑bank linkage, MUDRA, micro‑finance.  
- DBT = **Direct Benefit Transfer = Direct Bank Transfer** – banking inclusion core.

### Memory Tricks

- KISAN mnemonic → **KYC, Insurance, Subsidy, Account, Now** – paisa seedha account me.  
- DBT → **“Direct Bank Transfer”**.

## THEME 6: Payments &amp; Subsidy – PRB, NBS Rabi 2025‑26, ONGC–NSTFDC, FTA Deficit &amp; Payments Vision 2028

### Kya hua?

- **Payments Regulatory Board (PRB)** – Payment and Settlement Systems Act, 2007 me 2025 ke amendment ke baad  
  **BPSS** ko replace karne wali nayi body, jiska **पहला बैठक 5 Jan 2026, Mumbai** me hua;  
  Chairperson → **RBI Governor**.  
- Govt ne **Nutrient Based Subsidy (NBS)** ke Rabi 2025‑26 rates approve kiye – P&amp;K fertilisers ke लिये approx:  
  - N: ₹43.02/kg, P: ₹47.96/kg, K: ₹2.38/kg, S: ₹2.87/kg;  
  - Estimated subsidy bill ~**₹37,952 crore**.  
- **ONGC–NSTFDC CSR MoU** – लगभग **₹28 cr** CSR funds se **11 States/UTs ke ~144 EMRS schools** ka infra upgrade; ~35k tribal students indirect beneficiaries.  
- NITI Aayog ki **Trade Watch** report – **FTA partner देशों ke साथ trade deficit ~59% YoY** bada (Apr–Jun 2025);  
  RBI data ke mutabik week ended 26 Dec 2025 me **forex reserves ~US$ 3.3 bn** badhe.  
- RBI ka draft **Payments Vision 2028** – **4S**: **Safe, Secure, Smart, Swift payments** – UPI, RuPay, NEFT/RTGS, offline, cross‑border remittances sab ke लिये direction set.

### Bank exam angle

- PRB → payments governance, UPI/cards/NEFT/RTGS sab ka regulator; PSS Act, 2007.  
- NBS → subsidy–fiscal–agri–inflation link; fertiliser companies ki working‑capital financing.  
- ONGC–NSTFDC → CSR + inclusion example; future MSME borrowers / SHG members.  
- FTA deficit + forex reserves → CAD, rupee, bond yields, external stability.  
- Payments Vision 2028 → digital payments ke लिये 4S framework.

### Memory Tricks

- PRB → **“Payments Rules Board”**.  
- NBS → **“NPKS RATE card”**.  
- Payments Vision 2028 → **4S = Safe, Secure, Smart, Swift**.
