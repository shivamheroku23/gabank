## THEME 1: India 4th‑Largest Economy &amp; AI/Tech Push

### Kya hua?

- 2025‑26 ke nominal GDP estimates ke अनुसार **India ne Japan ko पीछे छोड़कर दुनिया की 4th‑largest economy** ka स्थान लिया:  
  - क्रम: **USA, China, Germany, India, Japan**.  
  - Approx GDP: **USD 4.1–4.2 trillion**.  
- Gujarat ne GIFT City me national‑level AI research संस्था **IAIRO – Indian Artificial Intelligence Research Organization** स्थापित की:  
  - Section‑8 (not‑for‑profit) company, PPP model; AI, pharma, fintech / reg‑tech research focus.  
- Defence &amp; tech side par:  
  - **K‑4 medium‑range SLBM (~3,500 km)** ka सफल test **INS Arighaat** se – sea‑based nuclear deterrence मजबूत.  
  - **Dhruv‑NG** – HAL ka indigenous next‑generation multi‑role helicopter variant, production flag‑off.

### SSC exam points:

- India ki GDP rank (nominal, ~2026) → **4th**, Japan se aage.  
- IAIRO → **GIFT City, Gujarat**, AI research body.  
- K‑4 → **Submarine‑launched ballistic missile (SLBM)**, range ~3,500 km, launch platform **INS Arighaat**, developed by **DRDO**.  
- Dhruv / Dhruv‑NG → **HAL**, multi‑role light helicopter.

**Memory Trick – “3G–4I–5J &amp; AI at GIFT”**

> GDP rank code: **3G (Germany), 4I (India), 5J (Japan)**.  
> AI ke लिये: **“AI ka GIFT Gujarat ko – IAIRO.”**

## THEME 3: Governance &amp; Social Security – EPFO, NMCG, NTH–NHAI, PM‑YUVA

### Kya hua?

- **EPFO &amp; PDUNASS:**  
  - PDUNASS – *Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya National Academy of Social Security* – EPFO ki training academy.  
  - PDUNASS ne **IC Centre for Governance (ICCfG)** ke साथ मिलकर social security administration me **ethics** पर focus किया.  
  - EPFO: स्थापना **1952**, HQ New Delhi, under Ministry of Labour &amp; Employment.  
- **NMCG / Namami Gange:**  
  - National Mission for Clean Ganga – 2011 me society, Ministry of Jal Shakti ke तहत;  
  - projects for **Indian Skimmer bird habitats**, nature‑based solutions for Yamuna drains, STPs in UP/Bihar/Jharkhand/WB.  
- **NTH–NHAI MoU:**  
  - National Test House (estd. **1912**, under Consumer Affairs) ko NHAI (estd. **1995**, HQ Dwarka Delhi) ke साथ  
    national highways ka quality‑testing partner banaya gaya.  
- **PM‑YUVA 3.0:**  
  - “Prime Minister’s Scheme for Mentoring Young Authors” – implementing agency **NBT India**;  
  - 43 young authors (<30 yrs), themes: diaspora in nation‑building, Indian Knowledge System, Makers of Modern India.

### SSC exam points:

- EPFO – 1952, HQ Delhi; PDUNASS – EPFO training academy.  
- NMCG – Namami Gange implementation, under **Ministry of Jal Shakti**.  
- NTH – 1912; NHAI – 1995, HQ Dwarka, New Delhi.  
- PM‑YUVA – young authors scheme, NBT.

**Memory Tricks**

- EPFO → **“Employee ka PF office – EPFO – 1952 se.”**  
- NMCG → **“Namami Gange Cleaning Group.”**  
- NHAI → National Highways Authority of India – **1995, Dwarka Delhi**.

## THEME 5: Payments, Subsidy &amp; Trade – PRB, NBS, ONGC–NSTFDC, FTA Deficit, Payments Vision 2028

### Kya hua?

- **Payments Regulatory Board (PRB):**  
  - Payment and Settlement Systems Act, 2007 me amendment ke बाद,  
    RBI ke अंदर nayi payments regulator body – PRB – ne **BPSS** ko replace kiya;  
  - First meeting **5 Jan 2026, Mumbai**; chair – **RBI Governor**; focus – UPI, cards, NEFT/RTGS, cross‑border payments.  
- **Nutrient Based Subsidy (NBS) – Rabi 2025‑26:**  
  - P&amp;K fertilisers ke लिये rates approve; per‑kg subsidy approx: N: ₹43.02, P: ₹47.96, K: ₹2.38, S: ₹2.87;  
  - Estimated subsidy bill ~**₹37,952 crore**.  
- **ONGC–NSTFDC CSR MoU:**  
  - ONGC se ~**₹28 cr** CSR funds; **11 states/UTs ke ~144 Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRS)** ka infrastructure &amp; digital upgrade;  
  - ~35k tribal students indirect beneficiaries.  
- **Trade &amp; Forex:**  
  - NITI Aayog Trade Watch report – **FTA partner देशों ke साथ trade deficit ~59% YoY** बढ़ा;  
  - RBI data – week ended 26 Dec 2025 me **forex reserves ~US$ 3.3 bn** बढ़े.  
- **Payments Vision 2028 (RBI):**  
  - Target: **4S – Safe, Secure, Smart, Swift** digital payments;  
  - Focus on UPI, RuPay, offline/feature‑phone payments, cross‑border remittances, fraud management &amp; cyber‑security.

### SSC exam points:

- PRB → Payment &amp; Settlement Systems Act, 2007; replaced **BPSS**; head – RBI Governor.  
- NBS → P&amp;K fertilisers ke लिये per‑kg subsidy; subsidy bill ~₹37,952 cr.  
- ONGC–NSTFDC → EMRS schools ke लिये CSR; tribal education &amp; inclusion.  
- 4S formula → Payments Vision 2028 ke लिये: **Safe, Secure, Smart, Swift**.

**Memory Tricks**

- PRB → **“Payments Rules Board”**.  
- NBS → **“NPKS ka RATE card.”**  
- PV 2028 → **“4S payments”**.
