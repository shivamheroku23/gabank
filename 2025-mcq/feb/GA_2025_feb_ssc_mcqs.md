1. In February 2025, which BJP leader was sworn in as the 9th Chief Minister of Delhi and the 4th woman to hold the post in the National Capital Territory?  
फरवरी 2025 में, किस भाजपा नेता ने राष्ट्रीय राजधानी क्षेत्र में 9वीं और चौथी महिला मुख्यमंत्री के रूप में पद ग्रहण किया?

A. Kiran Bedi / किरण बेदी  
B. Rekha Gupta / रेखा गुप्ता  
C. Smriti Irani / स्मृति ईरानी  
D. Meenakshi Lekhi / मीनाक्षी लेखी  

**Answer:** B  
**Explanation:** Rekha Gupta, a BJP leader and first‑time MLA, was sworn in as the 9th Chief Minister of Delhi and the 4th woman CM of the NCT in February 2025. (Source: DD News & current‑affairs compilations – Feb 2025.)

2. Purnima Devi Barman, featured in February–March 2025 news, was included in TIME’s 2025 “Women of the Year” list for her work in conserving which species?  
फरवरी–मार्च 2025 की खबरों में रही पूर्णिमा देवी बर्मन को TIME की 2025 “Women of the Year” सूची में किस प्रजाति के संरक्षण कार्य के लिए शामिल किया गया?

A. Snow leopard / हिम तेंदुआ  
B. Greater adjutant stork / ग्रेटर एडजुटेंट सारस  
C. Olive ridley sea turtle / ऑलिव रिडले समुद्री कछुआ  
D. Indian one‑horned rhinoceros / भारतीय एक‑सिंगी गैंडा  

**Answer:** B  
**Explanation:** Assamese conservationist Purnima Devi Barman was recognised by TIME for her community‑driven efforts to protect the endangered greater adjutant stork (Hargila) in Assam. (Source: TIME Women of the Year 2025 profile.)

3. Exercise “Cyclone‑III”, conducted in February 2025, was a joint special forces exercise between India and which country?  
फरवरी 2025 में आयोजित विशेष बल अभ्यास “Cyclone‑III” भारत और किस देश के बीच आयोजित संयुक्त सैन्य अभ्यास था?

A. Egypt / मिस्र  
B. France / फ्रांस  
C. Russia / रूस  
D. Indonesia / इंडोनेशिया  

**Answer:** A  
**Explanation:** Exercise Cyclone‑III was held between the special forces of India and Egypt at the Mahajan Field Firing Range in Rajasthan in February 2025. (Source: PIB – Exercise Cyclone‑III, Feb 2025.)
