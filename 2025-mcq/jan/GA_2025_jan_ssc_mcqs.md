1. In January 2025, which Indian state became the first to officially implement a Uniform Civil Code (UCC)?  
जनवरी 2025 में कौन‑सा भारतीय राज्य आधिकारिक रूप से समान नागरिक संहिता (UCC) लागू करने वाला पहला राज्य बना?

A. Gujarat / गुजरात  
B. Uttarakhand / उत्तराखंड  
C. Uttar Pradesh / उत्तर प्रदेश  
D. Madhya Pradesh / मध्य प्रदेश  

**Answer:** B  
**Explanation:** Uttarakhand brought its Uniform Civil Code into effect in January 2025, becoming the first Indian state to implement a UCC covering marriage, divorce, inheritance and related matters. (Source: Uttarakhand UCC implementation – Jan 2025 news & legal analyses.)

2. Grameen Bharat Mahotsav 2025, inaugurated in January 2025, was held at which venue with the theme “Building a Resilient Rural India for a Viksit Bharat 2047”?  
जनवरी 2025 में आयोजित ग्रामीन भारत महोत्सव 2025, जिसका थीम “Building a Resilient Rural India for a Viksit Bharat 2047” था, भारत में किस स्थान पर आयोजित किया गया?

A. Pragati Maidan, New Delhi / प्रगति मैदान, नई दिल्ली  
B. Bharat Mandapam, New Delhi / भारत मंडपम, नई दिल्ली  
C. India Expo Centre, Greater Noida / इंडिया एक्सपो सेंटर, ग्रेटर नोएडा  
D. Mahatma Mandir, Gandhinagar / महात्मा मंदिर, गांधीनगर  

**Answer:** B  
**Explanation:** Grameen Bharat Mahotsav 2025 was organised at Bharat Mandapam, New Delhi, from 4–9 January 2025 with a focus on rural resilience and financial inclusion. (Source: PIB – Grameen Bharat Mahotsav 2025, Jan 2025.)
