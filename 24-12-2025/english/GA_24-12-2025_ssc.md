## TOPIC 1: National Consumer Day 2025

### Kya hua?

- **24 December** is observed every year in India as **National Consumer Day**.
- The day marks the **presidential assent to the Consumer Protection Act, 1986** on 24 December 1986.
- In 2025, the Government and consumer organisations held awareness programmes on consumer rights, grievance redressal and e‑commerce protection.
- The day often highlights the role of the **Consumer Protection Act, 2019**, consumer courts and helplines.

### SSC exam points:

- Date: **24 December – National Consumer Day**.
- Linked Act: **Consumer Protection Act, 1986** (background), updated by **Act of 2019**.
- Focus: consumer rights, dispute redressal, e‑commerce, misleading ads.

**Memory Trick – “24 ko consumer ka score”**

> 24 December ko yaad rakho: **“24 ko consumer ka score”** – 24 = National Consumer Day.

## TOPIC 2: DRDO Pinaka LRGR‑120 maiden test

### Kya hua?

- DRDO successfully conducted the **maiden flight test of Pinaka LRGR‑120**, a **Long Range Guided Rocket**.
- The test was carried out from the **Integrated Test Range (ITR), Chandipur, Odisha**.
- The rocket has a **range of about 120 km** and is an advanced version of the Pinaka system.
- It improves precision and range for Indian artillery regiments, supporting “Make in India” in defence.

### SSC exam points:

- Organisation: **DRDO**.  
- System: **Pinaka LRGR‑120 – Long Range Guided Rocket (120 km range)**.  
- Test location: **ITR, Chandipur (Odisha)**.  

**Memory Trick – “Pinaka 120 = 1 rocket, 2 digits, 0 miss”**

> Pinaka LRGR‑120 ko yaad rakho **“1 rocket, 2 digits, 0 miss”** – 120 km range, precision strike.

## TOPIC 3: National Frequency Allocation Plan (NFAP) 2025

### Kya hua?

- The **Department of Telecommunications (DoT)** released the **NFAP 2025**.
- NFAP is the **radio spectrum allocation chart** for India, assigning frequencies to telecom, broadcasting, defence, satellite and other services.
- NFAP 2025 is designed to support **5G/6G, satellite broadband, critical communications and defence**.
- It aligns India’s spectrum use with **international ITU regulations** and future auctions.

### SSC exam points:

- Full form: **NFAP – National Frequency Allocation Plan**.
- Ministry/department: **Department of Telecommunications (DoT)**, under **Ministry of Communications**.
- Purpose: **allocation of radio spectrum** across sectors.

**Memory Trick – “NFAP = Network Frequency Allocation Plan”**

> NFAP ko yaad rakho as **“Network Frequency Allocation Plan”** – network ke liye frequency map.

## TOPIC 4: Rakesh Agarwal – Interim DG of NIA

### Kya hua?

- The Ministry of Home Affairs appointed **IPS officer Rakesh Agarwal** as **Interim Director‑General (DG) of the National Investigation Agency (NIA)**.
- He took over after the earlier DG, **Sadanand Vasant Date**, was repatriated to his state cadre.
- NIA is India’s premier central agency for investigating **terrorism and certain scheduled offences**.

### SSC exam points:

- Post: **Interim DG, NIA – Rakesh Agarwal** (as per latest update).
- NIA HQ: **New Delhi**.
- NIA comes under: **Ministry of Home Affairs**.

**Memory Trick – “RA–NIA: RAkesh at NIA”**

> **RA‑NIA**: **RAkesh Agarwal – NIA** DG, easy pairing.

## TOPIC 5: “Bhartiya Bhasha Utsav 2025”

### Kya hua?

- The Government celebrated **“Bhartiya Bhasha Utsav 2025”** to promote Indian languages.
- Linked to the **National Education Policy (NEP) 2020**, it emphasises **mother tongue and classical Indian languages**.
- Events included seminars, cultural programmes and competitions in schools and colleges.

### SSC exam points:

- Name: **Bhartiya Bhasha Utsav 2025**.
- Focus: promotion of **Indian languages** and NEP 2020 vision.
- Ministry: linked to **Ministry of Education** and related language bodies.

**Memory Trick – “Bhasha Utsav = language festival”**

> **Bhartiya Bhasha Utsav** ko simply yaad rakho as **“Indian language festival”** under NEP 2020.

## TOPIC 6: SHANTI Bill and other key bills (December 2025)

### Kya hua?

- Parliament passed the **SHANTI Bill, 2025**, dealing with **nuclear/radiological safety and liability**, and giving stronger statutory backing to the nuclear regulator.
- The **Central Excise (Amendment) Bill, 2025** was passed to further align excise provisions with **GST** and modern tax administration.
- The Government introduced the **“Health Security se National Security Cess Bill, 2025”** to levy a cess for strengthening health infrastructure.
- The **Insurance Laws (Amendment) Bill, 2025** was also introduced, aimed at liberalising the insurance sector.

### SSC exam points:

- **SHANTI Bill** – linked with **nuclear and radiological safety/liability**.
- **Central Excise (Amendment) Bill, 2025** – rationalises excise laws, GST-aligned.
- **Health Security se National Security Cess Bill** – new cess for health infrastructure.
- **Insurance Laws (Amendment) Bill, 2025** – insurance sector reforms.

**Memory Trick – “SHANTI in nuclear safety”**

> Yaad rakho: **“SHANTI Bill se nuclear ko shanti”** – nuclear/radiological safety and liability.
