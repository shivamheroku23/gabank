[Rajasthan Specific Current Affairs]  
## TOPIC 1: ₹9 lakh crore investment MoUs ka December 2025 tak grounding

### Kya hua?

- Rajasthan sarkar ne lakshya rakha hai ki **December 2025** tak lagbhag **₹9 lakh crore ke investment MoUs** ka “grounding” (zameen par shuruat) ki jaaye.
- Ye MoUs mukhyataḥ **Rising Rajasthan Global Investors Summit 2024 (Jaipur)** me sign huye the.
- 2025 ke ant tak, rajya ne lagbhag **₹7 lakh crore ke projects** ko ground karne ki disha me kaam badhaya.
- Vibhinna vibhaagon ko spasht nirdesh diya gaya hai ki approvals, land allotment aur clearances ko fast-track kiya jaye.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- MoUs ka ₹9 lakh crore lakshya – **industrialisation, jobs aur infrastructure** se juda hai.
- RAS/Patwari/Police exams me puchha ja sakta hai ki **December 2025 ka investment grounding target kitna rakha gaya** aur kaunse summit se sambandhit hai.
- Ye Rajasthan ko **“investment-friendly state”** ke roop me position karta hai.

**Memory Trick – “9 lakh = nav nivesh lahar”**

> Yaad rakho **“₹9 lakh crore = nav (9) nivesh lahar Rajasthan me”** – summit se aaye nivesh ki badi lehr.

## TOPIC 3: Rajasthan Logistics Policy 2025

### Kya hua?

- Rajasthan ne **Rajasthan Logistics Policy 2025** jari ki, jiska lakshya state ko **logistics aur warehousing hub** banana hai.
- Policy freight corridors, dry ports, multimodal parks, cold chains aur godaam suvidhaon par kendrit hai.
- Yeh policy **industrial areas, agriculture produce aur export clusters** ko better connectivity dene ke liye bani hai.
- Private sector investment ko attract karne ke liye incentives aur single-window system ka pravadhan hai.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Policy ka naam, varsh (2025) aur uddeshya – **raste/rail/warehouse sudhar** – seedha fact-based prashn ban sakta hai.
- Logistics se **export, MSME, agriculture value chain** directly judi hai, jo exams me hota hai.
- Rajasthan ki geographical position (landlocked, border state) ke liye logistics bahut mahatvapurn hai.

**Memory Trick – “Logistics 2025 = ‘Raste‑raaste Rajasthan’”**

> Yaad rakho **“Raste‑raaste Rajasthan”** – logistics policy se saamaan ka flow har raste par sudhrega.

## TOPIC 5: Rajasthan Krishi Subsidy 2025 – drip/sprinkler sinchai

### Kya hua?

- **Rajasthan Krishi Subsidy 2025** ke tahat kisanon ko **drip aur sprinkler sinchai pranali** par uchch anudan diya ja raha hai.
- Chhote aur simant kisanon ko lagbhag **70% tak subsidy**, jabki aam shreni ke kisanon ko lagbhag **50–55% tak** sahayata mil sakti hai.
- Lakshya hai ki parampraagat flood irrigation ki jagah **paani-bachao, fasal-bachao micro-irrigation** ka prayog badhe.
- Online aavedan, field verification aur installation ke baad subsidy seedhe kisan ke bank khate me transfer hoti hai.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Subsidy pratishat, target (drip/sprinkler) aur **paani sanrakshan** – sabhi prashn ke roop me aa sakte hain.
- Agriculture, irrigation aur jal-sansadhan Rajasthan exams ka core topic hai.
- Scheme ka connecting point – **jal ki kami wala rajya** hote hue Rajasthan ka focus water-efficient irrigation par hai.

**Memory Trick – “70–50 rule: chhote 70, bade 50”**

> Yaad rakho **“chhote kisan 70, aam kisan 50+”** – micro-irrigation subsidy ke approximate slab.

## TOPIC 6: Mukhya Mantri Ayushman Arogya (MAA) Yojana – portability

### Kya hua?

- Rajasthan ki **Mukhya Mantri Ayushman Arogya (MAA) Yojana** ko 2025 me majboot banaya gaya, jisme **portability** provision joda gaya.
- Ab labharthi **Rajasthan ke bahar bhi kuch nirdharit hospitalon me cashless upchar** le sakte hain.
- April se November 2025 ke beech, hazaron rogi is portability ka labh le chuke hain.
- Scheme ka uddeshya **universal health coverage** ki or badhna aur door-daraz ke mareezon ko suraksha dena hai.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- MAA Yojana Rajasthan ki **state health insurance** vyavastha ka mahatvapurn hissa hai.
- Exam me puchha ja sakta hai – scheme ka naam, rajya, aur “portability” ka arth kya hai.
- Health schemes aksar **RAS mains, Police, Patwari** ke samanya gyan me repeat hote hain.

**Memory Trick – “MAA ke saath kahin bhi ilaj”**

> Socho **“MAA Yojana = Maa jaisi suraksha, kahin bhi ilaj”** – portability se rajya ke bahar bhi upchar.

### Kya hua?

- Rajya sarkar ne December 2025 se **12 welfare schemes** ke bhugtan ko tez karte hue labharthiyon ke **bank khaton me seedha payment** shuru kiya.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Social security, DBT, financial inclusion – ye sab **state welfare** ke key topics hain.
- Police, Patwari aur REET me yeh sawal “governance aur schemes” section se aa sakta hai.

**Memory Trick – “12 yojna, 1 seedha rasta – bank khata”**

> Yaad rakho **“12 yojnaon ka ek hi rasta – seedha bank khata”** – DBT ke through payment.

### Kya hua?

- RBI ki December 2025 MPC baithak me **repo rate 25 bps ghat kar 5.25%** ki gayi.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Repo rate aur policy stance ka seedha asar **Rajasthan ke banks, MSME credit, housing loans** par hota hai.
- State exams me pooch sakte hain – **repo kitna** aur stance kya, kyunki ye economy ka basic fact hai.
- Financial awareness section me RBI policy ki terms (repo, SDF, MSF) se related prashn nishchit hain.

**Memory Trick – “5–5–5 combo”**

> Yaad rakho **repo 5.25, SDF 5.00, MSF 5.50** – teenon ko **“5–5–5 combo”** ke roop me soch lo.

[International Current Affairs]  
## TOPIC 9: India–Oman Free Trade Agreement (FTA)

### Kya hua?

- Bharat ne **Oman** ke saath ek **Free Trade Agreement (FTA)** ko manzoori di, jo energy, petrochemicals, textiles, khadya utpad aur services par kendrit hai.
- Is se dono deshon ke beech **customs duty me kami** aur vyapar volume me vriddhi ki aasha hai.
- Oman Bharat ke liye **crude oil, gas aur fertiliser** ka mahatvapurn srot hai, saath hi yahan vishal bhartiyaa pravasi samudaay hai.
- FTA Indian Ocean kshetra me strategic aur economic sahyog ko aur mazboot karega.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Question aa sakta hai – **India–Oman FTA – partner desh kaun?** (Oman).
- FTA se jude sawal economy, geography (Gulf region), aur international trade section me aate hain.
- Rajasthan ke students ke liye bhi ye UPSC pattern ke GA ke roop me relevant hai.

**Memory Trick – “OMAN = OIL MAN”**

> Socho **Oman = Oil Man** – Gulf ka oil partner jiske saath India ka FTA hai.
