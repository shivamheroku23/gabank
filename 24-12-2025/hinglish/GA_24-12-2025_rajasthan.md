## TOPIC 1: ₹9 lakh crore investment MoUs ka December 2025 tak grounding

### Kya hua?

- Rajasthan sarkar ka lakshya hai ki **December 2025** tak lagbhag **₹9 lakh crore ke investment MoUs** ko ground kiya ja sake.
- Ye MoUs mainly **Rising Rajasthan Global Investors Summit 2024 (Jaipur)** me sign huye the.
- 2025 ke end tak lagbhag **₹7 lakh crore ke projects** ke groundwork par जोर diya gaya hai.
- Vibhaagon ko clear timeline ke saath **land allotment, approvals aur clearances** fast-track karne ke nirdesh diye gaye.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- ₹9 lakh crore ka investment target Rajasthan ke **industrialisation, jobs aur infrastructure boom** ka symbol hai.
- RAS/Patwari/Police me direct sawal aa sakta hai – **December 2025 tak investment grounding ka target kitna?**
- State ko “**investment-friendly Rajasthan**” ke narrative se jodta hai.

**Memory Trick – “9 lakh = nav nav nivesh ki lehr”**

> Socho **“₹9 lakh crore = nav (9) nav nivesh ki lehr Rajasthan me”** – badi investment wave.

## TOPIC 3: Rajasthan Logistics Policy 2025

### Kya hua?

- State ne **Rajasthan Logistics Policy 2025** announce ki, jiska goal hai Rajasthan ko **logistics aur warehousing hub** banana.
- Policy freight corridors, dry ports, multimodal logistics parks, cold chain aur warehousing infrastucture par focus karti hai.
- Agricultural mandis, industrial clusters aur export hubs ko better connectivity dene ka target hai.
- Policy me private investment ke liye incentives, single-window clearance aur ease of doing business measures shamil hain.

- Policy ka naam + year – **Rajasthan Logistics Policy 2025** – direct fact hai.
- Logistics se **exports, MSMEs, agriculture value chain aur employment** sab connect hote hain.
- Rajasthan jaise landlocked state ke liye logistics reform ek important exam topic hai.

**Memory Trick – “RLP 2025 = Raste, Lorry, Port 2025”**

## TOPIC 5: Rajasthan Krishi Subsidy 2025 – drip/sprinkler sinchai

### Kya hua?

- **Rajasthan Krishi Subsidy 2025** ke under kisanon ko **drip aur sprinkler irrigation systems** par bada subsidy support diya ja raha hai.
- Chhote aur simant kisanon ke liye lagbhag **70% tak subsidy**, jabki general category kisanon ke liye lagbhag **50–55%** subsidy mil sakti hai.
- Target hai flood irrigation ki jagah **paani bachane wali micro-irrigation** ko promote karna.
- Online application, field verification aur installation ke baad subsidy **direct bank account** me transfer hoti hai.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Percentage slab (70% vs 50–55%) se direct MCQ ban sakta hai.
- Water-scarce state hone ke nate Rajasthan me **efficient irrigation schemes** hamesha important hain.
- Agriculture + water management Rajasthan exams ka most favourite mix hai.

**Memory Trick – “Chhote ko 70, bade ko 50+”**

> Yaad rakho **“chhote kisan 70%, bade kisan 50+% subsidy”** – ek simple 70–50 formula.

## TOPIC 6: Mukhya Mantri Ayushman Arogya (MAA) Yojana – portability

### Kya hua?

- Rajasthan ki **Mukhya Mantri Ayushman Arogya (MAA) Yojana** me 2025 ke dauran **portability feature** ko aur strong banaya gaya.
- Iske through Rajasthan ke labharthi **state ke bahar ke selected hospitals** me bhi cashless treatment le sakte hain.
- April–November 2025 period me hazaron patients ne is portability ka actual benefit liya.
- Yojana ka aim **universal health coverage** aur emergency ke samay rajya ke bahar bhi suraksha dena hai.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- MAA Yojana Rajasthan ka **flagship health insurance model** hai.
- Question aa sakta hai – MAA kis rajya ki scheme hai, ya portability ka base idea kya hai.
- Health schemes Police, RAS, Patwari, REET sab exams ke social sector section me repeat hoti rehti hain.

**Memory Trick – “MAA ke saath kahin bhi ilaj”**

> Bas yaad rakho **“MAA Yojana = Maa jaisi suraksha, kahin bhi ilaj”** – portability ka core idea.

## TOPIC 7: 12 welfare yojnaon ka DBT se seedha bhugtan

### Kya hua?

- Rajasthan sarkar ne December 2025 se **12 labhdayak welfare schemes** ke bhugtan ko **Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)** ke through seedhe bank khaton se jod diya.
- Inme vriddha pension, vidhwa sahayata, divyang sahayata jaise social security benefits shamil hain.
- DBT se middlemen role kam hota hai, leakages control hote hain aur payment time par pahunchta hai.
- Bank branches, BC points aur digital platforms ko is poori chain me integrate kiya gaya.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Ek seedha factual sawal – **“Rajasthan ne kitni schemes ke bhugtan ko DBT se joda?”** (12).
- DBT, financial inclusion aur social security – RAS/Police/Patwari ke liye core topics hain.
- State welfare model ka modernisation yahan clearly dikhai deta hai.

**Memory Trick – “12 yojna, ek rasta – bank khata”**

> Yaad rakho **“12 yojnaon ka ek hi rasta – seedha bank khata”** – DBT model.

## TOPIC 8: RBI December 2025 Monetary Policy – Rajasthan ke context me

### Kya hua?

- RBI MPC ne December 2025 me **repo rate 25 bps cut kar ke 5.25%** kar diya, SDF 5.00% aur MSF/Bank Rate 5.50% rakha.
- Policy stance **neutral** rakha gaya, matlab aage ki moves data-dependent rahengi.
- RBI ne G-Sec OMO purchases aur dollar–rupee swap ke through liquidity aur rupee stability dono par kaam kiya.
- Low inflation ke bawajood GDP growth forecast lagbhag **7.3%** ke aas‑paas maintained rakha gaya.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Repo rate aur stance har type ke competitive exams me basic macro question hai.
- Rajasthan ke MSME, housing, agriculture loans ke interest rate par iska indirect prabhav padhta hai.
- Economy section me **RBI + policy rates** se related sawal lagbhag fix hote hain.

**Memory Trick – “5–5–5 combo”**

> Yaad rakho **repo 5.25, SDF 5.00, MSF 5.50** – ek **“5–5–5 combo”**, stance neutral.

[International Current Affairs]  
## TOPIC 9: India–Oman Free Trade Agreement (FTA)

### Kya hua?

- Bharat ne **Oman** ke saath ek **Free Trade Agreement** approve kiya jo **energy, petrochemicals, textiles, food products aur services** par focus karega.
- Dono taraf bahut se tariff lines par custom duty kam ya zero hone ki ummeed hai.
- Oman Bharat ke liye **crude oil, gas, fertiliser** ka key source hai, aur yahan large Indian diaspora kaam karta hai.
- FTA se Indian Ocean region me Bharat ka **strategic aur economic footprint** aur strong hoga.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- MCQ aa sakta hai – India ka latest FTA kis Gulf desh (Oman) ke saath hai?
- FTA topics economy + geography (Gulf region) + international relations ke section me aate hain.
- UPSC pattern follow karne wale Rajasthan exams me bhi yeh news relevant hai.

**Memory Trick – “OMAN = OIL MAN friend”**

> Socho **“Oman = Oil Man friend of India”** – oil partner jiske saath FTA hua.
