## TOPIC 1: RBI December 2025 Monetary Policy – 25 bps repo cut

### Kya hua?

- RBI ki Monetary Policy Committee (MPC) ki December 2025 meeting me **repo rate 25 bps cut karke 5.25%** kar diya gaya, stance **neutral** rakha gaya.
- **Standing Deposit Facility (SDF)** ko **5.00%** aur **MSF / Bank Rate** ko **5.50%** par set kiya gaya.
- RBI ne saath hi **G-Sec OMO purchases lagbhag ₹1 lakh crore** tak announce ki, taaki durable liquidity manage ho sake.
- Ek **USD 5 billion dollar–rupee buy/sell swap** bhi plan kiya gaya, jo FX liquidity aur rupee ko support karega.
- CPI inflation bahut niche aa chuki hai (2% lower band se bhi neeche), lekin real GDP growth forecast lagbhag **7.3%** par hold kiya gaya.
- (Official reference: RBI Monetary Policy Statement – December 2025)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Repo, SDF, MSF/Bank Rate aur stance – ye sab **direct prelims facts** hain, har banking exam me favourite.
- Ye policy dikhati hai ki RBI **low inflation + achhi growth** ke combo me thoda easing allow kar raha hai.
- OMO + FX swap combo se samajh aata hai ki RBI **liquidity, bond yields aur rupee** tino ko parallel handle karta hai.
- Loan EMIs, deposit rates, bond market – sab par iska asar aata hai, jo banking awareness ka core part hai.

### Memory Trick – “5–5–5 ka repo combo”

> Yaad rakho: **Repo 5.25, SDF 5.00, MSF/Bank Rate 5.50** – teenon ko mila ke bolo **“5–5–5 ka repo combo, stance neutral”**.

## TOPIC 2: Insurance Laws (Amendment) Bill, 2025 – naya reform push

### Kya hua?

- Sarkar ne Parliament me **Insurance Laws (Amendment) Bill, 2025** introduce kiya.
- Bill ka focus hai **capital norms ko flexible banana, composite licences allow karna** aur special insurance players ko entry dena.
- Isse life, general, health, micro insurance, niche segments me **naye domestic + foreign investors** aa sakte hain.
- Yeh pehle ke insurance reforms (FDI increase, 2021–22 amendments) ko aage badhata hai.
- Customer protection, solvency aur rural penetration ko parallel strong rakhne par zor diya gaya.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Banking/insurance exams me **latest financial sector bills** se 1–2 direct questions nikalte hi nikalte hain.
- Composite licence concept bancassurance, cross-selling aur product innovation se link ho jata hai.
- Banks ka insurance partners se business model directly impact hota hai.

### Memory Trick – “INSURE = INvite SURe investors”

> Socho **“Insurance Amendment = INvite SURe investors”** – bill ka main motive hai naya paisa aur nayi companies ko invite karna.

## TOPIC 3: India–Oman Free Trade Agreement (FTA)

### Kya hua?

- Bharat ne **Oman** ke saath ek **Free Trade Agreement (FTA)** approve kiya, jo **energy, petrochemicals, textiles, food products aur services** par focus karta hai.
- FTA ka objective hai dono deshon ke beech kai tariff lines par **custom duty kam ya zero** karna.
- Oman India ke liye **crude oil, LNG, fertilisers** ka important source hai aur yahan par bahut bada Indian diaspora काम karta hai.
- Is deal se bilateral trade, investments aur supply-chain integration Indian Ocean region me strong honge.
- Ye India ke FTA roadmap (UAE, Australia, etc.) ke saath ek aur important Gulf link hai.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- FTA se **exports–imports, BoP, forex inflows** me change aata hai – ye sab banking + economy section ka part hai.
- Oman ka role **energy security + remittances** dono me important hai.
- Question aa sakta hai: **“India ne recent FTA kis Gulf desh (Oman) ke saath sign kiya?”**

### Memory Trick – “OMAN = OIL + MARINE”

> Bas yaad rakho **“OMAN = OIL + MARINE partner”** – Gulf ka oil aur samundri trade dono me key FTA partner.

## TOPIC 4: National Frequency Allocation Plan (NFAP) 2025

### Kya hua?

- **Department of Telecommunications (DoT)** ne **National Frequency Allocation Plan (NFAP) 2025** release kiya.
- NFAP basically ek **spectrum allocation chart** hai jisme telecom, broadcasting, defence, satellite, navigation sab ke liye alag-alag bands define hote hain.
- 2025 version specially **5G/6G, satellite internet, critical communications, defence use** ko dhyan me rakhkar banaya gaya hai.
- Isse interference kam karne, spectrum ka efficient use aur ITU norms ke saath alignment ka target hai.
- Future spectrum auctions aur administrative allotments ke liye ye hi base document rahega.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Telecom sector ke spectrum policy decisions **telecom companies ke debt, ARPU, profitability** par direct impact daalte hain – jinko banks finance karte hain.
- Digital banking, UPI, fintech, data centres – sab reliable networks aur spectrum par depend karte hain.
- NFAP 2025 ka full-form + department easily MCQ me poocha ja sakta hai.

### Memory Trick – “NFAP = Network Frequency Allocation Plan”

> NFAP ko yaad rakhne ke liye bolo **“Network Frequency Allocation Plan 2025”** – pura network ka frequency map.

## TOPIC 5: Jal Jeevan Mission – Sujal Gaon ID linkage

### Kya hua?

- **Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM)** ke under har gaon ko ek **“Sujal Gaon ID”** diya ja raha hai jo ek unique identifier hai.
- Ab JJM ke funds release strongly **Sujal Gaon ID onboarding** se linked ho gaye – matlab jo gaon system me sahi data ke saath registered hoga, usi ka monitoring aur funding clear hogi.
- Is ID se har village ke liye **tap-water coverage, quality aur functionality** ka real-time record maintain kiya ja sakta hai.
- Isse Har Ghar Jal target ke saath-saath **data-based planning aur accountability** improve hoti hai.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- JJM ek flagship rural infra scheme hai – ye **financial inclusion, rural development** topics se connected hai.
- Better water supply se health improve hoti hai, jo indirectly **productivity aur creditworthiness** ko support karti hai.
- Rural branch officers ke liye aise IDs aur schemes samajhna zaroori hai.

### Memory Trick – “SUJAL = Smart Unique JAL ID”

> Yaad rakho **“SUJAL = Smart Unique JAL ID”** – village ke paani data ka smart ID.
