## TOPIC 1: National Consumer Day 2025

### Kya hua?

- Bharat me har saal **24 December** ko **National Consumer Day** observe kiya jata hai.
- Ye din **Consumer Protection Act, 1986** ko President ki assent milne ki yaad me manaya jata hai.
- 2025 me Government, NGOs aur consumer groups ne awareness programs kiye – rights, grievance redressal, e‑commerce safety par focus.
- Saath hi **Consumer Protection Act, 2019** ke naye provisions (e‑commerce, misleading ads etc.) par bhi jagrukta badhai gayi.

### SSC exam points:

- Date: **24 December – National Consumer Day**.
- Background: **Consumer Protection Act, 1986** (assent date).
- Act in force now: **Consumer Protection Act, 2019**.
- Focus: consumer rights, consumer courts, helplines.

**Memory Trick – “24 ko consumer ka score”**

> Yaad rakho **“24 ko consumer ka score”** – 24 December = National Consumer Day.

## TOPIC 2: DRDO Pinaka LRGR‑120 maiden test

### Kya hua?

- DRDO ne **Pinaka LRGR‑120 (Long Range Guided Rocket)** ka **maiden flight test** safalta se kiya.
- Test **Integrated Test Range (ITR), Chandipur, Odisha** se conduct hua.
- System ki range lagbhag **120 km** hai, jo existing Pinaka system ka advanced guided version hai.
- Ye Indian Army ke artillery ko high-precision, long-range firepower deta hai.

### SSC exam points:

- Organisation: **DRDO**.  
- System: **Pinaka LRGR‑120 – 120 km Long Range Guided Rocket**.  
- Test place: **ITR Chandipur, Odisha**.  

**Memory Trick – “Pinaka 120 = 1 rocket, 2 digits, 0 miss”**

> Socho **“1 rocket, 2 digits, 0 miss”** – 120 km Pinaka LRGR.

## TOPIC 3: National Frequency Allocation Plan (NFAP) 2025

### Kya hua?

- **Department of Telecommunications (DoT)** ne **NFAP 2025** release kiya.
- NFAP ek national document hai jo **radio frequency spectrum** ko alag‑alag services (telecom, broadcasting, defence, satellite, navigation etc.) me allocate karta hai.
- NFAP 2025 special focus **5G/6G networks, satellite broadband, critical communications** par rakhta hai.
- Ye plan India ke spectrum use ko **international ITU norms** ke saath align karta hai.

### SSC exam points:

- NFAP full form: **National Frequency Allocation Plan**.
- Department: **DoT, Ministry of Communications**.
- Use: **spectrum allocation** for different services.

**Memory Trick – “NFAP = Network Frequency Allocation Plan”**

> NFAP ko simple tareeke se bolo **“Network Frequency Allocation Plan”** – network frequencies ka map.

- Grih Mantralaya ne **IPS officer Rakesh Agarwal** ko **National Investigation Agency (NIA) ka Interim Director‑General** appoint kiya.
- Ye posting tab aayi jab pehle DG **Sadanand Vasant Date** ko unke state cadre me wapas bheja gaya.
- NIA desh ki **main anti‑terror investigation agency** hai jo scheduled offences ko handle karti hai.

### SSC exam points:

- Pad: **Interim DG, NIA – Rakesh Agarwal**.
- NIA HQ: **New Delhi**.
- NIA under: **Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA)**.

**Memory Trick – “RA–NIA link”**

> **RA‑NIA**: **RAkesh – NIA** DG, naam ko agency se jod ke yaad rakho.

## TOPIC 5: “Bhartiya Bhasha Utsav 2025”

### Kya hua?

- Sarkar ne **“Bhartiya Bhasha Utsav 2025”** observe kiya, jiska aim **Indian languages ko promote** karna hai.
- Ye initiative **NEP 2020** ke focus – **mother tongue, regional languages aur classical languages** – se linked hai.
- Schools, colleges aur universities me seminars, competitions, cultural events organize huye.

### SSC exam points:

- Event: **Bhartiya Bhasha Utsav 2025**.
- Linked policy: **National Education Policy (NEP) 2020**.
- Focus: **Indian languages promotion**.

**Memory Trick – “Bhasha Utsav = language festival”**

> Simple yaad rakho: **Bhartiya Bhasha Utsav = Indian language festival** under NEP 2020.

## TOPIC 6: SHANTI Bill aur December 2025 ke important bills

### Kya hua?

- Parliament ne **SHANTI Bill, 2025** pass kiya jo **nuclear/radiological safety aur liability** par focus karta hai.
- **Central Excise (Amendment) Bill, 2025** pass hua jo excise provisions ko **GST era** ke hisaab se update karta hai.
- Sarkar ne **“Health Security se National Security Cess Bill, 2025”** introduce kiya – health infrastructure ko majboot karne ke liye ek naye cess ka proposal.
- **Insurance Laws (Amendment) Bill, 2025** bhi introduce hua, jo insurance sector ko liberalise karne ki direction me step hai.

### SSC exam points:

- **SHANTI Bill** – nuclear aur radiological safety/liability se related.
- **Central Excise (Amendment) Bill 2025** – excise law rationalisation.
- **Health Security se National Security Cess Bill** – health infra ke liye naye cess ka proposal.
- **Insurance Laws Amendment Bill 2025** – insurance sector reforms.

**Memory Trick – “SHANTI se nuclear ko shanti”**

> Yaad rakho **“SHANTI Bill se nuclear sector ko shanti aur safety”** – nuclear safety bill.
