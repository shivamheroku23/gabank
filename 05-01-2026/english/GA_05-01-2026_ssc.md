## TOPIC 1: Payments Regulatory Board (PRB) – First Meeting

- On **5 January 2026**, in Mumbai, the new **Payments Regulatory Board (PRB)** held its first meeting.  
- PRB was created by amending the **Payment and Settlement Systems Act, 2007** in 2025,  
  replacing RBI’s old board **BPSS**.  
- Chairperson: **RBI Governor (Sanjay Malhotra)**.  

### SSC exam points

- PRB is the new regulator for **payment &amp; settlement systems** (UPI, NEFT/RTGS, cards, PPIs).  
- Act: **Payment and Settlement Systems Act, 2007**.  
- Replaced body: **BPSS**.

**Memory Trick – “PRB = Payments Rules Board”**

> PRB = **Payments Rules Board** –  
> the board that watches over rules for digital payments.

## TOPIC 2: Nutrient Based Subsidy (NBS) – Rabi 2025‑26

- The Cabinet approved **Nutrient Based Subsidy (NBS)** rates  
  for **Rabi 2025‑26 (Oct–Mar)** for P&amp;K fertilisers.  
- Per‑kg subsidy (approx):  
  - N – ₹43.02, P – ₹47.96, K – ₹2.38, S – ₹2.87.  

### SSC exam points

- NBS started on **1 April 2010** for P&amp;K fertilisers.  
- Aim: encourage **balanced fertiliser use** and reduce over‑dependence on urea.

**Memory Trick – “NBS = Nutrient Budgeting System”**

> NBS = **Nutrient Budgeting System** –  
> the government decides a budget per kg of N, P, K, S.

## TOPIC 3: ONGC–NSTFDC MoU – Tribal Education Support

- **ONGC** (Maharatna PSU) signed a **CSR MoU** with  
  **NSTFDC (National Scheduled Tribes Finance &amp; Development Corporation)** worth about **₹28 crore**.  
- Funds will be used for **Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRS)**  
  to improve infrastructure and digital facilities.

### SSC exam points

- NSTFDC – PSU under **Ministry of Tribal Affairs**, works for ST community finance &amp; development.  
- EMRS – residential schools for tribal students under Ministry of Tribal Affairs.

**Memory Trick – “ONGC’s oil – EMRS school”**

> ONGC’s money from oil is being used to improve **EMRS schools**.

## TOPIC 4: NITI Trade Watch – Trade Deficit with FTA Partners

- NITI Aayog’s **Trade Watch report** states that  
  in **April–June 2025**, India’s trade deficit with **FTA partner countries**  
  increased by about **59%** (YoY).  

### SSC exam points

- FTA = **Free Trade Agreement** – low/zero tariffs between partner countries.  
- Trade deficit = imports > exports (value terms).

**Memory Trick – “FTA opens gate, deficit walks in”**

> Free trade opens the gate;  
> if exports are weak, **deficit** walks in.

## TOPIC 5: Payments Vision 2028 – 4S Formula

- RBI’s draft **Payments Vision 2028**  
  sets targets for India’s digital payment ecosystem:  
  - better fraud control and cyber security,  
  - improved cross‑border payments,  
  - offline solutions for inclusion.  

### SSC exam points

- Vision 2028 is a policy document for **digital payments** –  
  linked with UPI, RuPay, IMPS, NEFT/RTGS, etc.

**Memory Trick – “4S: Safe, Secure, Smart, Swift”**

> Payments Vision 2028 = **4S** –  
> Safe, Secure, Smart, Swift payments.
