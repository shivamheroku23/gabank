## TOPIC 1: Digital Banking Directions 2026 – Internet/Mobile Banking ka Naya Rulebook

### Kya hua?

- **Reserve Bank of India (RBI)** ne 2025 ke end me ek consolidated **Digital Banking Directions** notify kiye jo **1 January 2026** se effective ho gaye.  
- Ye directions banks aur kuch urban co‑operative banks ke **internet banking, mobile banking, UPI apps, digital account opening, card management** jaise sabhi digital services ko cover karte hain.  
- Ab banks ko har digital service ke liye **clear, granular customer consent** lena hoga; charges ko standard format me disclose karna hoga aur customers ko **easy opt‑out / consent revoke** ka option dena hoga.  
- Cyber risk ke liye guidelines me **strong multi‑factor authentication, device binding, real‑time fraud monitoring, data protection** aur incident‑reporting timelines tighten kiye gaye.  
- RBI ne boards ko direct responsibility di hai ki wo **digital strategy, fintech partnerships, outsourcing, cyber audits** aur tech risk ko approve aur oversee karein.  

(Official context: RBI – planned master directions on digital banking and technology risk; Photo: representative RBI website image)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Digital banking aaj ke har bank exam ka core topic hai – ab **poora rulebook “Digital Banking Directions 2026”** ke naam se yaad rakhna hai.  
- Yeh consumer protection (consent, charges), **cyber-security, operational risk, outsourcing** jaise multiple syllabus themes ko link karta hai.  
- Mains/interview me aap isse example ke roop me use kar sakte ho ki RBI ka focus ab **“access ke saath‑saath safety & governance”** par bhi equally strong hai.

### Memory Trick – “2026 = Digital Directions”

> Yaad rakho: **“26 = DD” → 2026 me Digital Directions aaye – consent + cyber + board **D**uty.

## TOPIC 2: Dormant Accounts Cleanup – Paisa DEA Fund me

### Kya hua?

- 2026 se RBI ke push ke baad banks ko **inactive aur dormant accounts** ko actively manage karne ke liye kaha gaya hai.  
- Definition clarify:  
  - **Inactive account** – lagbhag **12 mahine** tak customer‑initiated transaction nahi hua.  
  - **Dormant account** – lagatar **2 saal** tak koi customer transaction nahi hua.  
- Aise old dormant/zero‑balance accounts me cyber fraud, money laundering aur identity misuse ka risk hota hai; isliye banks ko **customers se contact, KYC update** aur phir bhi response na mile to policy ke mutabik **closure** ki direction di ja rahi hai.  
- Account close hone ke baad paisa seedha **Depositor Education and Awareness (DEA) Fund** me transfer hota hai jo RBI maintain karta hai – customer ya legal heir baad me bhi claim kar sakta hai, lekin process formal proofs ke saath hoga.  

(Official context: RBI guidelines on inoperative accounts & unclaimed deposits; Photo: https://bayhundredseniorcenter.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/Untitled-design-3.jpg)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Prelims level pe seedha sawal aa sakta hai: **inactive vs dormant**, kitne time baad, aur paisa **DEA Fund** me jata hai ya nahi.  
- Ye topic financial stability + consumer protection dono se जुड़ा hai – balance sheet me **unclaimed deposits**, fraud risk, database cleanup.  
- Interview me isko aap **“Jan Dhan se Jan Upyog”** ke shift ke example ke roop me use kar sakte ho – sirf account kholna nahi, active aur secure rakhna bhi important.

### Memory Trick – “12 mahine inactive, 2 saal dormant, paisa DEA”

> Code: **“1 saal chup = inactive, 2 saal chup = dormant, phir paisa DEA Fund ke naam.”**

## TOPIC 3: Crypto KYC Tight – Selfie, Location, Bank Check

### Kya hua?

- **Financial Intelligence Unit – India (FIU‑India)** ne crypto exchanges ke users ke liye **naye KYC/AML norms** issue kiye hain.  
- Ab onboarding ke time **live selfie with liveness detection** mandatory hai – matlab system check karega ki real insaan camera ke saamne hai, koi static photo ya deepfake nahi.  
- Saath me **geo‑tagging (location + IP)** aur **penny‑drop bank verification** se har account ko ek verified bank account aur PAN se link kiya jayega.  
- Framework ka goal hai ki virtual digital assets ke transactions **traceable aur accountable** ho jaayen aur **money laundering / terror financing** ka misuse kam ho.  

(Official context: FIU‑India advisory on Virtual Digital Assets under PMLA; Photo: https://www.insightsonindia.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/Terror-Financing.png)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Crypto ab officially **PMLA/FIU reporting** ka part hai – ye compliance, risk management aur FATF standards ke context me important hai.  
- Banks jo crypto exchanges ko **settlement accounts, payment gateways, cards** provide karte hain, unke liye customer risk assessment me yeh norms critical hain.  
- GA me pooch sakte hain ki **crypto KYC norms kis body ne laye – FIU‑India, not RBI/SEBI**.

### Memory Trick – “3‑step crypto KYC: Selfie, Geo, Bank”

> Ek line: **“Crypto user = live selfie + location on + bank account link, tabhi trading.”**

## TOPIC 4: Payment Aggregators & Digital Lending – Fintech Pipes Safai

### Kya hua?

- RBI ne **September 2025** me revised **Payment Aggregator (PA) Master Directions** issue kiye, jinka major compliance phase **2026** me chal raha hai.  
- Ye norms **offline POS/QR + cross‑border payment aggregation** ko cover karte hain aur **escrow account design, settlement timelines, merchant due‑diligence, data localisation** ko kaafi strict banate hain.  
- Saath hi RBI ke **Digital Lending Directions 2025** bhi 2026 me fully implement ho rahe hain – jisme loan apps ko **all‑inclusive charges, annualised interest, recovery practices** clearly dikhane padenge aur fund flow **direct borrower–RE bank accounts** ke beech hoga.  
- Regulated entity (bank/NBFC) ko hi full responsibility leni hogi – chahe loan sourcing kisi **third‑party app** ya fintech ke through kyon na ho.

(Official context: RBI master directions on Payment Aggregators & Digital Lending – https://www.rbi.org.in; Photo: https://st.etb2bimg.com/Themes/Release/theme4/images/default.jpg)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Payment Aggregators aur digital lenders UPI, cards, BNPL, instant loan apps ke centre me hain – ye **merchant acquiring + retail credit** ke question me directly aayega.  
- MCQ se ye test ho sakta hai ki **PAs ko kaun regulate karta hai, escrow account kya hota hai, loan fund flow kis ke beech hota hai**.  
- Descriptive answers me isko example bana sakte ho ki RBI **fintech innovation ko allow karte hue bhi regulatory arbitrage close** kar raha hai.

### Memory Trick – “PA + DL = clean pipes for paisa”

> Socho: **payments aur loans ke saare digital pipes pe RBI ne ‘filter’ laga diya – wahi PA & Digital Lending Directions.**

## TOPIC 5: 1 January 2026 ke Financial Resets – PAN–Aadhaar, Credit Scores & 8th Pay Commission

### Kya hua?

- **1 January 2026** se India me kai important financial reforms effective hue:  
  - **PAN–Aadhaar linking** practically mandatory ho gaya – non‑linked PAN ko **inoperative** treat kiya ja sakta hai, jisse tax filing, refunds aur high‑value banking transactions impact honge.  
  - **Credit bureaus** ab customer data ko **weekly** basis par refresh karenge, jisse repayments ya defaults jaldi credit score me reflect honge.  
  - Naye **co‑lending guidelines** me har lender ko loan ka kam se kam **10% apni book par rakhna** hoga – “skin in the game” ensure karne ke liye.  
  - Saath hi **8th Pay Commission** 1 Jan 2026 se implement maana ja raha hai, jisse central employees aur pensioners ki income badhegi aur demand boost mil sakta hai.  

(Official context: summary reports on Jan 2026 financial rule changes – NDTV etc.; Photo: https://c.ndtvimg.com/2025-12/9s7hnk6o_money_625x300_30_December_25.jpg)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- PAN–Aadhaar linking se **KYC, income‑tax, high‑value transactions** sab me direct effect hai – ye ek evergreen objective question hai.  
- Weekly credit updates risk models ko zyada **real‑time** banayenge – lending, provisioning aur pricing par impact.  
- 8th Pay Commission se **CASA deposits, retail loan demand (housing, vehicle, personal)** me uptick ke arguments mains/interview me use kiye ja sakte hain.

### Memory Trick – “1‑1‑26: PAN link, pay hike, fast score, 10% risk share”

> Code bana lo: **“1‑1‑26 = PAN link + 8th Pay + weekly score + 10% co‑lending stake.”**
