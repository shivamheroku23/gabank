## TOPIC 4: Orobanche (Margoja) – Rajasthan ke Mustard Kheton ka Parasite

### Kya hua?

- **Orobanche aegyptiaca (Margoja)** naam ka ek **root‑parasitic weed** north‑west Rajasthan ke **mustard fields** me tezi se फैल raha hai.  
- Ye weed host plant ke roots se chipak kar paani, carbon aur nutrients choos leta hai, jis se mustard plants **yellow, weak aur stunted** ho jaate hain.  
- Iske seeds itne chhote hote hain ki soil me **saalon–saalon tak (20 years tak) viable** reh sakte hain; irrigation water, tools, mitti movement se wide area me फैल jaate hain.  
- Farmer ko vaakya problem tab dikhti hai jab pehle irrigation ke baad weed ground se bahar nikalne lagta hai – tab tak kaafi crop loss ho chuka hota hai.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Direct ek-liner: **Orobanche (Margoja) → mustard crop → Rajasthan &amp; Haryana** – ye fact yaad rakho.  
- Agriculture, oilseeds aur Rajasthan economy ke context me ye ek important constraint/farm‑problem hai.  
- RAS mains me plant protection, extension services aur climate‑resilient farming ke jawab me example ke roop me quote kar sakte ho.

**Memory Trick – “Margoja = mustard ka underground chor”**

> Visualisation: **mustard ke roots par chipka ek chhota sa chor (weed)** jo plant ka sara saar choos raha hai.
