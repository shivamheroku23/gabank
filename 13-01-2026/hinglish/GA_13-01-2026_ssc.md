## TOPIC 1: Smartphones par Nayi Cyber-Security Rules – Source Code Tak Access

### Kya hua?

- Reports ke mutabik Government of India ek aise regulatory framework par kaam kar raha hai jisme **smartphone manufacturers ko apna source code ya equivalent audit access dena pad sakta hai**.  
- Concern ye hai ki foreign phones ke OS/apps me agar **hidden vulnerabilities, backdoors ya insecure components** hon to national security aur data privacy dono risk me aa sakte hain.  
- Naya framework device‑level **cyber-security, secure supply chain aur domestic testing/certification** ko strong karega.  
- Ye move broader **data protection, cyber law aur digital infrastructure** reforms ka part hai.  

(Official/detailed context: Indian Express – school assembly headlines 13 Jan 2026; Photo: https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/01/school-assembly-news-headlines-january-13-2026.jpg?w=1600)

### SSC exam points:

- Proposal: **smartphone makers → source code / audit access dena** for cyber-security.  
- Aim: **national security + user data protection**.  
- Likely nodal ministry: **MeitY (Ministry of Electronics &amp; IT)**.  
- Topics link: **Digital India, cyber law, critical information infrastructure**.

**Memory Trick – “Phone ka code, desh ka shield”**

## TOPIC 2: Crypto KYC Tight – Live Selfie + Geo-Tagging + Bank Validation

### Kya hua?

- **FIU‑India (Financial Intelligence Unit – India)** ne crypto exchanges ke users ke liye **naye KYC/AML norms** roll‑out kiye.  
- Ab onboarding ke time **live selfie with liveness detection** compulsory hai – system dekh lega ki real insaan hai, koi photo upload nahi.  
- Saath me **geo‑tagging (location + IP)** aur **penny‑drop bank verification** se har trading account ko ek verified bank account aur PAN se joda jayega.  
- Objective hai ki crypto transactions **traceable aur accountable** ban jaayen aur **money laundering/terror financing** ka misuse kam ho.  

(Official context: FIU‑India crypto KYC framework under PMLA; Photo: https://www.insightsonindia.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/Terror-Financing.png)

### SSC exam points:

- Implementing body → **FIU‑India (Finance Ministry)**, RBI nahi.  
- Key checks → **live selfie, geo‑tagging, PAN + secondary ID, penny‑drop bank check**.  
- Law base → **PMLA (Prevention of Money Laundering Act)**.  
- Global context → **FATF standards, terror funding control**.

**Memory Trick – “Selfie + Location + Bank = clean crypto”**

> Code: **“Crypto me entry tabhi – jab selfie live, location on, bank link ready.”**

## TOPIC 3: OrbitAid ka AyulSAT – Space Me Satellite Refuelling

### Kya hua?

- Chennai‑based Indian startup **OrbitAid Aerospace** ne **AyulSAT** naam ka 25‑kg satellite design kiya hai jo **in‑orbit satellite refuelling** ka demo karega.  
- AyulSAT ko **ISRO ke PSLV‑C62** mission se launch kiya gaya; ye ek tanker‑cum‑target spacecraft hai jisme **fuel, power aur data transfer** test honge.  
- Ye **SIDRP (Standard Interface for Docking and Refuelling Port)** use karta hai, jisse future me ek chaser satellite aakar isse dock karke real refuelling kar sake.  
- Successful demo ka matlab hoga ki satellites ko **orbit me hi refuel karke unki life extend** ki ja sakti hai, space debris bhi kam ho sakta hai.  

(Official context: OrbitAid AyulSAT coverage; Photo: https://www.insightsonindia.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/OrbitAids-AyulSAT.png)

### SSC exam points:

- AyulSAT → **Indian startup (OrbitAid)** ka satellite for **in‑orbit refuelling demo**.  
- Launch → **PSLV‑C62, ISRO**.  
- Aim → satellite life extension, **on‑orbit servicing and space economy**.  
- Interface → **SIDRP** docking/refuelling port.

**Memory Trick – “Ayul = आयु (life) + SAT = satellite ka life badhane wala mission”**

## TOPIC 4: Orobanche (Margoja) – Mustard Ka Hidden Parasite

### Kya hua?

- **Orobanche aegyptiaca**, jise local language me **Margoja** kehte hain, Rajasthan aur Haryana ke **mustard fields** me tezi se फैल raha hai.  
- Ye ek **root-parasitic weed** hai jo host plant ke roots se chipak kar paani aur nutrients chura leta hai; crop upar se weak, yellow aur stunted dikhne lagta hai.  
- Iske microscopic seeds soil me **20 saal tak viable** reh sakte hain, aur irrigation water, tools, wind se door‑door tak फैल jaate hain.  
- Pehla irrigation milte hi seeds germinate karke mustard roots se attach ho jaate hain – farmers ko jab pata chalta hai tab tak kaafi damage ho chuka hota hai.  

(Official context: reports on Orobanche infestation; Photo: https://www.insightsonindia.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/Orobanche-aegyptiaca.jpg)

### SSC exam points:

- Crop → mainly **mustard** (oilseed).  
- States → **Rajasthan &amp; Haryana**; north‑west India.  
- Nature → **root‑parasitic flowering weed**, fungus/insect nahi.  
- Effect → yield loss, **edible oil self‑reliance goal** par threat.

**Memory Trick – “Margoja = mustard ka underground vampire”**

> Socho: **mustard ke roots par ek chhota sa vampire chipka hai jo juice पी raha hai – wahi Margoja.**
