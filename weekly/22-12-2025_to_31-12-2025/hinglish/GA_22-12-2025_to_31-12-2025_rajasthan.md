## TOPIC 1: Shekhawati Haveli Conservation Yojana – 662 Rang-Birangi Haveliyan

### Kya hua?

- December 2025 coverage me **Shekhawati region** (Jhunjhunu, Sikar, Churu) ki historical haveliyon ke liye special **“Shekhawati Haveli Conservation Yojana”** highlight hui.  
- Plan ke under kareeb **662 purani haveliyan** identify ki gayi hain jinke frescoes, wall‑paintings, jharokhe, jali‑kaam aur traditional architecture abhi bhi kaafi had tak preserved hain.  
- Aim: in haveliyon ko **light restoration** karke ek **heritage tourism circuit** se jodna, local logon ke liye employment create karna aur Shekhawati ki pehchaan – “open‑air art gallery” – ko bachaye rakhna.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Direct one‑liner: **Shekhawati Haveli Conservation – ~662 haveliyan – districts: Jhunjhunu, Sikar, Churu**.  
- Art & Culture + Tourism + Rural economy – teenon me ye ek powerful Rajasthan‑specific example hai.

**Memory Trick – “Shekhawati = 600+ colourful haveliyan”**

> Yaad rakho: **Shekhawati = 600–700 ke beech rang‑birangi haveliyaan**,  
> exam me 662 isi range ka accurate number hai.
