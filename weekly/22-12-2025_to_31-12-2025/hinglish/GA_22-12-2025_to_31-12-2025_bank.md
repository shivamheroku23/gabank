## TOPIC 1: NFAP‑2025 – New Spectrum Blueprint for 5G/6G & Satellites

### Kya hua?

- **Department of Telecommunications (DoT)** ne **National Frequency Allocation Plan 2025 (NFAP‑2025)** issue kiya, jo **30 December 2025** se effective hua.  
- Ye document **8.3 kHz se 3000 GHz** tak ke radio‑frequency spectrum ko different radio services (mobile/IMT, satellite, broadcasting, defence, navigation, IoT, V2X, IFMC etc.) ke liye allocate karta hai.  
- NFAP‑2025 ne mid‑band **6,425–7,125 MHz** ko **IMT services (5G/5G‑Advanced/6G)** ke liye identify kiya, aur **Ka, Q, V bands** ko satellite broadband & communication ke liye reserve kiya.  
- Plan me **V2X communication, LEO/MEO satellites, In‑Flight & Maritime Connectivity (IFMC)** jaise Emerging use‑cases ko bhi support mila hai.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Telecom spectrum roadmap directly **mobile banking, UPI reliability, fintech apps, rural connectivity** pe impact daalta hai.  
- NFAP ka **full form + frequency range** factual MCQ ban sakte hain.  
- Mains me jab aap **Digital Public Infrastructure, 5G/6G, financial inclusion** par likhoge, to NFAP‑2025 ek strong policy reference hai.

### Memory Trick – “8.3 se 3000 – NFAP ka spectrum highway”

> Yaad rakho: **“NFAP‑2025 = 8.3 kHz se 3000 GHz tak का spectrum highway”** –  
> aur is highway me **6.425–7.125 GHz lane IMT/5G/6G ke naam** hai.

## TOPIC 2: RBI Trend & Progress – Frauds, ATMs, Jobs & Insurance

### Kya hua?

- RBI report ke hisaab se **H1 FY26** me bank fraud **cases** ki संख्या ghatkar lagbhag **5,092** ho gayi, lekin **fraud amount kareeb 30% badhkar ~₹21,515 crore** ho gaya – yani **kam cases, par bada size**.  
- **ATMs**: total ATMs thode ghatkar **~2.51 lakh** rahe, lekin **white‑label ATMs** ka number badh gaya; meanwhile bank branches ~**1.64 lakh** tak badh gayi, zyada growth rural/semi‑urban areas me thi.  
- **Employment**: banking workforce **17.87 lakh se 18.08 lakh** tak badhi; PSB staff me halka up‑tick, kuch private banks me rationalisation; **Small Finance Banks ne lagbhag 16,000 naye jobs** add kiye.  
- **Deposit insurance** (DICGC): lagbhag **97.6% bank accounts** ₹5 lakh ki limit ke andar **fully insured** hain, jabki value ke hisab se insured deposits ka share **~41–42%** around hai.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Fraud trend (case vs value), ATM pattern, SFB jobs – sab **system health** ke MCQ + mains points hai.  
- Deposit insurance coverage wale numbers se directly question aa sakta hai: **“Kitne % accounts ₹5 lakh ke andar fully insured hain?”**  
- Interviews me financial stability, consumer protection, DICGC roles explain karte waqt ye data helpful hai.

### Memory Trick – “5‑2‑1‑5 = fraud amount code”

> Fraud amount **₹21,515** ko yaad rakhne ke liye bolo: **“5‑2‑1‑5 pattern”** –  
> aur line add karo: **“fraud value up, fraud count down”**.

## TOPIC 3: RediffPay TPAP Licence & IDFC FIRST ‘Gaj’ Metal Credit Card

### Kya hua?

- **Rediff.com India Ltd** (Infibeam Avenues ki subsidiary) ko **NPCI** ne **TPAP (Third‑Party Application Provider)** authorisation diya, jiske basis par woh **RediffPay** naam ka UPI app launch kar raha hai.  
- RediffPay ko ek **financial wellness UPI app** ke roop me project kiya ja raha hai – jisme **UPI payments + investments (MF, FD, RD, equities) + pre‑approved credit line** ko ek hi interface me integrate kiya jayega (partner banks/NBFCs ke through).  
- **IDFC FIRST Bank** ne HNI customers ke liye invitation‑only **‘Gaj’ metal credit card** launch kiya.  
- ‘Gaj’ card features: **zero foreign exchange markup**, **interest‑free global ATM withdrawals (certain days/periods tak)**, airport lounge access, premium travel benefits, aur **₹12,500 + GST joining/annual fee** (high spend pe fee‑waiver options).

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- TPAP concept, UPI‑based credit, financial wellness – sab **digital banking + retail credit** ke favourite topics hai.  
- ‘Gaj’ card se FX markup, international ATM usage, HNI segment jaisi cheezen samjhayi ja sakti hain – jo retail banking theory me use hoti hain.  
- MCQs me pairing aa sakta hai: **RediffPay – TPAP UPI app; ‘Gaj’ – IDFC FIRST Bank ka invitation‑only metal card**.

### Memory Trick – “RediffPay = Wellness, Gaj = Wealth”

> Short line: **“RediffPay for wellness UPI, ‘Gaj’ for wealthy HNI card”** –  
> ek mass‑plus‑inclusion angle, ek HNI‑luxury angle.
