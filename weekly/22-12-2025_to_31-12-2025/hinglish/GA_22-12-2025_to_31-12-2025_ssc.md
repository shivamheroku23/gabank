## TOPIC 1: NFAP‑2025 – Spectrum Plan for 5G, 6G & Satellites

### Kya hua?

- **Department of Telecommunications (DoT)**, Ministry of Communications ne **National Frequency Allocation Plan 2025 (NFAP‑2025)** issue kiya, jo **30 December 2025** se lagu hua.  
- Document **8.3 kHz se 3000 GHz** tak ke spectrum ko different radio services – **mobile/IMT, satellite, broadcasting, defence, navigation, IoT, V2X, IFMC** – ke liye allocate karta hai.  
- NFAP‑2025 ne **6,425–7,125 MHz** band ko **IMT (5G/5G‑Advanced/6G)** ke liye identify kiya, aur **Ka, Q, V bands** ko satellite communication/broadband ke liye reserve kiya.  

### SSC exam points

- NFAP‑2025 – department: **DoT (Ministry of Communications)**.  
- Frequency range: **8.3 kHz–3000 GHz**; important for 5G/6G & satellite questions.  

**Memory Trick – “NFAP = National Frequency Allocation Plan 2025”**

> Full form yaad rakho: **NFAP‑2025** – telecom + satellite syllabus ke base me yehi document hai.

## TOPIC 2: DRDO – First Flight Test of Pinaka LRGR‑120

### Kya hua?

- **DRDO** ne **29 December 2025** ko **Pinaka Long Range Guided Rocket (LRGR‑120)** ka maiden flight test **Integrated Test Range (ITR), Chandipur, Odisha** se successfully conduct kiya.  
- Rocket ne around **120 km** ki range me high precision ke saath target ko hit kiya.  
- Test ek **in‑service Pinaka launcher** se kiya gaya, jo launcher compatibility aur system flexibility dikhata hai.  
- Design lead: **ARDE (Armament Research & Development Establishment)**; supporting labs: **HEMRL, DRDL, RCI**.

### SSC exam points

- System: **Pinaka LRGR‑120**; range: approx **120 km**.  
- Test site: **ITR, Chandipur, Odisha**; agency: **DRDO**.  

**Memory Trick – “Pinaka 120 = 120 km from Odisha”**

> Imagine: **Pinaka rocket Odisha se 120 km door target ko strike kar raha hai** –  
> naam ke saath range aur jagah dono fit ho jayenge.

## TOPIC 3: HAL Dhruv‑NG – Next-Generation Civil Helicopter

### Kya hua?

- **Union Civil Aviation Minister Ram Mohan Naidu** ne **HAL, Bengaluru (Karnataka)** me indigenous **Dhruv‑NG (Next Generation)** civil helicopter ka inauguration kiya aur first flight flag‑off ki.  
- Dhruv‑NG ek **5.5‑tonne, light twin‑engine, multi‑role civil helicopter** hai – Advanced Light Helicopter (ALH) ka upgraded civil avatar.  
- Max take‑off weight: **5,500 kg**; capacity: **around 14 passengers**; max speed ~**285 km/h**; range ~**630 km**.  
- Roles: **VIP transport, air ambulance, offshore operations, law‑enforcement, disaster relief, search & rescue**.

### SSC exam points

- Manufacturer: **Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL)**; location: **Bengaluru, Karnataka**.  
- Name: **Dhruv‑NG** = Dhruv ka **Next Generation** civil version.  

**Memory Trick – “Dhruv‑NG = Naye Generation ka chopper”**

> Dhruv + NG sochoge to turant click hoga: **Next Generation helicopter, Made in India**.

## TOPIC 4: Anti-Terror Conference 2025 & NIA Databases

### Kya hua?

- **Union Home Minister Amit Shah** ne **New Delhi** me held **Anti-Terror Conference (ATC) 2025** ka inauguration kiya; organiser: **National Investigation Agency (NIA)**.  
- Do‑din ke is conference me terrorism & organised crime ke खिलाफ **strategy, coordination, technology‑use** par charcha hui.  
- Naye digital tools/databases launch huye:  
  - Updated **NIA Crime Manual**  
  - **Organized Crime Network Database (OCND)** – organised criminal networks ke mapping ke liye  
  - **Lost, Looted & Recovered Weapons Database (LLRWD)** – official हथियारon ki tracking aur recovery ke liye  

### SSC exam points

- Organiser: **NIA**; inaugurator: **Amit Shah (Home Minister)**; venue: **New Delhi**.  
- Databases: **OCND** (organised crime network) & **LLRWD** (weapons tracking).  

**Memory Trick – “OCND + LLRWD = NIA ki digital shields”**

> Socho: terror aur organised crime ke against **do digital ढालें** –  
> ek **OCND**, doosri **LLRWD**.
