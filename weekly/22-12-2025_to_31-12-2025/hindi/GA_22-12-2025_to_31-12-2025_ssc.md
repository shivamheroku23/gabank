## TOPIC 1: NFAP‑2025 – 5G, 6G aur Satellites ke liye Spectrum Plan

### क्या हुआ?

- **Department of Telecommunications (DoT), Ministry of Communications** ne **National Frequency Allocation Plan 2025 (NFAP‑2025)** जारी किया, jo **30 दिसंबर 2025** se लागू हुआ।  
- Document **8.3 kHz se 3000 GHz** tak ke spectrum ko different radio services – jaise **mobile/IMT, satellite, broadcasting, defence, navigation, IMT‑applications, IoT, V2X** – ke लिये allocate karta hai.  
- NFAP‑2025 ne **6,425–7,125 MHz** band ko **IMT (5G/5G‑Advanced/6G)** ke liye identify kiya, aur **Ka, Q, V bands** ko satellite communication/broadband ke liye reserve kiya।  

### SSC exam points

- Iss plan ko जारी karne wali body: **DoT (Ministry of Communications)**.  
- Frequency range: **8.3 kHz–3000 GHz** – important for 5G/6G & satellite questions.  

**Memory Trick – “NFAP = New Frequency Allocation Plan 2025”**

> Full form yaad rakho: **National Frequency Allocation Plan 2025** –  
> telecom + satellite syllabus ka base document.

## TOPIC 2: DRDO – Pinaka LRGR‑120 ka pehla Flight Test

### क्या हुआ?

- **DRDO** ne **29 दिसंबर 2025** ko **Pinaka Long Range Guided Rocket (LRGR‑120)** ka maiden flight test **Integrated Test Range (ITR), Chandipur, Odisha** se safalta se kiya।  
- Rocket ne approx **120 km** ki range tak target ko high precision ke saath hit kiya।  
- Test ek **in‑service Pinaka launcher** se kiya gaya, jis se system ki field compatibility aur launcher versatility prove hui।  
- Design lead: **Armament Research & Development Establishment (ARDE)**; support labs: **HEMRL, DRDL, RCI**.

### SSC exam points

- System: **Pinaka LRGR‑120**, range ~**120 km**.  
- Test site: **ITR, Chandipur, Odisha**; agency: **DRDO (ARDE + allied labs)**.  

**Memory Trick – “Pinaka 120 = 120 km from Odisha”**

> Socho: **Pinaka rocket Odisha se 120 km door target ko hit kar raha hai** –  
> naam ke saath range aur jagah yaad ho jayegi।

## TOPIC 3: HAL Dhruv‑NG – Next-Generation Civil Helicopter

### क्या हुआ?

- **Union Civil Aviation Minister Ram Mohan Naidu** ne **Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL), Bengaluru** me indigenous **Dhruv‑NG (Next Generation)** civil helicopter ka inauguration aur first flight flag‑off kiya।  
- Dhruv‑NG ek **5.5‑tonne light twin‑engine multi‑role civil helicopter** hai, jo Advanced Light Helicopter (ALH) ka upgraded civil version hai।  
- Max take‑off weight: **5,500 kg**; capacity: **lagbhag 14 passengers**; max speed ~**285 km/h**, range ~**630 km**.  
- Use: **VIP transport, air ambulance, offshore operations, law enforcement, disaster relief, search & rescue** आदि।

### SSC exam points

- Manufacturer: **HAL**, city: **Bengaluru (Karnataka)**.  
- Name: **Dhruv‑NG = Next Generation** civil helicopter, twin‑engine, multi‑role.  

**Memory Trick – “Dhruv‑NG = Naye Generation ka helicopter”**

> Dhruv ke saath bas socho: **NG = Next Generation** –  
> civil aviation + Make in India dono cover ho jate hain।

## TOPIC 4: Anti-Terror Conference 2025 & NIA Digital Databases

### क्या हुआ?

- **Union Home Minister Amit Shah** ne **New Delhi** me आयोजित **Anti-Terror Conference (ATC) 2025** ka उद्घाटन किया, jise **National Investigation Agency (NIA)** ne organise kiya।  
- Do‑divasi conference me terrorism aur organised crime se ladne ke liye **strategy, coordination aur technology‑use** par चर्चा hui।  
- Key digital tools/databases launch:  
  - Updated **NIA Crime Manual**  
  - **Organized Crime Network Database (OCND)** – organised criminals & network mapping ke liye  
  - **Lost, Looted & Recovered Weapons Database (LLRWD)** – official हथियारों की tracking aur recovery ke liye  

### SSC exam points

- Organiser: **NIA**; inaugurate: **Amit Shah (Home Minister)**; place: **New Delhi**.  
- Databases: **OCND** (organised crime network) & **LLRWD** (weapons tracking).  

**Memory Trick – “OCND + LLRWD = NIA ki 2 digital ढालें”**

> Terror & organised crime ke against **do digital shields** –  
> **OCND** aur **LLRWD**.
