## TOPIC 1: NFAP‑2025 – Spectrum Plan for 5G, 6G & Satellites

### Kya hua?

- **Department of Telecommunications (DoT)** ne **National Frequency Allocation Plan 2025 (NFAP‑2025)** जारी किया, जो **30 दिसंबर 2025** से लागू हुआ।  
- यह document **8.3 kHz से 3000 GHz** तक spectrum ko अलग‑अलग radio services (mobile, satellite, broadcasting, defence, navigation आदि) ke liye allocate karta hai.  
- NFAP‑2025 ne **6,425–7,125 MHz** band ko **IMT (5G/5G‑Advanced/6G)** ke liye identify kiya aur **Ka, Q, V bands** ko satellite services ke लिए allocate kiya.  

### SSC exam points:

- NFAP‑2025 – जारी करने वाला विभाग: **DoT (Ministry of Communications)**.  
- Frequency range: **8.3 kHz–3000 GHz**; important for **5G, 6G, satellite communication**.  

**Memory Trick – “NFAP = New Frequency Allocation Plan 2025”**

> Bas full form yaad rakho: **National Frequency Allocation Plan 2025** –  
> telecom + satellite ke syllabus ka base document.

## TOPIC 2: DRDO – First Flight Test of Pinaka LRGR‑120

### Kya hua?

- **DRDO** ne **29 दिसंबर 2025** ko **Pinaka Long Range Guided Rocket (LRGR‑120)** ka maiden flight test **Integrated Test Range (ITR), Chandipur, Odisha** se सफलतापूर्वक किया।  
- Rocket ne लगभग **120 km** tak target ko precision ke साथ hit kiya.  
- Test ek **in‑service Pinaka launcher** se kiya गया, jis se system ki compatibility aur launcher versatility proof hui.  
- Design lead: **Armament Research and Development Establishment (ARDE)**; support: **HEMRL, DRDL, RCI**.

### SSC exam points:

- System: **Pinaka LRGR‑120**; range ~**120 km**; test site: **Chandipur, Odisha**.  
- Agencies: **DRDO, ARDE, HEMRL, DRDL, RCI** – Defence & Sci/Tech ke favourtie acronyms.

**Memory Trick – “Pinaka 120 = Odisha 120 km”**

> Socho: **Pinaka rocket Odisha se 120 km दूर target ko hit kar raha hai** –  
> naam ke साथ range aur स्थान yaad ho जाता है।

## TOPIC 3: HAL Dhruv‑NG – Next-Generation Civil Helicopter

### Kya hua?

- **Union Civil Aviation Minister Ram Mohan Naidu** ne **HAL ke Bengaluru plant** me indigenous **Dhruv‑NG (Next Generation)** civil helicopter ka उद्घाटन aur first flight flag‑off kiya।  
- Dhruv‑NG ek **5.5‑tonne light twin‑engine multi‑role civil helicopter** hai, jo Advanced Light Helicopter (ALH) ka upgraded civil version hai।  
- Max take‑off weight: **5,500 kg**, capacity: **14 passengers**, max speed ~**285 km/h**, range ~**630 km**.  
- उपयोग: VIP transport, air ambulance, offshore operations, law enforcement, disaster relief, search & rescue आदि।

### SSC exam points:

- Manufacturer: **HAL**, city: **Bengaluru (Karnataka)**.  
- Name: **Dhruv‑NG** – next‑generation civil helicopter, twin‑engine, multi‑role.  

**Memory Trick – “Dhruv‑NG = Naye Generation ka helicopter”**

> Dhruv ke नाम के साथ बस **NG = Next Generation** जोड़ दो –  
> civil aviation + Make in India dono याद रहेंगे।

## TOPIC 4: Anti-Terror Conference 2025 & NIA Databases

### Kya hua?

- **Union Home Minister Amit Shah** ne **New Delhi** me आयोजित **Anti-Terror Conference (ATC) 2025** ka उद्घाटन kiya, jise **NIA** ne organise kiya।  
- इस दो‑दिवसीय conference me terrorism aur organised crime se लड़ने ke लिए strategies, coordination aur technology use par चर्चा हुई।  
- Key digital tools launch:  
  - Updated **NIA Crime Manual**  
  - **Organized Crime Network Database (OCND)**  
  - **Lost, Looted and Recovered Weapons Database (LLRWD)** – official हथियारों की tracking ke लिए।

### SSC exam points:

- Organiser: **NIA**, inauguration by **Amit Shah** (Home Minister).  
- Databases: **OCND** (organised crime network) & **LLRWD** (weapons tracking).  

**Memory Trick – “OCND + LLRWD = NIA ki 2 digital ढालें”**

> Terror aur organised crime se लड़ने के लिए **दो डिजिटल shield** –  
> **OCND** and **LLRWD**.
