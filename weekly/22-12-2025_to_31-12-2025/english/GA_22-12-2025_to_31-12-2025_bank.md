## TOPIC 1: NFAP‑2025 – New Spectrum Blueprint for 5G/6G & Satellites

### What happened?

- **Department of Telecommunications (DoT)** ने **National Frequency Allocation Plan 2025 (NFAP‑2025)** जारी किया, जो **30 दिसंबर 2025** से प्रभावी हुआ।  
- यह डॉक्युमेंट **8.3 kHz से 3000 GHz** तक के radio‑frequency spectrum ko different services ke liye allocate karta hai.  
- NFAP‑2025 ne mid‑band **6,425–7,125 MHz** ko **IMT (5G/5G‑Advanced/6G)** ke liye identify kiya, aur **Ka, Q, V bands** ko satellite services ke liye allocate kiya.  
- Plan V2X communication, LEO/MEO satellites, In‑Flight & Maritime Connectivity (IFMC) jaise emerging use‑cases ko bhi support karta hai.

### Why is it important for Bank exams?

- Telecom spectrum roadmap directly impacts **digital payments, mobile banking, UPI, fintech connectivity**.  
- Shows how policy aligns with **future 5G/6G use‑cases**, important for mains answers on **Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI)**.  
- NFAP is a favourite for **“full form + objective”** type questions.

### Memory Trick – “8.3 to 3000: NFAP highway”

> Yaad rakho: **NFAP‑2025 = 8.3 kHz se 3000 GHz tak ka spectrum highway**  
> jisme mid‑band 6.425–7.125 GHz 5G/6G ke liye reserved hai.

## TOPIC 2: RBI Trend & Progress – Frauds, ATMs, Employment & Insurance

### What happened?

- RBI report ke mutabik **bank fraud cases ki संख्या** H1 FY26 me **घटी** (करीब 5,092 cases), lekin **amount ~₹21,515 crore** tak **30% बढ़ गया** – fewer but larger frauds.  
- **ATMs**: कुल ATMs 2.51 lakh tak slightly **कम हुए**, lekin **white‑label ATMs बढ़े**; branches 1.64 lakh tak बढ़ीं, zyada tar rural/semi‑urban me.  
- **Employment**: Banking workforce 17.87 lakh se बढ़कर **18.08 lakh**; PSB staff thoda बढ़ा, private banks me हल्की कमी; **SFBs ne ~16,000 naye jobs** add kiye.  
- **Deposit insurance**: लगभग **97.6% accounts** ₹5 lakh limit ke अंदर fully insured, lekin insured deposits ka share lagbhag **41–42%** ke आसपास (value wise).

### Why is it important for Bank exams?

- Fraud amount vs counts, ATMs vs digital, SFB employment – sab **system‑level trend** questions ke liye useful points.  
- Deposit insurance coverage ka data MCQs me puucha ja sakta hai; interviews me **financial stability** pe discussion ke liye helpful.  

### Memory Trick – “5–2–1–5: Fraud amount ~₹21,515 crore”

> Fraud amount yaad rakhne ke liye **21,515** ko bolo **“5–2–1–5 pattern”** –  
> aur connect karo **fraud value ↑, fraud count ↓** se.

## TOPIC 3: RediffPay TPAP Licence & IDFC FIRST Bank ‘Gaj’ Metal Credit Card

### What happened?

- **Rediff.com India Ltd** (Infibeam Avenues ki subsidiary) ko **NPCI se TPAP (Third‑Party Application Provider) licence** mila; is basis par woh **RediffPay** UPI app launch kar raha hai.  
- RediffPay ko **financial wellness‑focused UPI app** ke रूप me brand kiya ja raha hai, jisme **UPI payments + investments (MF, FD, RD, equities) + pre‑approved credit line** integrated honge.  
- **IDFC FIRST Bank** ne exclusive invitation‑only **‘Gaj’ metal credit card** launch kiya – HNI customers ke liye premium product.  
- ‘Gaj’ card pe **zero FX markup**, **interest‑free global ATM withdrawals**, lounge access, travel benefits, aur **₹12,500 + GST joining/annual fee** (high spends pe waiver) diya gaya.

### Why is it important for Bank exams?

- TPAP concept, UPI credit limits, financial‑wellness apps – sab **digital payments + retail credit** ke favourite topics.  
- Premium cards (‘Gaj’) se **HNI segment, FX markup, global ATM usage** jaise concepts samjhaaye ja sakte hain.  
- MCQs me **RediffPay – TPAP – NPCI – Axis Bank PSP** jaise combinations aa sakte hain.

### Memory Trick – “RediffPay for Wellness, Gaj for Wealth”

> Simple line: **“RediffPay = wellness UPI, Gaj = wealth credit card”** –  
> ek mass + inclusion angle, ek HNI premium angle.
