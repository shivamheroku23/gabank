## TOPIC 1: Khelo India University Games 2025 – Pehli Baar Rajasthan Host

### Kya hua?

- **Khelo India University Games (KIUG) 2025** ka **5th edition** pehli baar **Rajasthan** me organise hua.  
- Dates: **24 November se 5 December 2025**, aur **7 cities** – Jaipur, Jodhpur, Udaipur, Kota, Ajmer, Bharatpur, Bikaner – ko host banaya gaya.  
- Total **24 sports** (23 medal events + 1 demonstration game – Kho‑Kho) include hue, desh bhar ki universities ne participate kiya.  
- Mascot **“Khamma Ghani”** (camel theme) aur anthem **“Padharo Mhaare Rajasthan, Jeeto Saara Hindustan”** special highlight rahe.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Direct fact: KIUG ka **5वां edition, pehli baar Rajasthan**, 7 host cities, 24 sports – RAS/Police/Patwari ke liye high‑yield.  
- Sports + youth policy ke questions me Rajasthan ka role highlight ho sakta hai.  
- Rajasthan ki kai universities ne medals jeete, jo descriptive answers me example ke roop me use ho sakte hain.

**Memory Trick – “5–7–24 Rajasthan Formula”**

> Yaad rakho: **5th edition, 7 cities, 24 games** –  
> ek sports yatra jo **Jaipur se Bikaner tak 7 stations** par rukti hai.

## TOPIC 2: Raj Bhavan se “Lok Bhavan” – Naam me Badlaav

### Kya hua?

- **1 December 2025** ko notification jari karke **Rajasthan Raj Bhavan** ka naam badal kar **“Lok Bhavan”** kar diya gaya.  
- Idea ye hai ki Raj Bhavan ko sirf power symbol nahi, balki **logon ke liye khula, jan‑samvad ka sthal** mana jaye.  
- Governor ne official order sign kiya, jisse ye naam parivartan lagu hua.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Polity/administration ke static type question: **Raj Bhavan = Lok Bhavan (Jaipur)**.  
- Governance reforms me “Lok Bhavan” ek symbol hai **people‑centric rajvyavastha** ka.

**Memory Trick – “Raj → Lok”**

> Simple line: **“Raj Bhavan ab Lok Bhavan – raj se janta tak”** –  
> iss change se poora concept yaad ho jata hai.

## TOPIC 3: Rajasthan Tourism Policy 2025 & Trade Promotion Policy 2025

### Kya hua?

- **3 December 2025** ko Cabinet ne do badi policies ko मंजूरी di:  
  - **Rajasthan Tourism Policy 2025** – astro‑tourism, adventure tourism, wellness, eco & heritage circuits, safety (CCTV, panic button), tourist assistant system.  
  - **Rajasthan Trade Promotion Policy 2025** – **10 lakh+ retail traders** aur MSMEs ko support, E‑commerce network se jodna, jobs create karna.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Tourism = Rajasthan ki pehchaan, isliye **Tourism Policy 2025** par one‑liners almost sure hain.  
- Trade Promotion Policy 2025: **retail vyapariyon, MSME, Ease of Doing Business** – economy/industry wale section me aayega.  

**Memory Trick – “T2‑2025: Tourism + Trade”**

> 3 December ko Rajasthan ne ek hi din **do T launch kiye** –  
> **Tourism Policy** aur **Trade Promotion Policy**.

## TOPIC 4: National Marwar Horse Show – Ransi, Jodhpur

### Kya hua?

- **3–4 December 2025** ke beech **Ransi gaon, Jodhpur** me **National Marwar Horse Show (Ashwa Mahakumbh)** organise hua.  
- Yahan **Marwari horse breed** ki best bloodlines, performance aur grooming dikhayi gayi; all‑India se breeders aaye.  
- Event ka uddeshya **Marwari ghodon ko national/international level par brand** karna tha.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Marwari ghoda Rajasthan ki iconic **horse breed** hai – art & culture + animal husbandry dono me favourite.  
- Location yaad rakhna: **Ransi, Jodhpur** – direct MCQ ban sakta hai.

**Memory Trick – “Marwar ka Ashwa Mela = Ransi”**

> Socho Marwar ke ghode **Ransi ke maidan** me ek bada “Ashwa Mahakumbh” laga rahe hain.

## TOPIC 5: G‑RAM Ji Law – 125 Din ka Rozgar

### Kya hua?

- December 2025 me rashtriya star par **“Viksit Bharat Guarantee for Rozgar and Aajeevika Mission – Grameen”** (short: **G‑RAM Ji**) par Rashtrapati ki mohar lagi.  
- Ye framework **MGNREGA‑type rural employment** ko restructure karke **100 din se badha kar 125 din** tak guaranteed kaam ka option deta hai.  
- Rajasthan jaise rajyon me, jahan Panchayati Raj aur grameen rozgar bahut important hai, is law ka direct impact hoga.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- RAS, Patwari, Police, REET me rural employment schemes par direct questions aate hain.  
- **G‑RAM Ji** ka full form + **125 din** ka fact easily poocha ja sakta hai.

**Memory Trick – “G‑RAM = Gaon ka Rozgar Mitra”**

> Dimag me ek character banao **“G‑RAM Ji”**, jiska kaam hai  
> har parivar ko **125 din ka rozgar** dilaana.
