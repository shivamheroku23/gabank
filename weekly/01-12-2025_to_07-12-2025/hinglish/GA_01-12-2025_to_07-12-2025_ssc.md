## TOPIC 2: Winter Session 2025 – Insurance & Atomic Energy Reforms

### Kya hua?

- **1 December 2025** se **Winter Session of Parliament** start hua.  
- Govt ne ek bada **insurance reform Bill** introduce kiya jise media ne “**big reset**” bola – aim: regulation simplify karna, private participation aur capitalisation badhana.  
- **Atomic Energy Bill, 2025** se civil nuclear sector ke kuch हिस्सों me **private/PPP participation** ka raasta khola gaya (space sector reforms jaisa model).

### SSC exam points:

- Winter Session = major reforms in **insurance** + **atomic energy**.  
- Atomic Energy Bill 2025 ka core idea: **civil nuclear projects me controlled private role**.  
- Static ke sath link: **IRDAI** (insurance regulator), **DAE / NPCIL** (nuclear).

**Memory Trick – “WINTER = INSURE + NUCLEAR”**

> Winter Session ko yaad rakho: **Insurance reset + Nuclear reforms**  
> dono ek hi package me aaye – **WINTER = INSURE + NUCLEAR**.

## TOPIC 3: New Earthquake Map – Himalaya Sabse High Risk

### Kya hua?

- Naye **seismic zonation map** me **poora Himalayan belt** (J&K, Himachal, Uttarakhand, Nepal border, North‑East) ko **highest earthquake‑risk zone** me daala gaya.  
- Map ne recommend kiya ki in areas me **stronger building codes, retrofitting, land use planning** compulsorily follow kiye jaye.  
- Plains / coastal regions comparatively lower risk me, lekin city‑wise **micro‑zonation** ki salah di gayi.

### SSC exam points:

- Geography + Disaster Management ke mix se question aa sakta hai – **Himalayas = highest seismic risk**.  
- India ke earthquake zones, Himalayan orogeny, plate boundaries – yeh sab static ke sath link hota hai.  

**Memory Trick – “Himalaya = High‑Quake Line”**

> Socho Himalaya ek **High‑Quake Line** hai –  
> pura mountain chain red colour se mark ho gaya highest‑risk zone me.

## TOPIC 4: India Internet Governance Forum (IIGF) 2025

### Kya hua?

- December 2025 me **India Internet Governance Forum (IIGF) 2025** organise hua.  
- Theme broadly **safe, trusted, open internet** tha – focus on **data protection, cyber abuse, child safety, AI regulation, digital economy**.  
- Govt, industry, academia, civil society – sab stakeholders ne participation kiya.

### SSC exam points:

- IIGF full form: **India Internet Governance Forum**.  
- Topic: **internet governance, data protection, cyber safety** – SSC ke new‑age polity + IT wale questions me aa sakta hai.

**Memory Trick – “IIGF = Internet India Governance Forum”**

> Letters se hi full form bana lo:  
> **I = Internet, I = India, G = Governance, F = Forum**.
