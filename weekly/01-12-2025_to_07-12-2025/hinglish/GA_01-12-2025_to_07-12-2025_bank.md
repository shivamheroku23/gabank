## TOPIC 1: RBI December Monetary Policy – Repo ab 5.25%

### Kya hua?

- **3–5 December 2025** ke MPC meeting me RBI ne **repo rate 0.25% (25 bps) cut** karke **5.25%** kar diya, stance broadly **neutral / data‑dependent** rakha.  
- Corridor me: **SDF ~5.00%**, **Repo 5.25%**, **MSF ~5.50%** – matlab beech me repo, niche SDF, upar MSF.  
- RBI ne long‑term liquidity ke liye **₹1 lakh crore ke aas‑paas OMOs** (G‑sec kharid) announce kiye, jo mid‑December se start hone wale the.  
- Saath hi **USD 5 billion buy/sell FX swap** announce kiya gaya – jisse rupee liquidity + FX market dono ko support mile.  
- Background: 2025 me **CPI inflation 1–2% zone** me aa chuka tha (October ~0.3%), jabki **FY26 GDP growth** ka projection ~**7.3%** tha – yani growth strong, inflation bahut soft.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Direct MCQ point: **Repo = 5.25% (Dec 2025 policy)**, aur easing cycle start ho chuki hai.  
- OMOs + swap = **system liquidity, G‑sec yields, cost of funds, lending rates** – sab pe impact, mains answers me link karna zaroori.  
- Interview me question aa sakta hai: “Low inflation ke time RBI ko growth support kaise karna chahiye?” – yahan ye policy perfect example hai.

### Memory Trick – “5–5–5 triangle”

> Dimag me ek triangle banao:  
> **SDF 5.00% (niche), Repo 5.25% (beech), MSF 5.50% (upar)** –  
> teenon 5 ke aas‑paas, isliye yaad ho jayega **5–5–5 corridor**.

## TOPIC 2: Banking Sector Turnaround – GNPA ~2.2%, PCR 93%+

### Kya hua?

- December 2025 ke Finance Ministry review aur RBI ke **Financial Stability Report** ne dikhaya ki Indian banking sector ne strong recovery kar li hai.  
- **Scheduled Commercial Banks ka GNPA ratio ~2.22%**, jabki **Public Sector Banks ka GNPA ~2.58%** tak aa gaya.  
- **Provision Coverage Ratio (PCR) ~93%** – matlab purane NPA par banks ne heavy provisioning build kar li.  
- Capital adequacy (CRAR) bhi **regulatory minimum se kaafi upar**, stress tests ne dikhaya ki even severe macro shocks me system overall stable rahega.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- GNPA/PCR jaise numbers **factual MCQs** ke liye favourite hote hain, aur mains me “banking health” answers ka base bante hain.  
- Low GNPA + high PCR = banks ke paas **naya lending karne, credit growth push karne** ke liye space hai.  
- Interview me aapko NPA resolution (IBC, write‑offs, recovery, provisioning) ka impact samjha kar batana hoga.

### Memory Trick – “2–2–93 formula”

> Bas ek line bolo: **GNPA ~2.2%, PSB ~2.6%, PCR ~93%** –  
> yaad rakhne ke liye bol sakte ho: **“2–2 GNPA, 93% cover, tension kam”**.

## TOPIC 3: Digital Payments – DIGIDHAN, CLUPI & “Hello! UPI”

### Kya hua?

- **DIGIDHAN Mission** ke under FY 2024‑25 me digital payment transactions **22,800+ crore** cross kar gaye, value ~**₹3,500 lakh crore** tak pahunch gayi.  
- NPCI/RBI ne **“Credit Line on UPI (CLUPI)”** ko push kiya – jisme **banks/NBFCs ek revolving credit line sanction karte hain jo UPI app se swipe ki tarah use ho sakti hai**.  
- Saath hi **“Hello! UPI”** jaise pilots se **voice‑based payment** possible hua – IVR / voice assistant ke through UPI pay, jisse non‑smartphone / senior citizens / visually impaired logon ko help mile.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- CLUPI = **UPI + small ticket credit** – ye pura ek naya retail credit product hai, MCQ + mains dono ke liye important.  
- Voice‑enabled UPI financial inclusion ko **language + accessibility level** par aage le jaata hai.  
- Banks ko **IT risk, product design, collections** sab adjust karne padenge – ye mains me analysis point ban sakta hai.

### Memory Trick – “UPI ka 3C: Cashless, Credit, Conversation”

> Pehle UPI ka matlab tha **Cashless**.  
> Ab usme add ho gaya **Credit Line** aur **Conversation (voice)** –  
> yaad rakhne ke liye bolo: **UPI = Cashless + Credit + Conversation**.

## TOPIC 4: Jan Dhan, DBT & “Your Money, Your Right”

### Kya hua?

- December 2025 tak **PM Jan Dhan Yojana** me **57 crore+ accounts** open ho chuke the, total deposits **₹2.8 lakh crore+** ke aas‑paas.  
- Govt ne emphasize kiya ki **PM‑KISAN, LPG subsidy, pensions, scholarships, health schemes** ka bada hissa ab **direct DBT se inhi accounts me** ja raha hai.  
- Parallelly **“Your Money, Your Right”** campaign se **unclaimed deposits, dormant EPF, chhode gaye financial assets** ko identify karke **₹4,500 crore+** paisa rightful owners ko wapas diya gaya.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- PMJDY + DBT ecosystem **financial inclusion ke sabse bade examples** hain – SSC/Bank dono me baar‑baar pooche jaate hain.  
- Unclaimed asset clean‑up se **customer awareness + fraud risk kam** hota hai, banks ke liability side pe clarity aati hai.  
- Interview me aap bata sakte ho ki Jan Dhan + DBT ne **subsidy leakage** kaise kam kiya.

### Memory Trick – “3Y campaign”

> 3Y yaad rakho: **Your Account (Jan Dhan), Your DBT, Your Money (unclaimed) back** –  
> December 2025 ko “**3Y inclusion month**” soch lo.
