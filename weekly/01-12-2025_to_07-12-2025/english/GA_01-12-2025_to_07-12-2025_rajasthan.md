## TOPIC 1: Khelo India University Games 2025 – Pehli Baar Rajasthan Mein

### Kya hua?

- **Khelo India University Games (KIUG) 2025** ka **5वां संस्करण** pehli baar **Rajasthan** mein organise hua.  
- Games ka ayojan **24 November se 5 December 2025** tak **7 shahron** – Jaipur, Jodhpur, Udaipur, Kota, Ajmer, Bharatpur, Bikaner – mein hua.  
- Kul **24 sports** (23 medal events + 1 demonstration game – Kho‑Kho) include kiye gaye; desh bhar ki universities ne participate kiya.  
- Mascot **“Khamma Ghani”** (camel‑themed) aur anthem **“Padharo Mhaare Rajasthan, Jeeto Saara Hindustan”** kaafi highlight rahe.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Pehli baar KIUG ki **host state = Rajasthan**, yaad rakhne layak direct fact.  
- **7 host cities, 24 sports, 5th edition** – RAS/Patwari/Police written + interview me pucha ja sakta hai.  
- Rajasthan ki universities (jaise **Maharaja Ganga Singh University, Bikaner** etc.) ne accha medal performance diya – youth & sports policy se linked.

**Memory Trick – “5–7–24 in Rajasthan”**

> KIUG **5th edition**, **7 cities**, **24 games** –  
> socho ek huge sports train **Jaipur se Bikaner tak 7 stations** par ruk rahi hai.

## TOPIC 2: Raj Bhavan ka Naya Naam – “Lok Bhavan”

### Kya hua?

- **1 December 2025** ko notification ke through **Rajasthan Raj Bhavan** ka naam badal kar **“Lok Bhavan”** kar diya gaya.  
- Is kadam ka uddeshya Raj Bhavan ko sirf “Governor House” ke roop me nahi, balki **jan‑samvad aur public outreach ke kendr** ke roop me dekhna hai.  
- Governor **Hari Babu Kambhampati (Bagde)** ne is badlav ko sign‑off kiya.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Direct static‑type fact: **Raj Bhavan → Lok Bhavan (Jaipur)**.  
- Governance & polity me **rajyapal, raj bhavan, jan‑sampark** jaise topics par question ban sakte hain.

**Memory Trick – “Raj se Lok”**

> Bas line yaad rakho: **“Raj Bhavan ab Lok Bhavan – shakti se seva ki taraf”**.

## TOPIC 3: Rajasthan Tourism Policy 2025 & Trade Promotion Policy 2025

### Kya hua?

- **3 December 2025** ko Rajasthan Cabinet ne ek saath do badi policies ko मंजूरी di:  
  - **Rajasthan Tourism Policy 2025** – integrated tourism infrastructure, astro‑tourism, wellness, heritage circuits, safety, CCTV, panic buttons, tourist assistants.  
  - **Rajasthan Trade Promotion Policy 2025** – laghu vyapariyon, MSMEs, retail traders ko organise karne, E‑commerce se jodne aur employment badhane par focus.  
- Trade Promotion Policy ka target **10 lakh se adhik retail vyapariyon** ko policy benefits dena hai.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Dono policies **service sector, tourism, MSME, employment** – sabhi Rajasthan exams ke favourite areas.  
- Tourism Policy 2025: **astro‑tourism, adventure, film tourism, religious circuits** – short one‑liners ban sakte hain.  
- Trade Promotion Policy: **10+ lakh retail traders**, MSME, Ease of Doing Business – economy section me pucha ja sakta hai.

**Memory Trick – “T&T 2025 = Tourism & Trade 2025”**

> 3 December ko socho Rajasthan ne ek saath do “T” launch kiye –  
> **Tourism Policy** + **Trade Promotion Policy**.

## TOPIC 4: National Marwar Horse Show (Ashwa Mahakumbh) – Ransi, Jodhpur

### Kya hua?

- **3–4 December 2025** ke beech **Ransi gaon, Jodhpur** me **National Marwar Horse Show (Ashwa Mahakumbh)** ka ayojan hua.  
- **Marwari ghodon** ki best breeds, performance aur grooming dikhane ke liye ye ek bade star ka show tha.  
- All India Marwari Horse Association ne is event ko organise kiya, jahan desh ke kai states se breeders aaye.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- **Marwari ghoda** Rajasthan ki prasiddh breed hai – agriculture, livestock, art & culture topics me favourite.  
- Location: **Ransi gaon, Jodhpur** + theme: **Marwar Ashwa Mahakumbh** – direct factual MCQs possible.  

**Memory Trick – “Marwar ka Ashwa Mahakumbh = Ransi, Jodhpur”**

> Socho Marwar ke ghode **Jodhpur ke Ransi maidan** me ek “Mahakumbh” kar rahe hain –  
> naam hi yaad rakhwa dega: **Marwar Horse Show, Ransi (Jodhpur)**.

[National Current Affairs]  
## TOPIC 5: G‑RAM Ji Law – Rozgar Dinon ka Vistar

### Kya hua?

- December 2025 me rashtriya star par **“Viksit Bharat Guarantee for Rozgar and Aajeevika Mission – Grameen”** (short name: **G‑RAM Ji**) par Rashtrapati ki mohar lagi.  
- Ye law **MGNREGA ko replace/reshape** karke **grameen parivaron ko 125 din ka guaranteed rozgar** dene ka framework banata hai (100 din se badhakar).  
- Rajasthan me bhi Panchayati Raj aur rural development schemes ke sath iska linkage important ho jayega.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- RAS/Patwari/REET me rozgar, MNREGA‑type schemes, **dinon ka vistar** bahut favourite topic hai.  
- G‑RAM Ji ka full form aur **125 din** ka fact directly poocha ja sakta hai; Rajasthan me iske implementation pe sawal aa sakte hain.

**Memory Trick – “G‑RAM = Gaon ka Rozgar Aapka Mitra”**

> Socho **G‑RAM Ji** gaon ka ek mitra jise 125 din tak kaam dilaane ki zimmedari mili ho.
