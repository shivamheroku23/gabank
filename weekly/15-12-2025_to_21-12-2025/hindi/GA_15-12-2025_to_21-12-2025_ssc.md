## TOPIC 1: DAC द्वारा लगभग ₹79,000 करोड़ की Defence Procurement मंज़ूर

### क्या हुआ?

- **Defence Acquisition Council (DAC)** – जिसकी अध्यक्षता रक्षा मंत्री **राजनाथ सिंह** करते हैं – ने लगभग **₹79,000 करोड़** की defence procurement proposals को मंज़ूरी दी।  
- ये खरीदें तीनों सेनाओं – **सेना, नौसेना, वायुसेना** – के लिए हैं और मुख्यतः **Buy (Indian – IDDM)** category के तहत हैं, यानी indigenous design, development & manufacturing पर जोर।  
- प्रमुख प्रणालियों में **loiter munitions, Low Level Light Weight Radars (LLLWRs), Pinaka MLRS के लिए long‑range guided rockets, integrated drone detection & interdiction systems, HF software‑defined radios** आदि शामिल हैं।  

### SSC exam points

- DAC = **Defence Acquisition Council**, chairperson = **Union Defence Minister**.  
- कुल राशि: करीब **₹79,000 करोड़**, category: **Buy (Indian – IDDM)** → Make in India defence.  
- Defence + Economy + Science & Tech ke cross‑questions me poocha ja sakta hai.

**Memory Trick – “79 for Shakti”**

> Yaad rakho: **₹79k crore** se forces ko “aur ज़्यादा Shakti” milegi –  
> DAC = Rajnath Singh in chair.

## TOPIC 2: INSV Kaundinya – Porbandar se Muscat tak pracheen samudri yatra

### क्या हुआ?

- **Indian Naval Sailing Vessel (INSV) Kaundinya**, ek **engine‑less, traditional stitched wooden boat**, ne apni पहली विदेशी यात्रा **Porbandar (Gujarat) se Muscat (Oman)** tak शुरू की।  
- Yeh jahaz **ancient stitched shipbuilding technique** se bana hai, jiska inspiration **Ajanta caves ki 5th century ship paintings** se liya gaya hai।  
- लगभग **1,400 km** ki samudri yatra me 18 navik (officers + sailors) sirf **oars aur square sails** ka use karke navigation kar rahe hain।  
- Mission ka उद्देश्य pracheen **India–Oman maritime trade routes** ko highlight karna aur Bharat ki **samudri virasat** ko duniya ke saamne rakhna hai।

### SSC exam points

- Route: **Porbandar (Gujarat) → Muscat (Oman)**.  
- Ship type: **engine‑less stitched sailing vessel** – Indian Navy + heritage dono se जुड़ा।  

**Memory Trick – “Kaundinya: Porbandar to Muscat”**

> Bas ek line yaad rakho: **“INSV Kaundinya sails from Porbandar to Muscat”**.

## TOPIC 3: Russia ka Soyuz‑2.1b – 52 Satellites ka launch

### क्या हुआ?

- **Russia** ne **Soyuz‑2.1b rocket + Fregat upper stage** ko **Vostochny Cosmodrome** (Amur region) se successfully launch kiya।  
- Mission me कुल **52 satellites** the – jinme se do **Aist‑2T Earth‑observation satellites** aur **50 छोटे satellites (Universat programme)** shamil hain।  
- Aist‑2T satellites **stereoscopic Earth imaging** aur **3D terrain models (≈2-metre resolution)** banane ke liye use hongi – disaster management, climate, land‑use studies me madad karegi।

### SSC exam points

- Launch vehicle: **Soyuz‑2.1b + Fregat**, launch site: **Vostochny Cosmodrome (Russia)**.  
- Payload: **52 satellites**, jinme **Aist‑2T** Earth‑observation pair important hai.  

**Memory Trick – “52 stars from Soyuz”**

> Line: **“Soyuz‑2.1b ne 52 satellites ko space me bheja”** –  
> Russia + Vostochny + Aist‑2T teenon ek frame me aa jaate hain।
