## TOPIC 1: Bandh Mahotsav & Sambhar Festival – Jal aur Paryavaran ka Celebration

### Kya hua?

- December 2025 me Rajasthan ne **Bandh Mahotsav 2025** ke naam se state‑wide campaign run kiya, jisme bade बाँध/जलाशayon ke aas‑paas **cultural programmes, padyatra, awareness rallies** organise ki gayi.  
- Isi period me **Sambhar Lake (Jaipur–Nagaur border)** par **Sambhar Festival** organise hua, jahan **bird‑watching, salt‑tourism tours, local handicraft stalls, folk dance & music** ka combo dikha.  
- Dono events ka main theme: **water conservation, wetlands protection, eco‑tourism promotion** aur local communities ke liye income opportunities.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Sambhar Lake pehle se **Ramsar Site** hai – ab festival angle ke saath **environment + tourism** dono ke liye important ho gayi.  
- Bandh Mahotsav ko aap mains me **“people‑centric water awareness model”** ke roop me quote kar sakte ho.

**Memory Trick – “Bandh + Sambhar = Jal + Jashn”**

> Line yaad rakho: **“Bandh Mahotsav aur Sambhar Festival – pani ka jashn, paryavaran ka message”**.

### Kya hua?
