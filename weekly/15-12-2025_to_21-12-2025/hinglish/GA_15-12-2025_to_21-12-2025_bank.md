## TOPIC 1: RBI – Microfinance Sector ko “Monitor Closely”

### Kya hua?

- RBI ki Trend & Progress analysis ne highlight kiya ki kuch **southern states (especially Karnataka, Tamil Nadu)** me **microfinance disbursements** ki growth regulatory caps aur SRO guardrails ki wajah se slow hui hai.  
- Microfinance SROs – **MFIN** aur **Sa‑Dhan** – ne **responsible lending norms, borrower‑wise total indebtedness limits, multiple lending caps** tight kiye hain.  
- March 2025 tak, kuch NBFC‑MFIs ko chhodkar, kaafi categories of lenders me **ya to contraction** ya **much slower growth** dekhi gayi.  
- RBI ne sab lenders ko advise kiya hai ki wo apne **microfinance portfolios me stress build‑up** ko bahut closely monitor karein aur risk‑based pricing/provisioning adopt karein.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Microfinance **financial inclusion, SHGs, JLGs, women borrowers** ka key instrument hai – mains me baar‑baar aata hai.  
- Past MFI crises ne dikhaya ki **over‑indebtedness + harsh recovery** systemic risk create kar sakte hain; RBI is baar pro‑active rehna chahta hai.  
- Interview me “responsible microfinance” ya “lending ethics” par question aaye to ye RBI view directly use kar sakte ho.

### Memory Trick – “Micro = Monitor”

> Short line: **“Microfinance = monitor, not just multiply”** –  
> growth bhi, lekin borrower safety ke saath.

## TOPIC 2: ATMs Down Thode, Digital & White-Label Up

### Kya hua?

- RBI data ke hisaab se 31 March 2025 tak India me total **ATMs ~2.53 lakh se girkar ~2.51 lakh** ho gaye – main reason: banks ne low‑usage **off‑site ATMs** ko rationalise / close kiya.  
- Saath hi **white‑label ATMs (WLAs)**, jo non‑bank operators chalate hain, lagbhag **34,600 se badhkar 36,200+** ho gaye – especially semi‑urban/rural pockets me.  
- Bank branches ki count bhi **~2.8%** grow karke **1.64 lakh** tak pahunchi; PSBs ne jyada branches **rural & semi‑urban** areas me open ki.  
- Digital payments (UPI, IMPS, AePS) ki growth fast rahi, jis se cash withdrawals ki relative demand kam hui, but physical access network (branches + WLAs) spread hota raha.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Ye data explain karta hai ki **ATMs thode kam hone** ka matlab **banking access kam hona nahi** hota – access model **WLAs + branches + digital** ke mix se evolve ho raha hai.  
- Mains me “Digital India and banking outreach” type question me ye ek strong case study hai.  
- Interview me jab poochhen “Future me branch/ATM vs digital ka balance kaisa hoga?”, to aap ye numbers quote kar sakte ho.

### Memory Trick – “ATMs thode kam, Access fir bhi zyada”

> Line: **“ATMs down, WLAs + branches + UPI up – form change, access same ya better.”**

## TOPIC 3: India Adds 5 Unicorns in 2025 – Fintech ka Dum

### Kya hua?

- **Tracxn India Tech Annual Funding Report 2025** ke mutabik 2025 me global level par **111 new unicorns** bane, jisme **India ne 5 naye unicorns** add kiye.  
- New Indian unicorns: **Navi** (financial services), **Juspay** (digital payments), **JSW One MSME** (B2B steel platform), **Jumbotail** (B2B grocery), **Raise Financial Services** (brokerage/wealth).  
- Average timeline: Indian start‑ups ko **Series A se unicorn banne** tak approx **5.8 saal** lag rahe hain, aur is period me kareeb **USD 149 million** raise karte hain.  
- Unicorn count ke basis par India abhi bhi **US aur China ke baad 3rd largest ecosystem** hai.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- In 5 unicorns me se kaafi **fintech / financial‑services** se linked hain – directly banking, payments, credit, wealth management space me.  
- Mains me start‑up finance, venture capital, fintech regulation par likhte waqt yeh fresh data bohot kaam aayega.  
- Interview me aspirant se pucha ja sakta hai “India ka unicorn scene kaha jaa raha hai?” – yahan se 5U in 2025 ka reference useful hai.

### Memory Trick – “5U in 2025”

> Simple code: **“2025 me India ne 5 U – 5 Unicorns – add kiye”** –  
> yaad rakhne ke liye bolo: **“Five U in Twenty‑Twenty‑Five”**.
