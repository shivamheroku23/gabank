## TOPIC 1: DAC clears ~₹79,000 Crore Defence Procurement

### Kya hua?

- **Defence Acquisition Council (DAC)**, jiske chair **Defence Minister Rajnath Singh** hain, ne lagbhag **₹79,000 crore** ke defence acquisition proposals ko मंजूरी di.  
- Ye acquisitions Army, Navy aur Air Force – teenon services ke liye hain, mainly **Buy (Indian – IDDM)** category me – yaani indigenous design, development & manufacturing ko push.  
- Approvals me **loiter munitions, Low Level Light Weight Radars (LLLWRs), Pinaka MLRS ke liye long‑range guided rockets, integrated drone detection & interdiction systems, HF Software Defined Radios, HALE RPAS leasing** jaise items shamil hain.

### SSC exam points

- DAC = **Defence Acquisition Council**, chair = **Union Defence Minister**.  
- Approx amount: **₹79,000 crore**, category: **Buy (Indian – IDDM)** → Make in India defence.  

**Memory Trick – “79 se badhegi Sena ki Shakti”**

> Line: **“DAC ne 79k crore ka Indian‑IDDM package pass kiya – Sena ki Shakti boost”**.

## TOPIC 2: INSV Kaundinya – Porbandar se Muscat tak Heritage Voyage

### Kya hua?

- **INSV Kaundinya** ek **engine‑less, traditional stitched sailing vessel** hai jo Indian Navy ne heritage mission ke liye banwaya hai.  
- Iski maiden overseas voyage **Porbandar (Gujarat) se Muscat (Oman)** tak start hui – approx **1,400 km** samudri yatra.  
- Boat ko purane **stitched shipbuilding techniques** se banaya gaya hai, inspiration **Ajanta caves ki pracheen ship paintings** se li gayi.  
- 18 sailors (officers + sailors) sirf **oars aur square sails** se navigation kar rahe hain, mission ka aim **ancient India–Oman trade routes** ko revive karna hai.

### SSC exam points

- Route: **Porbandar, Gujarat → Muscat, Oman**.  
- Type: **engine‑less stitched wooden sailing vessel** – Indian Navy + maritime heritage dono mix.  

**Memory Trick – “Kaundinya: Porbandar to Muscat”**

> Statement yaad rakho: **“INSV Kaundinya sails from Porbandar to Muscat”** –  
> exam me direct question ban sakta hai.

## TOPIC 3: Russia ka Soyuz‑2.1b – 52 Satellites ka Launch

### Kya hua?

- **Russia** ne **Soyuz‑2.1b rocket + Fregat upper stage** ko **Vostochny Cosmodrome (Amur region)** se launch kiya.  
- Mission me total **52 satellites** the – jinme se do **Aist‑2T Earth‑observation satellites** aur **50 small satellites (Universat programme)** include the.  
- Aist‑2T satellites **stereoscopic Earth imaging** aur **3D terrain models (~2m resolution)** banane ke liye design ki gayi hain – disaster management, climate studies, land‑use mapping me kaam aayengi.

### SSC exam points

- Launch vehicle: **Soyuz‑2.1b + Fregat**, launch site: **Vostochny Cosmodrome, Russia**.  
- Payload: **52 satellites**, including **Aist‑2T pair** (Earth‑observation).  

**Memory Trick – “52 stars from Soyuz”**

> Socho: **Soyuz‑2.1b ek shot me 52 chhote ‘sitare’ (satellites) space me bhej raha hai**.
