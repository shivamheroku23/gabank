## TOPIC 2: First PPP‑Model Medical Colleges – Dhar & Betul (MP)

### Kya hua?

- Govt ne **India ke first Public–Private Partnership (PPP) model medical colleges** approve kiye, jo **Madhya Pradesh** me ban rahe hain.  
- Pehle phase me colleges **Dhar** aur **Betul** – dono tribal‑dominated districts – me set up honge.  
- Model: state govt **land + district hospital** degi; private partner **college building, hostel, labs etc.** banayega/operate karega.  
- District hospital govt ke control me hi rahega lekin usko **teaching hospital** ke roop me use kiya jayega.

### SSC exam points

- State: **Madhya Pradesh**; districts: **Dhar & Betul**.  
- Objective: tribal/backward areas me **doctors aur medical seats** badhana, regional imbalance kam karna.  
- Health infra + PPP concept + NMC norms – mains me bhi kaam ka example.

**Memory Trick – “Doctor Dhar & Doctor Betul”**

> Imagine two doctors – **Dr. Dhar** and **Dr. Betul** –  
> jo MP ke PPP medical colleges se pass‑out hue.

## TOPIC 3: Supreme Court – Passport Renewal & Article 21

### Kya hua?

- Supreme Court ne ek important judgement me kaha ki **personal liberty state ki favour nahi, uska first obligation hai**.  
- Court ne hold kiya ki **sirf criminal case pending hone se kisi ka passport deny ya renew na karna valid reason nahi**.  
- Case: **Mahesh Agarwal vs Union of India** – petitioner criminal cases pending hone ke baawajood passport renewal chahte the.  
- Court ne clear kiya ki agar **trial court/passport court kuch conditions ke saath passport allow kare**, to passport authority us order ko respect kare – extra hurdles nahi laga sakti.

### SSC exam points

- Linked to **Article 21 – Right to Life & Personal Liberty**.  
- Passport authorities ko **judicial orders ke upar apna rule nahi thopna** chahiye.  
- Polity/Constitution section me fact + concept dono ke liye helpful.

**Memory Trick – “Passport by Right, not by Favour”**

> Line: **“Court order > Passport office; liberty = right, not mercy.”**
