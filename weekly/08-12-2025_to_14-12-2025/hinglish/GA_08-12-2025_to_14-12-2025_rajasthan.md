## TOPIC 1: Non-Resident Rajasthani (NRR) Policy 2025

### Kya hua?

- **3 December 2025** ki cabinet meeting me pehli baar **Non-Resident Rajasthani (NRR) Policy 2025** ko approve kiya gaya.  
- Policy ka aim hai ki dunia bhar me base lagbhag **60 lakh Pravasi Rajasthanis** ko state ke **investment, development, knowledge‑sharing aur philanthropy** se systematically joda ja sake.  
- Iske under **single‑window support, diaspora roadshows (Dubai, London, New York), special credit lines, sector‑wise liaison officers** jaise steps plan kiye gaye hain.  
- Target roughly **₹5,000 crore Pravasi investment** aur aane wale kuch saalon me **~40,000 jobs** create karna hai.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Ye **Rajasthan ki first dedicated Pravasi policy** hai – naam **“NRR Policy 2025”** aur year dono याद rakhna zaroori hai.  
- RAS/Police mains me diaspora + development likhte time ye ek fresh aur Rajasthan‑specific example banega.  

**Memory Trick – “NRR = Non-Resident Rajasthani = Naye Rajasthan ki Rishteydaari”**

> Socho: duniya bhar ke Rajasthanis ek thread se jure hain jiska naam hai **NRR Policy 2025**.

## TOPIC 2: Siliserh Lake – Alwar ki Fresh Ramsar Site

### Kya hua?

- **Alwar district** ki **Siliserh Lake** ko **12 December 2025** ko internationally important wetland, yani **Ramsar Site**, ke roop me recognise kiya gaya.  
- Ye ek **man‑made freshwater lake** hai jo 1845 me **Maharaja Vinay Singh** ne mainly **drinking water supply** ke liye banwayi thi.  
- Siliserh Sariska Tiger Reserve ke paas hai, isliye use kabhi‑kabhi **“Alwar ka Nandan Kanan”** bhi bola jata hai.  
- Lake **groundwater recharge, local water security, biodiversity aur migratory birds** ke liye bahut important hai.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- One-liner: **Siliserh Lake – Alwar – Ramsar Site (12 Dec 2025)**.  
- Rajasthan ki Ramsar sites list (Sambhar, Keoladeo, etc.) me ab Siliserh bhi add ho chuki hai – environment/tourism questions me aa sakta hai.

**Memory Trick – “Siliserh = Silver jaisi झील of Alwar”**

> Dimag me picture banao – **Alwar ki pahadiyon ke beech ek silver‑shine wali lake** – wahi Siliserh.

## TOPIC 3: Niraamay Rajasthan Abhiyan – Rog-Mukt, Fit Rajasthan

### Kya hua?

- December 2025 ke is week me **“Niraamay Rajasthan”** health campaign ko districts, blocks aur panchayats tak aggressively rollout kiya gaya.  
- Abhiyan me **health check‑up camps, yoga sessions, khel‑kood activities, nutrition awareness, BMI/sugar/BP checks** jaise programs include hain.  
- Government schools, colleges aur panchayat bhavans me **lifestyle diseases, anaemia, hypertension** par counselling aur screening chal rahi hai.  
- Overall goal: **“Rog‑mukt, fit Rajasthan”** ko ek mass movement banana.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Straight fact: **Niraamay Rajasthan** ek **state‑level health & fitness campaign** hai.  
- Social sector / health wale mains answers me yeh ek latest state initiative ke roop me quote kiya ja sakta hai.

**Memory Trick – “Niraamay = No‑Rog Rajasthan”**

> Line: **“Niraamay Rajasthan = No‑Rog Rajasthan”** –  
> naam hi mission clear kar deta hai.
