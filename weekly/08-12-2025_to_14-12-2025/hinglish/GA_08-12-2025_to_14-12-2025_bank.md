## TOPIC 1: Q4 Treasury Bills – ₹3.84 Lakh Crore Short-Term Borrowing

### Kya hua?

- **Government of India** ne announce kiya ki **FY26 Q4** me woh lagbhag **₹3.84 lakh crore** amount **Treasury Bills (T‑Bills)** ke through raise karegi.  
- Weekly T‑Bill auctions ka notified amount generally **₹29,000–35,000 crore** ke beech rahega, jaise Finance Ministry ke calendar me diya hai.  
- Ye borrowing pichhle saal ke same quarter (approx **₹3.94 lakh crore**) se thodi kam hai – yani short‑term borrowing profile controlled hai.  
- India me T‑Bills **91, 182, 364 din** ke liye issue hote hain, **zero‑coupon instruments** hote hain – discount par bechte hain aur maturity par **face value** pay karte hain.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Yeh dikhata hai ki Sarkar **short‑term cash mismatches** aur din‑prati‑din funding requirement ko **money market** ke through kaise manage karti hai.  
- T‑Bill yields directly **liquidity, short‑term interest rates, CDs/CPs ke pricing** ko impact karte hain – jo banks ke funding cost aur lending rates se जुड़ा hai.  
- Mains me jab aap **fiscal–monetary coordination** ya government debt structure likhoge, to ye example kaam aayega (T‑Bills vs long‑term G‑secs).

### Memory Trick – “3–8–4 for Q4”

> Ek line: **“Q4 ke T‑Bills ≈ 3‑8‑4 lakh crore”** –  
> bas Q4 aur 3‑8‑4 ko pair kar lo.

## TOPIC 2: RBI Trend & Progress – GNPA ~2.2%, Capital Strong

### Kya hua?

- RBI ne apni annual **“Report on Trend and Progress of Banking in India 2024‑25”** publish ki.  
- **Scheduled Commercial Banks (RRBs chhodkar)** ki consolidated balance sheet FY25 me lagbhag **11.2%** grow hui (FY24 me ~15.5%).  
- **Gross NPA ratio** March 2025 tak girkar approx **2.2%** aur September 2025 me **2.1% ke nazdeek** aa gaya – multi‑decadal low.  
- **Capital adequacy (CRAR)** around **17.4%**, **RoA ~1.4%**, **RoE ~13.5%** – banks profitable aur capital‑wise comfortable dikh rahe hain.  
- PSBs ka share overall SCB balance sheet me halkasa ghatkar **~54.9%** ho gaya, kyunki private banks aur SFBs faster grow kar rahe hain.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- GNPA, CRAR, RoA, RoE jaise numbers **direct factual MCQs** me aa sakte hain.  
- Ye report prove karti hai ki 2010s ke NPA crisis ke baad system ne **cleanup + provisioning + reforms** ke through strong turnaround kiya.  
- Interview/mains me credit growth, asset quality, PSB vs private role discuss karte waqt ye data handy hoga.

### Memory Trick – “2–2 NPAs, 17 CRAR, 1.4 RoA”

> Short code: **“2‑2 NPA, 17+ capital, 1.4 RoA”** –  
> teenon mil ke healthy system ka snapshot dete hain.

## TOPIC 3: Microfinance, ATMs & Jobs – Structure Change On

### Kya hua?

- RBI ke analysis ke mutabik **microfinance disbursement** kuch southern states (especially Karnataka, Tamil Nadu) me slow hua hai, kyunki SROs (MFIN, Sa‑Dhan) ne **responsible lending norms** tighten kiye hain.  
- Past MFI crises ko dhyan me rakhte hue ab **borrower‑wise total debt** aur multiple loans par close monitoring ho rahi hai.  
- **Total ATMs** March 2025 tak thoda kam hoke ~**2.51 lakh** ho gaye (pehle ~2.53 lakh) – main reason: banks ne low‑usage **off‑site ATMs** band kiye; lekin **white‑label ATMs ~36k+** tak badh gaye.  
- Isi period me **bank branches** ki संख्या lagbhag **2.8%** badhkar **1.64 lakh** hui; PSBs ne zyada branches **rural/semi‑urban** areas me kholi.  
- Employment side par **Small Finance Banks (SFBs)** ne lagbhag **16,000** nayi jobs add karke apni workforce ko ~**1.77 lakh** tak pahucha diya – important job creators ban gaye.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Microfinance financial inclusion ka core tool hai – **SHGs/JLGs, MFIs, NBFC‑MFIs** – mains me favourite area.  
- ATM data se ek key message milta hai: **“ATMs thode kam, par branches + WLAs + digital zyada”** – yani access ka pattern change ho raha hai, vanish nahi ho raha.  
- SFB job growth se related question interviews me aa sakta hai: “Kaun se type ke banks zyada employment create kar rahe hain?”

### Memory Trick – “Less ATMs, More Access”

> Yaad rakho: **“ATMs down thoda, lekin WLAs + branches + UPI up”** –  
> form change hua, access strong hi hai.
