## TOPIC 1: India – 4th-Largest Economy (Ahead of Japan)

### What happened?

- According to latest data, India has overtaken **Japan** to become the **4th-largest economy** in the world by nominal GDP.  
- India’s GDP is around **USD 4.18 trillion**; it now ranks after the US, China and Germany.  
- Several projections say India may surpass **Germany** by 2030 to become the **3rd-largest economy**.  

### SSC exam points:

- Rank: **4th-largest economy** (nominal GDP).  
- Country overtaken: **Japan**.  
- Future projection: may surpass **Germany** by around 2030.  

**Memory Trick – “JAI G – Japan Ahead? India, then Germany”**

> Remember **JAI G** – **J**apan, **A**head **I**ndia, next **G**ermany –  
> India has moved ahead of Japan; Germany is the next target.

## TOPIC 4: Pralay Missile – DRDO’s Short-Range Ballistic Missile

### What happened?

- **DRDO** successfully conducted a **salvo launch** of **two Pralay missiles** from  
  the **Integrated Test Range (ITR), Chandipur, Odisha**, using the same launcher in quick succession.  
- The test was part of user evaluation trials by the armed forces.  

### SSC exam points:

- Missile type: **Short-range, surface‑to‑surface ballistic missile**.  
- Developer: **DRDO (Defence Research and Development Organisation)**.  
- Test range: **ITR Chandipur, Odisha**.  

**Memory Trick – “Pralay comes from the ground”**

> Remember: **Pralay** is launched **from ground to ground (surface‑to‑surface)** –  
> “Pralay comes from the ground”.

## TOPIC 8: Bulgaria Adopts Euro &amp; EU CBAM

### What happened?

- **Bulgaria** adopted the **Euro (EUR)** as its official currency from **1 January 2026**;  
  earlier currency: **Bulgarian Lev (BGN)**.  
- The **European Union (EU)** implemented the **Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism (CBAM)**,  
  imposing a carbon‑linked levy on imports such as steel, aluminium, cement, fertilisers, chemicals and glass.  

### SSC exam points:

- Bulgaria:  
  - New currency: **Euro (EUR)**  
  - Old currency: **Bulgarian Lev (BGN)**  
- CBAM:  
  - Full form: **Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism**  
  - Implemented by: **European Union (EU)**  
  - Focus: **Environmental levy on carbon‑intensive imports**  

**Memory Trick – “LEV to EURO, Carbon Levy = CBAM”**

> One line: **“LEV left for EURO; EU’s carbon levy is CBAM.”**  
> Catches both the currency change and the carbon border tax.
