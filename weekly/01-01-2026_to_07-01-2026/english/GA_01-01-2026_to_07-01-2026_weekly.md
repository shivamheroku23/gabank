### 21. EU – Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism (CBAM) Implemented

- The **European Union (EU)** implemented the **Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism (CBAM)** from **1 January 2026**.  
- Imposes a **carbon-linked levy** on imports of **carbon-intensive products** like steel, aluminium, cement, fertilisers, chemicals, glass and power-related products.  

**Exam Points:**

- Mechanism: **CBAM**  
- Region: **European Union**  
- Impact: costlier access for carbon-intensive exports to the EU market.
