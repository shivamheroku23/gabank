## Detailed Topics – Bank Weekly (01–07 Jan 2026)

### TOPIC 1: RBI Digital Banking CAAL Framework (All Channels)

### Kya hua?

- RBI ne 1 January 2026 se ek **unified digital banking framework** लागू kiya jise shorthand me **CAAL** bola ja raha hai –  
  **C = Consent, A = Alerts, A = Access control, L = Liability**.  
- Ye framework **internet banking, mobile banking, UPI, USSD, card‑not‑present transactions, SMS banking** sab par लागू hai.  
- Banks/PSB/Private SB ko:  
  - explicit **customer consent logs** maintain karne,  
  - har महत्वपूर्ण activity par **real‑time alerts** bhejne,  
  - customers ko **login / device / channel level access control** dene,  
  - fraud/dispute ke cases me **clear liability sharing &amp; grievance timelines** follow karne honge.  

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- **Digital frauds**, phishing, unauthorised transaction ke cases me **customer protection** ka direct exam angle.  
- Mains me question aa sakta hai: “RBI CAAL framework se operational risk aur customer trust kaise improve hota hai?”  
- CAAL related MCQs bhi aa sakte hain – **effective date, full form, kaun‑kaun se channels cover**.  

### Memory Trick – “CAAL = Call mat karo, App me Consent + Alert + Access + Liability sab dikhega”

> Socho tum branch ko call kiye bina hi app me har cheez manage kar sakte ho –  
> **Consent set**, **Alerts on**, **Access control** apne हाथ me, aur **Liability** clear likhi – ye hi hai **CAAL**.

### TOPIC 2: BSBD “4F” Accounts, PAN–Aadhaar Linking &amp; Weekly Credit Score

### Kya hua?

- **Basic Savings Bank Deposit (BSBD)** accounts ke लिये banks ko **“4F” model** follow karne ko push kiya gaya –  
  Free basic services, Full digital access, Flexible deposits, Free from minimum balance.  
- **PAN–Aadhaar link na hone** par PAN ko **inoperative treat** kiya ja sakta hai –  
  ITR filing, new account, high‑value transactions, loans par direct असर.  
- Credit bureaus ko ab **har week** borrower data update karna mandatory hai (pehle zyada gap hota tha).  

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- BSBD &amp; no‑frills accounts = **financial inclusion**, PMJDY, small ticket deposits – CASA mobilisation.  
- PAN–Aadhaar linking = **KYC compliance**, AML/CFT norms, wilful default tracking.  
- Weekly bureau updates = **risk‑based pricing, faster score improvement ya downgrade**, retail loan portfolio ke लिये important.  

### Memory Trick – “4F = Free, Full, Flexible, Free‑from‑minimum”

> Socho ek BSBD account jo **Free services**, **Full digital**, **Flexible deposits** de aur **Free from minimum balance** ho –  
> 4 baar F bolo, yaad rahega: **4F**.

### TOPIC 4: RBI Trend &amp; Progress Report – GNPA Baseline 1.9%

### Kya hua?

- RBI ne **“Report on Trend and Progress of Banking in India 2024–25”** release ki.  
- Isme ye projection diya gaya ki **Scheduled Commercial Banks (SCBs)** ka **GNPA ratio**  
  baseline scenario me **September 2025 ke 2.1% se March 2027 tak 1.9%** tak gir sakta hai.  
- Stress scenarios me kuch banks ko **Capital Conservation Buffer (CCB)** ka हिस्सा use karna pad sakta hai.  

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- NPA numbers, asset‑quality trend – **Banking awareness** ke सबसे important static+current topics me se ek.  
- GNPA projection se yeh signal milta hai ki large part of system **healthy** hai,  
  lekin stress pockets (NBFCs, unsecured retail, agri) monitor karne होंगे.  
- CCB ka concept – Basel‑III capital norms, Tier‑1/Tier‑2 capital ke सवाल bana sakta hai.  

### Memory Trick – “21 se 19 – NPAs line par aa rahe”

> 2.1% (Sept 2025) → 1.9% (Mar 2027)  
> decimal hata ke socho: **21 se 19** – matlab GNPA धीरे‑धीरे **नीचे aane ka projection**.

### TOPIC 10: Bulgaria Adopts Euro &amp; EU CBAM – External Sector Impact

### Kya hua?

- **Bulgaria** ne **1 January 2026** se **Euro (EUR)** ko adopt karke eurozone join kiya;  
  pehle currency **Bulgarian Lev (BGN)** thi.  
- **European Union (EU)** ne **Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism (CBAM)** implement kiya –  
  steel, aluminium, cement, fertilisers, chemicals, glass, power products par **carbon‑linked levy**.  

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Eurozone membership se Bulgaria ka **sovereign risk profile** change hota hai –  
  Indian banks ke trade finance &amp; external exposure me yeh indirectly relevant.  
- CBAM se Indian exporters (steel, fertiliser companies) ka **cost of exporting to EU** badh sakta hai –  
  forex earnings, LC volumes, working capital demand par असर.  

### Memory Trick – “Bulgaria ne ‘LEV’ se EURO ली, EU ne ‘Carbon Levy’ CBAM liya”

> Ek line: **“LEV chhoda, EURO liya; EU ne Carbon Levy liya (CBAM)”** –  
> currency change + carbon border tax dono same week yaad rahenge.
