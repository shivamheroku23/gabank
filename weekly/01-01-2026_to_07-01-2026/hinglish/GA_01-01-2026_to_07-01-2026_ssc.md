## TOPIC 8: Bulgaria Adopts Euro &amp; EU CBAM

### Kya hua?

- **Bulgaria** ne **1 January 2026** se **Euro (EUR)** ko official currency bana liya;  
  pehle currency **Bulgarian Lev (BGN)** thi.  
- **European Union (EU)** ne **Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism (CBAM)** implement kiya,  
  jisme steel, aluminium, cement, fertilisers, chemicals, glass wagairah par carbon‑linked levy lagega.  

### SSC exam points:

- Bulgaria:  
  - New currency: **Euro (EUR)**,  
  - Old currency: **Bulgarian Lev (BGN)**.  
- CBAM:  
  - Full form: **Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism**.  
  - Implemented by: **European Union (EU)**.  
  - Focus: **carbon‑intensive imports par environmental tax**.  

**Memory Trick – “LEV se EURO, Carbon Levy se CBAM”**

> Ek line: **“LEV chhoda EURO liya, Carbon levy se CBAM liya”** –  
> Bulgaria+Euro aur EU+CBAM dono yaad ho jayenge.
