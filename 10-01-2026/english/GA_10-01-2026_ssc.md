## TOPIC 1: World Hindi Day (Vishwa Hindi Diwas) – 10 January

### What happened?

- Every year **10 January** is observed as **World Hindi Day / Vishwa Hindi Diwas**:  
  - To promote Hindi as a **global language**.  
  - Indian embassies, foreign universities and Pravasi Bharatiya organisations conduct events worldwide.  
- Background facts:  
  - The **first official World Hindi Day** was celebrated on **10 January 2006**, after an announcement by PM Dr Manmohan Singh.  
  - On **10 January 1975**, the **first World Hindi Conference (Vishwa Hindi Sammelan)** was held in **Nagpur**, with delegates from about 30 countries.  
  - Some sources also link **10 January 1949** with the **first reported use of Hindi in the UN General Assembly**.  
- Important distinction:  
  - **World Hindi Day – 10 January**, focuses on international promotion of Hindi.  
  - **National Hindi Diwas – 14 September**, marks adoption of Hindi as an official language inside India (Article 343).

### SSC exam points:

- World Hindi Day / Vishwa Hindi Diwas → **10 January**.  
- First official observance → **2006**.  
- First World Hindi Conference (Nagpur) → **10 January 1975**.  
- Hindi Diwas (National) → **14 September**.

**Memory Trick – “10 Jan for the world, 14 Sept for the nation”**

> Remember: **“10 Jan – World Hindi Day; 14 Sept – Hindi Diwas (India only).”**

## TOPIC 5: Airport Lounge Rules – SBI Card &amp; HDFC Bank (From 10 Jan 2026)

### What happened?

- **SBI Card – Domestic Lounge Programme (from 10 Jan 2026):**  
  - Selected SBI credit cards now follow a **revised domestic airport lounge programme**.  
  - Lounges are divided into **Set A** and **Set B**; access depends on the specific card.  
  - At entry, customers must **swipe/tap the card** at lounge POS:  
    - For Visa/RuPay: a **₹2 non‑refundable validation charge** is taken.  
    - For Mastercard: a **₹25 temporary hold** is placed but not debited.  
  - Each card has a **limited number of complimentary visits**; once these are used,  
    further lounge access is on **paid / walk‑in rates** only.  

- **HDFC Bank – Debit Card Lounge Access (from 10 Jan 2026):**  
  - **Direct debit card swipes for free lounge access are discontinued.**  
  - New process:  
    - You must first **spend at least ₹10,000 per quarter** on your HDFC debit card.  
    - After you qualify, the bank sends an **SMS/Email with a lounge voucher link** (within about 2 working days).  
    - You must **claim the voucher** and then present it for lounge entry.  
  - Complimentary visits per quarter depend on card type:  
    - Millennia/Times/GIGA → **1 visit**,  
    - Platinum/Business → **2 visits**,  
    - Infiniti → **4 visits**.

### SSC exam points:

- Effective date for the new rules → **10 January 2026**.  
- SBI Card validation charge on Visa/RuPay → **₹2**; Mastercard → **₹25 hold**.  
- HDFC debit lounge access now **linked to ₹10,000 quarterly spend + voucher system**.

**Memory Trick – “Lounge now demands spend, not just a card swipe”**

> Remember: **“From 10 Jan 2026, lounge = swipe + spend condition.”**
