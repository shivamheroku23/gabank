## TOPIC 1: World Hindi Day (Vishwa Hindi Diwas) – 10 January

### Kya hua?

- Har saal **10 January** ko **World Hindi Day / Vishwa Hindi Diwas** मनाया जाता hai:  
  - Hindi ko **विश्व स्तर पर बढ़ावा** dene ke लिये.  
  - Indian missions, विदेशी विश्वविद्यालय, Pravasi Bharatiya groups programmes organise karte hain.  
- Background facts:  
  - **पहला World Hindi Day** आधिकारिक रूप से **10 January 2006** se मनाया gaya (PM Dr. Manmohan Singh ne announce kiya).  
  - 10 January **1975** ko **पहला World Hindi Conference (Vishwa Hindi Sammelan)** Nagpur me hua, jisme ~30 deshon se delegates aaye.  
  - 10 January ko kuch sources **1949 me UNGA me Hindi ke पहली बार उपयोग** se bhi जोड़ते hain.  
- ध्यान रहे:  
  - **World Hindi Day – 10 January** (international focus).  
  - **National Hindi Diwas – 14 September** (India ke अंदर, official language adoption – Article 343).

### SSC exam points:

- World Hindi Day / Vishwa Hindi Diwas kab? → **10 January**.  
- पहली बार कब मनाया gaya? → **2006**.  
- पहला World Hindi Conference (Nagpur) kab? → **10 January 1975**.  
- Hindi Diwas (National) kab? → **14 September**.

**Memory Trick – “10 Jan duniya ke liye, 14 Sept desh ke liye”**

> Line yaad: **“10 Jan – World Hindi Day, 14 Sept – Hindi Diwas (India ke liye).”**

  - **FY 2025‑26 (FY26)** me India ka real GDP growth **~7.5%** ho sakta hai, “upward bias” ke saath.

### SSC exam points:

## TOPIC 5: Airport Lounge Rules – SBI Card &amp; HDFC Bank (10 Jan 2026 se)

### Kya hua?

- **SBI Card – Domestic Lounge Programme (10 Jan 2026 se):**  
  - Selected SBI credit cards ke लिये domestic airport lounges ko **Set A** aur **Set B** me divide kiya gaya.  
  - Entry ke लिये lounge par **card swipe/tap** karna zaroori;  
    - Visa/RuPay cards par **₹2 non‑refundable validation charge**,  
    - Mastercard par **₹25 ka temporary hold** (jo debit nahi hota).  
  - Har card ke लिये **limited complimentary visits** – quota cross hone par normal lounge charges dene padenge.  

- **HDFC Bank – Debit Card Lounge Access (10 Jan 2026 se):**  
  - **Debit card se direct swipe entry band** – ab lounge ke लिये pehle **quarter me ₹10,000 spend** condition.  
  - Condition meet hone par bank **SMS/Email se lounge voucher link** bhejta hai;  
    customer voucher claim karke lounge use kar sakta hai.  
  - Complimentary visits per quarter card type par depend (Millennia/Times/GIGA – 1, Platinum/Business – 2, Infiniti – 4).  

### SSC exam points:

- SBI Card ke naye lounge rules effective: **10 January 2026**.  
- Validation charges (Visa/RuPay) → **₹2**; Mastercard par **₹25 hold**.  
- HDFC debit lounge ke लिये **₹10,000 quarterly spend** condition + voucher system.

**Memory Trick – “Lounge ab free nahi, spend ke sath hi sahi”**

> Yaad rakho: **“10 Jan 2026 se lounge = swipe + spend condition.”**
