## TOPIC 1: Indian Banks – Q3 FY26 Credit vs Deposit Growth, CD Ratio

### Kya hua?

- Leading brokerages / research reports (Jan 2026 coverage) ke mutabik **Q3 FY26 (Oct–Dec 2025)** ke liye:  
  - Scheduled commercial banks ka **credit growth ~12% YoY** aur ~4.5% QoQ ke aas‑paas estimate hua.  
  - **Deposit growth ~9.7% YoY** ke kareeb – credit se kaafi kam.  
  - System ka **credit–deposit (CD) ratio ~81%+** level par pahunch gaya (all‑time high ke near).  
- Margin (NIM) pressure 2025 me RBI rate cuts ke baad aaya tha, par ab:  
  - Q3 me **NIMs largely stable / slight 5 bps tak decline** expect kiya gaya.  
  - Asset quality side par **GNPA low aur stable** dikhaayi de rahi hai (Fitch/SBI data ke context me).  

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Numbers jo yaad rakhne layak hain (approx):  
  - Credit growth: **~12%**, Deposit growth: **~9.7%**, CD ratio: **~81%+**.  
- Concept angle:  
  - Credit deposit se fast badhe to **funding gap** create hota hai →  
    banks ko deposits ke liye higher rate dena, ya market se borrowing karni pad sakti hai.  
  - High CD ratio matlab: system me **liquidity tight** ho sakti hai;  
    RBI ko OMOs / liquidity operations se support dena pad sakta hai.  
- Mains / interview:  
  - “Explain why slower deposit growth relative to credit growth is a key risk for Indian banks in 2026.”

### Memory Trick – “12 vs 10 – CD 81”

> Ek line: **“Credit 12, deposit 10 se kam (~9.7), CD 81 ke upar.”**  
> Is se teenon cheezein yaad: तेज credit, relatively धीमा deposit, high CD ratio.

## TOPIC 2: SBI Ecowrap – FY26 GDP 7.5% (Upward Bias)

### Kya hua?

- **State Bank of India – Economic Research Department** ne apni report **Ecowrap** me estimate diya:  
  - **FY 2025‑26 (FY26)** me India ka **real GDP growth ~7.5%** hoga, “**upward bias**” ke saath.  
- Comparison:  
  - **NSO First Advance Estimate:** ~**7.4%** real GDP growth.  
  - **RBI projection:** ~**7.3%**.  
- Ecowrap ka point:  
  - Jab base year **2022‑23** se GDP series rebasing hogi,  
    to measured growth aur bhi thodi high dikh sakti hai.  
  - **Nominal GDP growth ~8%** ke aas‑paas project ki gayi hai.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Direct data points:  
  - SBI Ecowrap → **7.5%**, NSO → **7.4%**, RBI → **7.3%** (FY26 real GDP growth).  
- Macro link:  
  - High growth → credit demand strong, banks ke liye **loan growth opportunity**.  
  - Nominal growth ~8% → tax collection, fiscal maths, banking sector profitability sab se जुड़ा.  
- Mains:  
  - “How does India’s growth outlook (as per SBI/RBI/NSO) support a healthy banking credit cycle?”

### Memory Trick – “7.3–7.4–7.5 Ladder”

> Socho ek chhoti सी सीढ़ी:  
> **RBI 7.3, NSO 7.4, SBI 7.5** – teen steps, sab 7 se upar.

## TOPIC 3: Axis Finance “Vyapar Business Loan” – Collateral‑Free MSE Funding

### Kya hua?

- **Axis Finance Ltd (AFL)** – Axis Bank ki NBFC subsidiary – ne  
  media reports ke mutabik **January 2026** me **“Vyapar Business Loan”** product announce kiya:  
  - Target: **micro &amp; small enterprises** (MSEs) – छोटे dukaan‑दार, traders, service providers, small manufacturers.  
  - **Collateral‑free business loan** – property ya अन्य security ki requirement nahi.  
  - Focus: **working capital**, **shop renovation / expansion**, **equipment ya inventory purchase**.  
- Ticket size (CA reports ke basis par):  
  - Kuch news items is product ke liye **upto ₹10 lakh** ka collateral‑free ticket size mention karte hain  
    (official website par फिलहाल generic “Business Loan upto ₹50 lakh” framework hi listed hai –  
    isliye exam me number ko approx CA source ke रूप me yaad rakho).  

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- MSE financing = **Priority Sector Lending (PSL)** ka मुख्य हिस्सा.  
- Collateral‑free loans:  
  - Financial inclusion ke liye zaroori, lekin **credit appraisal** tough –  
    isliye banks/NBFCs ko **cash‑flow based lending, GST/Bank statement analytics** ka use karna पड़ता hai.  
- Interview / mains:  
  - “What challenges do lenders face in collateral‑free MSME lending and how can data‑based underwriting help?”

### Memory Trick – “Vyapar = बिना ज़मानत vyapari ka sahara”

> Line: **“Vyapar loan – bina girvi vyapari ka sahara, upto ~10 lakh tak ka sahara.”**

## TOPIC 4: 10 January 2026 – Second Saturday Bank Holiday &amp; Digital Alternatives

### Kya hua?

- RBI ke holiday calendar ke अनुसार **हर महीने ka 2nd aur 4th Saturday**  
  **scheduled commercial banks ke liye public holiday** hota hai (all‑India).  
- **10 January 2026** – **Saturday** – month ka **second Saturday** tha,  
  isliye:  
  - **Public / private / foreign banks ke branches बंद**,  
  - ATMs, digital channels (net banking, mobile, UPI, IMPS) normal chalte rahe.  
- Kuch states ke local holidays ke साथ club hone par customers ko confusion ho sakta tha,  
  isliye news portals ne specifically 10 Jan 2026 ko “banks closed” highlight kiya.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Static + practical:  
  - Banks **1st &amp; 3rd Saturday ko open**, **2nd &amp; 4th Saturday ko closed** (general rule; exceptions separately notified).  
- Interview angle:  
  - “How do digital channels ensure continuity of basic banking services even on bank‑branch holidays?”

### Memory Trick – “2–4 बंद, 1–3 खुला”

> Simple code: **“Saturday 1–3 open, 2–4 बंद.”**  
> 10 January 2026 = second Saturday → branches बंद, digital open.

## TOPIC 5: SBI Card &amp; HDFC – Domestic Airport Lounge Rules from 10 Jan 2026

### Kya hua?

- **SBI Card – New Domestic Lounge Programme (effective 10 Jan 2026):**  
  - Selected SBI credit cards ke लिये **नया domestic airport lounge access programme** launch hua.  
  - Lounges ko **Set A** aur **Set B** me divide kiya gaya; kaunsi Set ka access milega, ye card variant par depend karta hai.  
  - Entry process: lounge gate par **SBI credit card swipe/tap** karna hoga:  
    - **Visa / RuPay cards:** validation ke लिये **₹2 non‑refundable charge**.  
    - **Mastercard:** **₹25 temporary hold** (block) jo actually debit nahi hota.  
  - Har card ke लिये complimentary visits ka **fixed quota** hai; quota khatam hone par  
    customer ko **paid entry** leni hogi (lounge ke normal charge par).  

- **HDFC Bank – Debit Card Lounge Access Changes (from 10 Jan 2026):**  
  - HDFC ne announce kiya ki **10 Jan 2026 se debit card ko direct swipe karke lounge entry nahi milegi**.  
  - Ab process:  
    - Pehle **quarter me minimum ₹10,000 spending** apne HDFC debit card se complete karo.  
    - Us ke baad bank **SMS / Email ke through ek lounge access voucher link** bhejega (2 working days ke भीतर).  
    - Customer ko **voucher claim** karke us voucher se lounge entry leni hogi.  
  - Complimentary visits per quarter card type par depend:  
    - Millennia/Times/GIGA: **1 visit** per quarter,  
    - Platinum/Business: **2 visits**,  
    - Infiniti: **4 visits** (bank ke आधिकारिक terms ke mutabik).  

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Retail banking / cards section me **airport lounge, rewards, charges** pe MCQ aate hain.  
- Trend:  
  - Lounge access ab **spend condition + limited free quota** se linked hai –  
    banks apni **cost control** kar rahe hain.  
  - Customers ko benefits maintain karne ke लिये **minimum usage** dikhाना होगा.  

### Memory Trick – “Lounge point par Swipe + Spend”

> SBI ke लिये: **“Swipe ₹2 test, phir rest.”**  
> HDFC debit ke लिये: **“Pehle ₹10k spend, tabhi lounge voucher send.”**
