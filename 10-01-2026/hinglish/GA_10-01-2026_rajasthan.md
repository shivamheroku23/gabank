## TOPIC 1: Rajasthan AI-ML Policy 2026 aur Green Credit Voucher Initiative

### Kya hua?

- **30 December 2025** ko Rajasthan Cabinet ne **Rajasthan Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI-ML) Policy 2026** ke saath-saath **Green Credit Voucher Initiative 2025** ko approve kiya.  
- AI-ML Policy ka main aim hai ki Rajasthan ko **technology-driven investment, innovation aur digital governance ka hub** banaya jaye aur public services ko **faster, transparent aur citizen-centric** banaya jaye.  
- Policy **ethical AI** par zor deti hai – transparency, accountability, fairness, privacy protection, bias kam karna aur personal data ka secure handling, saath hi AI decisions ko explainable aur auditable banana.  
- **Green Credit Voucher Initiative 2025** ka focus hai **green circular economy** ko boost karna – jo bhi environment-friendly kaam kare, usko structured green credits/vouchers ke through incentivise kiya ja sake.  

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- RAS, REET, Police, Patwari sabhi exams ke liye yeh ek **high-value state policy combo** hai – naam aur saal dono yaad rakhne laayak: **AI-ML Policy 2026** + **Green Credit Voucher Initiative 2025**.  
- Isse related MCQ a sakta hai ki kis policy ka link **Digital Rajasthan, e-governance, climate action aur green finance** se hai.  
- Mains me yeh example use kar sakte ho ki Rajasthan ek saath **AI reforms + environment incentives** dono ko policy basket me laa raha hai.

## TOPIC 7: Adani–RRVUNL coal transport case – ₹1,400 crore ka “road vs rail” dispute

### Kya hua?

- **July 2025** me **Jaipur Commercial Court** ke Judge **Dinesh Kumar Gupta** ne **Parsa Kente Collieries Ltd (Adani-led JV)** aur **Rajasthan Rajya Vidyut Utpadan Nigam Limited (RRVUNL)** ke beech coal transport dispute par judgement diya.  
- Court ne dekha ki agreement ke mutabik coal ko rail connectivity ke through le jana tha, lekin JV ne kai saalon tak road transport use kiya aur iske liye **₹1,400 crore se jyada** ke charges RRVUNL se recover kiye – isko court ne **“wrongful gain”** maana aur company par **₹50 lakh ka fine** lagaya.  
- Isi din judge ko commercial court se hata kar Beawar district court me transfer kar diya gaya, aur baad me Rajasthan High Court ne is order ko stay kar diya; case abhi bhi judicial consideration me hai.  

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- RAS mains me **state PSUs, mining contracts, judicial oversight, accountability** jaise topics par example ke liye yeh case kaafi strong hai.  
- One-liners: parties – **RRVUNL vs Adani-led JV**, amount – **₹1,400+ crore “wrongful gain”**, fine – **₹50 lakh**, aur High Court ka **stay**.  
- Governance answers me isse **public resources, transparency, contract enforcement** jaise points ko case-study ke roop me use kiya ja sakta hai.  

**Memory Trick – “1,400 crore coal, 50 lakh fine”**

> Line yaad rakho: **“Adani–RRVUNL coal road charges ≈ ₹1,400 crore, Jaipur court fine ₹50 lakh – ab High Court ke paas line.”**

## TOPIC 8: NSO ke First Advance Estimates – FY26 me 7.4% real GDP growth

### Kya hua?

- **7 January 2026** ko **National Statistical Office (NSO)** ne **Financial Year 2025–26 (FY26)** ke liye **First Advance Estimates (FAE)** of GDP release kiye.  
- NSO ke mutabik India ki **real GDP growth** FY26 me kareeb **7.4%** rahegi, jisme real GDP level lagbhag **₹201.90 lakh crore** estimate kiya gaya hai (FY25 ke ₹187.97 lakh crore se upar).  
- **Nominal GDP growth** ko kareeb **8.0%** estimate kiya gaya hai, jisse nominal GDP lagbhag **₹357.14 lakh crore** tak pahunchne ka अंदाज़ hai.  
- Real **GVA growth** kareeb **7.3%** estimate hai, jisme **services/tertiary sector** lagbhag **9.1%** growth ke saath lead kar raha hai, jabki private consumption, govt spending aur investment sab positive direction me dikh rahe hain.  

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Rajasthan ke economy/GS papers me national-level numbers par direct sawal aate hain – **NSO, GDP growth rate, nominal vs real** jaise basics par.  
- One-liners: **real GDP – 7.4%**, **nominal GDP – 8%**, **real GVA – 7.3%** FY26 ke liye.  
- State-level answers me in numbers ko use karke **growth, jobs, investment climate** par broader discussion kiya ja sakta hai.
