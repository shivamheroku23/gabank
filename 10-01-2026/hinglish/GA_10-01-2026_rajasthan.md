### Topic 9: PARAM Shakti Supercomputer – IIT Madras, Chennai

### Kya Hua / What Happened?

- **Ministry of Electronics &amp; IT (MeitY)** ne **January 2026** me **IIT Madras, Chennai** me **“PARAM Shakti”** supercomputing facility inaugurate ki.  
- Ye facility **PARAM RUDRA supercomputing system** par based hai jiska **peak performance ~3.1 petaflops** (3.1 × 10^15 calculations per second) hai.  
- Supercomputer ka hardware &amp; software stack **C‑DAC (Centre for Development of Advanced Computing)** ne India me hi design &amp; develop kiya.  
- PARAM Shakti se research accelerate hogi:  
  - aerospace engineering, climate &amp; weather modelling,  
  - materials science, drug discovery, nuclear research, high‑end computational fluid dynamics, etc.  
- Facility ke liye 24×7 power backup, advanced cooling systems aur integrated data centre setup provide kiya gaya.
