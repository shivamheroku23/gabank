## TOPIC 1: RBI ne FEDAI ko Forex SRO ka दर्जा diya

### Kya hua?

- **Reserve Bank of India (RBI)** ne **Foreign Exchange Dealers’ Association of India (FEDAI)** ko **Self-Regulatory Organisation (SRO)** ke रूप me recognise kiya hai, jo saare **Authorised Dealers (ADs)** ke liye forex market me standards set karega.  
- Ye recognition RBI ke **Omnibus Framework for SROs** ke तहत diya gaya hai, jiska aim hai **market discipline, transparency aur self‑regulation** ko strong banana.  
- FEDAI ko **एक saal** ka time diya gaya hai ki woh apni **governance structure** ko SRO framework ke saath align kare aur membership ko **saare categories ke ADs** tak expand kare.  
- FEDAI ki shuruaat **1958** me hui thi; yeh ek **Section 8 company** (not‑for‑profit) hai jo **inter‑bank forex rules** banati hai aur banks aur RBI ke beech liaison ka kaam karti hai.  
- RBI norms ke mutabik kisi bhi SRO ko **कम se कम ₹2 crore ki net worth** rakhni hoti hai, shareholding **diversified** hona chahiye (koi single entity ≥10% nahin), aur Board ka **कम se कम ek‑third hissa independent directors** ka hona chahiye.  

(Official context: RBI Omnibus SRO Framework &amp; FEDAI recognition; Photo: https://cdn.guidely.in/images/sliders/176035399766.webp)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- FEDAI + forex market, bank GA ka favourite combination hai – exam me aksar pucha jata hai **“FEDAI ka role kya?”**  
- MCQ points:  
  - Forex ADs ka SRO? → **FEDAI**  
  - Establishment year? → **1958**  
  - Company type? → **Section 8, not‑for‑profit**  
- Mains/interview me isko example ke रूप me likh sakte ho ki RBI **“direct regulation + self‑regulation”** dono ka mix use karta hai.  

### Memory Trick – “FEDAI = Forex ka SRO Guide”

> Yaad rakho: **“FEDAI now = Forex SRO under RBI”** – mind me ek **“FEDAI Rule Book”** imagine karo jo saare Authorised Dealers follow karte hain.

## TOPIC 2: SMBC (Japan) ko India me Wholly-Owned Subsidiary (WOS) banane ki RBI se मंज़ूरी

### Kya hua?

- RBI ne **Japan ki Sumitomo Mitsui Banking Corporation (SMBC)** ko India me ek **Wholly‑Owned Subsidiary (WOS)** establish karne ka **in‑principle approval** de diya hai.  
- WOS ek **alag Indian legal entity** hoti hai – jisse foreign bank ko **domestic bank jaisa status** mil jata hai (branches kholne me zyada flexibility, capital norms etc.).  
- SMBC apni existing branches – **Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, Bengaluru** – ko is WOS me convert karega, jis se Indian market me unki growth aur operations easy ho jayenge.  
- 2025 me SMBC ne **YES Bank me 24.22% stake** liya tha, jisse SMBC uska **sabse bada shareholder** ban gaya – ab WOS ke through partnership aur deep hogi.  
- RBI ka rule hai ki foreign banks India me **single‑mode presence** choose karein (branch model ya WOS); jo yahan **systemically important** ho jate hain unko generally **WOS route** prefer kiya jata hai.  

(Official context: RBI foreign banks – mode of presence &amp; WOS policy; Photo: https://cdn.guidely.in/images/sliders/176035399766.webp)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Foreign bank presence, **branch vs WOS model, minimum capital ₹500 crore**, yeh sab conceptual topics banking awareness me baar‑baar aate hain.  
- MCQ angles:  
  - SMBC kis desh ka bank hai? → **Japan**  
  - WOS ka full form? → **Wholly‑Owned Subsidiary**  
  - WOS ke liye minimum paid‑up voting equity capital? → **₹500 crore**  
- Descriptive answers me is example se **financial openness + prudential regulation** ka balance explain kar sakte ho.  

### Memory Trick – “SMBC WOS = Japanese bank, Indian avatar”

> Picture banao: **Japanese flag ke side me ek Indian bank building jiska board hai “SMBC WOS – ₹500 crore”**.

## TOPIC 3: RBI ka naya rule – Automated Complaint Management System + Internal Ombudsman

### Kya hua?

- RBI ne **saare banks** (jinke 10+ branches hain) aur kuch **eligible NBFCs** ko निर्देश diya hai ki woh ek **fully automated Complaint Management System (CMS)** implement karein, jisme **Internal Ombudsman (IO)** aur **Deputy IO (DIO)** ko direct digital access ho.  
- Har bank me कम se कम ek **Internal Ombudsman** appoint karna mandatory hai; deposit‑taking NBFCs aur **₹5,000 crore+ assets** wale large NBFCs (public interface ke saath) bhi coverage me aate hain (kuch categories exempt).  
- Bank ko har complaint ko **“fully resolved”**, **“partially resolved”** ya **“wholly rejected”** category me classify karke **auto‑escalate** karna hoga IO ke paas, jahan independent review hoga.  
- IO ko review ke liye: **10 din** (un complaints ke liye jinke timeline networks/NPCl/card schemes se pre‑set hain) aur **20 din** (dusre cases me) milte hain; customer ko overall **30 din ke andar** final decision mil jana chahiye.  
- IO/DIO ki tenure **कम se कम 3 saal, maximum 5 saal** hogi; unko bina **Customer Service Committee (CSC)** ki मंज़ूरी ke remove nahi kiya ja sakta. Sirf **Whole‑Time/Executive Director** hi IO ke decision ko overrule kar sakta hai.  

(Official context: RBI circular on Internal Ombudsman framework &amp; automated CMS; Photo: https://cdn.guidely.in/images/sliders/176035399766.webp)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Ye topic **customer service, grievance redressal, internal control** – teeno ko cover karta hai, jo bank GA ka core part hain.  
- Possible MCQs:  
  - IO kis rank ka officer hota hai? → GM‑equivalent with **कम se कम 7 saal ka experience** in financial sector.  
  - IO ki maximum age? → **70 saal**.  
  - Complaint timeline: **30 days** ke andar final decision.  
- Ye framework externally chalne wale **RBI Integrated Ombudsman Scheme** ke saath milkar ek **two‑layer grievance system** create karta hai.  

### Memory Trick – “CMS pipeline: Bank → IO → RBI Ombudsman”

> Mind me ek pipeline imagine karo: **Customer → Bank CMS → IO/DIO review → (agar unresolved) RBI Ombudsman**.

## TOPIC 4: WPI Inflation December 2025 – Deflation se निकलकर +0.83% par

### Kya hua?

- India ki **Wholesale Price Index (WPI) inflation** December 2025 me **+0.83%** par aa gayi – yaani **do mahine ke deflation ke baad** pehli baar positive reading, aur lagbhag **8 mahine ka highest** level.  
- November 2025 me WPI **–0.32%** thi, to December me clear turnaround dekha gaya.  
- Turnaround ka major reason tha **food prices me deflation ka kam hona** – primary food articles ka deflation **–4.16% se सुधर kar –0.43%** ke aas‑paas aa gaya.  
- **Manufactured products inflation** bhi approx **1.82%** tak badh gayi, aur **core WPI (food &amp; fuel ko hata kar)** lagbhag **2%** tak pohonch gayi, jo industrial price pressures ko dikhata hai.  
- WPI ka **base year 2011‑12** hai, aur sabse bada weight **manufactured goods (~64.23%)** ko milta hai; experts expect kar rahe hain ki FY26 me WPI average **~0.4%** ke aas‑paas rahe.  

(Official context: Office of Economic Adviser – WPI press release; Photo: https://cdn.guidely.in/images/sliders/176035399766.webp)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- WPI vs CPI, base year, components, latest trend – ye sab **economy &amp; inflation** section ke favourite topics hain.  
- MCQ points:  
  - December 2025 WPI → **0.83%**.  
  - November 2025 WPI → **–0.32%** (deflation).  
  - Base year → **2011‑12**, aur sabse bada weight → **manufactured goods (~64%)**.  
- Mains me in numbers ko **inflation dynamics, policy stance, growth vs inflation** wale answers me easily quote kiya ja sakta hai.  

### Memory Trick – “–0.32 se +0.83 – WPI ne U‑turn liya”

> Ek arrow draw karo: **–0.32 → +0.83** – yaad rakho ki December me WPI ne **negative se positive** side shift ki.
