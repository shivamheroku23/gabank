## TOPIC 1: Kakinada, Andhra Pradesh – World’s Largest Green Ammonia Project

### Kya hua?

- **Andhra Pradesh** ke **Kakinada** me company **AM Green** world’s largest **green ammonia project** set karne wali hai, jisme lagbhag **USD 10 billion** ka investment plan hai.  
- Project ka target hai ki **2030 tak 1.5 MTPA (million tonnes per annum)** green ammonia capacity achieve ho, phase‑wise – **0.5 MTPA (2027)**, **1.0 MTPA (2028)**, phir **1.5 MTPA (2030)**.  
- Ye project existing **ammonia–urea complex** ko ek **purely renewable‑energy based green ammonia plant** me convert karega – around **7.5 GW solar + wind**, kareeb **1,950 MW electrolyser capacity** aur lagbhag **2 GW round‑the‑clock green power** ka use.  
- Yahan se banne wali green ammonia ko **Germany, Japan, Singapore** jaise देशों ko export karne ka plan hai, jisse **fertiliser, shipping aur power** sectors ke decarbonisation me help milegi.  

### SSC exam points:

- State & place: **Kakinada, Andhra Pradesh**.  
- Developer: **AM Green**.  
- Capacity target: **1.5 MTPA green ammonia by 2030**, investment ~**USD 10 billion**.  

**Memory Trick – “A for Andhra, A for Ammonia”**

> Yaad rakho: **“AP = Ammonia Power at Kakinada”** – Andhra Pradesh ke Kakinada ko **green ammonia hub** ke रूप me imagine karo.

## TOPIC 2: INSV Kaundinya – Porbandar se Muscat ki Maiden Overseas Voyage

### Kya hua?

- Indian Navy ki traditional **stitched‑plank sailing vessel INSV Kaundinya** ne apni **pehli foreign voyage** me **Porbandar (Gujarat)** se **Muscat (Oman)** tak lagbhag **1,400 km** ka safar **17 din** me complete kiya.  
- Yeh ship **5th century CE Ajanta cave painting** me dikhayi gayi ancient भारतीय nauko se inspired hai, aur isko **bina metal nails ke, wooden planks ko stitch karke** banaya gaya – isliye isse **stitched‑plank vessel** kehte hain.  
- Vessel ko **Commander Vikas Sheoran** lead kar rahe hain, crew me total **16 members** hain, jisme economist **Sanjeev Sanyal** bhi shamil hain.  
- Voyage ka symbolic purpose hai **India–Oman ke 5,000 saal purane maritime ties** ko celebrate karna; Muscat port par **Union Minister Sarbananda Sonowal** aur Omani officials ne welcome kiya, aur **Green Shipping Corridor** jaise ideas par baat hui.  

### SSC exam points:

- Vessel: **INSV Kaundinya** – traditional **stitched‑plank wooden sailing vessel**.  
- Route: **Porbandar (Gujarat) → Muscat (Oman)**, distance ~1,400 km.  
- Purpose: **India–Oman maritime heritage** aur ancient shipbuilding skills ko showcase karna.  

**Memory Trick – “Kaundinya = stitched‑plank ship to Oman”**

> Dimaag me ek wooden ship imagine karo jiske side par likha hai **“Kaundinya – Porbandar to Muscat”**.

## TOPIC 3: Antarctica ka World’s First Global Ice Vault

### Kya hua?

- Scientists ne **Ice Memory Foundation** ke under **Concordia Station, Antarctica** me **world’s first global ice vault** setup kiya hai.  
- Yahan duniya ke alag‑alag **mountain glaciers** se liye gaye **ice cores** ko kareeb **–52°C** par store kiya jayega, taaki climate change ki wajah se glaciers melt hone par bhi **long‑term climate records** safe rahen.  
- Pehle phase me **Mont Blanc (France)** aur **Grand Combin (Switzerland)** ke ice cores yahan shift kiye ja rahe hain; future me aur glaciers add honge.  
- Ye concept **Svalbard Global Seed Vault** jaisa hai, bas yahan **seeds ki jagah “ice archives”** ko future scientists ke liye preserve kiya ja raha hai.  

### SSC exam points:

- Location: **Concordia Station, Antarctica**.  
- Project: **Global ice vault**, lead organisation → **Ice Memory Foundation**.  
- Purpose: **Glacier ice cores** ko store karke **climate history** ko secure rakhna.  

**Memory Trick – “Ice Memory = Ice Library in Antarctica”**

> Ek **giant freezer‑library** imagine karo jahan shelves par labelled ice core tubes rakhe hain – yahi global ice vault hai.

## TOPIC 4: Adani Group–Embraer – India ki Pehli Commercial Aircraft FAL

### Kya hua?

- **Adani Group** ne **Brazilian aircraft manufacturer Embraer** ke saath MoU sign kiya hai, jiske through India me **pehli commercial aircraft Final Assembly Line (FAL)** establish hogi.  
- Is FAL me **Embraer ke regional jets** assemble honge, jo **short‑ to medium‑haul routes** ke liye suitable hain aur lagbhag **70–146 passengers** तक ki seating capacity rakhte hain.  
- Is deal ke baad India un kam deshon me shamil ho jayega jinke paas **commercial fixed‑wing aircraft ki final assembly** ka capacity hai – ye **Make in India + aviation manufacturing ecosystem** ke liye big push hai.  
- Exact location aur total investment figure अभी officially announce nahi hui, lekin expectation hai ki **jobs, exports aur aerospace tech transfer** me kaafi fayda hoga.  

### SSC exam points:

- Partnership: **Adani Group (India) + Embraer (Brazil)**.  
- Project: **India’s first commercial aircraft Final Assembly Line (FAL)**.  
- Product: **Regional jets, 70–146 seat capacity**, short/medium‑haul.  

**Memory Trick – “Adani + Embraer = aircraft made in India”**

> Plane imagine karo jiske body par likha ho **“Assembled in India – Embraer + Adani”**.

## TOPIC 5: Henley Passport Index 2026 – Indian Passport Rank 80

### Kya hua?

- **Henley Passport Index 2026** me **Indian passport** ko **80th rank** diya gaya, jo **2019 ke baad se sabse accha level** maana ja raha hai.  
- Ab Indian citizens ko lagbhag **55 destinations** par **visa‑free ya visa‑on‑arrival** access milta hai; pichhle saal ye number approx **52** tha.  
- Ye improvement mainly **naye bilateral visa agreements**, kuch देशों ke saath **transit visa waivers** aur **e‑visa partnerships** ke expansion ki wajah se aaya hai.  
- Phir bhi **US, UK, Schengen area, Canada** jaise major destinations ke liye abhi bhi regular visa required hai.  

### SSC exam points:

- Index: **Henley Passport Index 2026**.  
- India’s rank: **80th**.  
- Visa‑free / visa‑on‑arrival access: lagbhag **55 destinations**.  

**Memory Trick – “HP 2026: India @ 80 with 55 free/VOA destinations”**

> Code line: **“Henley 2026 – India rank 80, 55 देशों me easy entry”**.
