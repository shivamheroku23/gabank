## TOPIC 1: Rajasthan Budget 2025‑26 – Agriculture &amp; Farmers Focus

### Kya hua?

- Rajasthan Budget 2025‑26 me **कृषि aur kisaan** par khaas जोर diya gaya hai – **irrigation, input support, crop diversification** jaise क्षेत्रों me naye steps announce hue.  
- Budget me **परंपरागत कृषि विकास yojana**, **drip/sprinkler सिंचाई**, **micro‑irrigation**, aur **soil health &amp; organic farming** ke liye extra निधि rakhi gayi.  
- Kuch जिलों me **command area development** aur **कैचमेंट क्षेत्र में जल संरक्षण** ke projects ko priority दी गई, taaki baarish par निर्भरता kam ho aur yield stable रहे.  

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- RAS/Patwari me सीधे पूछ sakte hain: **किस budget me agriculture ko green theme &amp; climate adaptation ke saath integrate kiya gaya?** – answer: **Rajasthan Budget 2025‑26**.  
- Agriculture reforms ka link **Developed Rajasthan 2047**, rural livelihoods aur drought‑prone regions se hai – mains me ye point useful rahega.  

**Memory Trick – “Green Budget ka pehla स्तंभ – किसान aur पानी”**

> Yaad rakho: **Rajasthan Green Budget = pehla focus kisaan + irrigation + climate‑resilient farming**.

- Women‑centred measures ko mains me **“inclusive growth &amp; gender justice”** heading ke तहत quote kiya ja sakta hai.
