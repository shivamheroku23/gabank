## TOPIC 1: Rajasthan Budget 2025‑26 – Agriculture &amp; Farmers Focus

### Kya hua?

- The **Rajasthan Budget 2025‑26** places special emphasis on **agriculture and farmers**, particularly on **irrigation, input support and climate‑resilient farming**.  
- Additional allocations have been made for schemes related to **micro‑irrigation (drip and sprinkler)**, **soil health improvement**, **organic/natural farming** and **crop diversification**.  
- In selected districts, projects for **command area development** and **water conservation in catchment areas** have been prioritised to reduce dependence on erratic rainfall and stabilise yields.  

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Questions can directly ask: **Which Rajasthan budget integrated agriculture with a green/climate adaptation theme? → Rajasthan Budget 2025‑26.**  
- Agriculture reforms here link to **“Developed Rajasthan @2047”**, rural livelihoods and drought‑prone regions – useful for mains answers in economy and geography.  

**Memory Trick – “Green Budget’s first pillar = farmers and water”**

> Remember: **Rajasthan Green Budget = first focus on farmers + irrigation + climate‑resilient agriculture.**

## TOPIC 2: Social Security &amp; Women Welfare – Pensions, SHGs &amp; Safety

### Kya hua?

- The budget outlines a roadmap to **gradually enhance social security pensions** for **old age, widows and persons with disabilities**, benefiting lakhs of vulnerable citizens.  
- For women’s empowerment, special support is proposed for **Self‑Help Groups (SHGs)** in the form of credit, marketing and skill training to help more women become **“Lakhpati Didis”‑type earners** in rural areas.  
- On women’s safety, provisions have been made to strengthen **women police stations/desks, fast‑track courts and helpline systems**, along with better infrastructure for support services.  

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Social security and women‑centric schemes frequently appear in **RSMSSB, Patwari, LDC and police exams** as direct factual questions.  
- In mains, these measures can be cited under **“inclusive growth, social justice and gender empowerment”** for Rajasthan.  

**Memory Trick – “Pension + SHG income + safety = social shield”**

> Think: **“Budget 2025‑26 = pension boost + SHG women empowerment + women safety infrastructure.”**

## TOPIC 3: Tourism, Culture &amp; Heritage Circuits

### Kya hua?

- The budget projects **tourism, culture and heritage** as key drivers of Rajasthan’s economy.  
- It proposes to develop **heritage circuits**, **desert tourism routes**, **eco‑tourism sites** and **religious circuits** (including major pilgrimage centres, dhams and Sufi/Shakti Peeth clusters).  
- Important forts, stepwells (baoris) and historic town centres are earmarked for **facade improvement, basic amenities and digital tourist information facilities**.  

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Tourism &amp; culture is a classic Rajasthan exam topic – questions on **circuits, forts, festivals and tourism schemes** are common.  
- Linking **Budget 2025‑26** to a **heritage‑oriented tourism push** will help in both prelims and descriptive answers.  

**Memory Trick – “Fort + Desert + Circuit = Rajasthan tourism triangle”**

> Visualise a map where **forts, desert routes and pilgrimage circuits** form a triangular tourism hub.

## TOPIC 4: Law &amp; Order, Cyber Security &amp; E‑Governance

### Kya hua?

- The budget provides additional funds for **police modernisation**, including **cyber crime units, forensic laboratories and upgraded equipment**.  
- Special emphasis is given to **cyber security**: strengthening **cyber police stations, cyber forensics and digital awareness programmes**, as well as regular **security audits** of e‑governance systems.  
- On the e‑governance front, the government plans to expand **online citizen services, single‑window systems** and **digital records management** to improve transparency and efficiency.  

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Law &amp; order, cyber crime and e‑governance are important topics for **Police, Patwari and clerical exams** in the state.  
- Within the green budget framework, governance and digital reforms show how **administration and technology** are being integrated.  

**Memory Trick – “Three Cs: Crime, Cyber, Computerisation”**

## TOPIC 5: Education &amp; Health – Quality and Access

### Kya hua?

## TOPIC 6: Madhya Pradesh–BHASHINI MoU – Multilingual AI Governance (Learning for Rajasthan)

### Kya hua?

- The **Government of Madhya Pradesh** signed an MoU with the **Digital India BHASHINI Division** to integrate **multilingual, AI‑powered language technologies** into state digital platforms.  
- The MoU was signed during the **Madhya Pradesh Regional AI Impact Conference 2026 in Bhopal**, where the state announced plans for a **State Artificial Intelligence Policy** in mission mode.  
- BHASHINI aims to enable **voice‑first, Indian language AI solutions**, improving access to government services for **rural populations, elderly citizens and people with low digital literacy**.  

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Rajasthan can draw lessons from this model for future **AI‑enabled, multilingual governance**, especially for Hindi and regional dialects.  
- In essays and GS papers, this example illustrates how **Digital Public Infrastructure** can be made inclusive through language technology.  

**Memory Trick – “BHASHINI = Language + AI bridge”**

> Imagine a portal where citizens can talk in Hindi or Rajasthani and get government services through **AI voice assistants**.
