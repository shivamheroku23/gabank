## TOPIC 1: Andhra Pradesh to Host World’s Largest Green Ammonia Project at Kakinada

### Kya hua?

- **Andhra Pradesh** will host the **world’s largest green ammonia project** at **Kakinada**, being developed by **AM Green** with an investment of around **USD 10 billion**.  
- The project targets a final capacity of **1.5 million tonnes per annum (MTPA) of green ammonia** by **2030**, achieved in phases – 0.5 MTPA by 2027, 1.0 MTPA by 2028, and 1.5 MTPA by 2030.  
- It will convert an existing **ammonia–urea complex** into a green ammonia facility powered fully by **renewable energy** – about **7.5 GW** of solar and wind, nearly **1,950 MW of electrolysers** and almost **2 GW of round‑the‑clock green power**.  
- Green ammonia produced here will be exported to countries like **Germany, Japan and Singapore**, supporting global decarbonisation in fertilisers, shipping and power sectors.  

### SSC exam points:

- State & place: **Kakinada, Andhra Pradesh**.  
- Project: **World’s largest green ammonia project**, developed by **AM Green**.  
- Capacity target: **1.5 MTPA by 2030**; investment about **USD 10 billion**.  

**Memory Trick – “AP = Ammonia Power at Kakinada”**

> Think: **“A for Andhra, A for Ammonia”** – Andhra Pradesh at **Kakinada** = world’s largest green ammonia hub.

## TOPIC 2: INSV Kaundinya Voyage – Porbandar to Muscat

### Kya hua?

- The traditionally built **stitched‑plank sailing vessel INSV Kaundinya** completed its **maiden overseas voyage** from **Porbandar (Gujarat)** to **Muscat (Oman)**, covering about **1,400 km in 17 days**.  
- The ship is inspired by a **5th‑century CE Ajanta cave ship**, constructed using ancient **stitched‑plank techniques** without nails or metal, showcasing India’s traditional shipbuilding skills.  
- It is skippered by **Commander Vikas Sheoran** with a **16‑member crew**, including economist **Sanjeev Sanyal**, and commemorates **5,000 years of India–Oman maritime ties**.  
- The arrival at **Port Sultan Qaboos** was attended by Union Minister **Sarbananda Sonowal** and Omani officials, with discussions on maritime cooperation and a proposed **Green Shipping Corridor**.  

### SSC exam points:

- Vessel: **INSV Kaundinya** – traditionally built stitched‑plank sailing ship.  
- Route: **Porbandar (Gujarat) → Muscat (Oman)**.  
- Purpose: highlight **India–Oman maritime heritage** and indigenous naval craftsmanship.  

**Memory Trick – “Kaundinya = stitched‑plank ship to Oman”**

> Imagine a wooden ship labelled **“Kaundinya – Porbandar to Muscat”** sailing across the Arabian Sea.

## TOPIC 3: World’s First Global Ice Vault in Antarctica

### Kya hua?

- Scientists under the **Ice Memory Foundation** have launched the **world’s first global ice vault** at **Concordia Station in Antarctica**.  
- The vault will store **mountain glacier ice cores** from around the world at about **–52°C**, preserving long‑term **climate records** threatened by rapid glacier melting.  
- Initial ice cores are from **Mont Blanc (France)** and **Grand Combin (Switzerland)**, with more glaciers to be added later.  
- The project, inspired by the **Svalbard Global Seed Vault**, aims to safeguard physical climate archives for use by future generations of scientists.  

### SSC exam points:

- Location: **Concordia Station, Antarctica**.  
- Project: **Global ice vault** under the **Ice Memory Foundation**.  
- Purpose: preserve **glacier ice cores** as climate archives.  

**Memory Trick – “Ice Memory = ice library in Antarctica”**

> Visualise a **giant freezer library** in Antarctica storing labelled tubes of glacier ice.

## TOPIC 4: Adani Group & Embraer to Build India’s First Commercial Aircraft FAL

### Kya hua?

- The **Adani Group** has signed an MoU with **Brazilian aircraft manufacturer Embraer** to establish **India’s first Final Assembly Line (FAL)** for **commercial fixed‑wing aircraft**.  
- The FAL will assemble **Embraer regional jets** suited for **short‑ and medium‑haul routes**, with seating capacity roughly **70–146 passengers**.  
- This will make India part of a **small group of countries** with commercial aircraft final assembly capability, boosting **Make in India** and the **aviation manufacturing ecosystem**.  
- The exact location and investment details are yet to be announced, but the project is expected to support jobs, exports and technology transfer.  

### SSC exam points:

- Partnership: **Adani Group (India) + Embraer (Brazil)**.  
- Project: **India’s first commercial aircraft Final Assembly Line (FAL)**.  
- Aircraft type: **regional jets, 70–146 seats** – short/medium haul.  

**Memory Trick – “Adani–Embraer = aircraft made in India”**

> Picture a regional jet with **“Assembled in India – Embraer + Adani”** painted on its body.

## TOPIC 5: Henley Passport Index 2026 – India Rises to 80th Rank

### Kya hua?

- The **Henley Passport Index 2026** ranked the **Indian passport at 80th position**, its **best rank since 2019**.  
- Indian citizens now enjoy **visa‑free or visa‑on‑arrival access to about 55 destinations**, up from 52 in the previous year.  
- The improvement reflects India’s recent **bilateral visa arrangements**, including transit‑visa waivers with some European countries and expanded **e‑visa partnerships**.  
- However, visas are still required for major destinations such as the **US, UK, Schengen area and Canada**.  

### SSC exam points:

- Index: **Henley Passport Index 2026**.  
- India’s rank: **80th**.  
- Visa‑free/visa‑on‑arrival access: **around 55 destinations**.  

**Memory Trick – “80 = India’s passport rank”**

> Just remember **“HP 2026 → India @ 80 with 55 free/VOA destinations”**.
