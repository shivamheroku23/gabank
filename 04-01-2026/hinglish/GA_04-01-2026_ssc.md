## TOPIC 2: Health & National Security Cess – Sin Goods par naya cess

- Government ne tobacco, pan masala, gutkha jaise **sin goods** par  
  existing **GST Compensation Cess** ke badle  
  **Additional Excise + Health & National Security Cess** structure finalise kiya।  
- Aim:  
  - harmful consumption ko discourage,  
  - health system aur national security related kharch ke liye fund.

**SSC exam points:**

- Cess = **lage hue tax ke upar extra charge**, earmarked purpose ke liye।  
- Health & National Security Cess – sin goods se juda indirect tax.

**Memory Trick – “HN = Health + Nation”**

> **H**ealth ke naam par, **N**ation ki suraksha ke naam par – sin goods par extra cess.

## TOPIC 3: Musi Riverfront Development Project – Telangana + ADB

- Telangana sarkar ne Hyderabad ki **Musi River** ke kinare

- Focus:

  - sewerage treatment,  
  - riverfront park/promenade,  
  - slum redevelopment.

**SSC exam points:**

- Musi River – **Telangana (Hyderabad)** se judi hai।  
- ADB HQ: **Manila, Philippines**; स्थापना 1966.

**Memory Trick – “Musi = Hyderabad ki nadi, ADB se mili funding ready”**

> Bas aise socho: Hyderabad wali Musi ko ADB ne “makeover fund” diya।
