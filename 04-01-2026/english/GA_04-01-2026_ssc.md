## TOPIC 1: Agriculture Minister – Advisory on PM‑KISAN/PMFBY Fund Utilisation

- Union Agriculture &amp; Farmers’ Welfare Minister **Shivraj Singh Chouhan**  
  asked States to **fully utilise** funds under:  
  - PM‑KISAN,  
  - PMFBY (Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana),  
  - and other farm schemes  
  by **March 2026**.  
- Focus:  
  - clear pending claims and e‑KYC/land‑record verification quickly,  
  - ensure timely benefits to farmers,  
  - ease the release of the next instalments.

### SSC exam points

- PM‑KISAN: launched 2019, **₹6,000/year** per farmer (3 instalments), under Ministry of Agriculture.  
- PMFBY: launched 2016, flagship crop insurance scheme; premium shared by farmer + Centre + State.  
- Agriculture Minister (2025–26 context): **Shivraj Singh Chouhan**.

**Memory Trick – “KISAN’s instalment on time”**

> Line: **“Shivraj’s message – give KISAN his instalment on time.”**

## TOPIC 2: Health &amp; National Security Cess – New Cess on Sin Goods

- Government decided that on **tobacco, pan masala, gutkha** and similar **sin goods**,  
  the earlier **GST Compensation Cess** will be replaced by  
  **Additional Excise Duty + Health &amp; National Security Cess**.  
- Aim:  
  - discourage harmful consumption,  
  - raise funds for health systems and national‑security expenditure.

### SSC exam points

- A Cess is an **additional charge on an existing tax**, earmarked for a specific purpose.  
- Health &amp; National Security Cess is an indirect tax linked to **sin goods**.

**Memory Trick – “HN = Health + Nation”**

> HN Cess = extra tax in the name of **Health** and **Nation** on sin goods.

## TOPIC 3: Musi Riverfront Development Project – Telangana + ADB

- Telangana government launched a major **riverfront development project**  
  along the **Musi River in Hyderabad**.  
- For Phase‑I, the **Asian Development Bank (ADB)** approved  
  a loan of about **US$ 500 million (~₹4,100 crore)**.  
- Focus areas:  
  - flood‑risk management,  
  - sewage treatment,  
  - riverfront parks/promenades,  
  - slum redevelopment and urban amenities.

### SSC exam points

- Musi River is linked to **Hyderabad, Telangana**.  
- ADB HQ: **Manila, Philippines**; established in **1966**.

**Memory Trick – “Musi = Hyderabad’s river, ADB funding ready”**

> Think: Hyderabad’s Musi is getting a makeover fund from **ADB**.

## TOPIC 4: DGFT &amp; FCI – Key Appointments

- **Lav Aggarwal** – new **Director General of Foreign Trade (DGFT)**:  
  - implements the Foreign Trade Policy,  
  - handles export‑import licensing and promotion schemes.  
- **Rabindra Kumar Agarwal** – new **CMD, Food Corporation of India (FCI)**:  
  - responsible for MSP procurement, storage and PDS distribution.

### SSC exam points

- DGFT – under **Ministry of Commerce &amp; Industry**.  
- FCI – under **Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food &amp; Public Distribution**.

**Memory Trick – “Lav – trade, Rabindra – ration”**

> **Lav = foreign trade (DGFT)**,  
> **Rabindra = ration/food (FCI)**.

## TOPIC 5: Forex Reserves – External Sector Snapshot

- As per RBI data, in the **week ended 26 December 2025**,  
  India’s forex reserves **rose by about US$ 3.3 billion**.  
- Reserves include:  
  - Foreign Currency Assets (FCA),  
  - Gold, SDR, IMF reserve position.

### SSC exam points

- Forex reserves are managed by the **RBI**.  
- Importance:  
  - helps stabilise the rupee exchange rate,  
  - provides a buffer against external shocks,  
  - supports sovereign ratings.

**Memory Trick – “RBI’s shield – Forex Reserves”**
