## TOPIC 1: PMFBY (Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana) &amp; WBCIS

- The Cabinet decided to continue and improve crop insurance schemes:  
  - **PMFBY** – flagship crop insurance scheme (since 2016),  
  - **Weather‑Based Crop Insurance Scheme (WBCIS)** – based on weather indices.  
- Focus:  
  - using smart technology (remote sensing, drones) for damage assessment,  
  - faster claim settlement,  
  - better farmer awareness.  

### SSC exam points

- PMFBY launch: **2016**; ministry: Agriculture &amp; Farmers’ Welfare.  
- Premium sharing: farmers pay a capped share; Centre + State pay the rest.  
- WBCIS – based on parameters like rainfall, temperature, etc.

**Memory Trick – “PMFBY = guardian angel of crops”**

> Line: **“Drought or flood, PMFBY stands as crop’s guard.”**

## TOPIC 2: Grameen Bharat Mahotsav 2025 – Rural Products Mega‑Fair

- **Grameen Bharat Mahotsav 2025** was held in New Delhi:  
  - women Self‑Help Groups (SHGs),  
  - Farmer Producer Organisations (FPOs),

### SSC exam points

- FPO – farmer producer organisations; collective farming/marketing bodies.

## TOPIC 4: PM‑KISAN &amp; DBT Architecture

- **PM‑KISAN**:  
  - launched in 2019,  
  - provides **₹6,000 per year** to eligible farmers in 3 instalments.  
- Payments are made via **Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)** into bank accounts.  
- e‑KYC, land record verification and Aadhaar seeding are mandatory.  

### SSC exam points

- PM‑KISAN – under Ministry of Agriculture &amp; Farmers’ Welfare.  
- DBT – Direct Benefit Transfer; linked with JAM trinity (Jan‑Dhan, Aadhaar, Mobile).  

**Memory Trick – “3 × 2,000 = 6,000”**

> PM‑KISAN: 3 instalments × ₹2,000 = ₹6,000 per year.
