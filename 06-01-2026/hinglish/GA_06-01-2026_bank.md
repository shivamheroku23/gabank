## TOPIC 1: PMFBY &amp; Weather‑based Crop Insurance – Extension/Reforms

### Kya hua?

- Union Cabinet ne **Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)**  
  aur **Weather‑Based Crop Insurance Scheme (WBCIS)** ko  
  **aage ke seasons ke liye continue** karne,  
  aur kuch operational sudhaar lane ka nirnay kiya (official announcements).  
- Focus:  
  - SMART‑tech (remote sensing, drones, satellites) se crop cutting experiment reduce,  
  - claim settlement tez karna,  
  - grievance redressal majboot karna.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- PMFBY me **banks nodal role** play karte hain:  
  - premium debit, crop loan link, claim settlement.  
- PSL (Priority Sector Lending) me **agri insurance** ek important cross‑link hai.

### Memory Trick – “Fasal = Risk cover, Bank = Channel”

> Line: **“Fasal ka risk – PMFBY, risk ka paisa – bank ke madhyam se.”**

## TOPIC 2: Grameen Bharat Mahotsav 2025 – Rural Products Showcase

### Kya hua?

- New Delhi me **Grameen Bharat Mahotsav 2025** ka aayojan hua:  
  - SHGs, Farmer Producer Organisations (FPOs), rural artisans ne  
    apne products (handicrafts, handlooms, agri‑produce, ODOP items) pradarshit kiye।  
- Ministries: Rural Development, Agriculture, Panchayati Raj aadi milkar event organise karte hain।

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Ye show karta hai ki rural SHGs, FPOs ke liye **market linkages + finance** kitne important hain:  
  - SHG‑bank linkage,  
  - MUDRA loans,  
  - micro‑finance,  
  - Kisan Credit Card (KCC) – ye sab topics directly connected hain।

### Memory Trick – “Grameen Mahotsav = Gaon ka Bazaar Delhi me”

> Bas yaad rakho: **“Gaon ke products ka mahotsav – Delhi me Grameen Bharat Mahotsav.”**

## TOPIC 3: New Crop Varieties &amp; Agri Research

### Kya hua?

- ICAR / State agricultural universities ne  
  **nayi high‑yielding / climate‑resilient crop varieties** release ki (2025‑26 season ke liye):  
  - drought tolerant rice/wheat varieties,  
  - pest‑resistant pulses,  
  - horticulture hybrids।  

*(Exam me exact variety names se zyada, concept aur scheme linking important hota hai.)*

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Better varieties → behtar yield → farmers ki **repayment capacity** improve,  
  agri NPAs kam ho sakte hain।  
- Kisan Credit Card, term loans, agri infra fund – sab isi ecosystem se jude hain।

### Memory Trick – “Beej sudhre, to bank ka risk kam”

> Simple line: **“Achchha beej, kam risk – bank ko bhi profit.”**

## TOPIC 4: Rural DBT – PM‑KISAN &amp; allied schemes

### Kya hua?

- PM‑KISAN me nayi kiston ke payment schedule,  
  e‑KYC updates, land record se linking par zor diya gaya।  
- DBT platform se:  
  - fertiliser subsidy,  
  - LPG subsidy,  
  - PM‑KISAN, PM‑Awas, jal/health schemes –  
    sab ek integrated financial architecture me linked ho rahe hain।

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- DBT ka matlab – **har eligible beneficiary ka bank account** sahi hona zaroori,  
  isliye:  
  - **Jan Dhan accounts**,  
  - KYC,  
  - Aadhaar‑seeding – bahut high priority topics।

### Memory Trick – “DBT = Direct Bank Transfer”

> Always remember: **DBT = Direct Benefit Transfer = Direct Bank Transfer** –  
> sara benefit bank account me.
