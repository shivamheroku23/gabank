## TOPIC 1: PMFBY (Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana) &amp; WBCIS

- Cabinet ne crop insurance schemes ko continue &amp; improve karne ka faisla kiya:  
  - **PMFBY** – 2016 se chal rahi flagship crop insurance scheme,  
  - **Weather‑Based Crop Insurance Scheme (WBCIS)** – weather indices par aadharit।  
- Focus:  
  - smart technology, remote sensing, drones se damage assessment,  
  - claim settlement tez karna,  
  - farmer awareness badhana।

**SSC exam points:**

- PMFBY launch: **2016**, Ministry of Agriculture &amp; Farmers’ Welfare।  
- Premium share: farmer ka hissa limited, baaki Centre + State।  
- WBCIS – rainfall/temperature jaise parameters par aadharit।

**Memory Trick – “Fasal ka farishtaa – PMFBY”**

> Yaad line: **“Baarish ya sookha – Fasal ka farishtaa PMFBY।”**

## TOPIC 2: Grameen Bharat Mahotsav 2025 – Rural Products ka Mahamela

- New Delhi me **Grameen Bharat Mahotsav 2025** aayojit hua:

**SSC exam points:**

- SHGs – **DAY‑NRLM** ke tahat women SHGs important।  
- FPO – farmers ki producer company, collective marketing।

**Memory Trick – “Gaon ka Mall – Grameen Mahotsav”**

> Socho Delhi me ek bada **“Gaon ka Mall”** laga hai – yehi Grameen Bharat Mahotsav।

## TOPIC 3: Nayi Crop Varieties – ICAR initiatives

- ICAR &amp; state agricultural universities ne 2025‑26 ke liye  
  kai nayi **high‑yielding / disease‑resistant** varieties release ki:  
  - drought tolerant rice/wheat,  
  - pest‑resistant pulses,  
  - improved oilseeds/horticulture।  

**SSC exam points:**

- ICAR – Indian Council of Agricultural Research, HQ New Delhi।  
- Krishi Vigyan Kendras (KVKs) farmer training &amp; demo ke liye।

**Memory Trick – “ICAR = India ka Crop Research”**

> ICAR = **India Crop And Research** (yaad rakhne ke liye fake expansion)।

## TOPIC 4: PM‑KISAN &amp; DBT Architecture

- PM‑KISAN:  
  - 2019 me launch,  
  - har eligible kisan ko **₹6,000/year** (3 instalments)।  
- Payments **Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)** through bank accounts se hote hain।  
- e‑KYC, land record verification, Aadhaar seeding – sab mandatory steps।

**SSC exam points:**

- PM‑KISAN – Ministry of Agriculture &amp; Farmers’ Welfare।  
- DBT – Direct Benefit Transfer (Jan‑Dhan, Aadhaar, mobile – JAM trinity se link)।

**Memory Trick – “3K = 3 kist, 2K + 2K + 2K”**

> PM‑KISAN: 3 kist × ₹2,000 = ₹6,000।
