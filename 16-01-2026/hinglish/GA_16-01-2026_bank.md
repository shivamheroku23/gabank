## TOPIC 1: Decade of Startup India – National Startup Day 2026

### Kya hua?

- **16 January** ko **National Startup Day** manaaya jaata hai, kyunki **Startup India 16 January 2016** ko launch hua tha.  
- 16 January 2026 ko PM **Bharat Mandapam, New Delhi** me ek special programme me **Startup India ke 10 saal** complete hone par startup founders se interact karenge.  
- Event me **different sectors ke founders, incubators, investors** apna experience share karenge; PM next phase ke reforms aur vision batayenge.  
- Pichhle 10 saalon me **2,00,000+ startups** ko Startup India framework ke tahat recognise kiya gaya hai (DPIIT‑recognised).  
- Objective yehi hai ki India **“job creator nation”** bane – capital, incubation, mentorship aur regulatory support ka ecosystem strong ho.

(Official source: PIB – https://www.pib.gov.in/PressReleasePage.aspx?PRID=2214764, Photo: https://static.pib.gov.in/WriteReadData/specificdocs/photo/2021/aug/ph202183101.png)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Startup India se **credit demand, VC/PE flows, fintech growth aur SME banking** directly linked hai – Banking GA me baar‑baar aata hai.  
- MCQ me pooch sakte hain: **Startup India launch year? (2016)**, **National Startup Day kab? (16 January)**, **approx startups kitne? (2 lakh+)**.  
- Mains/interview me ye example use kar sakte ho ki kaise **policy + digital infra + finance** milkar entrepreneurship ko push karte hain.

### Memory Trick – “16–16 for Startups”

> Yaad rakho: **Startup India 16‑01‑2016 ko launch hua, aur National Startup Day har saal 16‑01 ko.** Code: **“16–16 = Startup India”**.

## TOPIC 2: National Campaign on Entrepreneurship – Women‑Led Enterprises ka Booster

### Kya hua?

- **Ministry of Rural Development** ne **National Campaign on Entrepreneurship** launch kiya hai, jiska focus **women‑led enterprises** ko scale karna hai.  
- Ye campaign **DAY–NRLM (Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Rural Livelihoods Mission)** ke under chal raha hai.  
- Targets:  
  - **50,000 Community Resource Persons (CRPs)** ko **enterprise promotion** me train karna.  
  - **50 lakh mahila Self Help Group (SHG) members** ko **Entrepreneurship Development Programme (EDP)** training dena.  
- Focus mainly **non‑farm rural enterprises** par hai – dukaan, services, food processing, chhota manufacturing, etc.  
- Ye initiative pehle se chal rahe **Start‑up Village Entrepreneurship Programme (SVEP)** jaise successful models par based hai, jisse kaafi SHG didiyan chhote business chala rahi hain.  
- Sarkar ka broad goal: **3 crore “Lakhpati Didis”** create karna – har saal कम से कम ₹1 lakh kamane wali SHG members.

(Official context: Rural Development Ministry / DAY–NRLM entrepreneurship campaign; Photo: https://d23flp6yshmrmn.cloudfront.net/static/images/ebimg/entrepreneurship_shg_women_2026.jpg)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Aise programmes se **rural micro‑credit, working capital loans, OD facilities aur digital payments** ki demand badh jati hai – jo banks ke liye business opportunity hai.  
- MCQ angle: **ye campaign kis mission ke under? → DAY–NRLM**, **CRP target? → 50,000**, **EDP target? → 50 lakh mahila SHG members**.  
- Mains me ye ek strong case study hai **financial inclusion + women entrepreneurship + rural non‑farm growth** ka.

### Memory Trick – “50k trainer, 50 lakh learner”

> Short code: **“50k CRP, 50 lakh didi”** – 50,000 trainers, 50 lakh women entrepreneurs.

## TOPIC 4: Global Risks Report 2026 – WEF ne Geoeconomic Confrontation ko No. 1 Risk bola

### Kya hua?

- **World Economic Forum (WEF)** ne **Global Risks Report 2026** release ki, jo near term (2026–2028) ke major global risks ko rank karta hai.  
- Is report ke mutabik **“geoeconomic confrontation”** – yani trade, technology, sanctions, investment controls, supply‑chain restrictions ko geopolitical weapon ki tarah use karna – **sabse bada risk** hai.  
- Doosre top risks me **state‑based armed conflict, misinformation/disinformation, societal polarisation, extreme weather events** shamil hain.  
- India ke context me report **cyber insecurity, inequality aur weak public services** jaise risks ko highlight karti hai.

(Official context: WEF – Global Risks Report 2026; Photo: https://www3.weforum.org/docs/WEF_Global_Risks_Report_2026_Cover.jpg)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Factual Q: **Global Risks Report kaun release karta hai? → World Economic Forum (WEF).**  
- Geoeconomic confrontation ka direct impact **capital flows, FX markets, sanctions compliance, trade finance aur market volatility** par hota hai – jo banking ke liye critical hai.  
- Mains me global headwinds ki baat karte waqt ye report quote kar sakte ho.

### Memory Trick – “Geo‑economics top risk”

> Line yaad rakho: **“WEF Global Risks 2026 me No. 1 = geoeconomic confrontation.”**

## TOPIC 5: Namami Gange – Dehradun me Aqua Life Centre &amp; Dolphin Rescue Ambulance

### Kya hua?

- **Union Jal Shakti Minister C.R. Paatil** ne **Namami Gange Mission** ke under **Wildlife Institute of India (WII), Dehradun** me kuch important aquatic biodiversity initiatives inaugurate kiye.  
- Yahan ek dedicated **“Aqua Life Conservation Monitoring Centre for Ganga and Other Rivers”** set up kiya gaya hai, jisme  
  - **ecotoxicology, aquatic ecology, spatial ecology labs**,  
  - aur **microplastics lab** banaye gaye hain.  
- Ek specialised **Dolphin Rescue Ambulance** bhi launch ki gayi, jo **Ganga river dolphins** – jo India ka **national aquatic animal** hai – ke liye rapid scientific rescue support degi.  
- Saath hi **Indian Skimmer Conservation Project (BNHS)**, **gharial conservation action plan** aur **“Millets for Life”** publication bhi launch hui, jo biodiversity ko nutrition se link karti hai.

(Official source: PIB – https://www.pib.gov.in/PressReleasePage.aspx?PRID=2214531, Photo: https://static.pib.gov.in/WriteReadData/userfiles/image/image003SALC.jpg)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Namami Gange jaise projects **green finance, ESG lending, river‑rejuvenation bonds** se linked hain – modern banking syllabus ka part.  
- MCQ points: **mission ka naam (Namami Gange), institute (WII Dehradun), species (Ganga river dolphin)**.  
- Mains me **sustainable development + climate risk + green banking** ke answer me ye example fit baithta hai.

### Memory Trick – “Dehradun me dolphin wali ambulance”

> Socho **Dehradun ke WII campus me ek van khadi hai jiske upar dolphin ka logo bana hai – Namami Gange Dolphin Rescue Ambulance**.
