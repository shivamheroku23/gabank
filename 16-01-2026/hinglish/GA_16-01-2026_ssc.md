## TOPIC 1: Namami Gange – Dehradun me Aqua Life Centre &amp; Dolphin Rescue Ambulance

### Kya hua?

- **Union Jal Shakti Minister C.R. Paatil** ne **Namami Gange Mission** ke under **Wildlife Institute of India (WII), Dehradun** me kuch bade biodiversity projects ka inauguration kiya.  
- Yahan **“Aqua Life Conservation Monitoring Centre for Ganga and Other Rivers”** establish hua, jisme **ecotoxicology, aquatic ecology, spatial ecology aur microplastics labs** banaye gaye hain.  
- Ek **Dolphin Rescue Ambulance** bhi launch hui, jo **Ganga river dolphins** (India ka national aquatic animal) ko distress me rescue karne ke liye specially design ki gayi hai.  
- Event me **Indian Skimmer Conservation Project (BNHS)**, **gharial conservation action plan** aur **“Millets for Life”** jaise initiatives bhi launch kiye gaye.

(Official source: PIB – Jal Shakti / Namami Gange @ WII; Photo: https://static.pib.gov.in/WriteReadData/userfiles/image/image003SALC.jpg)

### SSC exam points:

- Mission → **Namami Gange**; jagah → **Wildlife Institute of India, Dehradun (Uttarakhand)**.  
- Important species: **Ganga river dolphin (national aquatic animal), Indian Skimmer, gharial**.  
- Focus: sirf river cleaning nahi, balki **aquatic biodiversity conservation + rescue**.

**Memory Trick – “Dehradun = dolphin ambulance + labs”**

> Socho **Dehradun ke campus me ek research building aur ek dolphin wali ambulance khadi hai** – Namami Gange ka biodiversity hub.

## TOPIC 2: Mizoram – “Ginger Capital of India”

### Kya hua?

- **NITI Aayog** ne **Mizoram** ko officially **“Ginger Capital of India”** declare kiya, kyunki yahan ginger ki **production growth aur quality dono high** hain.  
- CM **Lalduhoma** ne farmers ko credit dete hue **Sairang Horticulture Centre (Aizawl se karib 21 km)** me ek **Ginger Processing Unit** ka inauguration kiya.  
- Mizoram Agricultural Marketing Board ke mutabik 2025 me **3.38 crore kg se zyada ginger** procure kiya gaya.  
- Sarkar **passion fruit** jaise high‑value crops ko bhi promote karne ki planning kar rahi hai.

(Official context: Mizoram government / NITI Aayog updates; Photo: https://d23flp6yshmrmn.cloudfront.net/static/images/state_mizoram_ginger_unit_2026.jpg)

### SSC exam points:

- Title **“Ginger Capital of India” → Mizoram**.  
- Important place: **Sairang Horticulture Centre near Aizawl**.  
- Crop: **Ginger (main)**, plus **passion fruit (future focus)**.

**Memory Trick – “Mizo hill = ginger hill”**

> Dimag me **Mizoram ke pahadon par ginger ke खेत aur board likha ‘Ginger Capital of India’** imagine karo.

- Is Task Force ke head **Pullela Gopichand (Chief National Badminton Coach)** hain.  
- Report me ek **National Coach Accreditation Board** banane ka proposal hai jo **coach education, certification aur accreditation** ka apex body hoga.  
- Ek **tiered coaching pathway** recommend kiya gaya – grassroots se le kar national team level tak.  
- Athletes ke TOPS model ki tarah **“TOPS for Coaches”** program propose kiya gaya hai.

(Official source: Ministry of Youth Affairs &amp; Sports; Photo: https://d23flp6yshmrmn.cloudfront.net/static/images/coach_task_force_gopichand_2026.jpg)

### SSC exam points:

- Chairman → **Pullela Gopichand**.  
- Proposed body → **National Coach Accreditation Board**.  
- Analogy → **“TOPS for Coaches”** on lines of **Target Olympic Podium Scheme**.
