## TOPIC 1: Namami Gange – Aquatic Life Monitoring Centre &amp; Dolphin Rescue Ambulance

### What happened?

- **Union Jal Shakti Minister C.R. Paatil** inaugurated landmark **aquatic biodiversity conservation initiatives** under the **Namami Gange Mission** at the **Wildlife Institute of India (WII), Dehradun**.  
- A dedicated **“Aqua Life Conservation Monitoring Centre for Ganga and Other Rivers”** was established to monitor river ecosystems using **ecotoxicology, aquatic ecology, spatial ecology and microplastics labs**.  
- A specialised **Dolphin Rescue Ambulance** was launched to provide rapid, scientifically equipped rescue for **Ganga river dolphins**, India’s **national aquatic animal**.  
- The event also saw the launch of the **Indian Skimmer Conservation Project (BNHS)**, a **gharial conservation action plan**, and a publication **“Millets for Life”** linking biodiversity with food and nutrition.  

(Official source: PIB – Jal Shakti Ministry, Namami Gange initiatives at WII; Photo: https://static.pib.gov.in/WriteReadData/userfiles/image/image003SALC.jpg)

### SSC exam points:

- Mission → **Namami Gange**; location → **Wildlife Institute of India, Dehradun (Uttarakhand)**.  
- Key species: **Ganga river dolphin (national aquatic animal)**, **Indian Skimmer**, **gharial**.  
- Focus → scientific monitoring + rescue of aquatic life, not just river cleaning.

**Memory Trick – “Dehradun = dolphin ambulance &amp; labs”**

> Picture **a dolphin ambulance parked inside a research campus in Dehradun** – that’s the Namami Gange biodiversity hub.

## TOPIC 2: Mizoram – “Ginger Capital of India”

### What happened?

- **NITI Aayog** officially recognised **Mizoram** as the **“Ginger Capital of India”** based on rapid growth and high quality of ginger production.  
- Mizoram Chief Minister **Lalduhoma** highlighted farmers’ role and inaugurated a **Ginger Processing Unit at Sairang Horticulture Centre**, about 21 km from **Aizawl**.  
- The state’s agricultural marketing board reported procurement of **over 3.38 crore kg of ginger in 2025**, signalling strong commercial potential.  
- The government will also promote crops like **passion fruit** as high‑value income generators.

(Official context: Mizoram government agriculture updates &amp; NITI Aayog recognition; Photo: https://d23flp6yshmrmn.cloudfront.net/static/images/state_mizoram_ginger_unit_2026.jpg)

- Important place: **Sairang Horticulture Centre** near **Aizawl**.  
- Crop focus → **ginger** (plus passion fruit as a future high‑value crop).

## TOPIC 3: Gandhinagar Kite Festival 2026 – Patang Utsav

### What happened?

- **Gujarat Chief Minister Bhupendra Patel** inaugurated the **Gandhinagar Kite Festival 2026**, marking the start of **Patang Utsav** in the city.  
- The event coincides with **Uttarayan**, the auspicious period when the sun moves northwards and days start becoming longer.  
- The festival features **colourful kites, international participants and cultural performances**, and is part of the broader **International Kite Festival** brand of Gujarat.  
- While **Ahmedabad** is the traditional hub, the festival now spans cities like **Gandhinagar, Surat, Rajkot and Dholavira**.

(Official context: Gujarat tourism &amp; state government festival coverage; Photo: https://d23flp6yshmrmn.cloudfront.net/static/images/gandhinagar_kite_festival_2026.jpg)

### SSC exam points:

- State → **Gujarat**; city → **Gandhinagar**.  
- Festival linked to **Uttarayan / Makar Sankranti** and kite‑flying culture.  
- One‑liners: International Kite Festival → important for **tourism and culture**.

## TOPIC 4: Coaching Ecosystem Task Force – Report Submitted

### What happened?

- The **Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports** announced that the **Task Force on Unified Framework for Development of Coaching Ecosystem in India** has submitted its report.  
- The Task Force, headed by **Pullela Gopichand (Chief National Badminton Coach)**, designs a **national structure for coach education and accreditation**.  
- Key recommendations include establishing a **National Coach Accreditation Board** as the apex body, and creating a **tiered coaching pathway** from grassroots to national‑team level.  
- The report also proposes a **“TOPS for Coaches”** model, inspired by the **Target Olympic Podium Scheme (TOPS)** for athletes.

(Official source: Ministry of Youth Affairs &amp; Sports release; Photo: https://d23flp6yshmrmn.cloudfront.net/static/images/coach_task_force_gopichand_2026.jpg)

### SSC exam points:

- Chairperson of Task Force → **Pullela Gopichand**.  
- Proposed body → **National Coach Accreditation Board**.  
- Scheme analogy → **“TOPS for Coaches”** on lines of **Target Olympic Podium Scheme**.

**Memory Trick – “Gopichand’s new syllabus – for coaches, not players”**

> Think of **Gopichand holding a textbook titled ‘National Coaching Pathway’**.
