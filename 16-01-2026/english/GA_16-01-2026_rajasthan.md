## TOPIC 1: Heerapura Bus Terminal – New Hub on Ajmer Road, Jaipur

### Kya hua?

- Jaipur city got a new **Heerapura Bus Terminal** on **Ajmer Road**, which has now been formally inaugurated.  
- Around **133 Rajasthan Roadways (RSRTC) buses** on the **Ajmer route** will operate from this terminal.  
- About **76 private stage carriage buses on the Khatu Shyamji route** have also been shifted here.  
- The move is expected to significantly reduce **bus congestion and traffic pressure on the 200‑feet bypass** on the city’s outskirts.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Location‑based MCQ: **Heerapura Bus Terminal is on which road? → Ajmer Road (Jaipur).**  
- Numbers can be tested: **approximately 133 RSRTC buses (Ajmer route) + 76 private buses (Khatu Shyamji route)**.  
- In mains, this can be used as an example of **urban transport decongestion and better terminal planning in Jaipur**.

## TOPIC 5: Khejri Tree Felling in Bikaner’s Pugal Area – Solar Plant Controversy

### Kya hua?

- In **Pugal region of Bikaner district**, large‑scale felling of **khejri trees (Rajasthan’s state tree)** has been reported for setting up a **solar plant**.  
- Villagers and environmental activists allege that around **900 khejri trees** have already been cut in areas like **Bhanipura and Karnisar Bhatiyan**.  
- Protests have grown against perceived administrative delay and alleged misuse of permissions by the so‑called “forest mafia”.  
- Under the banner of **Jeev Raksha Sansthan and other groups**, a **large mahapadav in Bikaner city on 2 February** has been announced to “save khejri”.

- MCQ: **Khejri is the state tree of which state? → Rajasthan.**
