## TOPIC 1: Payments Regulatory Board (PRB)

### Kya hua?

- 2025 me Parliament ne **Payment and Settlement Systems Act, 2007** me संशोधन karke  
  RBI ke andar ek nayi niyamak body **Payments Regulatory Board (PRB)** banayi.  
- Ye संशोधन **9 May 2025** se लागू hua aur PRB ne purani body  
  **BPSS – Board for Regulation and Supervision of Payment and Settlement Systems** ko replace kar diya.  
- Iske baad **5 January 2026** ko Mumbai me PRB ki **pehli meeting** hui:  
  - meeting ki adhyakshata RBI Governor ne ki,  
  - Board ne RBI ke **Department of Payment &amp; Settlement Systems (DPSS)** ke kaam,  
    payment schemes (UPI, NEFT/RTGS, card networks, PPIs) aur risk areas ka review kiya,  
  - draft **“Payments Vision 2028”** par charcha hui,  
  - RBI ke **Survey on Digital Payments** ke main findings members ke saath share kiye gaye.

### SSC exam points:

- PRB kisko replace karta hai? → **BPSS.**  
- PRB kis Act se linked hai? → **Payment and Settlement Systems Act, 2007 (amended).**  
- First meeting: **5 January 2026, Mumbai** (RBI context).

**Memory Trick – “PRB – Payments Rules Board”**

> Bas yaad: **“PRB = Payments Rules Board”** – jo digital payments ke rules dekh raha hai.

## TOPIC 2: Nutrient Based Subsidy (NBS) – Rabi 2025‑26

- Sarkar ne **Rabi 2025‑26** (Oct–Mar) ke liye  
  P&amp;K fertilisers ke liye **Nutrient Based Subsidy (NBS)** rates approve kiye:  
  - N – ₹43.02/kg, P – ₹47.96/kg, K – ₹2.38/kg, S – ₹2.87/kg.  
- Scheme 2010 se chal rahi hai – fertiliser companies ko subsidy nutrient content ke hisaab se milti hai.

### SSC exam points:

- NBS – kis ke liye? → **Phosphatic &amp; Potassic (P&amp;K) fertilisers.**  
- Aim – **balanced fertiliser use**, urea पर dependency kam karna.

**Memory Trick – “NBS = Nutrient Budgeting System”**

> Naam yaad: **“Nutrient Budgeting System”** – govt har kg N‑P‑K‑S ka budget set karti hai.

## TOPIC 3: ONGC–NSTFDC CSR MoU – EMRS Support

- **ONGC** ne **NSTFDC (National Scheduled Tribes Finance &amp; Development Corporation)** ke sath  
  approx **₹28 crore** ka CSR MoU sign kiya:  
  - 11 States/UTs ke **144 Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRS)** ke liye  
    infrastructure &amp; digital facilities improve karne ke liye.  

### SSC exam points:

- NSTFDC – Ministry of Tribal Affairs ke under PSU – STs ke liye financial support.  
- EMRS – tribal students ke liye residential school system.

**Memory Trick – “ONGC ka tel – EMRS ka mel”**

> ONGC ke tel se kamaye paise ka **mel** EMRS schools ke sath – NSTFDC ke through.

## TOPIC 4: Trade Deficit with FTA Partners &amp; Forex Reserves

- NITI Aayog ki **Trade Watch** report ke mutabik  
  **April–June 2025** me **FTA partner deshon** ke sath  
  India ka trade deficit approx **59%** badha (YoY).  
- RBI ke mutabik week ended **26 Dec 2025** me  
  forex reserves me ~**US$ 3.3 bn** ka increase hua.

### SSC exam points:

- Trade deficit = jab imports > exports (value terms).  
- Forex reserves – RBI manage karta hai.

**Memory Trick – “FTA se gate, deficit bhi saath”**

> Free trade agreements se gate khulta hai –  
> agar exports kamzor hon to **deficit** bhi saath aa jata hai.

## TOPIC 5: Payments Vision 2028 – 4S Payments

- RBI ka **Payments Vision 2028** document digital payments ecosystem ke liye direction set karta hai.  
- Goal: **4S** – **Safe, Secure, Smart, Swift** payments.  

### SSC exam points:

- UPI, RuPay, IMPS, NEFT/RTGS, offline payments sab is vision framework me fit hote hain।

**Memory Trick – “4S – chaar S ka sankalp”**

> Safe, Secure, Smart, Swift –  
> chaar S = Bharat ke digital payments ka sankalp.
