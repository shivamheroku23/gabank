### Kya hua?

- January 2026 ke madhya me Rajasthan sarkar ne **lagbhag 25 flagship yojanaon** par hui pragati ka ek consolidated review note jari kiya.  
- In schemes me **grameen‑shehri vikas, krishi, jal sanrakshan, mahila sashaktikaran, rozgar, skill training, aawas, urja** jaise sector cover kiye gaye.

- Mains me “**Rajasthan model of welfare &amp; development**” likhte waqt aap in flagship yojnayon ka group example de sakte ho.  
- Ye yaad dilata hai ki alag‑alag naam wali schemes ka overall goal **poverty reduction + social security + infrastructure** hai.

**Memory Trick – “25 torch, 4 pillar”**

## TOPIC 2: Mukhyamantri Swanidhi Yojana – Shehri Asangathit Mazdooron ke liye Sahyog

### Kya hua?

- Review note me highlight hua ki **Mukhyamantri Swanidhi Yojana** ke zariye **shehri asangathit mazdoor**, jaise construction worker, gig worker, auto/tempo driver, domestic worker, delivery boy etc. ko **short‑term loan** support diya ja raha hai.  
- Yojana ka aim hai ki in logon ko **chhote amount ka sasta loan** mil sake taaki emergency me unhe mahajani/sahukari karz par निर्भर na rehna pade.  
- Loan ke saath‑saath unko **Jan‑Aadhaar se jodkar health, insurance, food security** jaise anya benefits se bhi connect kiya ja raha hai.  
- Urban local bodies aur labour department milkar data collect aur verification ka kaam kar rahe hain.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Rajasthan‑specific question aa sakta hai: **Mukhyamantri Swanidhi Yojana kis varg ke logon ko support karti hai?** – urban unorganised workers.  
- RAS mains me urban livelihood, street vendors, gig economy ke context me ye scheme ek achha local example hai.  
- Police/Patwari level par “**urban livelihood yojana – Mukhyamantri Swanidhi**” yaad rakhna zaroori hai.

**Memory Trick – “Swanidhi = shehar ka swavlambi mazdoor”**

> Visualise karo: **ek mazdoor ya delivery boy jise sahukar ke bajay sarkari scheme se chhota loan mil raha hai – wahi Swanidhi ka idea hai.**

## TOPIC 3: Lado Protsahan Yojana – Ladkiyon ke Janm se Siksha tak Sahayata

### Kya hua?

- State ne apna focus **ladkiyon ke janm se lekar school/college tak** ke liye chal rahi **Lado type protsahan yojnayein** par bhi dikhaya.  
- In schemes me janm par chhoti rakam se shuru karke **school admission, board exam pass, higher education** jaise milestones par alag‑alag kisto me amount diya jata hai.  
- Uddeshya hai ki parivar **beti ko bojh** na samjhe, balki uske janm se hi usko **arthik suraksha + shiksha ka sahyog** mile.  
- Kuch version me cycle/scooter, scholarship, coaching support jaise components bhi jode ja rahe hain.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- RAS/REET me sawal aa sakta hai ki **Rajasthan me ladkiyon ke liye incentive based scheme ka broad purpose kya hai** – janm se shiksha tak samagra support.  
- Women empowerment, gender ratio, girl education wale answers me aap “**Lado Protsahan type yojana**” ka naam likh sakte ho.  
- Rajasthan ka focus **“Beti bachao, beti padhao” ko state‑specific incentives se ground par lana** hai – ye exam ke liye ek clear line hai.

**Memory Trick – “Lado = ladki ki life roadmap”**

> Yaad rakho: **“Lado ke janm se degree tak, har padav par chhota deposit”** – yahi yojana ka simple picture hai.

### Kya hua?

- Review me bataya gaya ki gram panchayat star par **Atal Gyan Kendras** banaye ja rahe hain jahan youth ke liye **computer, internet, competitive exam material, digital learning** ki facility di jaati hai.

- Panchayat level pe ye centres local students ke liye **study hub / career guidance point** ke roop me kaam karte hain.

- Patwari, LDC jaise exams me yeh ek simple one‑liner fact ban sakta hai.

## TOPIC 5: PM Awas (Urban), Swachh Bharat Urban, AMRUT – Shehri Rajasthan ka Infra Push

### Kya hua?

- 25 flagship review me **shehri Rajasthan** ke liye chal rahe kai programme highlight huye:  
  - **Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban)** – EWS/LIG parivaron ke liye affordable houses.  
  - **Swachh Bharat Mission (Urban)** – solid waste, sewage, public toilet aur cleanliness projects.  
  - **AMRUT (Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation)** – shehri drinking water supply, sewerage aur green spaces ke liye.  
- Jaipur, Jodhpur, Kota, Udaipur jaise bade shaharon ke saath‑saath chhote nagar palikaon me bhi in yojnayon se ghar, paani aur safai ke projects chal rahe hain.

### Rajasthan exams ke liye kyun important?

- Rajasthan exams me bar‑bar poochha jata hai ki **PM Awas (Urban), AMRUT, Swachh Bharat Urban** kis type ke sector se related hain.  
- Shehri development chapter me aap likh sakte ho ki **state + centre milkar housing, water, sanitation** par integrated kaam kar rahe hain.  
- Map‑based/ matching type questions me scheme ka naam aur uska focus yaad rakhna important hai.

**Memory Trick – “Shehar ka triangle: Awas–Swachhta–Paani”**

> Ek triangle imagine karo: **ghar (Awas), kachra‑toilet (Swachh Bharat), paani‑nali (AMRUT)** – ye teen shehri Rajasthan ke main theme hain.
