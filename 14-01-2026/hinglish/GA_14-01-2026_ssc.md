## TOPIC 1: December 2025 WPI – Wholesale Inflation 0.83%

### Kya hua?

- Government ne announce kiya ki **December 2025 ka Wholesale Price Index (WPI) inflation 0.83%** raha.  
- October aur November 2025 me WPI negative tha (deflation), isliye December me **positive zone me entry** important change hai.  
- Ye rise mainly **manufactured products, food products, textiles** ki prices badhne se aaya.  
- WPI Food Index ka change approx **0%** ke aas‑paas; CPI retail inflation December me lagbhag **1.3%**.  

(Official source: DPIIT – WPI Press Release; Photo: https://www.insightsonindia.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/wpi_index.png)

### SSC exam points:

- December 2025 WPI inflation = **0.83% (positive)**.  
- WPI → **wholesale/producer prices** ka index; CPI → **retail consumer prices**.  
- Data release body → **Ministry of Commerce &amp; Industry (DPIIT)**.  
- Inflation topic SSC ke **Economy + सरकारी योजनाएँ/मुद्रास्फीति** section me important.

**Memory Trick – “0.83 – WPI ne minus se free”**

> Line: **“0 point 8‑3 – WPI minus se ho gaya free”** → deflation se positive par shift.

## TOPIC 3: RBI Discussion Paper – Urban Co‑operative Banks ko naya licence

### Kya hua?

- **13 January 2026** ko **RBI** ne ek **Discussion Paper** release kiya jisme **Urban Co‑operative Banks (UCBs)** ke लिये phir se naya licensing window kholne ka proposal hai.  
- Focus un **multi‑state co‑operative credit societies** par hoga jo already bade level par kaam kar rahi hain.  
- Proposal me kuch main norms:  
  - Minimum **paid‑up capital ~₹300 crore**.  
  - Kam se kam **10 saal ka operations record**.  
  - **CRAR around 12%**, **Net NPA ~3% se kam**.  
- RBI ne stakeholders se **February 2026 ke madhya tak** comments maange hain.  

(Official source: RBI – Discussion Paper on Licensing of UCBs; Photo: https://www.rbi.org.in/scripts/BS_PressRelease_UCB2026.jpg)

### SSC exam points:

- Discussion Paper → **Urban Co‑operative Banks ke licensing** par.  
- Regulator → **RBI (भारतीय रिज़र्व बैंक)**.  
- Ek line: **minimum paid‑up capital ka figure – ~₹300 crore**.  
- Topic SSC ke **Indian Economy + Banking system** wale part me poocha ja sakta hai.

**Memory Trick – “3 ka rule – 300 capital, 3% NPA”**

> Yaad rakho: **“Urban co‑op bank = 300 crore + 3% se kam NPA”** – bas itna yaad ho to MCQ crack.

## TOPIC 4: Tripura Gramin Bank – India ka पहला solar-powered ATM van

### Kya hua?

- **Tripura Gramin Bank (TGB)** ne **India ka pehla fully solar-powered ATM van** launch kiya, jise naam diya gaya **“TGB on Wheels”**.  
- Ye vans rural/remote areas me ghoom kar logon ko **cash withdrawal, balance enquiry, mini‑statement** jaisi basic services dete hain.  
- Poora system **roof par lagay solar panels + batteries** se chalta hai – yani diesel generator ya normal grid par dependent nahi.  
- Project ko **NABARD** se support mila, aur innovation ke liye **SKOCH Silver Award** bhi mila.  

(Official source: Tripura Gramin Bank / NABARD release; Photo: https://currentaffairs.adda247.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/tripura_gramin_bank_solar_atm_van.jpg)

### SSC exam points:

- Bank ka naam → **Tripura Gramin Bank (Regional Rural Bank)**.  
- Claim → **India ka pehla fully solar‑powered ATM van**.  
- Support → **NABARD**.  
- Topic SSC me **banking + environment (green energy) + rural development** teen section me linked hai.

**Memory Trick – “Tripura ki dhoop se chalne wala ATM”**

> Ek image socho: **छोटी वैन, ऊपर solar panel, side me लिखा TGB on Wheels** – bas yahi picture yaad rakhni hai.
