## TOPIC 1: WPI Inflation December 2025 – 0.83% par back in positive

### Kya hua?

- **Ministry of Commerce &amp; Industry** ne data release kiya ki **Wholesale Price Index (WPI) inflation December 2025** me year‑on‑year **+0.83%** ho gaya.  
- October–November 2025 me WPI negative (deflation zone) tha; December se fir se **positive territory** me aa gaya.  
- Ye jump mainly **manufactured products, food products aur textiles** ke prices badhne ki wajah se aaya; WPI Food Index ka annual change approx **0%** ke aas‑paas raha.  
- Month‑on‑month basis par WPI lagbhag **0.7%** ke aas‑paas badha – matlab cost pressures wapas pick up ho rahe hain.  
- Retail level par **CPI (Consumer Price Index) inflation December 2025 me ~1.3%** ke aas‑paas report hua – abhi bhi RBI ke 4% target se niche, lekin direction important hai.  

(Official source: DPIIT – WPI Press Release December 2025; Photo: https://www.insightsonindia.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/inflation_chart.png)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Prelims me seedha sawal aa sakta hai: **December 2025 ka WPI kitna tha / kya WPI deflation se positive me gaya?**  
- Mains/interview me aap WPI vs CPI ka example de sakte ho – **WPI mainly wholesale/producer prices, CPI household consumption** ko capture karta hai.  
- Monetary policy discussion me ye point useful hai ki **input‑cost pressure** (WPI) wapas aa raha hai, jo future me CPI ko bhi upar push kar sakta hai.

### Memory Trick – “0.83 = WPI back to tree”

> Yaad rakho: **“0 point 8‑3 – WPI chadh gaya tree”** → 0.83% ka matlab WPI deflation se wapas upar chadh gaya.

## TOPIC 2: Growth Outlook – FAE 7.4%, World Bank GEP me India 7.2%

### Kya hua?

- **MoSPI (Ministry of Statistics &amp; Programme Implementation)** ne **First Advance Estimates (FAE) of National Income FY 2025-26** release kiye:  
  - Real GDP growth estimate lagbhag **7.4%** ke aas‑paas.  
  - Real GVA growth ~**7.3%**; manufacturing aur services dono strong drivers.  
- Saath hi **World Bank ke Global Economic Prospects (GEP) – January 2026** report ne India ki growth FY 2025-26 ke liye lagbhag **7.2%** project ki, jabki global growth sirf **2.6% (2026)** ke kareeb dikhaya.  
- Report ke mutabik India **fastest growing major economy** bani rahegi – domestic demand, investment aur reforms iske main pillars hain.  
- Ye sab estimates January ke first‑half me news me the, isliye 14 January ke CA ka core part bante hain.  

(Official source: MoSPI FAE Press Note; World Bank – Global Economic Prospects Jan 2026; Photo: https://www.worldbank.org/content/dam/photos/780x439/2026/jan/growth_india_gep2026.jpg)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Pre level MCQ me pooch sakte hain: **FAE ke hisaab se FY 2025-26 ke liye India ki GDP growth approx kitni estimate ki gayi hai?**  
- World Bank/IMF/ADB forecasts har saal Banking GA ka favourite topic hote hain – **kis institution ne India ke liye kitna forecast diya** ye yaad rakhna hota hai.  
- Mains/interview me aap isko use kar sakte ho ki **India growth story abhi bhi global slowdown ke beech relatively bright spot** hai.

### Memory Trick – “7 ka family: 7.4 (FAE), 7.2 (World Bank)”

> Code banao: **“FAE four, Bank two – dono 7 ke family crew”** → FAE ≈ 7.4%, World Bank ≈ 7.2%.

## TOPIC 3: RBI Discussion Paper – Urban Co-operative Banks ko phir se licence

### Kya hua?

- **13 January 2026** ko **Reserve Bank of India (RBI)** ne ek **Discussion Paper (DP)** release kiya jisme **Urban Co‑operative Banks (UCBs)** ko **phir se licence dene** ka proposal rakha gaya.  
- New licensing ka focus mainly un **multi‑state co‑operative credit societies** par hoga jo already large scale par kaam kar rahi hain.  
- Proposed eligibility norms (illustrative):  
  - Minimum **paid‑up capital ~₹300 crore**.  
  - Kam se kam **10 saal ka operations track record**.  
  - **CRAR ≥ 12%** aur **Net NPA &lt; 3%** ke aas‑paas.  
- RBI ne stakeholders se **13 February 2026** tak comments mangey hain; uske baad final guidelines aayengi.  

(Official source: RBI Discussion Paper on Licensing of UCBs – Jan 2026; Photo: https://www.rbi.org.in/scripts/BS_PressRelease_UCB2026.jpg)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Ye topic **co‑operative banking + financial inclusion + regulation** teenon ko link karta hai.  
- MCQ me pooch sakte hain ki **RBI ka proposal kis segment ke liye hai? (Urban Co‑operative Banks)**, ya **minimum capital / NPAs / CRAR type conditions** conceptual level par.  
- Mains/interview me ye ek fresh example hai ki RBI ka approach ab **“limited but strong”** UCB sector banane ki taraf hai – weak chhote players ke bajay strong well‑governed entities.

### Memory Trick – “300 capital, 10 saal, 3% NPA – tabhi naya UCB bana”

> Line yaad karo: **“3 number ka rule – 300 crore capital, 10 years history (1+0=1 digit), 3% se kam NPA”**.

## TOPIC 4: Tripura Gramin Bank – India ka pehla fully solar-powered ATM Van

### Kya hua?

- **Tripura Gramin Bank (TGB)** ne January 2026 me **India ka pehla fully solar-powered ATM van** launch kiya, jise branding di gayi **“TGB on Wheels”**.  
- Pehle ye mobile ATM vans normal power par chal rahe the; ab upgrade ke baad **100% solar energy se powered** hain – roof par solar panels, battery storage aur energy‑efficient systems.  
- Ye vans remote aur rural areas me ghoom‑ghoom kar **ATM withdrawal, balance enquiry, mini‑statement** jaise basic services provide karte hain – jahan regular branches ya ATMs nahi pahunch paate.  
- Project ko **NABARD support** mila aur innovation ke liye **SKOCH Silver Award** bhi recognize kiya gaya.  

(Official source: Tripura Gramin Bank / NABARD release; Photo: https://currentaffairs.adda247.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/tripura_gramin_bank_solar_atm_van.jpg)

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- MCQ me direct puch sakte hain: **India ka pehla fully solar‑powered ATM van kis bank ne launch kiya? → Tripura Gramin Bank.**  
- Ye example **green banking + financial inclusion + RRBs ki role** ko ek hi story me dikhata hai – mains answer me quote karne layak.  
- RRBs (Regional Rural Banks) aur NABARD ke beech partnership ka ek fresh case study bhi mil jata hai.

### Memory Trick – “Tripura ki dhoop + ATM ka roop”

> Socho: **chhota sa bank van, roof par solar panel, neeche likha ‘TGB on Wheels’ – dhoop se chalta ATM**.
