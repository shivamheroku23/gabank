## TOPIC 1: DoT – NCCS ke 3 Telecom Security Reforms

### Kya hua?

- **Department of Telecommunications (DoT)** ne apne **National Centre for Communication Security (NCCS)** ke zariye **3 major reforms** launch kiye (December 2025, 31 Dec CA compilations me cover):  
  - Telecom equipment ke liye **stronger security testing & certification**.  
  - **Telecom Security Assurance Requirements (TSAR)** ko update/tighten karna – khaaskar 5G, core network, signalling.  
  - Bharat me **testing labs ecosystem** ko expand/support karna – taaki desh ka telecom network “secure-by-design” ho.  
- Objective:  
  - Bharat ke **telecom network, data & voice traffic** ko cyber attacks, spyware, malware se protect karna.  
  - Future digital services (UPI, DigiLocker, e‑governance, online education etc.) ko secure base provide karna.

### SSC exam points:

- NCCS full form: **National Centre for Communication Security**.  
- NCCS kis department ke under hai? → **Department of Telecommunications (DoT)**, Ministry of Communications.  
- Reforms ka main focus: **Telecom network security & testing.**

### Memory Trick:

- Line yaad rakho:  
  > **“Network ka Security Doctor – NCCS, DoT ka lab.”**

- Code: **NCCS = Network & Communication Cyber Security.**

## TOPIC 2: National Weapon Database – India ka Pehla National Arms Record

### Kya hua?

- Bharat Sarkar ne **India ka pehla National Weapon Database** launch kiya (Different CA compilations 31 Dec 2025 ke daily current affairs me include karte hain):  
  - Desh bhar ke **licensed firearms** ka central digital record banega.  
  - State police, central agencies, intelligence units – sab is database se linked rahenge.  
- Objective:  
  - **Terrorism, organised crime, extremism** ke against strong data support.  
  - Illegal weapons, fake licences, suspicious pattern (bar‑bar transfer, heavy concentration) detect karna.

### SSC exam points:

- Question: “National Weapon Database ka main objective kya hai?”  
  → **Licensed weapons ka national-level digital record, internal security strengthen karna.**  
- Related areas:  
  - Internal Security, Police reforms, Modernisation of Criminal Justice.

### Memory Trick:

- Socho har bandook ko ek **Aadhaar number** diya gaya.  
- Line:  
  > **“Gun ka Aadhaar = National Weapon Database.”**

## TOPIC 3: EPFO Modernisation & Social Security Expansion

### Kya hua?

- Government ne **Employees’ Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO)** ko modernise karne ke multiple steps highlight kiye (2025 end tak):  
  - EPFO processes ko **end-to-end digital** banane ki direction:  
    - e‑Nomination, e‑KYC, online claim, digital passbook.  
  - **Coverage expand** karne ki planning – unorganised sector, gig workers tak social security le jana.  
  - **PDUNASS (Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya National Academy of Social Security)** ne IC Centre for Governance ke saath mil kar **ethics in social security administration** par kaam start kiya.

*(Kuch details CA compilations ke aadhar par; exact language sources ke beech vary ho sakta hai.)*

### SSC exam points:

- EPFO: estd **1952**, HQ **New Delhi**, ministry – **Ministry of Labour & Employment**.  
- PDUNASS: **EPFO ki training academy**.  
- Keywords: **social security**, **retirement savings**, **pension, PF**.

### Memory Trick:

- EPFO ke liye line:  
  > **“Employee ka PF Office – EPFO, 1952 se future secure kar raha hai.”**  

- PDUNASS:  
  > **“Deendayal ji ki Social Security Academy.”**
