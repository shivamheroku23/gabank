## TOPIC 1: DoT – NCCS ke 3 Bade Telecom Security Reforms

### Kya hua?

- **Department of Telecommunications (DoT)** ne apne **National Centre for Communication Security (NCCS)** ke through **3 major reforms** announce kiye (end-December 2025 context, 31 Dec CA compilations me highlight):  
  - Telecom equipment & networks ke liye **security testing / certification framework ko strengthen** karna.  
  - **Telecom Security Assurance Requirements (TSAR)** ko aur tight karna – khaaskar 5G, core network, signalling, etc.  
  - **Testing labs & ecosystem** (IN country) ko empower karna, taaki Bharat imported equipment par blindly dependent na rahe.
- Aim:  
  - Bharat ki **telecom infrastructure ko cyber attacks, espionage, fraud** se protect karna.  
  - **Secure-by-design networks** jisme har naya software/hardware pehle certified ho.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Aaj ke time me banking = **digital + telecom**:  
  - UPI, IMPS, Net/Mobile banking, OTP – sab **telecom network** par chal rahe hain.  
- Strong telecom security ka matlab:  
  - **Less call/SMS spoofing → kam OTP fraud.**  
  - Network outage kam → **UPI/NEFT downtime risk kam.**  
- Exams me pucha ja sakta hai:  
  - NCCS kis ministry/department ke under hai? → **DoT (Ministry of Communications).**  
  - Telecom security reforms ka main objective – **secure digital ecosystem** jisme banking / fintech ko indirect benefit.

### Memory Trick – “NCCS = Network ka Cyber Security Shield”

- Code line yaad rakho:  
  > **NCCS – Network ka Cyber Security Shield**, jisse **UPI / Net banking ka lifeline (telecom)** secure rahe.

- Link: **N (Network), C (Communication), C (Cyber), S (Security).**

## TOPIC 2: National Weapon Database – Internal Security & KYC Angle

### Kya hua?

- Centre ne India ka **pehla National Weapon Database** launch kiya (compilations me 31 Dec 2025 CA ke part me):  
  - Desh bhar me licensed weapons ka **centralised digital record** banega.  
  - State police, intelligence agencies, central agencies – sabko **real-time access** milega.  
- Objective:  
  - **Terrorism, organised crime, extremism** ke khilaaf ladai strong karna.  
  - Illegal weapons tracking + suspicious patterns (bulk purchase, repeated transfer) detect karna.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Direct banking nahi, lekin **KYC + suspicious transaction reporting (STR)** ke context me important:  
  - Jab bhi cash/transfer **terror / organised crime / arms dealing** se linked ho, banks ko **STR file** karna hota hai (PMLA, FIU-IND).  
  - National Weapon Database se **law enforcement ko lead** mil sakta hai; bank transaction data + weapon database → joint analysis.  
- Mains me essay/analysis:  
  - **“Fintech, KYC & internal security”** – isme example ke roop me use kar sakte ho.

### Memory Trick – “Gun ka Aadhaar”

- Socho har legal weapon ko ek **Aadhaar-type ID** mil gaya.  
- Line yaad rakho:  
  > National Weapon Database = **“Gun ka Aadhaar”**, jo **terror funding aur arms network ko trace** karta hai.

- Exams me:

## TOPIC 4: India ka Banking & Credit Cycle – 2025 ke End Tak Picture

### Kya hua?

- 2025 ke end (mid-Dec tak RBI data, December research reports):  
  - **Bank credit growth ~12% YoY**,  
  - **Deposit growth ~9.4% YoY** ke aas-paas.  
  - Matlab: **loans deposits se tez badh rahe hain** → credit–deposit gap.  
- **Incremental Credit–Deposit Ratio (CDR)** rolling 3‑month basis pe high tha:  
  - Fresh deposits ke mukable **fresh loans** zyada.  
- Liquidity:  
  - 2025 me zyada time **structurally easy liquidity**, OMO & FX operations ke through support.  
- Market expectation (reports ke mutabik):  
  - 2026 ke shuru me RBI se **ek aur 25 bps repo cut** ki possibility.

### Bank exam ke liye kyun important?

- Concepts:  
  - **Credit growth vs deposit growth** – agar credit faster hai →  
    - Banks ko **CDs, bonds, borrowings** se funding leni pad sakti hai.  
    - **Cost of funds** rise → NIM pressure.  
  - CD ratio, incremental CD ratio – **classic exam topics**.  
- Mains descriptive:  
  - “Discuss India’s banking credit cycle in 2025 and implications for 2026.”

### Memory Trick – “12–9 ka Gap”

- Code line:  
  > 2025 end – **Credit 12%, Deposit 9%** – teen point ka gap, **NIM pe pressure ka trap.**

- “12–9 = 3” – **3 point ka gap** = banks ko **funding aur risk management** pe dhyan dena.
