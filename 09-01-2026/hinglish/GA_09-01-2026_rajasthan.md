### TOPIC 1: Rajasthan AI-ML Policy 2026 – “Smart Rajasthan” ke liye AI

### Kya Hua?

- Rajasthan sarkar ne **Rajasthan Artificial Intelligence–Machine Learning (AI-ML) Policy 2026** launch ki, jise **Department of Information Technology &amp; Communication (DoIT&amp;C)** ne **7 January 2026** ko officially notify kiya.  
- Chief Minister **Bhajanlal Sharma** ne policy ke saath **National AI Literacy Programme** bhi shuru kiya, jiska aim students, govt staff aur youth ko **basic + advanced AI skills** dena hai.  
- Policy ka focus **ethical, transparent aur citizen-centric AI use** par hai – yani AI ka istemal public service ko fast, fair aur safe banane ke liye.  
- Priority sectors: **public service delivery, urban governance, health, education, agriculture, policing, grievance redressal** – jahan AI se data-based decisions liye ja sakte hain.  
- Document mein **AI Centre of Excellence**, common computing &amp; data infra, aur **data protection + cyber security** ke liye clear framework propose kiya gaya hai.  
- Har department ko apne **AI use-cases identify** karne, **AI nodal officer** appoint karne aur pehle pilot projects chalakar phir scale-up karne ko kaha gaya hai.  
- (Official source: DoIT&amp;C Rajasthan – “RAJASTHAN AI/ML POLICY 2026”, order 07/01/2026 – https://doitc.rajasthan.gov.in/writereaddata/PoliciesGuidelinesOrders/202601081149516478189AIMLpolicy2026.pdf)  

### Rajasthan Exams ke liye Important Kyun?

- **State policies** – jaise Rajasthan AI-ML Policy 2026 – RAS prelims, mains, Junior Accountant, Informatics Assistant, sabhi exams ke liye hot topics hain.  
- Yaad rakhne layak points: **policy year 2026**, **nodal department – DoIT&amp;C**, CM **Bhajanlal Sharma**, focus – ethical &amp; practical AI in governance.  
- Essays / mains answers mein is policy ko **Digital Rajasthan, employment in new-age sectors, cyber security, start-up ecosystem** ke context mein quote kiya ja sakta hai.  
- Police/administration exams ke liye yeh example hai ki **predictive policing, smart city, grievance analytics** jaisi cheezein AI se kaise strengthen ho sakti hain.  

### Memory Trick – “AI se Smart Rajasthan 2026”

> Line banao: **“AI-ML Policy 2026 = AI se Smart Rajasthan 2026”** – yaad karo: AI governance + AI literacy, dono ek hi policy umbrella ke andar.

### TOPIC 2: Divisional-level pre-budget consultations – “Budget pehle, baat sabse”

### Kya Hua?

- **Finance Department, Government of Rajasthan** ne **7 January 2026** ko ek notice issue kiya – title: **“Prior budget stakeholders consultations at Divisional level”**.  
- Is circular ke through sabhi **Divisional Commissioners** ko bola gaya ki **State Budget se pehle** apne- apne division mein stakeholders ke saath meetings organise karein.  
- In meetings mein aam taur par **industry &amp; trade bodies, farmers’ organisations, small businesses, social-sector organisations, experts** jaise groups se budget ke liye suggestions liye jaate hain.  
- Ye suggestions phir compile karke Finance Department ko bheje jaayenge, jisse **tax proposals, schemes aur departmental allocations** decide karte waqt ground realities reflect ho sakein.  
- Is step se Rajasthan ka budget process aur bhi **participatory, consultative aur evidence-based** ban raha hai.  
- (Official source: Finance Dept, Govt of Rajasthan – “Prior budget stakeholders consultations at Divisional level”, What’s New, 07/01/2026 – https://finance.rajasthan.gov.in)  

### Rajasthan Exams ke liye Important Kyun?

- **Budget-making process** RAS, Junior Accountant, Accounts Assistant, Patwari jaise exams ka favourite theory topic hai.  
- Simple MCQ: consultations kis level par – **Divisional level**.  
- Governance angle se ye **decentralisation + participation** ka strong example hai – field inputs se state budget ban raha hai.  
- Mains answers mein “participatory budgeting”, “fiscal transparency”, “inclusive policy making in Rajasthan” ke example ke roop mein likha ja sakta hai.  

### Memory Trick – “Budget se pehle Division ka decision”

> Yaad rakho: **“Budget se pehle Division ka decision”** – matlab divisional-level meetings se hi Rajasthan budget ke ideas upar tak pahunchte hain.

### TOPIC 3: NGSP + Jan Soochna – Rajasthan ki digital “one-stop” window

### Kya Hua?

- **National Government Services Portal (NGSP)** – https://services.india.gov.in – par **Rajasthan state services page (state ID 3065)** bana hua hai jahan Rajasthan ke dozens online/related services list hain.  
- Portal metadata ke mutabik Rajasthan listing ka last update **9 January 2026** ke around dikh raha hai – matlab yahan di gayi services fairly recent status ko reflect karti hain.  
- Yahin se ek jagah par aapko milte hain:  
  - **Online social pension &amp; medical reimbursement services**,  
  - **Rajasthan Sampark** grievance portal,  
  - Vehicle ke liye **fancy number booking**,  
  - **Soil Health Card, PM-Kisan instalment payment services**,  
  - **Ration card Aadhaar seeding, transfer, re-print** jaise options.  
- Saath hi **Jan Soochna Portal** – https://jansoochna.rajasthan.gov.in/Scheme – alag-alag departments ki **scheme-wise details, eligibility, benefits aur entitlements** public domain mein deta hai.  
- Dono milkar Rajasthan ke liye **“service discovery + scheme information” ka single-window layer** bana dete hain; actual application submission related departmental portals par hota hai.  

### Rajasthan Exams ke liye Important Kyun?

- RAS, REET, Police, Patwari sabhi exams mein **state schemes aur portals** par direct one-liner MCQs aa sakte hain.  
- Typical sawal:  
  - central aggregator portal ka naam? – **NGSP (services.india.gov.in)**.  
  - Rajasthan ki schemes ka detailed info kahan? – **Jan Soochna Portal**.  
- Mains ke liye yeh **pro-active disclosure, RTI 2.0, digital inclusion** jaise buzzwords ke saath likhne layak solid Rajasthan example hai.  
- Social pension, food security, grievance redress, agriculture support – in sab par sawal aane par aap in portals ka reference de sakte hain.  

### Memory Trick – “Services NGSP par, Schemes Jan Soochna par”

> Short formula: **“Services NGSP par, Schemes Jan Soochna par – dono milkar One-stop Rajasthan banate hain.”**

### TOPIC 4: Pravasi Bharatiya Diwas 2026 – Diaspora as “Rashtradoots”

### Kya Hua?

- **Pravasi Bharatiya Diwas (PBD)** har saal **9 January** ko celebrate hota hai, purpose – India ke bahar rehne wale Bharatiyaon ke contribution ko salute karna.  
- Ye date isliye chuni gayi kyunki **9 January 1915** ko **Mahatma Gandhi South Africa se Bharat wapas aaye** the – jo freedom struggle ka turning point maana jata hai.  
- **9 January 2026** ko PM **Narendra Modi** ne Indian diaspora ko greet karte hue unhe **“Rashtradoots” (nation ke ambassadors)** aur **India–world ke beech powerful bridge** kaha.  
- PM ne diaspora ki role ko highlight kiya – **Indian culture, yoga, cuisine, democracy** ko duniya bhar mein popular karne mein unka yogdan.  
- PBD convention (jo do saal mein ek baar hota hai) diaspora engagement, investment aur knowledge-sharing ka bada platform hai.  

### Rajasthan Exams ke liye Important Kyun?

- Date-based questions – **9 January = Pravasi Bharatiya Diwas** – RAS, REET, police, RSMSSB har jagah puchhe ja sakte hain.  
- Rajasthan ke bahut se log Gulf, Africa, Europe jaise regions mein kaam karte hain – migration/remittance wale answers mein PBD ka reference strong point ban sakta hai.  
- Confusion wala MCQ: **Pravasi Bharatiya Diwas (9 January)** vs **Hindi Diwas (14 September)** – is difference ko clear rakhna zaroori hai.  

### Memory Trick – “Gandhi ghar aaye, NRI din ban gaya”

> Line yaad rakho: **“9 January – Gandhi ghar aaye, isi din NRI Day banaya gaya.”** – Gandhi ka return South Africa se, aur aaj ke din diaspora ko honour.
