## TOPIC 1: RBI–Delhi Government MoU – Capital ka Banker RBI

### Kya hua?

- **RBI** aur **Government of NCT of Delhi** ne **6 January 2026** ko ek **Memorandum of Understanding (MoU)** sign kiya.  
- Is MoU ke baad **9 January 2026** se:  
  - RBI Delhi sarkar ka **official banker, debt manager aur financial agent** ban gaya.  
  - Delhi ke saare **receipts, payments, treasury operations** RBI ke through chalenge.  
  - Delhi ke **State Development Loans (SDLs)** RBI auction karega, yani market se direct borrowing.  
- Pehle Delhi ko infrastructure ke liye zyadatar **12–13% ke high-cost loans** lene padte the;  
  ab **SDLs ke through ~7% ke aas‑paas** pe fund raise ho paayega.  
- RBI Delhi ko **Ways and Means Advances (WMA) &amp; Special Drawing Facility (SDF)** bhi dega,  
  jisse short‑term cash shortage handle ho sakta hai.  

### SSC exam points:

- RBI – **Government ke banker, debt manager, financial advisor** ke roop me kaam karta hai.  
- Ab **Delhi (UT with legislature)** bhi **RBI banking arrangement** me include hai.  
- Borrowing instrument: **State Development Loans (SDLs)**.  
- Impact: **low-cost borrowing + better fiscal discipline**.  

**Memory Trick – “Dilli ka khata, RBI ka data”**

> Yaad rakho: **“Dilli ka khata RBI ke saath, sasta loan aur zyada saaf hisaab‑kitab.”**

## TOPIC 2: India ki Pehli Hydrogen-Powered Train – Jind–Sonipat Route

### Kya hua?

- Indian Railways ne announce kiya ki **India ki pehli hydrogen-powered train**  
  ka trial **Haryana ke Jind–Sonipat route (approx 90 km)** par hoga.  
- Media reports ke mutabik trial **January 2026**, expected start **26 January 2026** ke aas‑paas planned hai  
  (final safety clearance ke subject me).  
- Train:  
  - **Integral Coach Factory (ICF), Chennai** ne develop ki,  
  - **Hydrogen fuel-cell technology** par chalegi – exhaust me mainly **water vapour**,  
    yani local level par **zero CO₂ emission**.  
- Jind me **hydrogen production &amp; refuelling plant** set up ho raha hai  
  jo electrolysis se **green hydrogen** banayega.  

### SSC exam points:

- Route: **Jind–Sonipat (Haryana)** – first hydrogen train pilot section.  
- Objective: **clean energy, low-emission transport, Make in India tech**.  
- Develop kiya gaya by: **Integral Coach Factory (ICF), Chennai** + **RDSO** specs.  

**Memory Trick – “Paani ka dhuan, Haryana ka train safar”**

> Socho train ke engine se **dhuaan nahi, paani ka bhaap** nikal raha hai –  
> yaad rahega hydrogen train **Jind–Sonipat, Haryana** par.

## TOPIC 3: Prime Minister Research Chair (PMRC) – 120 Global Indian Scientists ko IITs me Lana

### Kya hua?

- IIT Council ne ek naya proposal discuss kiya: **Prime Minister Research Chair (PMRC) scheme**.  
- Plan: **5 saal me lagbhag 120 top Indian-origin scientists &amp; researchers** ko India wapas laana  
  aur unke liye **IITs me high-level research positions** create karna.  
- Scheme ke under 3 broad categories:  
  - **Young Research Fellows**,  
  - **Senior Research Fellows**,  
  - **Research Chairs**.  
- Focus areas: ~**14 national priority sectors** – jaise **semiconductors, AI, clean energy, advanced materials** etc.  

### SSC exam points:

- Scheme ka naam: **Prime Minister Research Chair (PMRC)**.  
- Target: **~120 Indian-origin scientists** over 5 years.  
- Implementing system: **Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs)**.  
- Purpose: **reverse brain drain, high-end R&amp;D boost, Viksit Bharat 2047 vision**.  

**Memory Trick – “120 Chairs, Brain Gain not Brain Drain”**

> Line: **“PM Research Chair: 120 kursiyan, bade-bade scientists ke liye – brain drain ko reverse.”**

### Kya hua?

  - **A. K. Balasubrahmanian** ne **1 January 2026** ko **AERB Chairman** ke roop me charge liya.

- **Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO)**:  
  - **1 January 2026** ko **68th Foundation Day** celebrate kiya.  
  - DRDO ki स्थापना **1958** me hui thi (1 January 1958).  
  - Recent achievements highlight: **22 AoN approvals (~₹1.3 lakh crore)**,  
    **11 defence contracts (~₹26,000 crore)**, aur **cyber, space, AI** jaise new domains par focus.  

### SSC exam points:

- AERB:  
  - Full form: **Atomic Energy Regulatory Board**.  
  - New Chairman: **A. K. Balasubrahmanian**.  
- DRDO:  
  - Full form: **Defence Research and Development Organisation**.  
  - Established: **1958**, Headquarters: **New Delhi**.  
  - Foundation Day: **1 January** (2026 me 68th).  

**Memory Trick – “AERB ka AK, DRDO ka Day”**

## TOPIC 5: e-B-4 Electronic Business Visa – Chinese Nationals ke Liye

### Kya hua?

- India ne **1 January 2026** se ek naya **e-B-4 Production Investment Business Visa** launch kiya.  
- Target group: **Chinese nationals** jo India me  
  manufacturing, equipment installation, commissioning, training, IT/ERP implementation,  
  supply chain support jaise business works ke liye aate hain.  
- Key features:  
  - Stay up to **6 mahine**.  
  - Application **purely online** – Indian e-visa portal + National Single Window System (NSWS)  
    jahan se Indian companies sponsorship initiate kar sakti hain.  
  - Processing time: approx **45–50 din**.  
  - Arrival par **biometrics capture**, 14 din ke andar **FRRO electronic registration**.  

### SSC exam points:

- Visa naam: **e-B-4 Production Investment Business Visa**.  
- Country focus: **China ke nationals**.  
- Maximum stay: **6 months**.  
- Objective: **electronics, renewable energy, manufacturing projects ke liye technical experts ko fast-track entry**.  

**Memory Trick – “B-4 matlab Business For China”**

> Yaad rakho: **“e-B-4 = Business For (Chinese experts)”** –  
> naya visa category jo India–China industrial projects ke liye banaya gaya.
